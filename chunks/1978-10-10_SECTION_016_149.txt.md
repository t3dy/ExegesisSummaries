# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-149

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-149
- **Themes:** Coaxial Switchover, The Final World, Time Surgery, Trans-realm documents.

## Key Claims
- **Coaxial World-Spinner:** Valis is a "Spinner" that generates multiple coaxial worlds. He moves you from one to another by changing your "memory and personality" so you think you've always been in the new one.
- **"Tears" as Evidence:** *Flow My Tears* is a "trans-realm document" smuggled from the Prison-world into our Purgatory-world to provide "news" of the "Happy King's" success.
- **The Happy King:** The name for the successful introduction of Christ into the "Fully Fallen/Tears" realm.
- **Transplantation from Track A to B:** The "Rescuer" was physically inserted (as an embryo into a womb) into our world via "surgery-like time-travel tinkering." This made the "Final World" possible.
- **Memory as Reality:** If memory changes, *reality* changes. The 3-74 "phosphene graphics" were decades of new memories being laid down in hours.

## Entities
- **Valis / The Spinner:** The entity behind the "multiple world" masks.
- **Thomas:** The Catholic/well-to-do persona from the "Tears" world.
- **The Head Apollo:** The returning divine intellect.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Orthogonal Form Axes:** The layout of reality across which Valis creates "fake memories" at high speed.
- **Signature of Authentication:** The "two-word cypher" in *Tears* proving its trans-realm source.
- **Virgin Birth / Immaculate Conception:** The technological insertion of a "Psyche" or "Embryo" into a woman's womb.

## Theological Motifs
- **Geheimnistrager (Secret-Carrier):** PKD's new status as one who knows the "Work is Completed."
- **Purgatorio:** Our mixed world; a fusion of two signals (Upper and Lower).
- **The Garden:** The "Paradiso" track that has now been successfully brought into existence.
