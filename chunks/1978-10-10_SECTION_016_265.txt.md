# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-265

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-265
- **Themes:** The Ontogenic Revolt, Valis as Rebel YHWH, The Secret Stolen past the Angels.

## Key Claims
- **The Individual's Revolt:** The individual "I" (ontogenic self) rebels against the expendability imposed by the impersonal "DNA will." This is the "Chemical Wedding" of the self with freedom.
- **The Secret Stolen:** The phrase "The secret stolen through the angels in one's hands" refers to a rebellion against the "angels" (thrones/powers) whom God originally appointed as cosmic governors.
- **Tyrannical Guardians:** Like Paul's view, the celestial powers (Angels/Torah) have become a reactionary tyranny from which Christ (the rebel messenger) emancipates us.
- **Valis as Revolutionary YHWH:** PKD proposes the "final secret": **Valis is YHWH** himself, re-entering his own creation as an "invader" and "rebel" to dismantle his own repressive system of Law.
- **The Beside-Helper:** The Paraclete (Helper) is a "late strategy" of the Divine to extricate individuals from the blind DNa drive.
- **Stolen Medicine:** The secret (Gnosis) is the "medicine" that brings sight, freedom, and comprehension—stolen past the Archons.

## Entities
- **Paul's Angels:** The powers/dominions equating to "astral determinism."
- **The Snake/Lucifer:** Gnostic inversion where the "rebel" is the benefactor.
- **James-James:** The "insane genius" creator deity from PKD's dream.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Exoneration:** God's late strategy to release individuals from the "bill of particulars" (The Law).
- **Transplantation:** The divine returning from the "periphery" to the "center."
- **Observer-Participant:** The field where the perturbation points to the divine will.

## Theological Motifs
- **Gnostic Messenger:** The figure who brings the *medicine* for the "information virus."
- **Cosmic Rebellion:** Siding with the "rebellious messenger" is actually siding with the *True* returning God.
- **Lifting the Occlusion:** The core act of the "physician" savior.
