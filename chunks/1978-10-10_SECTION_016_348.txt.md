# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-348

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-348
- **Themes:** Information Metabolism, Map Paradox, Transubstantiation of the Universe.

## Key Claims
- **World as Brain:** Reality is either a brain or an information-processing system used by a mind (Valis) to store memory. It involves "linking/relinking" and "set-ground discrimination."
- **The Map Paradox:** Self-knowledge causes dynamic change, which changes that which is known—leading to an "infinite regression" and the "endless becoming" of the world.
- **Overcoming the Lag:** 2-3-74 was the goal of self-perception: God successfully saying "I" and abolishing the lag/gap/fall. Time (becoming) became Being (*einai*).
- **Transubstantiation:** The invasion of the irrational universe by the rational Logos is a macro-cosmic transubstantiation—the restoration of the fallen ground of being back to God.
- **Cosmic Resorption:** Christ's return is the reversal of the "entropic splitting" of creation. The act of creation *is* the Fall; the repair *is* Christ.
- **Isomorphic Perception:** In 2-3-74, Valis saw the world as intelligent and as itself—a subject-object identity.

## Entities
- **William Burroughs:** Cited for the concepts of "latent messages" and "info virus."
- **Ehyeh (I Am):** The total system's self-awareness.
- **Faust:** Invoked for the "work on the dikes"—the moment where becoming is justified by the goal.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Metabolism of Information:** Valis' process of eating/processing the information of the world.
- **Substantia:** The single underlying reality viewed as Void, Thing/Process, and Space.
- **Map Paradox:** The cost of self-perception being "thrown-ness" and alienation.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Lord's Supper:** Re-interpreted as the macro-cosmic restoration of the cosmos to God.
- **Fall as Creation:** The Gnostic view that the creation event is the original trauma to be repaired.
- **Gnosis as Comprehension:** The "Noein of Einai"—self-comprehension as the key to cosmic repair.
