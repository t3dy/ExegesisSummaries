# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-325

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-325
- **Themes:** Valis as Force, Elijah as Thomas, The Secret Presence.

## Key Claims
- **Valis as Field/Force:** Valis is God's will hypostatized (possibly the Logos), acting as a physical "field" of force that replaces causation. It "warps the field away from plumb."
- **Progressive Victory:** God's will is "gaining" on reality, fitting pieces of the world into the permanent macrometasomakosmos.
- **Elijah/Thomas Connection:** PKD revisits his 1974 belief that he was taken over by the spirit of **Elijah**. (Links to dreams of Carmel, fire engines, and the name "Elias").
- **The Holy Spirit vs. Valis:** The Spirit is in the person (enthusiasmos), while Valis (the Will) is outside in the world-order.
- **Secret Kingdom:** The Kingdom is already here, secretly at the heart of the world. The "Second Coming" is actually the *disclosure* of its existing secret presence.
- **The Stone Rejected:** PKD asks "why me?" and identifies with the "thieves and murderers" from the Herbert poem—the unlikely one sought out by Christ.

## Entities
- **Elijah (Elias):** The prophet whose "takeover" of PKD explains his behavior in 1974.
- **George Herbert:** His poem "Thieves and Murderers" provides comfort.
- **Joachim of Fiore:** Foresaw the divine kingdom secretly here.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Kingship of God:** The rule of God's will (Valis) as taught by Jesus.
- **Sovereignty:** God as the supreme king whose will is law.
- **Camouflage:** Valis mimicking natural laws to remain hidden.

## Theological Motifs
- **Abba (The Father):** Encountered on 11-17-80 as the essence of Agape and infinite personality.
- **The 5th Kingdom:** The secret rule being established/ruled by God/Christ.
- **The Savior Transplanted:** The realization that the Messiah is "alive" and active in the world now.
