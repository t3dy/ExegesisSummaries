## concise_summary
Dick refines his identify-shift theory, viewing the "Steersman" (Wise Old Man archetype) as a temporary but necessary "possession" to solve his mid-life crisis and political dangers. He introduces the "Orphic Egg" (Firebright) as a latent divine seed within him and considers the 1914 return of Christ theory (JW).
## key_claims
- Mid-life Crisis / Augenblick: The 3-74 event was a "midpoint life-crisis" where the bankrupt first-half values were replaced by a "new but genuine self-system."
- The Steersman's Exit: The divinity who "steered" him through the high school physics test and the 1974 crisis has "sunk back," leaving him in charge again.
- The Orphic Egg (Firebright): A "pearl" or "egg" of divine light slumbers within his mind, protected by him until the "birth" at death.
- 1914 Invisible Return: Considers the Jehovah's Witness (JW) claim that Christ returned invisibly in 1914, noting his own vision of a "1915 tape lapse."
- Empire of Iron: Identifies the Anglo-American Empire as the "iron empire with clay feet" from Daniel, which was toppled by a "stone not cut by man" (Nixon's fall).
