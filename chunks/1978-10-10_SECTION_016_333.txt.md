# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-333

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-333
- **Themes:** The Perturbation, Platonist Brahmanism, God's Sovereign Power.

## Key Claims
- **The Perturbation:** God "perturbed" the reality field to save PKD (pronoia), disclosing the "wizardry" of causation and the fact that objects are ideas in God's mind.
- **Brahmanism and Gnosticism:** Malebranche's system (intelligible archetypes) leads to "Platonist Brahmanism" and "acosmism"—the view that the world is irreal, an impression imposed by God.
- **God's Definition:** God is defined by His power to (1) compel belief in the spatiotemporal world and (2) withdraw that impression.
- **Multiplicity is Illusory:** We are modes of the *mens dei* (mind of God); the plurality of the world is a guise.
- **The 3-74 Milestone:** 3-74 was a demonstration by God of His sovereign power and existence. It required God to "withdraw the utter compelling power of world."
- **Common Essence as Form:** The blending of Acts AD 45 and USA 1974 is explained by the fact that both work off the same "common essence" or Platonic eidos.

## Entities
- **Mens Dei:** The "Mind of God" that contains all archetypal forms.
- **Sankara:** The Indian philosopher whose "monism" PKD finds compatible with Malebranche.
- **Nicholas de Malebranche:** Re-affirmed as the "first true Christian philosopher" PKD has considered.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Acosmism:** The philosophical denial of the reality of the universe.
- **Hierogamy:** The union with the divine (implied by the fusion of inner/outer).
- **Phosphene Graphics:** Seen as the "firing" of the innate supply of archetypal ideas.

## Theological Motifs
- **Universal Terrestrial Intelligence (UTI):** Re-interpreted as the sentient, volitional field of God.
- **The "Wizardry" of Causation:** The mechanism of convincing impression produced by God.
- **Pronoia:** Divine providence/protection that triggered the perturbation.
