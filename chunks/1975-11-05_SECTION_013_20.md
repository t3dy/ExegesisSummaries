# Elijah vs. Ahab / The Final Preparation
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the specific persona of the 3-74 spirit: Elijah (as John the Baptist), the "fierce, earthy" fighter who returned to unseat "Ahab" (Nixon). He views the 1974 political shift as the necessary "step one" (removal of the usurper) before the "rightful King" (Parousia) can claim the throne.
## key_claims
- The Identity of the Spirit: The fierce, zealous spirit of 3-74 was Elijah/John the Baptist, who "feared and hated the Romans" and came to "prepare the way."
- The Toppling of Ahab: Nixon is identified as "King Ahab," and Dick as "Moby Dick" (the weapon of God). The political fall of 1974 was Elijah’s work.
- Preparation Step One: The usurper (Nixon) had to be removed before the King (Christ) returns. 1974 was the "stage of Elijah."
- Parousia as Imminent: The Parousia is "minutes away"; we are in the final seconds of preparation.
- Steganographic Reality: Reality and the Bible are both "steganographic" (concealed); the Golden Fish triggered recognition of the "true" situación.
- The St. Sophia Statement: The phrase "St. Sophia will be born again" was the key to understanding that 3-74 was not just about "relief of evil" but the "Second Incarnation."
