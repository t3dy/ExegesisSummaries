# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-360

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-360
- **Themes:** The Gnostic Restoration, Reverse Polarization, Es ist vollbracht.

## Key Claims
- **Restored Union:** World (in the Gnostic sense) is broken, and the "person and God are correctly allied and restored to union" against it.
- **Reverse Polarity:** Force now operates from *Self* onto *World*, not vice versa. Potential reality (Sein) belongs to the Self, while the "World" is merely an information field to be "perturbed" by the Self.
- **Historic-ness:** PKD identifies his role as "historic collective destiny"—the revolutionary Christian.
- **Consummation:** "Es ist vollbracht" (It is finished)—the realization that the primordial Fall has been reversed in him.
- **Revisiting Dionysos:** Re-affirms that only the "Mad God" (Dionysos) could discern the camouflage of the "Reason God" (Valis). The two are modes of the same deity.

## Entities
- **Dionysos/Valis:** The two modes (latent/active) of the same invading deity.
- **Yaldabaoth:** The "Mad God" who is ironically revealed to be the narrator's own lower nature (Phil/Fat).
- **Jim Pike book:** The project that sparked the "Acts" realization.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Perturbation:** The act of the "God-Self" exerting will on the reality field.
- **Camouflage:** The "reason-God" (Valis) hiding within the "irrationality" of the world.
- **Epiphany:** Not possession (*enthusiasmos*) but the manifestation of one’s own intrinsic (divine) nature.

## Theological Motifs
- **Sovereign Will:** The logic that the Self is now the master of information.
- **The Heroic Deed:** The transition to authenticity that "abolishes world."
- **Omega Point:** The end of forties/decades of inquiry; logic and experience merge.
