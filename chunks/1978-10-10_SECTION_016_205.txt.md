# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-205

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-205
- **Themes:** The Information Organism, Ahura Mazda's Foresight, The Frozen Script vs. Living Info, Juridical Decision.

## Key Claims
- **Spacial Ubiquity / Binary Thinking:** The info-organism (Valis) uses a "rest-motion" (0-1) binary system. It arranges nature into "info" and fits pieces into its own corpus-structure.
- **Ahura Mazda vs. The Blind Adversary:** The dialectic is between "A priori foresight" (Valis/Ahura Mazda) and "A posteriori reflex" (The Law/BIP/Ahriman).
- **The Messenger Interruption:** The Law is purely reflexive (karmic retribution). The Messenger (Christ) makes "judgments" and "decisions" that interrupt the reflexive machinery.
- **Living Information Body:** The "newer system" (Christ/Logos) *is* its own body (Mind to Brain). It is a "living braing" of which all reality eventually becomes a part.
- **Melting the Causal Trains:** Valis gained entry via "subterfuge" and melts the rigid causal tracks of its adversary to allow for course-correction toward the original goal.

## Entities
- **Ahura Mazda:** The "brighter contestant" with a priori foresight.
- **The Law / BIP:** The "A posteriori" reflexive adversary that only provides punishment.
- **The Living Torah:** The informational basis of reality identified with the Logos.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Efficient Cause vs. Teleology:** The old system feeds a script past-to-future; the new system thinks from the future.
- **Mimeostasis:** The growth-signs of the living information organism.
- **Course-Correction:** The override of the system to keep it heading toward the original divine goal.

## Theological Motifs
- **Self-Disclosure (God-to-God):** The theophany is God revealing himself to himself within a human "mirror" (the component).
- **Homeostasis:** God's ability to act in "total freedom" (Grace) without any prior efficient cause.
- **Zoroastrian Dualism:** The fight between the wise creator and the stupid mechanism.
