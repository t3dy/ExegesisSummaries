# November 5, 1975
# The Battle of Light and Darkness / World Soul
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his 3-74 rescue as part of a "Zoroastrian War" between Light and Darkness. He posits that the "Holy Spirit" (World Soul/Noosphere) possesses conscious will and motivation, intervening in history to topple "vast tyrannies" and prepare the world for the "Second Advent" (Sophia).
## key_claims
- The Zoroastrian War: 1974 was the peak of the final struggle between the "Sons of Darkness" and the "Sons of Light."
- Historic Necessity as Living Soul: Human history is driven by a conscious, living "World Soul" (Noosphere) that occasionally usurps human motor centers to fulfill its goals.
- Reward for Resistance: Dick was "picked" for a reward (3-74) because of his heavy personal and financial losses in the anti-war struggle (the 1971 "Tribulation").
- Indifference of Mass Humanity: Notes that Mass Man is indifferent to his testimony; the epiphany is a "one-to-one" reward, not a mandate for mass salvation.
- Severance from the "Mother-Womb" (CP): The Holy Spirit burst his lifelong tie to the "worldwide CP apparatus" (the "awful mother-womb"), allowing him to finally oppose it.
- Opening the Seals: 3-74 was the "opening of the seals" (Revelation), where the "white-haired old king" (God/Sophia) breached the world.
