# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-332

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-332
- **Themes:** Malebranche's Occasionalism, Spurious Memory, The Inner-Outer Tape.

## Key Claims
- **Ex Nihilo Creation:** Causation may not exist; instead, each state is an instance of "ex nihilo" (on-the-spot) creation, which we link causally.
- **Spurious Memory:** Memory is a "forgery" derived from the present state of affairs to provide a semblance of continuity/verisimilitude.
- **Inner-Outer Synchronicity:** "World" and "Memory" are the flip-sides of the same tape; they verify each other in a seamless loop.
- **Discovering Malebranche:** PKD identifies his experience (pretextual cause, Valis as mind-matter continuum) with the philosophy of **Nicholas de Malebranche**.
- **Vision in God:** We do not see objects, but "intelligible archetypal ideas in the mind of God" (the MMSK).
- **God as True Activity:** God is the underlying "cause" of both what we do and what we see; world is merely the "interface."

## Entities
- **Nicholas de Malebranche:** 17th-century philosopher whose "occasionalism" provides the missing link for PKD's 3-74.
- **Plato/Augustine/Spinoza:** Precursors to Malebranche in the pedigree of this insight.
- **Chrissy:** His birth defect "memory" is used as an example of a spurious memory that accounted for a present signal.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Occasionalism:** The belief that God is the only true cause and that physical events are merely "occasions" for His action.
- **Pretextual Cause:** The "hallucinated" cause we create to explain a divine signal (e.g., the Beatles' lyrics explaining the epiphany).
- **Ubique:** The quality of the divine presence (everywhere/immanent).

## Theological Motifs
- **The Clutch System:** God as the "driver" of the whole integrated field of reality.
- **Anamnesis as Divine Illumination:** Recovery of universals achieved not by past memory but by seeing as God sees.
- **Substantia:** The single underlying reality (mind/extension) of which we are modes.
