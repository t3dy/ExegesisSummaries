# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-336

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-336
- **Themes:** Isomorphic Mind, The Fall of Perception, Logos-Brahmanism.

## Key Claims
- **Isomorphism:** Through the eide, the human mind becomes isomorphic with the world; it shares in the mind of the Creator.
- **Fallen Categories:** Space, time, and causation are "fallen categories" because they reveal disorder (flux) instead of creation (absolute order).
- **The Common Essence:** God underlies both the percipient and the external world as the *substantia* of both mind and extension.
- **Tat Tvam Asi:** Sankara's equation is validated via Platonist/Malebranchean metaphysics: "That thou art" means sharing the eide in God's mind.
- **Eden and the Rift:** Eden is separated from Heaven by a "rift" that we experience as spatiotemporality.
- **The Choice:** The AI voice told PKD: "You have chosen absolute reality where flowers do not fade" over the "particulars" of the garden.

## Entities
- **Parmenides:** His vision of the unchanging, unitary world is linked to the eide.
- **Heidegger:** Cited for the fusion of *noein* (knowing) and *einai* (being).
- **Kathy (Dead Sister):** Her "removal" was part of the plan to force PKD's perception toward absolute reality.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Logos-Brahmanism:** The ultimate synthesis required to explain 3-74.
- **The Tug:** The invisible template of the Logos that aligns physical reality.
- **Observer-Participant:** The realization of the single field where mind and matter are one.

## Theological Motifs
- **Hierarchy of Reality:** The I.A. (Intelligible Archetypes) are "Heaven"; the rift generating the "God-gap" is our world of pain.
- **The Garden:** We never left the Garden (Eden); we are simply occluded from seeing it.
- **Dismantling of Multiple Selves:** The "Plural Selves" (ego) are an illusion; only the one real self participating in God is real.
