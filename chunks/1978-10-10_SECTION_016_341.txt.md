# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-341

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-341
- **Themes:** The Revolutionary Bible, Christ Camouflaged, The Scrutiny of Authority.

## Key Claims
- **Valis as Political Handbook:** *VALIS* is a "political handbook," a "red book" for the messianic revolution. Its authority comes from the fact that PKD "saw Christ."
- **The Counter-Culture Overthrow:** The 1960s counter-culture failed, but the 5th Savior (Christ in the people) will now complete the revolution against the Reagan/Empire tyranny.
- **Realized Eschatology:** Christ did not "return"; He has been here "camouflaged" in the coaxial Acts realm all along.
- **The Secrecy Tactic:** To avoid sedition laws, the revolution must present itself as a "religious group" aiming for the restoration of early Christianity.
- **The 5th Savior Identity:** The 5th savior is "Suffering Man" (as in the Britannica); the ordinary people of the movement are Christ.
- **Illo Tempore:** The "other reality" where Christ exists now is always here, not in the past; it is the "second signal" in the groove of total reality.

## Entities
- **The Sibyl:** Noted for observing that the leaders of the 60s were all killed.
- **Jim Pike:** Associated with the "part of Jim" (Spirit) PKD received to carry on the message.
- **The Reagan Tyranny:** The necessary catalyst for the completion of the divine plan.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Sedition Laws:** The reason for the "theological cover" of the political manifesto.
- **Coaxial Realms:** Reality as a two-component system where we normally see only the outer "groove."
- **Camouflage:** The essential state of Christ/The Kingdom in the world-order.

## Theological Motifs
- **Parousia as Retrieval:** The expectation is not for a future coming but for the retrieval of the already-present Christ.
- **Divine Sanction:** The claim that the political revolution is willed by God.
- **The Message Traffic:** The "covert information" by which Valis/Christ operates in the and-signal world.
