# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-257

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-257
- **Themes:** Ubiquitous Transubstantiation, Illo Tempore, The Gnostic "Letter" Myth.

## Key Claims
- **World-Transmutation:** The Spirit doesn't just reveal Christ; it *transmutes* the surrounding world (even alley weeds) into Christ's body. There is no world left, only total transubstantiation.
- **Ubik as Logos:** The entity Ubik is identified with the Logos of John: everywhere, anti-entropic, appearing in "trash forms" (commercials).
- **Entering Dream Time:** By reenacting a sacred myth with faith, one enters *illo tempore* (sacred origins). PKD did this accidentally via *Flow My Tears*, the fish necklace, and the pentothal.
- **The Gnostic "Pearl" Narrative:** The Xerox missive acted as the "Letter to the Prince" from the Gnostic myth of the Pearl—a message that reminds the amnesiac of their royal mission and true identity.
- **Reenactment Formula:** (1) Pure Faith, (2) Reenactment of the Rite, (3) Abolition of the Mundane World.
- **Self-Engineering Salvation:** PKD questions if God "chose" him or if he "engineered it existentially" by correctly following a formula he didn't even understand.

## Entities
- **The Plasmate:** Christ's blood as living information.
- **The Corn King:** The dying-and-rising agricultural deity (Osiris/Dionysus/Christ).
- **The Girl with the Fish Necklace:** The "disinhibiting stimulus" for the rite.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Illo Tempore:** "In that time"—the sacred time of the beginning.
- **Mimesis:** The camouflaged nature of the divine in the world.
- **Noumenal:** The spiritual reality underlying the profane.

## Theological Motifs
- **Pistis (Faith):** The absolute belief required for the mythic "feedback loop" to fire.
- **Passover Paradigm:** Emancipation from slavery occurring in sacred time.
- **The Gnostic Pearl:** Information arriving from the home world to wake the sleeper.
