# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-277

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-277
- **Themes:** Philosophical Realism, The Freeze-Frame Theory, The Counterfeit World.

## Key Claims
- **Medieval Realism:** PKD identifies with the "medieval realist" view (Platonic/Neoplatonist) that universals are real and the sensible world is "printed out" from a higher realm.
- **The Ultimate Deception (Gnostic):** PKD proposes a "fantasy" that the real world was "frozen" or "halted" in C.E. 70 (the time of Acts) to prevent the Parousia. A "counterfeit world" run by Belial/Satan replaced it to stall judgment.
- **Acts as the Freeze-Frame:** History since Acts is a "spurious interpolation." Time and space since then are simulated; change is merely accidents "reticulating" a frozen essence.
- **Recovery of the Universal:** 3-74 was the "recovery" (anamnesis) of the universal "Black Iron Prison" vs. "Secret Christians." This universal "prints out" at various points in history (e.g., 17th Century, 1974).
- **The Platonist Leap:** PKD views his 3-74 experience as a "Platonic leap"—deducing the higher Form World from empirical observations of coinciding space-time continua.
- **God as World forced into being:** Paraphrasing his own 1971 views (p. 485), God is "world under the threat of death," forced into the open to preserve reality.
- **Ubik as Information-Storage:** The real world (Acts) is preserved in God's memory. This "world-remembered" is what breaks through in 3-74.

## Entities
- **Belial/Satan:** The architect of the "counterfeit world."
- **The Messenger:** Christ/Valis/Ubik who de-occludes us and wakes us to reality.
- **Thomas:** The apostolic personality belonging to the "real" time of Acts.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Spurious Interpolation:** The fake history being fed to humanity.
- **Reticulation and Arborizing:** The thickening of detail within a static, frozen structure.
- **Universals:** The real substance (*substantia*) behind appearances.

## Theological Motifs
- **Deocculsion:** Waking up from the "drugged" state induced by the Archons.
- **Parousia:** The "imminent" return of Christ that has been delayed by a "diabolical stratagem."
- **Sacred Gnosis:** The hidden knowledge that we are living in an irreal world.
