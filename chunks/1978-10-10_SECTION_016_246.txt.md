# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-246

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-246
- **Themes:** Jumping Realms, Cumulative Creation, The Secret Partnership.

## Key Claims
- **Ascending Realms:** Life consists of multiple realms (Dante's Commedia). PKD "jumped" to a higher, milder realm in 3-74 where determinism is weaker.
- **Valis' Memory:** The past isn't gone; it's retained as "phylogons" in Valis' memory. Anamnesis is the retrieval of these universal memories into the present.
- **The Dialectical Match:** Creation is a cumulative "contest" between the Wise Mind (form) and the Serpent of Chaos (entropy).
- **Enantiodromia (The 11th Datum):** The Wise Mind wins by letting the Dark make moves, then adding a "completing move" that flips the entire pattern from Evil to Good.
- **Secret Partnership:** Behind the Zoroastrian dualistic combat lies a "secret partnership." Both sides are halves of the one Godhead (God’s Wisdom vs. God’s Power).
- **Satan as God in a Suit:** In the cosmic game, the Godhead plays against itself. Entropy provides the "test" that evolution overcomes.

## Entities
- **Dante / Joachim of Fiore:** Source of the "three ages" or "realms" schema.
- **Pythagoras:** Ratio/Structure as the basis of the holy Kosmos.
- **The Demiurge:** The worker taming chaos (Plato's *Timaeus*).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Krasis:** A local organized vortex or "cell" (Valis).
- **Ananke:** Primordial chaos/necessity.
- **Bitheism vs. Monotheism:** The realization that the two players are one.

## Theological Motifs
- **Felix Culpa:** Evil unintentionally contributing to the perfection of the world-order.
- **The Eleventh Datum:** The small missing piece that triggers a total paradigm shift.
- **Arborization:** The growing, branching structure of the Divine Memory.
