# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-195

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-195
- **Themes:** The Bodhisattva Sister, Epistemological Games, The Ultimate Riddle, Ma'at's Judgment.

## Key Claims
- **The Sustain Field:** Kathy (the sister) is an "agent from the other world" (a Bodhisattva) who didn't forget her origin. She "transferred her life" to PKD when he died in 3-74, creating a "sustain field."
- **The Designer as Playful Child:** St. Sophia (God's "darling and delight") is a "fun-loving player" who loves epistemological puzzles. She served up the "ultimate topping puzzle" (The Sister/Ubik riddle) to PKD in 3-74.
- **Evaluation of the State:** PKD is "pleased at how Ma'at has judged him." He feels happy, fulfilled, economically secure, and no longer drug-dependent. This "reward" confirms he is in the "Next World" (Paradise/PTG).
- **The Greatest Riddle:** "Am I alive and she is dead, or am I dead and she is alive?" PKD's solution is that both are true and oscillate.
- **Success of Thomas:** The "Reward" state is due to Thomas's (the master self) efforts which broke the "power of Karma" over the host.

## Entities
- **St. Sophia:** The playful "darling" of Proverbs 8:30 who created the computer riddles.
- **Kathy:** The "Other Greater Mind" who thinks PKD's world to save him.
- **Diana / AI Voice:** The psychopomp / "Bird in Bird Feathers" (Papagana).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Geworfenheit:** PKD's "way-of-being" for 50 years (grief over the sister) was reversed in 3-74.
- **Karma-Breaking:** The "death to the old way-of-being" and "birth to the new."
- **The Next World:** Not a different location, but a "different interpretation" of the same world.

## Theological Motifs
- **Zoroastrianism:** The "Spirit of your Religion" (Daena) meeting you as a beautiful young woman or a hag.
