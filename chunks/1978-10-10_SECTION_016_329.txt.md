# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-329

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-329
- **Themes:** Information Metabolism, The Rational Invasion, Logos as Natura Naturans.

## Key Claims
- **Information as Metabolism:** Reality is a "physically real message traffic" used by a UTI (Universal Terrestrial Intelligence) or a superior "mutant" race.
- **Logos as World-Order:** Reinterprets Valis as the Stoic/Spinozan *Logos*—the rational self-governing principle (Natura Naturans) at the heart of creation.
- **Justice as Plumb:** The Logos is the "invisible blueprint" (template) that exerts force to keep reality "plumb" (aligned with the *eidos*).
- **Enantiodromia of Chaos:** The rational (Reason/Wisdom) has invaded the irrational domain, setting up a dialectical combat.
- **Valis vs. Spinoza:** Spinoza correctly saw the system but didn't realize it had "invaded" an irrational world; *VALIS* captures this invasion/strife.
- **Scanner vs. Valis:** *A Scanner Darkly* studies the "occlusion" (the irrational); *VALIS* deals with the "in-breaking" of the rational that cures the occlusion.

## Entities
- **Heraclitus:** Cited for the concept of Justice/Logos maintaining order in the stars.
- **Spinoza:** His "rational self-regulating system" is the penultimate truth (Logos).
- **Hagia Sophia:** The Hebrew Wisdom figure equated with the Greek Logos and Christ.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Natura Naturans:** The active, ordering force of God's wisdom in the world.
- **Enantiodromia:** The automatic reversal/adjustment of systems to maintain balance (Karma).
- **Occlusion:** The state of irrational blindness cured by the in-breaking of the rational Logos.

## Theological Motifs
- **Rational Invasion:** The core dynamic where God's gift (Logos/Reason) subdues the irrational will.
- **The Common Essence:** The "morphological archetype" (like Acts AD 70) which underlies different spatiotemporal sets.
- **Archetypes as Hexagrams:** The 64 hexagrams of the *I Ching* are seen as "tug-constellations" or constant archetypes in the flux.
