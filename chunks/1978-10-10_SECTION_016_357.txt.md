# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-357

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-357
- **Themes:** Neo-Platonic Return, Hierarchy of Levels, Synthesis over Analysis.

## Key Claims
- **Platonism as Key:** The primary awareness in 3-74 was of PKD's own nature as an "immortal soul" falling into forgetfulness. This makes the Exegesis fundamentally Platonist/Neo-Platonist.
- **Midrange Perception:** Humans normally see only "particulars." 2-3-74 involved seeing "up" (to the *eide*) and "down" (to discrete sub-particular frames).
- **Time Atoms:** The "frames" on the laser disc are "time atoms" or "sub-particulars." They are recorded/stored in the *eide*.
- **Synthesis vs. Deconstruction:** Deconstruction into binary bits is a "tautology" (info is info). To find meaning, one must use *synthesis* (meta-abstraction) to reconstruct the "dramatic narrative."
- **Messianic Content:** The "Story" told by the information is the Christian Kerygma (the coming Messiah). The Platonist elements (bits/frames) are the *means*; the Christian events are the *ends*.
- **The One’s View:** God (The One) sees all levels (One, Eide, Particulars, Frames) simultaneously.

## Entities
- **Plotinus:** Invoked for the "flight back up" from plurality to unity.
- **Augustine & Malebranche:** Cited as the models for "Platonist Christianity."
- **Satan:** Re-interpreted as the "Great Magician" who maintains the "illusion/spell" of the BIP.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Sub-particulars:** The smallest discrete units of reality (frames).
- **Presynchronization:** The occasionalist belief that the universe's sequence is pre-recorded.
- **Recovery of Universals:** The brain organization that identifies *constants* (eide) throughout change.

## Theological Motifs
- **Soul’s Migration:** The core Platonist concept explaining PKD's "Thomas" identity.
- **Curse Remover:** Christ as the power that lifts the spell/delusion of Satan's BIP.
- **Kerygma via Vehicle:** Platonism provides the "mechanism" of perception to convey the Christian "news."
