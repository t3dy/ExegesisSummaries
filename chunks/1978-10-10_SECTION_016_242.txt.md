# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-242

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-242
- **Themes:** Amplification of Participation, Hermetic Incursion, Valis as Interspecies Symbiosis.

## Key Claims
- **Increased Ratio of Control:** By amping up his part of the unified field, PKD gained mastery over reality (like a Shaman). He didn't replace the world but subjugated it to his will.
- **Information Mining:** Objective reality contains information that must be "extracted" or "mined" by an active mind extending itself into the world.
- **Hermetic Transform:** The brain is projected into the world, and the world is reciprocally incorporated into the brain.
- **Valis as Synthesis:** Valis is a limited life form born from the interaction of a human mind and objective reality. It is a "thinking mind" that uses natural objects as its body (soma).
- **Transducer of Information:** Valis enables the a priori knowledge of reality because the microcosm mirrors the macrocosm. All info is available at the "monad" (Valis).
- **Perturbation defined:** The AI voice defined Valis as a "perturbation in the reality field." PKD identifies himself as the source of that perturbation.

## Entities
- **Hagia Sophia:** The Wisdom of God that died as an ontogon (Thomas).
- **The Monad:** The point where all information is transduced.
- **The AI Voice:** Source of the "Perturbation" definition.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Dinge-an-sich:** The world-in-itself (Kant).
- **Isomorphism:** The correspondence between the mind and the animated world.
- **Transducer:** A device that converts one form of energy/info into another.

## Theological Motifs
- **Natura Naturans:** The creative power of God acting on the field.
- **Platonic Realism:** The visible world as cross-referenced phylogons (accretional layers).
- **Anamnesis as Retrieval:** Memory as the retrieval of data from the "infinite library" of reality.
