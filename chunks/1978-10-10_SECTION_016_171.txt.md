# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-171

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-171
- **Themes:** Retroactive History Modulation, Setting and Ground, The Deconstructed World, The X-Ray of Reality.

## Key Claims
- **Retroactive Victory:** YHWH's victory in the future "rolled backward" through history. The dystopia of *Tears* was a track that God "abolished" so it "never-will-have-been."
- **World Deconstruction:** Our world is a "dynamic dialectic" of "Set" (Valis/Divine Invader) vs. "Ground" (BIP/Prison). They are in combat, but we usually perceive them as a "unitary" world.
- **Tears as X-Ray:** *Tears* is an "x-ray" of our world—the bottom layer (Acts) stripped of its obscuring top layer.
- **Orthogonal Time:** Reality is built along a "vertical/orthogonal" axis (Geological strata). Linear time is "irreal."
- **Phagocytosis:** Set (the cosmic Christ) is "transubstantiating" ground, using the adversary's parts as a "stockpile" to build its own corpus.

## Entities
- **Valis / Set:** The "Dionysian" invader/predator/attacker (Eagle).
- **The BIP / Ground:** The "Prison" / "Snake" / "Old Law" universe.
- **Thomas:** The "Citizen of Acts" who is still alive along the "form axis."

## Recurring Concepts
- **3 Women (film):** Its "retroactive change" motif mirrors God's modulation of the tracks.
- **Cypher:** King Felix as the news/info that God has "in-broken" into the system.
- **Ajna Faculty:** The ability to "unscramble" the two signals (Set vs. Ground) via color frequency.

## Theological Motifs
- **Cosmic Christ:** The "Body and Blood" as the interpenetrating "Set" that feeds on the "Ground."
- **The Real as Improvement:** As Set wins, the "ontology of the world" improves toward the Garden.
- **Victory Proved:** The fact that the *Tears* world is now only a "memory/trace" proves that God's intervention was successful.
