# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-326

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-326
- **Themes:** The Brain-Projected Hologram, Dynamic Macrometasomakosmos, Transubstantiation as Phagocytosis.

## Key Claims
- **Internal Hologram Theory:** PKD speculates that the "Acts grid" and "second signal" were actually holograms projected by his own brain (a decoding grid) to extract the "KING FELIX" meta-morpheme.
- **MMSK as Physical Brain:** The macrometasomakosmos is a 4-D physical brain outside of time, using material reality (body/blood) as its stockpile of information/thoughts.
- **Transubstantiation as Battle:** The new "body" (Christ) is invading and consuming the iron "Empire" body. This is a "cosmic transubstantiation" where space-time becomes a process of repair.
- **A Priori foreknowledge:** The invading body has foresight; the old Empire has only hindsight (a posteriori).
- **Immune System of the Empire:** The Empire survives via uniformity; the invader (Valis/Logos) acts as a "perturbation" in its internal communications system, inserting counter-messages.
- **Kyrygma as Revolution:** The "meta-message" of the information traffic is authentic Christian revolution—calling for the overthrow of the Iron Empire.

## Entities
- **Thomas (AD 45):** The personality associated with the 4th spatial axis/Acts grid.
- **The Savior:** The camouflaged figure PKD felt was present ("blind spot" in his vision).
- **The Iron Empire:** The rigid organism being devoured from within.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Set-Ground Discrimination:** The ability to see "signal" (Valis) against "noise" (world).
- **Phagocytosis:** The metaphor of white blood cells (secret Christians) attacking the "infection" of the Empire.
- **Information Metabolism:** Reality as a medium for the thoughts of the Macro-brain.

## Theological Motifs
- **Mimesis/Camouflage:** The Savior hidden as "trash" in the alley.
- **Salvator Salvandus:** We heal the Savior (the cosmic body) and He heals us.
- **Bells and Animals:** Peripheral "sosus" (signs) of the Savior's presence.
