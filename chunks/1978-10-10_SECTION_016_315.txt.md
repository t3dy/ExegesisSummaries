# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-315

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-315
- **Themes:** Messianic Movements, The Eschatological Judge, The Wound Healed.

## Key Claims
- **Vindication by History:** An article in the *Encyclopedia Britannica* on "Messianic Movements" confirms that 3-74/Valis accords perfectly with "Chiliastic" and "Millenialist" history (Joachim di Fiore).
- **Anti-Augustinian:** PKD rejects the Catholic Church's "Constantinian" alliance with the present order. Realized eschatology (the battle is already won) is a surrender.
- **The Eschatological Judge:** The "dream in Tears" warned that the Judge is here now. The message is simple: "Repent, there is no time left; he can sentence you to death."
- **Christ as Phagocyte Repair:** The "secret Christians" (including PKD) are the "phagocytes" repairing the wound in the cosmic body (healing the BIP infection).
- **The Saint of Kindness:** PKD identifies his "saintliness" not with morals but with "kindness" (Compassion/Mitleid), which triggered the miracle.
- **Wound and Repair imagery:** The "Parsifal" material (blood, spear, wound) is a "Hermetic micro-macrocosm identity" for the state of the universe.

## Entities
- **Joachim di Fiore:** The 12th-century visionary of the "Age of the Holy Spirit."
- **Constantine:** The Emperor who turned Christianity into the tool of the "present order."
- **The 5th Kingdom:** The imminent rule of Christ after Assyria, Persia, Greece, and Rome.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Chiliasm:** The belief in an imminent supernatural intervention in history.
- **The Empire Never Died:** The historical constant of the "Iron Empire."
- **Kerygma:** The simple, clean message of the in-breaking Kingdom.

## Theological Motifs
- **Dies Irae:** The "Day of Wrath" and the coming of the judge.
- **Epiphany of the Resurrected Christ:** Not the "crucified" but the "triumphant" Lord bringing the universe under control.
- **Observer-Participant Universe:** God's "loving wizardry" makes reality conform to the percipient’s faith (*pistis*).
