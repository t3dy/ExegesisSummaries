# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-267

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-267
- **Themes:** The Third Torah, Information vs. Disinformation, The Stuck LP (Repetition).

## Key Claims
- **The Third Torah:** The "written narrative" underlying reality (Acts/Gospels/Tears) is what PKD calls the **Third Torah**. This code allows for the "retrieval" of total reality.
- **Information as Ground of Being:** Reality is based on a "sacred narrative." Valis (Christ) is the generator of information; the Empire is the suppressor of information.
- **Disinformation:** A "Pigspurt" invention—far worse than non-information. You can tell "which side is which" by their relationship to information flow.
- **Thomas as Paraclete:** PKD concludes that the personality "Thomas" within him was the **Paraclete** (Second Comforter).
- **Feeding the Soma:** The contention between the Empire and the Christians feeds the building of the Cosmic Christ's body. Even the Empire's *suppression* of info contributes to the growth of Christ's macrosoma (unwittingly).
- **The Stuck LP:** PKD acknowledges his obsessive repetition and "scurrying" mind, but justifies the exegesis as a tribute to keep the "cherished memory" of 3-74 alive.
