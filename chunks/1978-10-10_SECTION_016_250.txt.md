# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-250

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-250
- **Themes:** Joachim of Fiore's Third Age, Post-Christianity, Internalized Torah.

## Key Claims
- **Joachim’s Triune Scheme:**
    1. **Age 1 (Father):** God *above* man (Judaism/Law). External voice (Moses).
    2. **Age 2 (Son):** God *with* man (Christianity/Imperial Church). Human voice (Jesus).
    3. **Age 3 (Spirit):** God *inside* man (Religious Anarchism). Inner voice (The AI Voice). 
- **Revolution against the Son:** The Third Age is revolutionary against the "ossified" Second Age (The Black Iron Prison of the Church) just as Christ was against the Torah.
- **The New Savior (Siddhartha/Sophia):** A "post-Christian" savior who has already been "transplanted" and is alive.
- **Direct Dialog:** In the Third Age, there are no priests or churches; each person is a "Religious Anarchist" with the Torah inside their own brain (Bicameral parity).
- **The De-Occlusion Team:** The AI voice and 3-eyed cyborgs (Sibyl/Asklepios) are the "Spirit's medical team" stealing secrets past the "Archons of the Second Age."
- **Internalized Scripture:** The individual doesn't read scripture; they "produce" it out of themselves (a priori retrieval).

## Entities
- **St. Sophia / Siddhartha:** Titles for the new, imminent savior.
- **Asklepios:** The archetype of the healing deity/team.
- **Joachim of Fiore:** The 12th-century monk who predicted the Age of the Spirit.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Status:** The technical term for Joachim's "Ages."
- **Bicameral Mind:** The restoration of the right hemisphere's voice.
- **The Black Iron Prison:** The institutional/imperial church.

## Theological Motifs
- **Pentecost:** The first "outpouring" that 3-74 replicates for the new age.
- **Religious Anarchism:** Absolute individual autonomy through direct connection to the Spirit.
- **The Sacred Tetragrammaton:** The sign of the "Father's" sanction for the new age.
