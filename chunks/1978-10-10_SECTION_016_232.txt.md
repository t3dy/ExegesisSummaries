# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-232

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-232
- **Themes:** The "Smart Typewriter" Universe, Anamnesis of the Future, Information as Poured-In Time.

## Key Claims
- **Simultaneous Correction:** Solution B (3-74) overruns and erases Solution A (original failure) as it unfolds. It's like a spider web that exists before the strand is tossed.
- **Decade of Retracking:** Insights and clues for 3-74 appeared in PKD's 1960s novels (e.g., *Penultimate Truth*). This proves he was already retracking his life years before 3-74.
- **Time as Information:** Time is "energy-information" poured into the material system. It translates into causality (change). 
- **The Reverse Pouring:** In 3-74, PKD "reversed the pouring-in process." He saw reality yield up its original "Instructions" (Torah/Information) instead of absorbing them.
- **Cosmogony in Reverse:** The "Plasmate" is time seen in its true, primal form (Information/Blood). Everything turned into language.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "waking" persona who handles the retrack.
- **Ubik / Valis:** The central mind maintaining the simulation.
- **Eliade:** Cited for the "reversing of the cosmogonical process."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Track A/B/C:** The sequence of refinements leading to release.
- **Illo Tempore:** The "beginning of time" found at the end of the reverse process.
- **Transducer:** The material system’s role in converting info into causality.

## Theological Motifs
- **Torah as Blueprint:** The pre-existent information behind creation.
- **Postmortem Anamnesis:** Memory derived from having lived the future already.
- **The Smart Typewriter:** A universe where errors are edited out through retracking.
