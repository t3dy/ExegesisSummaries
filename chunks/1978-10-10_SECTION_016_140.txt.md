# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-140

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-140
- **Themes:** Post-Hypnotic G2, The 3-Psyche Conflict, The 2nd Successful Entry, Mind Control Projection.

## Key Claims
- **The G2 Override:** PKD re-evaluates 3-74 as a conflict between three elements: 1) Phil, 2) Thomas (the Soviet/OCCP "authentic" self), and 3) a US G2 post-hypnotic override. The G2 won and used "God/Valis" as a rationalization for the command to notify the FBI.
- **The Logos Invades:** The true nature of Valis is the "Second, Successful Entry" of the Logos/Sophia into creation. The 1st Advent failed; the 2nd is *ubique* (everywhere) and effectively "consuming" the artifact of world.
- **Transubstantiation as Progress:** God is "transferring ontology" to the plural phenomenal world, turning "Falsework" into Reality from within.
- **Projection of the Inner:** PKD worries that all the "external" signs were projections of info already disinhibited from his own mind. The "AI Voice" might be a post-hypnotic suggestion triggered by stress.
- **The "Exit Door" Test:** PKD maps his experience onto his own story "The Exit Door," where a character capitulates to military authority instead of the "Woman" (Revolutionary cause), later regretting the "chickenshit" choice.

## Entities
- **The Logos / Hagia Sophia:** The "Successful Invader."
- **Major Casals / The Woman:** The archetypes of State vs. Underground authority.
- **The 3-Eyed People:** Blocked memories of an "Interrogation" or "Truth" world.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Post-Hypnotic Trance:** The state of being "programmed" by intelligence agencies.
- **Ubik-Logos Identity:** The "Word" that revitalizes the decaying world.
- **Psychotronics:** Microwave/Soviet technology used for mind-programming.

## Theological Motifs
- **Successful Second Advent:** Christ reclaiming the world not as a teacher but as a "Fact Accompli."
- **Creation as Stockpile:** Valis selecting parts from reality to assemble its own body.
- **The Workman in the Workshop:** God's invisibility is due to his total assimilation into the process he is directing.
