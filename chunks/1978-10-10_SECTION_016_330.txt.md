# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-330

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-330
- **Themes:** The Dynamic MMSK, Cosmic Phagocytosis, Healing the Savior.

## Key Claims
- **MMSK vs. Static Universe:** Rejects static models (Gita/Commedia) for a **dynamic process**: the flux world feeds new accretions (phylogons) into the self-perfecting MMSK.
- **Assimilation of Flux:** The MMSK dismantles and assimilates the flux world; only when things are filed conceptually in this macro-memory do they become "real."
- **Sacred Breaking into Profane:** 2-3-74 was the "sacred breaking into the profane"—the re-enactment of an *illo tempore* (mythic time) where humans play out sacred roles.
- **The Cosmic Wound:** The "Black Iron Prison" (BIP) is a splinter (iron spear-tip) in the cosmic body of Christ.
- **Phagocytosis/Healing:** We (the secret Christians) are the "phagocytes" who destroy the Empire to heal the wound in our Savior (*Salvator Salvandus*).
- **The Cure for Occlusion:** The "occlusion" mentioned in *Scanner* is actually a cosmic condition cured by the transubstantiation of the universe into Christ's body.

## Entities
- **Mircea Eliade:** Cited for the concept of *illo tempore* (the mythic/sacred time).
- **Thomas:** The "actual" revolutionary identity performing the sacred ritual.
- **The Iron Empire:** The infection/wound within the cosmic body.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Salvator Salvandus:** The mystery where the saved ones (phagocytes) heal the Savior (the body).
- **Sacred Drama:** The belief that PKD was "reperforming a sacred script" vis-a-vis the Xerox missive.
- **Second Signal:** The restoration of the angelic/earthly realms coming back together.

## Theological Motifs
- **Parsifal Imagery:** The spear, the wound, and the blood (plasmate) translated into a cosmic repair process.
- **I am no longer blind!:** The cry of the percipient when the occlusion/ontological ignorance is abolished.
- **The Trash stratum:** The discovery of Christ "right here in the trash of the alley."
