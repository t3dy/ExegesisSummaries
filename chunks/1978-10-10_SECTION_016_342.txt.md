# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-342

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-342
- **Themes:** Satori of Logic, Incommensurate Systems, Plato's Failure.

## Key Claims
- **The Satori of February 13, 1981:** PKD brings into consciousness the verbal logic of 2-74: (1) One object cannot be in two places/times; (2) The sign was in two; (3) therefore space-time signifies nothing.
- **Clash of Ordering Systems:** The brain uses two systems: (A) Spatiotemporality; (B) Form-Abstraction ("one mother"). When they clash, spatiotemporality (the uncritical one) falls, and the Forms (the logical one) win.
- **Logic of the Forms:** Recognition of the Forms is a "normal process" that only goes to completion when the spatiotemporal system collapses.
- **Plato's Oversight:** Plato didn't realize that if the Forms are real, space and time *must* be illusory. He tried to have both, which led to the problem of "pluralization."
- **Multiple Foci of Perception:** Malebranche's ideas explain why "plural selves" see "plural objects"—it's an internal mode of organization, not an external reality.
- **The Vanishing Division:** When the logic is accepted, the division between Acts and USA 1974 vanishes; they superimpose syntonically.

## Entities
- **The 1981 Satori:** A moment of "invincible logic" that raised the unconscious 2-74 epiphany into words.
- **Aristotle/Plato:** Cited for the problem of how eide get into particulars.
- **Buddhist Idealists:** Cited for the "illusory fragmentation" of the one real self.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Oxymoron:** The concept of one object in two places, which forces a logical crisis.
- **Vacuum of Belief:** The state left by the collapse of space-time metrics where the eide take over.
- **Semantic Restoration:** The "Former" life is not former; it is another coaxial life that was "unremembered."

## Theological Motifs
- **Abolition of Multplicity:** The realization that "two" is always "one" perceived twice.
- **The Ultimate Realization:** Plato's "recovery of universals" as the final goal of all perception.
- **MMSK:** The true arborizing structure of eide that remains when space-time is stripped away.
