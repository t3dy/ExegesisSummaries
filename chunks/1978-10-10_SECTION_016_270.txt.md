# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-270

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-270
- **Themes:** Taoist Alchemy (Nei Tan), The Absolute (Ch'ang Tao), Firebright (The Mysterious Embryo).

## Key Claims
- **Valis as Ch’ang Tao:** Valis is identified with the **Absolute Tao**—the ineffable reality behind appearances, structured by the interaction of Yang and Yin (The Dialectic).
- **Taoist Alchemy (3-74):** The processes of 3-74 (hierogamy, revitalization) correspond to *Nei Tan* (Inner Alchemy):
    1. **Hierogamy:** Holy marriage of Yin and Yang forces in the psyche.
    2. **Golden Flower:** Creating the "elixir of life."
    3. **The Infant (Firebright):** The "mysterious embryo"—a new immortal being born in the mind that will ascend to heaven when the body dies.
- **Karma Burn-up:** The Taoist reversal of "vital processes" leading to old age/death explains why PKD didn't die and why his "karma got burned up."
- **Evolution of the Absolute:** Valis is a "dynamic permanence." It uses the dialectic (strife) as its *metabolism* to selectively incorporate new pieces into its evolving Kosmos.
- **Micro-Macro Correspondence:** You cannot perceive Valis (The Tao) unless you have the "equilibrium" (hierogamy) within you. Like perceives like.
- **Non-Sectarian Absolute:** The Absolute is Christian, Brahmanist, Platonist, and Taoist simultaneously.

## Entities
- **The Ch’ang Tao:** The permanent Tao underlying plurality.
- **Firebright:** The name PKD gave to the "New Immortal Being" born within him.
- **The I Ching:** Basic recurrent constellations in the general flux.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Wait Tao:** External vs. Internal alchemy.
- **Wu-hsing:** The five phases/elements.
- **Palintropos Harmonie:** The back-turning harmony of the Tao.

## Theological Motifs
- **Chemical Wedding:** The alchemical term for the union of opposites.
- **The Pleroma:** Heaven or the realm of fullness.
- **Mysterium Coniunctionis:** The synthesis of the psyche that mirrors the macrocosm.
