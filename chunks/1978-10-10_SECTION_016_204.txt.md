# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-204

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-204
- **Themes:** The Slave Labor State, God's Exit from History, The Returning Elijah, Hegel's Absolute Spirit.

## Key Claims
- **Slave Labor Goal:** The "true goal of evil" revealed in 3-74 is "slave labor." This is depicted in *Tears* and identified as the basic historical fight Christ is engaged in.
- **God's Exit in 1 AD:** Conventional theology says God *entered* history in the 1st century AD. PKD claims the opposite: God *left* history 2000 years ago and fell silent.
- **The Born-Again Monster:** The return of YHWH to Earth will be regarded as the "birth of a monster baby" (*VR*). This is a 180-degree reversal of religious expectations.
- **The Elijah Archetype:** PKD's experience (phosphenes, "still small voice") correspondence exactly to Elijah's. He is an "Elisha" given the "Elijah spirit" to continue the fight against the "madman" (Nixon).
- **The Spirit of Freedom:** 2-3-74 was a "total emancipation" from the 2-tape system; a titanic leap in human evolution (from "slave" to "Beethoven").

## Entities
- **The Secret Christian Underground:** The 2000-year-old opposition to the "organized establishment society."
- **Nixon / James-James:** The "madman in charge" / demented monarch of the slave labor state.
- **Elijah:** The archetype of the "peripheral" prophet dealing with a fallen power center.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Total Emancipation:** The shift from "robot reflex" to "Spirit of Freedom."
- **Historical Dialectic:** The absolute Spirit emancipating itself through history (Hegel).
- **A I Voice:** The "still small voice" of the peripheral prophet.

## Theological Motifs
- **Peripheral God:** God has been forced out of the power centers (churches/governments) and works only with "peripheral people."
- **Monotheistic Prejudice:** The bias against YHWH (viewed as a monster) which PKD only overcame via Martin Buber.
- **The gauntlet:** *Tears* as a revolutionary manifesto thrown down to the authorities.
