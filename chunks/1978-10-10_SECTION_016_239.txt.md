# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-239

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-239
- **Themes:** Retracking and the "Machine" Principle, Hierarchy of Coaxial Realms, Valis as Self-Regulating Entelechy.

## Key Claims
- **Retracking to Freedom:** Precognition is the result of living an event twice. The "machine" feeling was the pressure of the 1st tracking; "Divine Providence" (Thomas) was the "anti-machine" override.
- **3 Realms of Causality:**
    1. **Prison:** Total external determinism (Heimarmene).
    2. **Transition:** Mixture of external law and internal growth.
    3. **Garden (Kingdom of God):** Total internal entelechy (Valis).
- **Valis as God:** Valis is God because all causation is *internal* to it. It knows no external law; it only knows its own "will to become."
- **Pretextual Cause:** What we call "cause and effect" is just an imperfect view of Valis' internal growth-decisions.
- **Christian Distinction:** "Love your enemy" is the "Christian logic" that breaks the retributive (Justice) system. This "Blank Document" intervention is only possible in Christianity.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** Memory of the 1st tracking used for the 2nd.
- **Ubik / Valis:** The macromind running the coaxial system.
- **Spinoza:** Described "Realm One" (External Law).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Coaxial Realms:** Levels of reality occupying the same space but different "depths."
- **Entelechy:** The internal goal-seeking growth of a system.
- **Hittite Covenant:** Protecting the weak (O.T.) vs. Christian aid to the "unworthy."

## Theological Motifs
- **The Kingdom of God:** Freedom from all external coercion.
- **Adoption:** Being taken into the "Plan" due to an act of "unworthy love."
- **Acosmism:** The realization that the "external world" is a simulation/growth-stage of Valis.
