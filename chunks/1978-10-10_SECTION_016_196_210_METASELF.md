# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-196-210 Summary Note

## Overview
Chunks 196-210 represent the "Internal Tipping Point" of Section 16. PKD transitions from a purely externalized Gnosticism to a **Hermetic Introjection Model**. The central theme is the realization that **"I am Valis"**—that the universe became isomorphic to his own brain, allowing for a priori knowledge of a secret "World within the World."

### Key Transitions:
1.  **Pythagorean Synthesis:** The term "Valis" is definitively mapped onto the Pythagorean *Kosmos* (the rational invading the irrational).
2.  **Grace as Code-Interruption:** The "Messenger" dream introduces the model of Christ as a "blank sheet" inserted into the retributive/karmic machinery of the Law.
3.  **The Meta-Self:** The discovery that humans are "components" in an information-processing system. Our "life" is actually the system's "thinking."
4.  **A Priorism over Empiricism:** The "introjection" of the outer world allows PKD to know about the Xerox missive and "Olive Holt" before they manifest empirically.
5.  **Monotheism as Moksa:** A radical shift where God is not "behind" reality but *is* Reality. Evil is simply "Dokos" (Illusion).
6.  **The Soviet/Thomas Shadow:** The persistent "dark" theory that he was a sleeper agent (dissociated personality) and the religious experience was a "hypnotic scramble" for military info.
