# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-211

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-211
- **Themes:** Temporal Form Axis, Information as Blood, Plasmate Activation, Reality as Notation System.

## Key Claims
- **Temporal Form Axis:** Valis scans "up" and "down" the time axis like we scan space. It sees the "completion" of things. Every event is a thought in Valis’s mind (Xenophanes).
- **Information as Plasmate:** Information is the "blood" of the macro-brain. It is "traveling form" that joins spatial loci together.
- **Latent vs. Active Info:** Information in a manuscript is "frozen" or "latent." It only becomes "active plasmate" (news) when it flows and acts to complete a broken form.
- **Reality as notation:** Valis uses reality like a computer uses chips (arbitrary notation system). It must control reality to control the information.
- **Psychosomatic Macro-entity:** God is the psyche of reality (Pre-Socratic view). Creation is essential to God; he cannot think without this physical "brain."

## Entities
- **Valis:** The macro-organism/brain.
- **Xenophanes:** Cited for the idea that change is due to the deity's thinking.
- **The Plasmate:** Liquid information / the blood of Noos.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Form Axis:** The axis along which things grow toward their Platonic eidos.
- **Dialectic:** A binary (0-1) system with built-in imbalance.
- **Rest-Motion Mode:** Off-on binary states; linked vs. unlinked.

## Theological Motifs
- **Invasion & Camouflage:** Valis has invaded and plundered our reality, masking itself as world-objects.
- **Anamnesis:** Its memories (going back to Mycenae and the stars) are what PKD experienced.
- **Observer-Participant Universe:** The message only exists when inner (brain print) and outer (reality) are superimposed.
