# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-227

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-227
- **Themes:** Resurrection as Bio-Grounding, The Fall as Punishment, Gnostic Faculties, The Tree of Life.

## Key Claims
- **Resurrection as Grounding:** Jesus' resurrection in an "incorruptible body" proved his teachings were backed by the "primal ground of being." God and life are inseparable.
- **Letter vs. Spirit:** Paul is a "reasonable man" who understands that the Law is mindless letter (ritual/letter) vs. Christ as pure substance (spirit).
- **Rejecting the Fall?** PKD questions whether Judaism is right to deny "The Fall." He sees the expulsion from Eden as explicit in Genesis.
- **Tree of Life:** Christ offers the fruit of the Tree of Life (bread of eternal life). This "stolen secret" (Gnostic) restores us to Eden and our original paranormal faculties.
- **Occlusion/Impairment:** By denying the Fall, Judaism fails to realize man is "impaired" (faculties lost). Christianity's "Saving Gnosis" is recognizing we are fallen so we can seek restoration.
- **Faustian Christianity:** The covert message is: "We were once as gods, and Christ shall restore us."

## Entities
- **St. Paul:** The architect of the "Spirit vs. Letter" distinction.
- **Hagia Sophia:** God's Wisdom, identified with Christ.
- **Milton:** "The Second Adam who undoes the work of the first."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Occlusion:** The impairment of human faculties since the Fall.
- **Eden:** The state of original brightness and paranormal perception.
- **Mechanical Entity:** The status of the Law (as a slave entity).

## Theological Motifs
- **Felix Culpa:** The "fortunate fall" where the remedy outdoes the disease.
- **Gnostic Fall:** Punishment by a "dumb demiurge" (YHWH) to keep us enslaved.
- **The Bread of Life:** The mechanism of immortality (eating the Type to become the Type).
