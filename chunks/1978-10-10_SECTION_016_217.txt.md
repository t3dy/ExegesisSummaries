# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-217

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-217
- **Themes:** Valis as Homeostatic Organism, Pretextual Causality Revisited, The 8-Point Realization, Psychomorphic Reality.

## Key Claims
- **Finite Organism:** Valis is NOT God. It is a "high-level homeostatic biological organism" that is *finite* and has an "environment." It grows by assimilating negentropy.
- **The 8-Point Realization:** 1) Unitary, 2) In process, 3) Self-motivating, 4) Resembled his mind, 5) Karma-awareness, 6) No longer blind, 7) All time/vast space, 8) Conscious throughout.
- **Pretextual Causality:** The semblance of normal law masking a "conscious volition" that has integrated reality into its own "Soma."
- **Psychomorphic Reality:** The world "obliges with whatever universe you expect." It is psychomorphic—mirroring your inner world back to you.
- **The Syzygy:** Valis is an **interpenetration** of "me and the whatever-it-is." PKD helped shape its nature as it presented itself to him.

## Entities
- **Valis:** The "finite living organism" using objects as its body.
- **Noos:** The mind that builds itself a body spanning 1000s of years (Kosmos).
- **The Creator Deity:** The one who "conjured up a world based on my mind."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Set/Ground Discrimination:** Waking up means realizing the "sentient" part of reality (Valis) vs the ground.
- **Magdeburg Hemisphere:** The "unsupported" external world collapsing when an impossible event occurs.
- **Memory aid:** The Christ story triggered the real memory to "come on."

## Theological Motifs
- **Esoteric Gnosis:** Belief system controls Valis; the intersection of fields (me and not-me).
- **The Great Return:** Reabsorption into God (Brahman) where mind and world are one.
- **A Priorism:** The meta-self's ability to know the world from the inside.
