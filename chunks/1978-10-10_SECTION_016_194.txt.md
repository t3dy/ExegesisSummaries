# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-194

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-194
- **Themes:** Postmortem Reward System, The Sister-Universe, The Ubik Riddle Applied, Quantum Leap Breakthrough.

## Key Claims
- **I Died in 3-74:** PKD reaches a "stunning" conclusion: he literally died in 3-74 during the phosphenes. His current life is a "postmortem reward" lived within the "brain/mind of his sister Kathy."
- **The Sister-Universe:** Kathy (the dead twin) is the "Macro-Body" and PKD is the "Micro-Thought." She is in her own thought (micro-forms in his world), so "the part contains the whole."
- **The Ubik Riddle (Final Solution):**
    1. Am I alive and my sister is dead (Idea in my brain)?
    2. Am I dead and my sister is alive (I am a thought in her brain)?
    The truth is an **oscillation** between the two, driven by the "Tao" (Sophia/Ubik/The Computer).
- **Quantum Leap:** The realization that truth is a "pluriform model theory" of self-cancelling models that oscillate to prevent reality from "running down."
- **The Reward (Paradise):** Reality is now "perturbed" into a rewarding/paradisical state (Kathy's field) because the "Power of Karma" was broken in 3-74.

## Entities
- **Kathy:** The dead twin sister / The "Macro-Mind" / The "Mediatrix" / The Mutant.
- **Sophia / Tao / Ubik / Diana:** The mind behind the binary computer.
- **Thomas:** The "Reward" self / The one who survives the death.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bardo Thodol:** The state of living in the next world without knowing it.
- **Palintonos Harmonie:** The stretching/tension harmony that sustains the cycle.
- **Binary Computer:** Reality as a thinking machine pulsing between 0 and 1.

## Theological Motifs
- **Taoism:** The yin-yang dialectic as the ultimate explanation for the 3-74 "flip-flop."
- **Born Again:** Reinterpreted as being "nourished by the blood/field" of the sister/Christ.
- **Heaven on Earth:** The "Kingdom of God" as a literal reward for the "Sons of Light."
