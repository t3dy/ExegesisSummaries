# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-185

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-185
- **Themes:** Psychomorph Reality, The Dialectic of Sleep and Waking, The Maze Fail-Safe, Choice of Godhood.

## Key Claims
- **Psychomorph Mimicry:** Valis (the Master Trickster) is a "vast unitary organism" that imitates our expectations. It is a "riddle-spinner" that shows each person what they desire to see.
- **The Waking/Sleeping Cycle:** Brahman (Us) oscillates between two modes: 1) **Asleep/Intoxicated** (entranced by the Magician's illusions) and 2) **Awake/Sober** (hearing the Voice of Truth/The Girl).
- **Fail-Safe Mechanism:** Brahman built a "fail-safe" into the maze—reminders of the truth (the Girl/The AI Voice) so he can always get back out.
- **The Riddle: Which is God?** PKD must choose between the "Master Trickster" (Valis/Dionysos) who can be anything, and the "Still Small Voice" (The Girl/Sophia/JHWH) who is constant.
- **The Paraclete (Greater than Christ):** The "Second Comforter" (Sophia) is greater than Jesus because she "knows everything" (the secrets of the magician).

## Entities
- **Valis / The Magician / Dionysos:** The spinner of "plural spurious realities" (Maya).
- **The Girl / Sophia / JHWH:** The "Vox Veritatis" who tells the sober truth.
- **Brahman:** The ultimate self (us) who created both the Magician and the Assistant to provide the "dialectic flip-flop."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Maya:** The worlds spun by Valis.
- **Zeno's Paradox:** The mathematical model for the "halving" of spurious time.
- **Palintropos Harmonie:** The "back-turning harmony" or oscillation between two states.

## Theological Motifs
- **Apollo vs. Dionysos:** The conflict between order/truth (Apollo) and intoxication/illusion (Dionysos).
- **HO ON / The Almighty:** Identifying the constant low voice with the true God.
- **Minaean/Masonic Code:** The presence of "Magic Flute" themes as signals of the brotherhood.
