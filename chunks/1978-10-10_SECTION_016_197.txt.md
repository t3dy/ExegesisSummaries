# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-197

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-197
- **Themes:** The Secret Pythagorean Brotherhood, Ubik as Plasmate, The Strife-Free Mind, Observer-Participant Kosmos.

## Key Claims
- **Secret Political Influence:** PKD wonders if a secret Pythagorean brotherhood still exists and exerts covert influence. He notes that the "plasmate" resembles the intelligent/living ideas that Empedocles and Pythagoras wrote about.
- **Ubik as Neo-Pythagorean Gnosis:** *Ubik* is identified as a transmission of the "esoteric occult revelations" of Pythagoras. Only someone "latently a Pythagorean" could have written it.
- **Valis = Kosmos:** The term *kosmos* (beautiful order) was coined by Pythagoras. Valis is this immanent, living organism/structure that fits everything together (Harmonia).
- **The Strife-Free Mind:** Perception is "like by like." To see the *kosmos* (Valis) as a "thinking unity," the percipient's mind must be free from strife. If the mind fluctuates, the proportion of *kosmos* seen fluctuates.
- **Synchronicity as Higher Perception:** Synchronicity isn't a "pretentious neologism" but the perception of the Gestalt (structure) of *kosmos* fitting parts together.

## Entities
- **Empedocles:** His secret ideas (living ideas) are found in *Ubik*.
- **Apollonius of Tyana:** The leader of 1st-century Neo-Pythagoreanism, identified with the space-time of *Acts* and Thomas.
- **Zebra / Valis / Ubik:** All different names for the same structural, living deity.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Harmonia:** The "fitting together" of opposites into a unity.
- **Anamnesis:** The "cynchpin" of the Pythagorean system; memory across lifetimes.
- **Krasis:** The mixture of elements (Love vs. Strife) that introduces multiplicity.

## Theological Motifs
- **Atheistic/Monistic Deity:** God not as a "substance" or "thing" but as a *use* things are put to—a way elements are linked together.
- **Micro-Valis vs. Macro-Valis:** To see God, one must become divine (the "divine spark" or "plasmate" must wake up).
- **Feature Extraction:** The percipient's neural system uses a memory hologram (phylogenic memory) as a template to discriminate Valis against the ground.
