# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-293

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-293
- **Themes:** The Heroic Deed, Breaking Fate, The Rim of Reality.

## Key Claims
- **The Surd:** After all analysis, there remains a "surd"—a "perturbation in the reality field" that cannot be explained, only felt as a "tug" from outside reality.
- **Heroic Act vs. Fate:** 3-74 was a "heroic act" that broke the power of *Heimarmene* (fate/karma). By performing one unique action (thwarting the Xerox threat), the "dead time" of programming was ruptured.
- **Ex Nihilo Creation:** The heroic deed was an "impossibility" given the self's history. It created a "new self" (Thomas) *ex nihilo*, much like the original cosmogenesis.
- **Second Birth:** This is the "birth from above"—a quantum transfer of essence from "world" to "self," where the self is no longer a "thing" shaped by the world but a creator acting on it.
- **The Rim of Reality:** PKD ends his exegesis by finding "the ultimate Source": a rustle of wind in the alley weeds and a faint, murmuring voice (the *Ruah*).
- **Ubik as Reality-Invader:** VALIS/Ubik is the "real world" (Realm 3) invading the "fake world" (Realm 4) to de-occlude us.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "new self" born from the heroic deed.
- **Ruah:** The Spirit of God breathing creation into existence.
- **Dante:** Cited for the description of God as the "Book of the Universe" whose pages are scattered.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Perturbation/Tug:** The evidence of a divine volitional force outside the spatiotemporal field.
- **Dead Time:** The mechanical repetition of fate/programming.
- **Live Time:** The genuine future that rushes in once the "great chain" of determinism is broken.

## Theological Motifs
- **Moksa/Enlightenment:** PKD admits he still doesn't "understand" the final mystery, only its effects.
- **Chemical Wedding/Hierogamy:** The marriage of opposites that produces the new birth.
- **De-occlusion:** The "waking up" that allows for the one decisive free act.
