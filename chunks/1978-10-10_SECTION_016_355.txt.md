# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-355

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-355
- **Themes:** Living Information, The Dionysian Narrator, Sophia’s Healing.

## Key Claims
- **Plasmate / Living Info:** Information that can think. Even the arrangement of objects is "verbal info" (language).
- **I Ching as Binary Converter:** The *I Ching* converts binary (0-1) into Gestalts (hexagrams), just as the brain converts pixels into objects.
- **VALIS as a Demonic Joke:** PKD re-evaluates *VALIS* as a demonic paradox. The narrator (Horselover Fat) is "possessed by Dionysos."
- **The Mad God Narrator:** Dionysos (the mad God) is the true narrator of *VALIS*. Only a "mad God" can see the invading "Reason-God" (Valis/Sophia) that mortal men cannot perceive.
- **The Healing Miracle:** 2-3-74 was the "Healing Miracle" where Sophia caused Phil Dick and Horselover Fat to merge back into one sane person.
- **The Antitoxin:** Sophia (Logos) is the "antitoxin" that sobers the landscape and combats the irrationality of Dionysos.

## Entities
- **Dionysos:** The "mad God" of the counter-culture, drugs, and *The Bacchae*.
- **Sophia (Logos):** The rational invading deity that "sobers the landscape."
- **Horselover Fat:** The "engulfed" vantage point of madness.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Enantiodromia:** The decision between Dark and Light that retrieves the universe.
- **Hexagrams:** The "lines" of reality.
- **Camouflage:** Only the mad (or the God-possessed) can see through the world’s camouflage.

## Theological Motifs
- **Madness vs. Wisdom:** The central tension in *VALIS*.
- **Shamanism:** PKD as a shaman taken over by the God to prophecy.
- **The King-Felix Cypher:** A reference to the "Joy God" (Dionysos) who is "here."
