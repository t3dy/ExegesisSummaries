# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-292

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-292
- **Themes:** Valis as the Tao, Retrograde Time, Microcosmic Resorption.

## Key Claims
- **Valis as the Tao:** If forced to give a one-word answer, Valis is "The Tao as the Absolute," regulating Yin and Yang via enantiodromia.
- **Imploding Self:** Valis was the "splintered self imploding back together." Time running backward was the experience of this "explosion turning into an implosion."
- **World as Familiar Mind:** The "perturbation in the reality field" was the percipient’s own mind-contents coming back as "world" as time reversed.
- **Microcosm of Cosmogenesis:** 3-74 was a "regression to the moment before creation"—a cosmic resorption described by Eliade. PKD acted as a microcosm of the "Big Bang" reversal.
- **Regaining the Christos:** The re-collected self is a "Light Being," the "Christos" or "Daimon" possessed before the Fall (the original lost self).
- **Thomas as Restored Self:** Thomas was the result of this "re-collecting" across thousands of years to the "time and place of Acts."
- **Physics of the Tao:** Synchronicity (Pauli) and quantum markers are the "sentient physics" through which the Tao asserts its mastery over flux.
