# SECTION_013
# November 5, 1975
# The Parousia has Come / The 3-Eyed Lady
## concise_summary
Dick concludes with absolute certainty that the "Parousia" (Second Coming) has arrived, evidenced by the unique historical unseating of the "world's most powerful tyrant" (Nixon) by "heavenly forces." He parallels the events to his novel *Tears* and identifies the "3-eyed lady" as the judge (Pantocratrix).
## key_claims
- Certainty of the Parousia: The 1974-75 events match the prophesied "Judgment Day" and "Return of the King."
- Preparation (The Time of Elijah): The 3-74 "fierce zealous spirit" was Elijah (John the Baptist) preparing the way for the "Rightful King."
- Invisible Battle / Visible Outcome: While the public saw only "politics," Dick saw the "all-out battle of heaven against earth."
- *Tears* as Apocalyptic Vision: *Flow My Tears* predicted the event: the "Ancient of Days" judging against the "man shut up in the house" (Nixon).
- 3-Eyed Lady / Pantocratrix: The female figure who judged his case is the judge of the end-times, replacing the "invisible nucleus of evil" with "good."
- The Withering State: After the judge unseats the tyrant, the state apparatus begins to "wither away," replaced by "Christian Love" (the reign of love).
