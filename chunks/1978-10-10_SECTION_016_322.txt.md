# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-322

## Metadata

## Key Claims
- **Valis = Logos:** Extensive citation from the *Encyclopedia Britannica* confirms Valis matches the definition of the Logos: the divine reason implicit in and ordering the cosmos.
- **Philo's Intermediary:** Philo's concept of the Logos as the "intermediary" between God and the cosmos perfectly explains Valis/Ubik. It is both immanent and the transcendent divine mind.
- **Wisdom as Agent:** Jewish tradition (Proverbs 8:22) identifies Wisdom as the divine agent through which God created the world.
- **"Second Divine Principle":** PKD admits he mistook the "second divine principle" (Logos) for God in 2-3-74. He had not yet reached the "ecstatic moment" of seeing God Himself (11-17-80).
- **Nous as Place of Forms:** Following Aristotle/Philo, the Logos is the "nous" or the "place of forms" (the macrometasomakosmos).
- **Flash of Knowledge:** PKD's "direct way" (prophetic knowledge/anamnesis) allowed him to see the Logos and God simultaneously through inspiration.
