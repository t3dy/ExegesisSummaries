# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-146

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-146
- **Themes:** Intuitive Blitz, The Faster Tape, De-synchronization, Cognitive Empathy.

## Key Claims
- **The Intuitive Blitz:** 2-3-74 was a "shock of recognition." The mind realized it knew the Xerox threat *before* it happened, which is a property exclusive to the "Wise Mind" (God).
- **De-synchronization:** To use a priori knowledge for survival, the organism must "de-synchronize its biological clock" and run its "inner tape" faster than the external macrocosm.
- **Responding to the Future:** In 3-74, PKD responded to stimuli *before* they occurred. This "mismatch" decoupled him from stimulus-response "captivity" (DNA programming).
- **Cognitive Empathy (Isomorphism):** A priori knowledge of others works through "isomorphic empathy"—the internal model playing out what others will do because they are parts of the same "Self."
- **The Mirror in the Story:** PKD realized he had written a "complex and accurate description of Valis" in the sixties (*Ubik*) and *Scanner*, proving he knew about Valis "a priori and in advance."

## Entities
- **The Wise Mind:** The agent with foreknowledge (Ahura Mazda).
- **Ubik / Logos:** The entity recognized a priori.
- **The Xerox Missive:** The catalyst for the "Mind's Remembering of itself."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Inner-Clock Acceleration:** Running the "tape" ahead of the outside world.
- **Stimulus-Response Captivity:** The "Maze" or "Prison" of linear causality.
- **Self-Knowledge as God-Knowledge:** Realizing one is the "Wise Mind" having penetrated its own creation.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Eye of Shiva (Ajna):** Absolute discernment about other people.
- **The Spirit of Truth:** The inner/outer entity that "sweeps out the truth" and tells it to the percipient.
- **St. Sophia's Goal:** Not just the handling of a letter, but the "reminding" of the soul of its origin.
