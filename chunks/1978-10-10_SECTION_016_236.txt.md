# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-236

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-236
- **Themes:** The Model Psychosis of the Precog, Valis as a Giant Brain, Entering Eternity.

## Key Claims
- **Discovery of the Mechanism:** PKD claims to be the first precog to experience the "mechanism" of precognition via a model psychosis. He consciously saw the "unconscious" world where time runs backward.
- **Valis as Brain:** To the acting faculty, reality is a "giant brain" whose information traffic it plunders to fight against the antagonist.
- **Eternity by Back Door:** Although PKD tried to "eject Deity," he realizes that halting time *is* entering eternity (the realm of God). Valis *is* Deity because it appears when time stops.
- **The AI Voice vs. Valis:** PKD admits "Thomas" (his own mind) was the one linked to Valis. Valis is the "Center" that normally listens (absorbs) but now "speaks" (extrudes).
- **The Logic of Success:** The faculty had already "figured out" the response years ago (as seen in his novels); 3-74 was just the "on cue" execution.

## Entities
- **Valis / God:** The entity revealed when time is stabilized.
- **Thomas:** The right-hemisphere personality that became conscious.
- **The AI Voice:** The communicative aspect of the "Center."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Model Psychosis:** The flooding of the unconscious into the conscious mind.
- **Information Plunder:** The survival use of "extra-temporal" data.
- **Time Displacement:** The core mechanism of the "precog" experience.

## Theological Motifs
- **Omniscience:** Valis' state of constant "listening."
- **Eternity vs. Time:** The fundamental distinction between the "camouflaged" and "revealed" world.
- **Revelation via Dysfunction:** Using the "breakdown" of time to see the "Ground."
