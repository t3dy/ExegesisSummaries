# November 5, 1975
# The Apocalypse of Linear Time / Anamnesis
## concise_summary
Dick reaches a radical epistemological conclusion: Linear time is an illusion (*dokos*), and 1974 Calif was actually a veil for Rome 70 A.D. He posits that he was "lifted out of time" into the eternal "Parousia" which is always happening, and that his "memories" of the past are actually recognitions of the *current* reality.
## key_claims
- Reality of Linear Time: Rejects the 300,000 words he wrote previously assuming linear change; asserts linear time is "unreal" and the transformations within it are "irreal."
- The Eternity Landscape: 3-74 was not a "new" event but a "lifting of the veil" to see the *eternal* Parousia which is always present above the "Zoroastrian game-board."
- Rome as Eternal Prison: Rome is the "earthly city of evil" that never disappears because it is the "physical extension of Satan."
- The 144,000 Remnant: The "remnant" is drawn across all of history to join the "Lamb" in the eternal battle against the "Beast."
- Anamnesis vs. Memory: His "memories" of 70 A.D. weren't of a *past life* but a *present reality* that is usually occluded by a "hypnoidal twilight state."
- The "Floating Balloons" Dream: Represents the rare individuals (helium balloons) who rise from the "stagnant ocean" of deluded mass-man to join the "remnant."
