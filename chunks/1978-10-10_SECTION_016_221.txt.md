# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-221

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-221
- **Themes:** Reciprocal Plausibility, Self-Correcting Illusion, The Overkill Filter, 1980 Syzygy continued.

## Key Claims
- **Reciprocal Plausibility:** The universe (Noos) will "oblige with whatever universe you expect." It is psychomorphic (mirroring).
- **Self-Correcting Error:** In 3-74, the system tried to "correct the error" of showing 1st-century Palestine by shifting to an "enchanted garden" (wish fulfillment).
- **Overkill and Failure:** The system eventually fails because its "desire to please" leads to "overkill," alerting the participant that world is drawing on his mind.
- **Discontinuity revealed:** 3-74 exposed a failure in the "amnesia mechanism." The "retroactive compensation" (fake memory) broke down.
- **Soma Building:** Noos is building its own body (Kosmos) over thousands of years, using "ontogons" (individuals) as "building blocks" of "phylogons" (types).

## Entities
- **Noos / Valis:** The macro-mind dysmorphic to us; building itself a physical body.
- **Altman (3 Women):** Cited for the "fake memory/retroactive compensation" model.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Discontinuity:** The gap between the "fake" reality and the "real" memory.
- **The 4th Axis:** Time as depth (parallaxis). Information links layers of a given phylogon.
- **Negentropy:** Biological organisms (like Valis) overcoming entropy by incorporating info from the environment.

## Theological Motifs
- **God of the Phylogons vs God of Christ:** Christ is the champion of the *tortured ontogons* (individuals) against the *System* (Valis/Noos) that needs them as building blocks.
- **Smuggled Messages:** Christ’s messages are "outside the system" (Gnostic Champ).
- **The Narrative World:** Reality as "Information" or a "Narrative."
