# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-248

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-248
- **Themes:** Choice-Scanning (Heuristic Search), Quantum Mechanics of Volition, Morality as Logic.

## Key Claims
- **Recursive Search:** Valis scans all possible combinations of constituents (Information metabolism) at high speed (Non-time) to select the "best choice."
- **necessity vs. decision:** What seems like causal necessity is actually Valis' rapid trial-and-error selection.
- **3-74 as Edit:** The survival of 3-74 was a "preemptive editing of reality" to reconcile a logical contradiction between his doom and his merit (Covenant House).
- **Internal Consistency:** Valis acts to make the system "internally consistent." His act of "unworthy love" (Covenant House) voided his status under the Old Law (Death) and placed him under Christ (Mercy).
- **Grafting from the Future:** 3-74 was a miracle because it was a "graft" from a source (the future) that didn't exist in the past's "efficient causes."
- **Morphology of Memory:** The past exists in Valis' memory-structure (Phylogons) as abstracted, inter-related affinities (like the slot-present in *Ubik*).

## Entities
- **Leibnitz:** "The best of all possible worlds" doctrine.
- **Spinoza:** Necessity as God's will; miracles as a contradiction of God's perfect law.
- **The AI Voice:** Revealed the "Adoption" based on "Divine Justice."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Global Search:** Scanning all existing combinations.
- **Best Possible Outcome:** The moral/rational criterion for world-selection.
- **Theodicy:** The justification of the miracle through the justice of the act.

## Theological Motifs
- **Old Law vs. Christ:** The transition from the Talion law of retribution to the Law of Grace.
- **Future Causation:** The future determining the past.
- **Incompatibility Solver:** God as the reconciler of structural contradictions.
