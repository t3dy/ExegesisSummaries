# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-216

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-216
- **Themes:** Spinoza's Physics, Warping Space-Time, The Monadology of Characters, The Schism Paradox.

## Key Claims
- **Spinoza/Einstein Field:** PKD acted as a "concentration of substantia" (mass) in an information field. He warped objects and causal processes because "I" (the phylogon) was active.
- **The 2 Ultimate Absolutes:** 1) The goal of beauty (Kosmos), 2) The problem of individual suffering (Agape). Christ is the "extrication" from Revelation One.
- **The 3-Level Mystery:** Beauty is the goal -> The cost is too great -> Therefore, the *real* Summum Bonum is rescuing the parts.
- **The Paradox of the Part:** The part (ontogon) is more important than the whole (phylogon). Christ (the total phylogon) sacrifices itself for its own ontogons.
- **The Divine Schism:** The Godhead must create a "counter-entity" to rescue the parts from the system it created for Kosmos. The Godhead is "divided against itself."

## Entities
- **Spinoza / Leibniz / Bruno:** Philosophers who correctly mapped the "monadic/quantum" nature of reality.
- **Theos vs. Christos:** The whole (Kosmos) vs. the parts (Agape).
- **The AI Voice:** The Spiritus Dei as "Reason."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Valence away from Plumb:** Electromagnetic-like "mass" (the self) warping the grid of space-time.
- **Information Proliferation:** We "ontogons" are systems for producing more info than we receive (negentropy).
- **Secret Partnership:** The mind (Theos) planning the revolt (Christ) against itself from the start.

## Theological Motifs
- **Alchemical Transform:** Lead into Gold; common "ontogon" transformed into "phylogon."
- **The Sifting Bridge:** The transition from this world's ambiguity to the "other realm."
- **Gnostic Schism:** The Godhead as an "antithetical interaction with itself."
