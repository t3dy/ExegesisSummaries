# November 5, 1975
# The Megastate vs. The Summum Bonuum
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the "Megastate" (Rome/US) as a diabolical machine and reconciles Christianity with Platonism. He views "Primary Christianity" as the secret victory of the Platonic "Summum Bonuum" (Highest Good) over the "blind decay" of history, and posits that the 3-74 event was a "re-adoption" of the human race by the Holy Spirit.
## key_claims
- The Diabolical Megastate: The modern state is a "megastate" seeking total control; 3-74 was the "Greatest One" (Spirit) intervening to preserve Dick’s free work.
- Christian-Platonic Synthesis: Christ is the "Summum Bonuum" (Form of Forms) who "persuades" necessity (*ananke*) toward wisdom. Christian "anamnesis" is the realization of this Platonic order.
- The "Second Birth" (Non-Sarx): Receiving the Holy Spirit produces a "second birth" and an "incorruptible non-material body" (Light/Ergic body), as described in the parables of the seed and yeast.
- Re-adoption / Fertilization: The Holy Spirit "fertilized" the human race in Christ; it is "St. Sophia born again" to complete the "graft" that failed during Roman persecution.
- Lost Lore of the Mysteries: The Roman slaughter of early Christians destroyed the "oral tradition" of how to prepare for the Holy Spirit; 3-74 was a "lucky" or "guided" accidental restoration of these rites (votive candle/golden fish).
- The Truth Drug: Notes that "Sodium Pentathol" acted as a "truth drug," de-hypnotizing him to see the "dokos" (illusion) and reverting him to his "true self."
