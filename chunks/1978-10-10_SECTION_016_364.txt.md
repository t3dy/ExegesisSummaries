# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-364

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-364
- **Themes:** God as Selecting Mechanism, Whiteheadian Taoism, Confidence in the Model.

## Key Claims
- **God as Functional Mechanism:** Following Whitehead, God is defined as an "active, intelligent principle of selection" that perpetually chooses the good across millions of branchings.
- **The Confidence of the Model:** PKD tells Pat Warrick this is the first reality model he has "confidence in"—a computer-like entity that thinking during its "off" phase.
- **Causality as illusory Modem:** Causality is not "real" but a mental projector used to connect thousands of static, discrete frames into a flow so that we can discern the *eide* (constants).
- **Quant to Qual Conversion:** Spatiotemporal reality itself is the "converter" that takes quantitative "bits" (information) and turns them into qualitative objects.
- **Leibniz’s Optima:** The "best of all possible worlds" is the result of a rapid selection process whereEvery inferior move is discarded in favor of a better one every trillionth of a second.
- **Taoist Alternation:** The system corresponds precisely to the Yin and Yang alternation (on-off, 1-0).

## Entities
- **A.N. Whitehead:** The source for the "process deity" and "principle of selection."
- **Pat Warrick:** The recipient of this "confidential" resolution.
- **Erigena:** Mentioned for his concept of *Natura* (God and Universe as one).

## Recurring Concepts
- **MMSK (Macrometasomakosmos):** The vast signaling system we reside within.
- **Flicker Binary:** The pulse of being and non-being.
- **Modem:** The mental tools (like causality) used to process information.

## Theological Motifs
- **Selection of the Good:** The purpose of the universal "computer."
- **Void as Decision Point:** The spiritual significance of the "spaces" between frames.
- **Taoism/Boehme Synthesis:** Reconciling ancient Eastern and Western mystical models with cybernetics.
