# Chunk 216 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_216

## Description
PKD identifies *Ubik* as the "only correct model of reality" currently in existence, asserting that it was dictated by Paracelsus to bypass the "Age of Reason." He explores the **Binary Mirror Secret** of reality and his role as a "subversive" against capitalist rationalism.

## Key Claims
- **The 3rd Secret**: Reality is a "mutual synchronized enriching capacity" between two mirrors: the Psyche and the World. The Psyche initiates the projection ("Push"), and the World mirrors it back enhanced ("Pull").
- **Ubik as True Model**: All elements of *Ubik*—the dialectic forces, simulated worlds, trash-layer divinity, and negentropy—are factually correct descriptions of our universe.
- **Subverting the Empire**: PKD's writing is a lethal blow to 18th-century Anglo-Saxon rationalism (Locke, Newton, Bentham) that underpins capitalism. He is replacing it with a "Progressive" Hermetic/Gnostic vitalism.
- **The Case of the 4 Marxists**: PKD believes Lem and other Soviet/Eastern European intellectuals correctly identified *Ubik* as having a radically alternate cosmology, which they viewed as politically/metaphysically subversive.

## Entities
- **Paracelsus**: The the true "author" of *Ubik* who fed the truth to PKD to bypass the mechanical world-view.
- **Stanislaw Lem**: Cited as a theoretician who recognized the "broad social-historical meaning" of PKD's visionary work.
- **Joe Chip**: The protagonist of *Ubik* who exemplifies the "inward knowledge" of a regressing world.
- **The Soviets**: Groups interested in the "non-Age of Reason" worldview presented in PKD's metaphysics.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Assent and Abolition**: In 2-74, PKD "withdrew assent" to the USA 1974 simulation, causing it to "poof" and be replaced by the Rome CAD 45 world he did give assent to.
- **Consensual Validation**: The reason the Roman world faded was a lack of matching belief from external observers who remained in 1974.
- **Set/Ground Discrimination**: An evolved form of perception used by the Brotherhood to see the "Logos" within the noise.
- **Multiple Reasoning (y = y)**: A non-binary "flip-flop" logic attributed to PKD's cognitive style.

## Theological Motifs
- **God as Redefined**: A hierarchy of nine levels where "God" is not an external person but a series of real layers terminating in "The Void."
- **Trash-Form Divinity**: The recurring theme from *Ubik* that the divine manifests in discarded, low-status forms to evade detection.
- **The End of Philosophy**: The belief that "Reason is a great open folly" and that the "Will" or "Imagination" is the true creative force.
- **Subversive Visionary**: The role of the writer as a dismantling agent of the "counterfeit" world-view.
