# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-235

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-235
- **Themes:** Biological Adaptation to Stress, Scientific Parsimony, Speeding Up Time (Lithium).

## Key Claims
- **Scientific Parsimony:** PKD identifies "Time Running Backward" as the single factor that explains 3-74 more sanely than religious "miracles."
- **Biological adaptation:** The precog faculty is like a lensed eye; it anticipated the crisis (death) years in advance so it would be "ready to operate."
- **Lithium and Time Rate:** PKD notes his inner time was speeding up (lithium slows down neural firing, causing outer events to speed up). He "overran" external time until it flipped.
- **The Acting Faculty:** Precognition is not just "knowing"; it must transform into "acting" on cue. "Thomas" seized motor centers because the "ego" was an obsolete species unable to do battle.
- **Abolition of the Ego:** In the crisis, the "Acting" part of the faculty pre-empted the normal personality. It "told me" to get out of the way.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "next generator of life" (the actor).
- **The Lensed Eye:** A metaphor for the evolution of the precog faculty.
- **Lithium:** The chemical catalyst for the time-rate shift.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Thresholding:** The point where a subliminal faculty floods into consciousness.
- **Pretextual Cause:** Re-confirmed as the byproduct of reverse time.
- **Explosion in Reverse:** Metaphor for the pattern and purpose found in backward time.

## Theological Motifs
- **God as "I don't know":** Religion as a "fancy way of saying I don't know what happened."
- **Evolutionary Goal:** The survival of the organism.
- **Mental Capitulation:** Rejecting the "florid religious" for the "biological."
