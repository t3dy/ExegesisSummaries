# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-255

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-255
- **Themes:** Self-Causing Loops, Ex Nihilo Information, The Observer-Participant Ground.

## Key Claims
- **Self-Generating Information:** PKD concludes that the info in 3-74 (e.g. Acts, the beam) was **self-causing**. It had no source (not even the future). Information can create itself ex nihilo.
- **Perception as Repair:** To see the "Secret Partnership" (Unity) is to *cause* it to be. In the Observer-Participant universe, perception is an ontological act.
- **Anamnesis as De-Maze:** We are the "Ground of Being" (Dedalus) who built the maze (the world) and then forgot. Anamnesis is remembering our own identity as the Creator.
- **The Holy Spirit as Progenitor:** No one causes the Spirit to come being; it is its own progenitor, moving retrograde in time to cause its own epiphany.
- **Self-Awareness as Process:** The universe began upon the Godhead's self-perception (Schizophrenia), which caused flux to begin, leading to an infinite regress of evolution.
- **The Eucharist as Remainder:** The original purpose of the Eucharist was to trigger this "Self-caused loop" of anamnesis.

## Entities
- **Dedalus:** The architect who fell into his own maze.
- **Brahman / Atman:** The Divine Mind and the Individual Self (They are One).
- **Burroughs / Diana:** Alternative theories (Virus / Fairy Queen) for the 3-74 intervention.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Ex Nihilo:** Out of nothing.
- **Push-Pull Oscillation:** The inner-outer loop of perception and reality.
- **Mysterium Coniunctionis:** The chemical wedding/union of opposites.

## Theological Motifs
- **Valentinian Gnosticism:** The fall into ignorance and the restoration through gnosis.
- **The Shekhina's "Flickering":** The presence that is and is not, depending on diaspora/perception.
- **Esse is Intelligere:** To be is to be known/understood.
