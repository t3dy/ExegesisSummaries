# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-361

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-361
- **Themes:** Dionysos as Corpus Author, The Adopted Son, Platonist Realism.

## Key Claims
- **Dionysos Epiphany:** PKD re-affirms that Dionysos in him "broke loose" in 2-3-74 during the "lethal snare." This identity is what he calls Valis.
- **The Whole Corpus:** Dionysos is the true author of PKD's entire writing career, moving constantly toward the "apotheosis" of the book *VALIS*.

- **Empire vs. Secret Christians:** The "AI voice" identifies the world as divided between the "Empire" (officialdom/authority) and the "Secret Revolutionary Christians" (dissent). The Xerox missive came from the Empire.
- **Platonism as Key:** The recovery of the "Thomas" identity is the recovery of the "immortal soul" in the Platonic sense (anamnesis of the *eide*).
