# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-300

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-300
- **Themes:** Valis as Brahman, Burning up Karma, The Yogi's Reverse-Creation.

## Key Claims
- **Valis is Brahman:** 3-74 was a "cosmic resorption"—the inverse of creation—where the self "goes back" to the primordial unity (Brahman).
- **Platonism as the "How":** Platonism explains *why* this is possible (accidents are semireal/time is null), while Eastern techniques (Yogi/Alchemist) provide the *what* and the *method*.
- **The Bardo Thodol Trip:** The phosphene graphics were the literal "Bardo Thodol" experience: traveling backward through lives to the moment "before nothing was manifested."
- **Burning up Karma:** Regressing in time "burns up" the residue of past lives and breaks the "weary wheel."
- **Regressus ad Uterum:** The "regression to the womb" is a return to the state of "Chaos before Creation" (the primordial unity).
- **Thomas as Original Self:** PKD didn't just remember Thomas; he *became* him by traveling back to the "Paradise state before the Fall."

## Entities
- **Brahman:** The ultimate, unmanifested unity identified with Valis.
- **Shiva:** The deity whose creation process is reversed by the yogi.
- **The Buddha:** The "King of Physicians" who provides the medicine for time/suffering.
- **Eliade:** The scholar whose work on "regression to the origin" solved the 3-74 puzzle for PKD.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Cosmic Resorption:** The "inverse of the process of creation."
- **Illo Tempore:** The "paradoxical instant" before time began.
- **Synchretism:** PKD’s conclusion that only a blend of Plato, Christ, and the Yogi explains his experience.

## Theological Motifs
- **Immortality:** Attained by "halting manifestation" and going "against the current."
- **Brahman-Atman Identity:** Sharing in the identity equation of the soul and the absolute.
- **The Golden Flower:** The Taoist/Alchemical symbol of this transformation.
