# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-314

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-314
- **Themes:** Melded Worldviews, The Wounded Christ, Phagocytosis of the Empire.

## Key Claims
- **Syncretic Victory:** PKD successfully "homologizes" Christianity and Buddhism/Brahmanism through the "slayer-slain" identification/compassion.
- **The Wounded Cosmic Christ:** The "Iron Empire" (BIP) is a "wound" (spear/iron particle) lodged in the cosmic body of Christ.
- **Secret Christians as Phagocytes:** The underground Christians act as "phagocytes" within the cosmic body, attacking and repairing the "iron" injury.
- **The Plasmate as Living Information:** The blood of Christ is "living information" (the "3rd Torah") currently transubstantiating reality.
- **Process vs. Static Universe:** The "Rational" (Christ/Valis) has invaded the "Irrational" (Old Creation/Karma/Heimarmene). This is a dynamic evolution, not a static state.
- **Twin Revelations:** (1) Secular History: Secret Christians vs. the BIP (2-74). (2) Cosmic/Mystical: Christ defeating the Old Order (3-74). These are the two "regions" of *VALIS*.

## Entities
- **The BIP (Black Black Empire):** Identified with the "Iron Particle" and the spear-wound.
- **The Pantocrator:** Christ as sovereign power over the regenerated "reality field."
- **Richard Wagner:** The *Parsifal* imagery (wound, spear, blood) is applied to the cosmic situation.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Cosmic Corpus Christi:** The entire universe as the body undergoing repair.
- **Logos Made Flesh:** The penetration of "hyle" (matter) by spirit.
- **Chiliasm:** The "Secret Messianic" history of the 5th Kingdom (Christ's).

## Theological Motifs
- **Heilouwele Myth:** Christ camouflaged as "this and that" after death/resurrection.
- **The Captivity Letters:** Paul's writings as the scriptural basis for the "cosmic battle."
- **Third Torah:** The "Living Word" that replaces the Law.
