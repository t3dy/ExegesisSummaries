# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-266

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-266
- **Themes:** Scribe for Scripture, Gothic Space Abreaction, The 13 Theories Abandoned.

## Key Claims
- **Scribe for Scripture:** God approved of *Flow My Tears* and staged 3-74 to prove it was true. PKD's role is to tell the story of the prisoners and their rescue by God.
- **Gothic Space Abreaction:** PKD realizes that in 3-74 he abreacted to a **Medieval/Gothic** worldview (Huizinga's "Vertical Space"). In this world-model, theophany and miracles are logically possible, whereas they are impossible in the modern model.
- **Thirteen Theories Abandoned:** PKD lists 13 explanations he is abandoning (ETI, USSR, Drugs, Psychosis, Rosicrucians, etc.) in favor of one conclusion: **It was the Christian God.**
- **The Ground of Being in Process:** God exists because he *knows* (*intelligere* is *esse*). The universe is God's decision to know himself, which started the dialectic.
- **Rigid Causality as Simulation:** God simulates "laws of nature" (probabilities) but can suspend them (miracle) because his "thought is his will."
- **Pretextual Cause:** God is the real cause behind all "events," exerting power over synchronicity and acausal factors.

## Entities
- **Huizinga (The Waning of the Middle Ages):** The source for the idea of "spatial extension" as a mystical category.
- **The lamb's book of life:** The "hotel register" in his dream.
- **Spinoza/Berkeley:** Philosophers of immanence and the universe-as-divine-thought.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Acosmic Panentheism:** The view that the universe exists within God, and the plurality we see is a simulation of the One.
- **The 11th Datum:** The fact that changes the entire chain of reasoning (The Flip-Flop).
- **Vertical Space:** The sense of height and "extension in depth" found in Gothic cathedrals and 3-74.

## Theological Motifs
- **Theophany:** The direct manifestation of God to man.
- **Pronoia:** Divine foresight and care (providence) based on analysis of the individual.
- **The Black Iron Prison:** The timeless condition of servitude that God attacks.
