# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-215

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-215
- **Themes:** The Anti-Greek Drama, Abolishing the Flux, Phylogon-Ontogon Reversal, Christ as the Human Phylum.

## Key Claims
- **The Anti-Greek Tragedy:** Christianity is an anti-Greek drama because its point is to **valorize the transient individual** against the eternal type. 
- **Turning the Universe Inside Out:** By "Christifying," the means (creature) assimilates the type (God). Flux stops; time becomes space. The individual becomes eternal.
- **Christ as Phylum:** Christ is the "phylogon of our species." When the individual (ontogon) "remembers" Christ, they undergo "resurrection"—transformation into the mind of the whole phylum.
- **Agape as the Key:** Conversion is the "blitz" where the brain leaps to понимать that the Christ story is its own "phylogenic story."
- **Halting the Moment:** Like Faust, Christ "halts the fleeting moment." The individual is no longer a "flickering means" to the species but an "end."

## Entities
- **Hagia Sophia:** The evolved far-future state of the human phylum.
- **Prometheus:** The archetypal precursor to the ontogenic self stealing the "fire" (timeless self) of the phylum.
- **Zagreus:** The God-man prototype torn by Titans.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Weary Wheel / Astral Determinism:** The 2-tape script of Flux/Process that erases the individual.
- **DNA Script:** The physical mechanism of the "Law."
- **Epiperson / Epiself:** The individual (ontogon) vs the timeless species-self (phylogon).

## Theological Motifs
- **Transubstantiation of Time:** The ontogon "eats" the phylogon in the Eucharist, acquiring its immortality.
- **Homology:** Mythic identification with the God-type.
- **Pistis vs. Gnosis:** Trust/Belief vs. Insight into the "phylogenic identity."
