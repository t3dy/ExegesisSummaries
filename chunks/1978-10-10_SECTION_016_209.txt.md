# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-209

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-209
- **Themes:** The Prime Mover's Grace, The Grid between Worlds, Historical Self-Awareness, Waking up the Deity.

## Key Claims
- **God's Total Freedom:** Grace (as in 3-74) is God acting as a "Prime Mover" even vis-à-vis his own nature. It is a "totally free action" without efficient cause.
- **The Interposed Grid:** A "Grid" exists between the percipient and world. It allows for "World within World" and "Info within Info." This lets a select group (True Xtians) live in a different *Umwelt* (70 AD) within 1974.
- **Human History as God's Awakening:** History is the process of the deity waking up to self-awareness. The "opponent" to YHWH is his own antecedent (mechanical) self.
- **The Torah Correction Circuit:** Built into the Torah-World system is a "correction circuit" (Christ/Grace) that keeps reality from becoming sterile and reflexive.
- **Dissolution of Identity:** In 3-74, the fiction of "PKD" dissolved under pressure. What took its place was a "vast mind/brain tracking all persons."

## Entities
- **YHWH:** The dynamic deity involved in history (vs. the static Brahman).
- **The Grid:** The decoding mechanism used to see Valis and the "World Within World."
- **The Messenger:** The one who judged (sentience) instead of reacting reflexively.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Entelechy:** God selects pieces from the antecedent universe to fit into his evolving "soma."
- **Self-Disclosure:** The "tongue of God" (the wisdom-word hypostasis) permeating the macro-soma.
- **Pronoia / Charis:** Providence and Grace; the forces that prevent the system from running down.

## Theological Motifs
- **Spinoza's Necessary System:** A monistic universe where there is only one "I" (God) and human will is a delusion.
- **Original Sin as Blasphemy:** A rejection of the doctrine in favor of the Rabbinical view of man's goodness.
- **The observer-participant Universe:** Valis comes into existence only when perceived by the awareness of the percipient.
