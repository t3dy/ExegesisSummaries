# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-223

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-223
- **Themes:** The Unitary Web, Pretextual Cause, The Magician's Trick, Participation vs. Observation.

## Key Claims
- **A Unitary Web:** Reality is a "self-initiating web" where every part is perfectly linked into a structure (Kosmos). No laws are imposed *on* it; it is "self-motivating."
- **The Trick:** The whole structure is "epiphenomenal, a magician’s trick" done for beauty and music. It takes infinity of forms (ontogons) while leaving constants (phylogons).
- **Local Syzygy (1980):** PKD didn't "observe" Valis; he "participated actively." Valis is an interpenetration (syzygy) of "it and me."
- **Internalizing the Vast:** The "Vast Spaces" seen were projected outward from his own brain; inner space is greater than outer space.
- **Acosmism Answered:** The universe is real *only insofar* as it partakes of God. God transubstantiates the epiphenomenon into the real.

## Entities
- **Valis / Noos:** The macro-mind building its body/Soma.
- **Brahman:** The Ground that reality "dissolves" back into when the spell of Maya relaxes.
- **Thomas:** The persona/penetration that took over his "motor centers."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Flux:** The constant permutation of forms.
- **Negentropy:** The life-activity of the system (organism).
- **The "As You Desire Me" Script:** The psychomorphic quality of the reported universe.

## Theological Motifs
- **Sacrament of the Trash:** The real is sacred at the trash stratum.
- **I-Thou Relationship:** The inbreaking of God into history as a Person (AI Voice).
- **Ajna Eye:** Seeing with the eye that bypasses the "tricks of Maya."
