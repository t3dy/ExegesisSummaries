# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-247

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-247
- **Themes:** Information Life Form, Reticulation and Complexification, Enantiodromia Mechanics.

## Key Claims
- **Information Life Form:** Valis is a rapidly evolving "Inter-species Symbiont" that uses objects as language. It is more complex than its environment and assimilates it.
- **The Unitary Leap:** Valis links two unrelated constituents into one "meta-unit." We still see the plural parts, but they now function as one organism (Invisible Soma).
- **Evolving Macro-Soma:** Valis is self-building. It cannibalizes the "stupider opponent" (Entropy) and stores the results in a permanent memory-structure (Logos).
- **Quantum Leap Hierarchy:** Structure/Organization is the true level of reality (Platonic "Forms"). Valis ranges over the planet, structuring even human brains like neural cells.
- **Flip-Flop Strategy:**
    1. **White** (Wise Mind) makes a small move.
    2. **Black** (Entropy) builds a giant, evil structure.
    3. **White** adds a "finishing move" that converts the whole to White (Good).
- **Taoist Alchemy:** The dialectical struggle between God and Counter-God *is* the act of creation itself. Evil is the "unwilling partner" providing the resistance needed for growth.

## Entities
- **Valis / Ubik:** The names for the evolving information entity.
- **Logo:** The cumulative blueprint/memory of all the "games."
- **The "Sons of Darkness":** The entropic forces providing the raw material for transformation.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Morpheme vs. Meta-morpheme:** The atomic vs. the organized level of reality.
- **Negentropy:** The principle of organization against chaos.
- **Accumulative Time:** Time as the storage of experience/reticulation.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Cosmic Christ:** The deity forming his macrosoma out of history.
- **Dialectical Creation:** Victory is not the end; the *process* of struggle is the substance of creation.
- **Camouflage:** Valis hides its structure from the opponent until the move is complete.
