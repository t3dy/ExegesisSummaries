# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-313

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-313
- **Themes:** The Evolution of Compassion, Tat Tvam Asi, The Extinction of the Individual Will.

## Key Claims
- **Progression of Moksa:** PKD traces his spiritual journey through "evolutionary steps" of compassion: (1) cat, (2) beetle, (3) rat, (4) Galapagos turtles, (5) Honor Jackson.
- **Tat Tvam Asi (Slayer as Slain):** The pivotal insight: "I am that which I slay." This identification destroys the "predatory individual will" and the "ego."
- **Extinction of the Will:** Following Schopenhauer, the "will to persist" in individual essence must be annihilated. Compassion (Mitleid) is the means to this "Nirvana."
- **Tears to Agape:** In *Flow My Tears*, sorrow becomes compassion, then agape, and finally bliss. This "way of the cross" leads from this world to the Father.
- **Parsifal as Catalyst:** PKD realized at age 15 (listening to Wagner) that if "Parsifal" exists, the Christ story must be true. It is "shrewdness" (aesthetic intuition) rather than logic.
- **Dual Initiation:** 3-74 required two ingredients: (1) the Plasmate (living information/blood) and (2) a "catalyst" (possibly implied as the "vortex" of drugs/suffering).

## Entities
- **Parsifal:** The "pure fool" whose quest mirrors PKD's search for the "Good Friday Spell."
- **Schopenhauer:** Cited for the theory of the "denial of the will."
- **Honor Jackson:** A character from *Tears* used in the "slayer-slain" dream realization.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Mitleid:** Compassion that converts sorrow into the presence of God.
- **Aesthetic Criterion:** "What is beautiful is real."
- **Brahman-consciousness:** The state where the distinction between self and other is abolished.

## Theological Motifs
- **Adopted Son:** PKD sees himself as a "Christos" (adopted son) due to his *pistis* (faith).
- **The Nativity in 3-74:** The "birth of Christ in the soul."
- **The Gate of the Son:** Christ (Mitleid/Sorrow) is the gateway to the Father (Agape/Joy).
