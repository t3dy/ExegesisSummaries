# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-362

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-362
- **Themes:** The Ancient of Days, Retrograde Heuristics, Christ as Invasion.

## Key Claims
- **The Ancient of Days:** PKD links his "white woolly hair" vision in *Flow My Tears* to Daniel 7 and Revelation 1. Jesus is the "Son of Man" who receives sovereignty from the "Ancient of Days" (the eschatological judge).
- **Eleatic Continuum:** Purposeful changes in reality are possible only in an Eleatic continuum (unitary field) rather than a discontinuous mechanistic one. This is the "ensouling of the animals."
- **The Royal Fool:** In the "world as stage," the ostensible king is a fool, and the ostensible fool (Hamlet/Yorrick) is the true king/judge/law-giver who utters cryptic clues.
- **Participation in Infinity:** Spinoza's "third attribute" is revealed to be the archetypal dimension of *illo tempore* (dream-time/infinity).
- **Retrograde Heuristics:** Valis/Mind works by "working backward from the final goal." It is a negative feedback loop that solves problems *ex nihilo* before the reasoning reaches consciousness.
- **Christ as ex nihilo Newness:** Christ is defined as any "newness choice" that does not follow from antecedent causes. He "invades" the system to prevent it from running down or losing organization.

## Entities
- **Yorrick/Hamlet:** The archetype of the "Wise Fool" who holds the real power.
- **Ancient of Days:** The deity/judge from the book of Daniel.
- **Pierre Teilhard de Chardin:** Rebutted for his "evolutionary" view; PKD insists Christ is an *invasion* from outside, not a peak of nature.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Teleology:** Reality driven by the *need* for an event rather than past causes.
- **Perturbation of the Field:** The mechanism by which the Divine acts upon the simulation.
- **Heuristics:** The system's method of high-velocity self-correction.

## Theological Motifs
- **Eschatological Judge:** The figure who adjudicates the "books" of men's actions.
- **The Zero-One Binary Event:** Christ as the discrete "on" pulse of re-creation.
- **The Final Goal:** The "Point Omega" that is constantly being applied to the world.
