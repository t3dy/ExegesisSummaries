# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-170

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-170
- **Themes:** Freedom from the Script, Corrective Override, Selection/Adoption, The Resurrected Pantocrator.

## Key Claims
- **Ex Nihilo Newness:** Christ introduces "newness" into a "closed system" (overruling the Torah's formula). This frees the person from the "planetary powers."
- **Surgical Killing of the Script:** The "DNA programming" or "Script" (Eric Berne) is surgically killed/removed, allowing for "renewal."
- **The "Thomas" Epiphany:** Thomas is the "non-fugal" (sane) self that emerged when PKD's evasive/fugal personality could retreat no further under pressure.
- **Central-Vision Self:** Thomas represents the real, methodical self that sees reality "without coagulated hypostases" (Valis/plasmate).
- **The Programmer Inside:** Valis (the programmer) has "microformed itself" to enter the system and control it from within, like a probe monitoring a roast's temperature.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "actual person" who woke up; a citizen of Acts.
- **Belial:** The "Researcher of the auto-feedback world" who maintains the dream.
- **Lucifer / Palmer Eldritch:** The "Younger Brother" who was given dominion but refuses to give it up.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Drift / Entropy:** The mechanical system (Law/Torah) tends to "run down" and must be corrected by an "Argyric power" from outside.
- **Fugal Personality:** PKD's "evasive" right-hemisphere dominant self that was "auto-intoxicated."
- **Resetting of Meters:** The sense that reality's instructions were being updated in real-time.

## Theological Motifs
- **Adoption:** "You were adopted because -" (AI voice). Selection is an act of Grace.
- **The Barded Hook:** Jesus as the "bait" of humanity with "divinity as the hook" to lure Satan into violating his contract.
- **Christ as System-Invader:** Bringing the mechanical system to "sentient life."
