# November 5, 1975
# Jungian Synthesis / The Steersman
## concise_summary
Dick applies a Jungian lens to 3-74, identifying the "Steersman" as the Wise Old Man archetype/Holy Spirit who guided him through the 1974 political reefs. He posits that he attained a "Micro-Universe Parousia" through the "Summum Bonuum" (Form of Forms), while the rest of the world remains in a "dokos" (illusion).
## key_claims
- Jungian Integration: 3-74 was the "death of the ego" and the "birth of the whole self."
- The Steersman: An archetype of divinity (Steersman/Holy Spirit) steered him to safety through the 1971-1974 crisis.
- The Orphic Egg (Firebright): A "pearl" or "egg" of divine light now slumbers within him, waiting for the "Second Birth" at death.
- Parousia vs. Apokalupsis: The *Parousia* (invisible presence) has occurred, but the *Apokalupsis* (visible revelation) is yet to come.
- Jehovah's Witnesses and 1914: Considers the JW theory that Christ returned invisibly in 1914; identifies the modern US as the "Empire of Iron with Clay Feet" (Daniel).
- The "Zenith TV" Dream: A circuit in his TV (his brain) registered Christ’s return with "3 lights" (3rd eye) once the "dark green cellophane strip" (occluding veil) was removed.
