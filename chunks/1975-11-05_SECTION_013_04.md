## concise_summary
Dick explores the "Dionysus in America" theme, linking Christ to the Bacchic "breaker of prisons" who toppled the Nixon gang. - Personality of the Daimon: The entity is a wise, educated Greek woman who remembers the stars and the mysteries.
- Syzygy / Completeness: Sophia + Christ = the "missing half" (Plato) and the "Jungian whole self."
- Steganography: He identifies "porno subtracks" and "filth on KNX-FM" as sensory preparation for "transliminating" the daimon and the secret Xerox info.
- Gnosis vs. Parousia: The Kingdom is accessed through "gnosis" (knowledge) for the Gnostics, but Dick believes his was a "descent" or "Parousia" of Eternity into time.
