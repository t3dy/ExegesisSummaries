# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-143

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-143
- **Themes:** Phylogenic Abreaction, Freud's Trauma Model, The 2000-Year Exit, Body as Tomb.

## Key Claims
- **The 2000-Year Abreaction:** PKD applies Freud's theory of "recovering trauma": 3-74 was a reliving of a trauma from *70 AD Rome*. The "chronic anxiety" of his life was an unexorcised memory of being killed as an early Xtian.
- **Thomas as the Real Me:** "Thomas" is not a spirit or a ghost, but PKD's *actual* life/personality, whereas "Phil" is the enchanted/sleeping mask.
- **Conduit of Space-Time:** The Paraclete created a "conduit" or "garden hose" through time, allowing the 1st Century spirit-filled self to "exit" into the 1974 brain and solve the Xerox trap.
- **Body as Tomb (Soma Sema):** Following Pythagoras and Plato, PKD concludes we are "dead" in this world, which is a "prison" or "tomb." Waking up is "coming to life" elsewhere.
- **Polyencephalic Fusion:** We are "plugged into" a system that feeds forgotten memories back to us as world. We are "possessions of the Gods" in a laboratory or cocoon.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "Apostolic Self" who lived and died in Rome.
- **The Paraclete:** The time-tube that "rode" the phylogenic self into the present.
- **Pythagoras / Plato:** Philosophers of the "Prison-World" and "Remembered Soul."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Abreaction:** The therapeutic reliving of a primal trauma to exorcise fear.
- **Soma Sema:** The Body-as-Tomb theory.
- **Fake Memories:** The "ersatz world" generated to keep the soul asleep.

## Theological Motifs
- **Born from Above:** Resurrection as "waking up" to the fact that we were already elsewhere.
- **Post-Mortem Experience:** The 3-74 perception of "vast technology" (Valis) as a vision of the afterlife.
- **The Second Birth:** The "short-circuiting" of time to link the two advents into a single event.
