# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-241_253_TRANSITION_NOTE.md

4.  **Bicameral Parity:** "Thomas" and the AI Voice are explained as the **Right Hemisphere** becoming conscious, leading to a "two-brained" human of the Third Age.
5.  **The Fiddler Sync:** The 1980 "Fiddler" experience serves as a 1980 "validating miracle," proving that Valis (Adonay) still organizes the "Stockpile" of his life into "Kosmos."
6.  **Quantum Subjectivity:** PKD flirts with the idea that he **caused** Valis through his own perception (Observer-Participant), suggesting he is a "Spark" of the Ground of Being waking up.
