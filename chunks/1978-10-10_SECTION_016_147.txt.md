# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-147

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-147
- **Themes:** Evolutionary Superiority, The Common Mind, Termination of Competitive Plurality, Point Omega.

## Key Claims
- **Evolutionary Shift:** Humanity is rising from "hindsight" (a posteriori) to "foresight" (a priori). A priori knowing will inevitably drive out "approximate" competitive knowing, just as mammals drove out lizards.
- **The Common Mind:** Solving the Xerox problem ended PKD's status as "mens solus" (individual mind). He has now joined the "Unitary Composite Entity" (Valis).
- **The System as Alchemical Crucible:** The "BIP/Maze" is a laboratory designed to "spark off" a priori knowing. The reward for finding the "New Way" (dis-synchronization) is the beatific vision.
- **Transformation of Plurality into Unity:** Evolution leads toward "Point Omega"—a convergence where plural competition is replaced by "Total Integration and Subsumation."
- **Valis as the Breeding System:** Valis is the "Workshop" breeding higher organisms until they reach the level where they can perceive reality *as Valis*.

## Entities
- **Valis:** The "Extant Unitary Composite Entity."
- **Ahura Mazda:** The prototype of the "Wise Mind" possessing a priori knowledge.
- **Jesus:** The first human to reach this evolved a priori level.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Survival of the Fit (A Priori):** The "functional edge" of foresighted knowledge in antagonistic situations.
- **Transmutation of the Solver:** The "Alchemical" result of correctly solving the system's lethal problems.
- **Mis-phasing Stimulus and Response:** The technique of "sabotaging the engrammed response" using high-speed inner tapes.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Spirit of Truth:** The agent that "reminds" disciples and allows them to see the truth.
- **The Lamb's Book of Life:** The registry of the "new breed" of problem-solvers.
- **The Tao / Golden Flower:** The immortal life form that results from the "internal alchemy."
