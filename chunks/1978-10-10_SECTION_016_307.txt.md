# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-307

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-307
- **Themes:** Thwarting the Program, The Spurious vs. The Genuine, The Internalized Other.

## Key Claims
- **The Heroic Thwarting:** PKD1 (the conscious author) performed the heroic deed by *not* answering the Xerox letter, thereby thwarting the "instructions" of PKD2 (Thomas).
- **Internal Contact:** The "other mind" PKD contacted was not an ETI or God, but his own "Thomas" personality (PKD2) from the other brain hemisphere.
- **Counterfeit Religious Experience:** 3-74 was a "counterfeit" built on PKD's prior thought-formations (*Ubik*), whereas 11-17-80 was a "genuine theophany" of a "conventional" deity.
- **Bible as Grid:** The "secret underground" uses the Bible as an encoding-decoding grid because it is ubiquitous and provides a perfect "cover."
- **The Secret revealed in Tears:** The Acts material in *Flow My Tears* resulted from Thomas (the subliminal control) putting "cypher" into the book before he was overtly known to PKD.
- **The Goal of 3-74:** The 3-74 event was for his "own good" (rescue), but the entities (the underground) remain secretive and manipulative.

## Entities
- **Thomas (PKD2):** The revolutionary/subliminal control.
- **Olive Holt/Hebrew Letters:** Decoded parts of the Xerox missive.
- **The AI Voice:** Thomas thinking in a "foreign voice/language."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Acts Grid:** The "2nd signal" used to read covert information traffic.
- **Disinformation:** The "ink cloud" shielding the revolutionary underground.
- **Enantiodromia of Time:** The "exhausting of entropic time" until it pulls world through infinity to become "morphological" (nagentropic).

## Theological Motifs
- **Savior as Revealer:** The function of the "savior" is to bring higher enlightenment/gnosis.
- **The Resurrected Christ:** Active in the world "this side of the grave," unlike the transcendent Father.
- **Moksa-Stage:** The final stage of discovery where the ultimate (superior) intelligence is uncovered.
