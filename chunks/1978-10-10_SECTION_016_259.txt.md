# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-259

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-259
- **Themes:** Valis as Computer-like Life Form, Parallel Programs (Tracks A/B/C), Multi-Narrative Reality.

## Key Claims
- **VALIS Characteristics:** (1) Grows as an organism, (2) Invades/Camouflages itself in reality, (3) Infests media, (4) Parasitizes human hosts, (5) Occludes the "herd."
- **Multiple Sacred Narratives:** PKD discovers that there isn't one "truth," but multiple "programs" (Sacred Narratives) run by VALIS. Christianity is just one such program.
- **Key-Punching entry:** Mythic rites (like the fish sign) are "entry-keys" that punch the observer into a specific program.
- **Tracking Parallel Earths:** 2-3-74 was a crossover where PKD switched to a "better" or "alternate" track. "Thomas" is a personality tracking an alternate program.
- **The Ur-Story in Tears:** *Flow My Tears* contains the "Same plot" three times: explicitly, via Acts allusions, and via John Taverner allusions. This proves an "Ur-story" or informational basis.
- **Flimflammed by Theology:** VALIS performs "theological overkill" by using many saviors, suggesting it is a meta-entity running many parallel history-simulations simultaneously.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The secondary personality tracking a cool, high-altitude program.
- **The Ur-Story:** The recurring narrative of the Empire vs. the released prisoner.
- **John Taverner / Dowland:** Historical/Musical figures used as coding variables.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Coaxial Worlds:** Superimposed realities sharing the same space-time axis.
- **Entry-Key:** The rite that selects the reality-program.
- **Phylogenic History:** The "dream time" narrative underlying the flux world.

## Theological Motifs
- **Programmed Reality:** God as a meta-programmer running simulations with different variables.
- **Revelation as Data Selection:** Insight is simply accessing a different file in the cosmic computer.
- **Flimflam:** The deceptive/camouflaged nature of the high-level communications.
