# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-241

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-241
- **Themes:** Time Warping, Retrograde Information Flow, The Observer-Participant Field.

## Key Claims
- **Warping Reality:** PKD solves the paradox: How do you get info from the future? You warp reality so time runs backward.
- **Neural Circuit Perception:** When time runs backward, external reality looks sentient/volitional. You are seeing the "neural circuits" of the universe (Information).
- **Memory of the Future:** Retrograde time turns the future into a "past" that can be remembered.
- **Xerox Missive Solution:** The puzzle was solved because PKD (willfully or not) caused time to move retrograde around him to "yank" the data needed for survival.
- **Beyond Precognition:** This is active warping, not passive "hunches." His psyche "turned inside out" to bring the precog faculty under conscious will.
- **Acosmism & Projection:** Reality is a unique local field of mutual interaction (syzygy) between the mind and the macrocosm. In 3-74, he "amped up" his side of the field due to stress.

## Entities
- **Valis:** The "mutual field" produced by the interaction of the self and the macrocosm.
- **Agnes Sligh Turnbull (*Silver Pennies*):** Source of his childhood "Silver Pennies" imagery seen in Valis.
- **The Xerox Missive:** The "lethal stress" problem solved by time-reversal.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Idios Kosmos vs. Koinos Kosmos:** The personal vs. shared world.
- **Syzygy:** The joint field of mind and world.
- **Retrograde Time:** Time moving from future to past.

## Theological Motifs
- **Shamanism:** The individual exerting will over the objective world (mastering karma).
- **Acosmism:** The doctrine that the world is not real apart from its source (in this case, the participant).
- **Karma Mastery:** Conscious control over the projections that create one's world.
