# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-245

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-245
- **Themes:** Conversion into Phylogons, The "Plantation" Universe, Retroactive Ontology.

## Key Claims
- **Ontogon-Phylogon Conversion:** 3-74 represented PKD's conversion from an "ontogon" (ephemeral individual) into a "phylogon" (permanent structure in Kosmos).
- **The Plantation Concept:** Constituents are raised like crops on a "plantation" (Stockpile) and, when "ripe" (perfected), are incorporated into the permanent edifice of Valis.
- **Retroactive Ontology:** Pattern/Meaning confers Being. The "knitting together" of themes (as in the Fiddler sync) makes the previous "irreal" life real retroactively.
- **Valis as Steersman:** In the moment of completion, God acts as "steersman" or "trump card," allowing no latitude for error as the structure enters the total Kosmos.
- **Aesthetic Criteria:** Valis fits parts into the Soma based on "benignity" and "beauty," subsuming evil elements into a larger good.
- **Chaos to Kosmos:** The primary activity of God is converting chaos (stockpile) into Kosmos (organized soma).

## Entities
- **Valis:** The "Principle of Organization" that organizes its own Soma.
- **Whitehead:** Cited for God as a "Principle of Selection."
- **The Plasmate:** The "cypher" or information-trace in *Flow My Tears*.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bedeutung ist Sein:** Meaning is Being.
- **Stockpile vs. Soma:** The chaotic potential vs. the organized body.
- **Enantiodromia:** The flip-flop of components into their opposites (Evil to Good).

## Theological Motifs
- **Continuous Creation:** God is still in the "days of creation," taming the "obdurate antagonist" of chaos.
- **The Master Builder:** God as the architect refining the structural involvement of all beings.
- **The Wiser Horn:** The superior intelligence that always finds the "good" fit.
