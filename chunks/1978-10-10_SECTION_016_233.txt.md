# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-233

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-233
- **Themes:** Information Extrusion, Reverse Causality Mechanics, The Precog-Psychosis.

## Key Claims
- **Valis as Listener/Speaker:** Normally, Valis "absorbs" info (intellegere). In reverse time, Valis "articulates" or "divulges" secrets.
- **Explosion Run Backward:** Reverse causality looks like "sentient volition" because plural pieces move together to reassemble into a unity. This is the origin of the "Valis" perception.
- **The "Smart Typewriter" Precog:** Precognition is the result of tracking an interval, failing, running time back, and remastery.
- **Thomas as retrograde:** "Thomas" or the right hemisphere moves retrograde, feeding info to the left (forward) hemisphere. This explains the "Another century" / Acts perception.
- **Ubik Cycle:** "Thomas" is PKD's postmortem self returning to his living brain. The "breakaway" failed, creating a syzygy between the dead/living selves.

## Entities
- **Adam Kadmon:** The idea of the universe being "inside" the percipient.
- **Thomas:** The persona tracking the BIP (Black Iron Prison) world.
- **Jason Taverner (*Flow My Tears*):** The archetype for lateral world movement.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bardo Thodol:** The postmortem state where the soul divest itself of karma.
- **Heimarmene:** Interchangeable with "Normal Cause and Effect."
- **Syzygy:** The fusion of the forward and retrograde minds.

## Theological Motifs
- **Divulging Secrets:** Revelation as the "yield" of a reversed timeline.
- **Bicameral Mind:** The split-hemisphere model of time perception.
- **Alternate Worlds:** The lateral shift between tracks triggered by a change in the past.
