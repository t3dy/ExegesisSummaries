# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-331

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-331
- **Themes:** Restoration from Occlusion, Scanner/Valis Continuity, The 2nd Signal.

## Key Claims
- **Trash and Sanctity:** The "trash of the alley" is revealed as sanctified; the occlusion prevented seeing the Kingdom that was here all along.
- **Scanner as The Fall:** *A Scanner Darkly* is identified as a "Paradise Lost" (the story of the Fall into occlusion/impairment). Bob Arctor represents the occluded man who does not know he is occluded.
- **Valis as Restoration:** *VALIS* is "Paradise Regained," the story of restoration through Gnosis. Horselover Fat is the "fused" Arctor, healed from the wound.
- **The Memory Test:** Saving Gnosis is the ability to recognize a picture of oneself as oneself ("Mein eigenes Gestalt"), ending self-estrangement.
- **Coherent Revelation:** Starting in 2-74, PKD experienced a coherent disclosure of an apocalyptic nature diametrically opposed to orthodox theism.
- **Collapse of the Profane:** The 2-74/3-74 event was the collapse of the present profane world back into the sacred world of Acts, which is the "actual" reality.

## Entities
- **Bob Arctor:** The symbol of "fallen" man in *Scanner*.
- **Horselover Fat:** The symbol of "restored" man in *VALIS*.
- **Thomas:** The resurrected/true identity.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Occlusion:** Ontological ignorance created by the Fall, equated with the condition of characters in *Scanner*.
- **The 2nd Signal:** The "added" reality of the Holy Spirit that restores the fallen cosmos.
- **MMSK (Macrometasomakosmos):** The complex, articulated "secret history" that is already well-along in transubstantiating the universe.

## Theological Motifs
- **Transubstantiation:** The rational (Nous) invading the irrational (Flux) to transmute the essence of reality.
- **Steganography:** The secret history and presence of Christ hidden "in the trash."
- **Physician and Victim:** Christ as the High Priest and the offering, healing the cosmic occlusion.
