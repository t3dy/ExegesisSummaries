# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-308

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-308
- **Themes:** The Perception of Valis, Double Inference, The Missing Template.

## Key Claims
- **Deviation from the Template:** Valis is defined not as a *thing* but as a "deviation" or "perturbation" from a "straight line." To see it, the observer must possess the "straight line" (the Gestalt/Template) internally.
- **Double Inference:** (1) The straight line is inferred from visible contours; (2) the "something" (Valis) is inferred from the deviation from that line.
- **The Acts Grid as Form World:** The "Acts glass/grid" is a supra-spatiotemporal constant—a Platonic *eidos* (Form world) used to measure the flux world.
- **Anamnesis as Template Recovery:** Finding Valis requires "recovery" (anamnesis) of these prenatal universals. Postnatal experience serves to "revive" this latent knowledge.
- **Accidental disinhibition:** PKD continues to weigh if 2-74 (the awakening) was a "divine miracle" or a "finite technology accident" (sodium pentathol).
- **The Combat as True State:** The "grid" doesn't disclose a conspiracy but the "true state of affairs": the titanic Zoroastrian/Gnostic struggle between light and dark across history.

## Entities
- **Plato:** Referenced via the *Encyclopedia of Philosophy* for his views on universals and anamnesis.
- **Valis:** The "deviation" or "tug" on the reality field.
- **Zoroaster:** The source of the light/dark dialectic.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Straight Line:** The internal Platonic form used as a baseline for measuring reality.
- **Acts Lens/Grid:** The "saved" historical template (Acts AD 45).
- **Set-Ground Discrimination:** The process of extracting "signal" (Valis) from "noise" (world).

## Theological Motifs
- **Light vs. Dark:** The dialectic combat as the "true use of the world."
- **Invasion by Mind:** Something outside reality (mind/spirit) using it as an interface.
- **BIP (Black Iron Prison):** The "Empire" as the dark side of the dialectic.
