# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-210

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-210
- **Themes:** The Divided Message, The Meta-Self for Info-Processing, Steganographic Retrieval, Omnipresent Pattern.

## Key Claims
- **The Divided Message:** "Part of the message is in the person and part outside." The "inner Grid" (e.g. Olive Holt) must be applied to the "outer Signal" (the missive) to form a coherent whole.
- **Integrate Info-System:** Humans are not "observers" of the system but "parts" (components). We process metainformation all the time without knowing it.
- **Retrieval Awareness:** The Xerox missive crisis brought his "meta-self/meta-functioning" into conscious awareness because it involved "steganographic information."
- **Valis as Unitary Entity:** Valis is simply "the total entity which includes us as it really is." The A I voice is the system's own thought-process.
- **The 4th Axis Scanning:** Valis scans the 4th axis (Time) like we scan space. It sees what is "coming" because for it, the form-completion axis is already laid down.

## Entities
- **The Meta-Self:** The higher part of the mind that solves info-problems (steganography).
- **Valis:** The omnidistributed mind who connects the layers of a form logically.
- **The Shekhina:** The immanent deity who "formed" in him to overrule normal causality.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Linking/Clutch/Engramming:** The terms for how the meta-mind processes data.
- **Jell-O Box:** The metaphor for the divided message (the two halves of a code).
- **Plasmate:** The living Torah/Info-blood identified with the messages.

## Theological Motifs
- **Steganography:** The hidden information within information.
- **Zenophane's God:** The entity that "does not move" because it is everywhere already.
- **A Priorism:** The meta-self's ability to know things without empirical experience (e.g. the AI voice knowing about the army officer).
