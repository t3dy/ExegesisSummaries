# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-328

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-328
- **Themes:** Meta-Cognition and Meta-Perception, The 4-D Life Form, Universal Evolution.

## Key Claims
- **Evolutionary Sequence:** 
    1. **Meta-Cognition:** Abstracting spatiotemporal sets.
    2. **Meta-Perception:** The "Ajna eye" opening to 4-D space.
    3. **The Vision:** Perceiving the MMSK (partially) and Valis (the life form).
- **Valis as 4-D Permanent:** Valis is a more evolved life form because it already lives in 4-D space, making it immortal and camouflaged to 3-D beings.
- **Time as Spatial Axis:** Linear time is an illusion caused by our 3-D limitation. Ages are actually "depths" along the 4th spatial axis.
- **Plato’s Anamnesis:** Validated as the "recovery of universals," which is the cognitive foundation for 4-D perception.
- **Bilateral Hemispheric Parity:** The "3rd eye" may be the right hemisphere of the brain coming on (as it apprehends space).
- **Asher as Depth Marker:** The word "ASHER" is so ancient that it serves as a "linking device" at a great depth, visible only to 4-D perception.

## Entities
- **Plato:** His theory of universals is the "correct reportage" for this evolutionary step.
- **Buddha:** Correlated experience of "enlightenment" (waking up).
- **Linda Ronstadt:** Her albums contain the word **ASHER**, which PKD sees as a deep-depth morpheme in a secret traffic.

## Recurring Concepts
- **UTI (Universal Terrestrial Intelligence):** The sophisticated 4-D life form.
- **Phylogons:** Accretions along the temporal-spatial axis.
- **Age as Depth:** Estimating the age of a text by its perceived spatial depth.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Eye of God:** The Ajna eye as a sense organ previously denied to humans.
- **The "2-Eyed Standpoint":** Most of *VALIS* is written from a 2-eyed perspective about a 3-eyed reality.
- **Spontaneous Extraction:** Deciphering messages becomes spontaneous once one sees age as depth.
