# The Zenith TV Dream / Terms of Presence
## concise_summary
Dick defines the stages of the Parousia (Presence), Epiphaneia (Appearing), and Apokalupsis (Revelation). He interprets a dream about a "Zenith TV set" as a brain-circuit detecting Christ's invisible return through the "3 lights" (3rd eye) once a dark-green occluding "strip" is removed.
## key_claims
- Stages of Advent:
  - *Parousia*: Invisible arrival (Current/since 1914).
  - *Epiphaneia*: Manifestation to the individual (Dick's 3-74).
  - *Apokalupsis*: General visible manifestation (Future/Sophian birth).
- Toppling the Prince of This World: The 1974 historical intervention was the "rightful King" unseating a "functional diabolic" (Nixon) from the "citadel" (Washington).
- The Zenith TV Dream: Dick’s brain has a dormant circuit (3 lights) that registers the Parousia; the "Manual" (Bible) explains it.
- Green Cellophane Strip: Represents the "occluding membrane" (Original Sin/deformed faculties) that must be "stripped" away (referencing Calvin/puns).
- Plasmatic Presence: The spirit is "atmospheric" and "plasmatic," dwelling at "cloud level" (Noosphere).
