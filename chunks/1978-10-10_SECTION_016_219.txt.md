# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-219

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-219
- **Themes:** Restore Man to Pre-Fallen State, Judaism vs. Christianity (Pauline Critique), The Frozen Torah vs. Living Christ.

## Key Claims
- **The Pauline Leap:** Paul's "Original Sin" is a necessary premise for the concept of "Salvation." If man isn't fallen, he doesn't need saving.
- **Judaism’s Stasis:** Judaism accepts "Fallen Man" as eternal and focuses on "what not to do." It lacks a mechanism to restore man to Eden.
- **Christianity’s Dynamics:** Christianity preaches the "Second Adam" who undoes the Fall. Man is "restored."
- **Living Torah:** Jesus is the "Tongue of God" and the "Living Torah" who supercedes the "Frozen/Ossified" Mosaic Law.
- **Personal Verification:** PKD claims he was "restored to his pre-fallen state" in 1974, giving him personal proof of the Gospel.

## Entities
- **St. Paul:** The daring architect of the "Salvation/Fall" framework.
- **Adam:** The prototype of the "primal catastrophe."
- **Rabbi Herz:** Cited as the opposing view (Judaism lacks the "Fall" concept).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Apostolic Succession of Ossification:** Judaism ossified -> Christianity (1300) ossified -> Protestants ossified. Now: a "New Revelation" (Parousia) is due.
- **Vicarious Atonement:** The "heroic idea" of God redeeming man through abasement.
- **Eden / Paradise:** The "pre-fallen state" PKD saw (the palm tree garden) in 1974.

## Theological Motifs
- **The "Felix Culpa":** The "fortunate fall" where the remedy (Christ) is greater than the malady.
- **National Ethnic State:** A critique of Judaism's "static" end-goal vs. Christianity's "reversal of all creation's fate."
- **Zoroastrian Grounding:** Christianity's grounding in individual soul-survival and eschatology.
