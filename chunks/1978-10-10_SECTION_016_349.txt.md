# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-349

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-349
- **Themes:** Retelling Acts, The New Kerygma, Revolutionary Millenarianism.

## Key Claims
- **VALIS as Acts Retold:** PKD realizes *VALIS* is a retelling of the Acts of the Apostles. Horselover Fat's grief over Gloria is the grief of the disciples after the crucifixion. The "Spirit" returns to turn grief into joy.
- **The Rhipidon Society as the Eleven:** The secret group in *VALIS* represents the original apostles receiving the "Kerygma" (message).
- **Sequential Parousia:** 2-3-74 was the time of Acts (Pentecost/Holy Spirit); 3-18-81 is the revelation that the Parousia (the actual return) has now occurred.
- **Sophia’s Revolutionary Charge:** The new Kerygma is millenarian and political: "You will take his power from him... Go and do this." It is about establishing the earthly Kingdom now.
- **Superimposed Advents:** The First and Second Advents are seen as two "passes" of a single event. This explains the superposition of Acts AD 45 and USA 1974.
- **Victory in the Trash:** The Holy Spirit’s victory is seeing the "crucified and risen Christ" in the "debris of the alley."

## Entities
- **Sophia:** The giver of the new Kerygma.
- **The Rhipidon Society:** The modern "apostolic" group.
- **King of Tears:** The tyrant/tyranny that must be overthrown (Nixon/BIP).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Kerygma:** The essential proclamation of the faith, now updated for the Second Advent.
- **Enthusiasmos:** Divine possession that reveals the mysteries of Christ.
- **Millenarianism:** The belief in a literal, earthly Kingdom of God.

## Theological Motifs
- **Transubstantiation of Debris:** The miracle of finding the divine in the profane.
- **The Comforter:** The Holy Spirit as the advocate who prepares the way for the King.
- **OT View of Time:** Seeing historical events as parts of a unified supernatural narrative.
