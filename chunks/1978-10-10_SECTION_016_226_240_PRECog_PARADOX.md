# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-226-240 Summary Note

## Overview
Chunks 226-240 mark a profound breakthrough in PKD's 1980 re-evaluation. He shifts from a "biological/scientific" explanation of 3-74 back to a **Scientific-Theological synthesis**. The core discovery is the **Time-Travel Paradox of the Precog**.

### Key Developments:
1.  **The Precog Paradox:** Precognition is explained as **Retracking**. PKD lived through the Xerox missive once, died (Track A), and is now reliving it with memories of failure (Track B).
2.  **Thomas as Former Self:** In a reversal, PKD identifies "Thomas" as his **former living self** (from the "real" world) whose memory/will intruded into PKD's current **postmortem simulation**. 
3.  **Reverse Time Mechanics:** 3-74 is seen as the "future" (Track A) moving backward into the "present" (Track B). This creates "Pretextual Cause"—the appearance of purpose/design when effects precede causes.
4.  **Miracle as Edit:** A miracle is an orthogonal intervention ("Point X") from outside time. It retroactively generates a "fake" history (A) to lead up to the miracle (B).
5.  **Jesus vs. Torah:** PKD validates his 3-74 "Adoption" by grounding it in **Christian Logic**: "unworthy love" (aiding worthless kids) is the only act that "overrides" the retributive machine of Law/Justice (Torah).
6.  **Valis as God:** Valis is defined as God because it is the **Unitary Entelechy** where all causation is internal. The "laws of physics" are merely our perception of Valis' internal growth decisions.
