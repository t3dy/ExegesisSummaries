# SECTION_013
# November 5, 1975
# Letter to Lem and Rottensteiner / Vertical Intervention
## concise_summary
Dick reflects on the "destructive vectors" represented by Lem (USSR) and Rottensteiner (US/Establishment) and how "vertical intervention" (3-74) saved his independence. He sees his work as a battleground for "two hostile megaentities" and identifies himself as a member of the "144,000" who avoided the "Mark of the Beast."
## key_claims
- The Lem/Rottensteiner Trap: Dick believes Stanislaw Lem (USSR) and Franz Rottensteiner (Western critics) intended to "capture" and "manage" his work. 3-74 was a "vertical intervention" by God to preserve his autonomy.
- The Xerox Letter as a Test: The Xerox letter was a "fishing" expedition (possibly US authorities) to smoke out subversives; the Holy Spirit gave him a "compulsive" behavior (responding to the Martyrs ad) that confused the investigators.
- Mark of the Beast: Identifies "computer-like numbering" and "modern polyester suits" as the Mark of the Beast. The "144,000" are those who resist this numbering and follow the "Lamb."
- 3rd Eye Vision: His "3rd eye" vision of the "helium balloons" (elect) rising from the "stagnant ocean" (deluded mass) confirms the "selection" process.
- Stagnant History: History is a "determinism" rolling downhill until God "reaps" the over-ripe crop (the harvest of the end-times).
