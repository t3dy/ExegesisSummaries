# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-234

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-234
- **Themes:** Reverse Causality as Art/Design, The Image of Deity, Information Retrieval.

## Key Claims
- **The Illusion of Design:** If time runs backward, mechanical causality disappears, leaving the image of "volition, sentience, and unity." This "Design" is Valis.
- **The "Backward" Revelation:** Running the tape backward yields information and pattern where "forward" was white noise. This suggests "forward" is the wrong way to use reality.
- **Information Extrusion:** Valis is no longer camouflaged when time stops/reverses. It revealed vital medical info about Chrissy because intelligence is palpably present when effects precede causes.
- **Precognition as Survival:** Subjective time-reversal is a "coping mechanism" of high survival value. It allows an organism to net information it would otherwise get too late.
- **Dropping Theology?** PKD attempts to explain Valis purely as a "time-disruption perception," removing the need for God—only to realize that halting time *is* entering eternity (God’s realm).

## Entities
- **Valis:** The "pretextual cause" entity; the image of Deity flushed from camouflage.
- **Chrissy:** PKD's son, whose birth defect was revealed by the information-yield.
- **The AI Voice:** "Thomas" moving retrograde.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Pretextual Cause:** The semblance of purpose that masking the absence of physical cause.
- **Eternity:** The realm lying outside of time (where Deity is seen).
- **Survival Talent:** The lensed eye of the mind for perceiveing the future.

## Theological Motifs
- **Shekhina:** Identfied as the presence that/who works backward in time.
- **The Void:** The state preceding creation, found at the end of the reverse process.
- **Information vs. Noise:** God as the "Meaning" of the reversed tape.
