# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-347

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-347
- **Themes:** Driving Time with Logic, Idios vs. Koinos, The Merciless Appraisal.

## Key Claims
- **Understanding vs. Revelation:** The 3-18-81 realization is that the Parousia is "understood" through a logical sequence, not just "revealed."
- **Abolishing the Idios Kosmos:** When the "one mother seen 3 times" logic strikes, the private world (*idios kosmos*) is abolished in favor of the shared reality (*koinos*), which turns out to be Christian apocalyptic history.
- **Real Time as Logic:** Real time is driven forward by the necessity of a priori reasoning. Spurious time is a "programmed monadic internal clock" preset by a third factor.
- **Transcending the Preset:** Consciousness can transcend the pre-synchronized *idios kosmos* only through "ineluctable abstracting logic."
- **The Merciless Appraisal:** The 2-3-74 experience stemmed from a "merciless appraisal" and logical scrutiny of reality that eventually wore down the "veil of seeming."
- **Constants of the Field:** Under exceptional circumstances, reality reveals "constants"—plural spatiotemporal sets (like Acts and USA) working off a common basis.

## Entities
- **Malebranche:** His occasionalism and "intelligible archetypal ideas" are the framework for seeing "all in God."
- **Parmenides:** The source for the discrepancy between a priori truth and sensory report.
- **Wittgenstein:** Invoked for the idea of "world as case" defined by logic.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Idios Kosmos vs. Koinos:** The private dream vs. the shared, true creation.
- **Causation as Pretext:** The realization (from 1949) that causation is irreal, finally confirmed in 3-74.
- **Syntax vs. Inflection:** Reality processed as information where meaning derives from distribution (position).

## Theological Motifs
- **Parousia as Exhaustion:** The end-state that sets in when all "evasive modifications" in reality have been exhausted by scrutiny.
- **D-Day Logic:** The "Identifying Name" or sign of recognition (the Golden Fish) that matches the percipient's inner template.
- **The Secret History:** Actual time moving from Acts to the Parousia, bypassing the simulation.
