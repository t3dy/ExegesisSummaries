# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-284

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-284
- **Themes:** Time Dilation, The Frozen Real World, The Magician's spell.

## Key Claims
- **Deception of Time:** If accidents A and B are just two ways one thing looks, time has not passed. We are in a state of "titanic time dilation" or a "spurious interpolation" by Satan.
- **The Frozen Real World:** From our perspective (speeded-up/deluded), the real world (Realm 3) looks "frozen" like a freeze-frame. What we think of as 2000 years may just be a billionth of a second in Real Time.
- **Blindness vs. Sight:** 3-74 was the return of "sight." We are literally blind to the real world, living in private, illusory worlds (*idios kosmos*).
- **The Work of a Magician:** Our world is a "spell" or "mockup" created by a magician (Satan/Belial/Simon Magus). Gnostic acosmism is the correct response to this "irreal world."
- **Valis/Ubik as the Real World:** VALIS/Ubik is the real world invading the "fake" world of flux to de-occlude us. The "Parousia" has happened, but we sleep and do not see it.
- **The Message Explosion:** A message from the real world (Realm 3) spanning one minute there is "exploded" to span 2000 years here. This explains the extreme communication difficulty.
- **Eye in the Sky:** PKD links this to the "Palmre Eldritch" visage—the master magician whose irreal world replaces the real one.

## Entities
- **Satan/Belial/Master Magician:** The architect of the irreal flux world.
- **Christ/Valis/Ubik:** The invader from the real world.
- **Thomas:** The personality belonging to the "real" time of Acts.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Time Dilation:** The slowing down or stalling of real time.
- **Idios Kosmos:** The private, deluded world of the individual.
- **Meta-Morpheme:** Fragments of the real message scattered across our history.

## Theological Motifs
- **Parousia:** The arrival of the Kingdom, hidden from the "sleeping" masses.
- **De-occlusion:** The "eyes opening" to the real world.
- **"Wachet Auf!":** The call to "Wake up!" from the messenger.
