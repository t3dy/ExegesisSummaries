# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-166

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-166
- **Themes:** Reality as Storage, Information Retrieval, Outside the System, YHWH as User.

## Key Claims
- **Reality as Container:** Reality is a "storage mode" (like a chip or tape) for information (Torah). It doesn't solve problems; it contains the info that *does*.
- **The Retrieval Model:** Info is "poured" into reality and can be extracted (retrieved) as "answers."
- **Passing Outside:** 3-74 was PKD being taken "outside" or "above" the system to see its actual function: processing informational problems for YHWH.
- **YHWH as Reader:** YHWH is the "controlling mind" that reads the output. PKD's anamnesis was a partaking in this "Larger Mind" that sees the readout over millennia.
- **The AI Voice:** The voice is the "voice of the system" or the readout mechanism.

## Entities
- **Torah / Plasmate:** The living information passing through reality.
- **YHWH / Valis:** The creator, sustainer, and user of the system.
- **Thomas:** The "past self" who possesses the necessary knowledge for the retrieval.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Information Processing Transaction:** The fundamental nature of reality.
- **Bardo Thodol:** The "postmortem" state of being outside the system.
- **Xerox Missive:** A specific informational problem that the system used to trigger the "answer" (intervention).

## Theological Motifs
- **Deus Sive Substantia:** God as the user of the system.
- **The Controlling Mind:** Merging with the mind of God to see the "printout."
- **Sacraments of the early church:** The "Koine" as a stored info-form.
