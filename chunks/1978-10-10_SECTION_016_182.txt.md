# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-182

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-182
- **Themes:** Hypertime C, Evolutionary Homeostasis, Transubstantiated Parts, The Master Self.

## Key Claims
- **Hypertime C:** There is a "real time" (Acts period AD 70-95) that exists in parallel to our "spurious time B" (1974). Waking up means moving from B to C.
- **Christ already here:** Christ didn't "come" in 3-74; he was already here camouflaged. The 3rd eye (set-ground discrimination) was required to see Him.
- **Thomas as "Track C" Self:** Thomas might be the "Remote Descendant" or "Track C" self that replaces the obsolete PKD. PKD is the "dormant" state; Thomas is the "waking" state.
- **Interspecies Symbiosis:** Evolution aims toward a "crossbonding" of humans and the "plasmate" (Valis) to form a "binary organism" (Christ/Man-God).
- **Valis as Dominant Species:** Valis is a trans-temporal info-form that monitors and corrects the "signal" of our world by replacing disfigured reality-bits with "real" bits of itself.

## Entities
- **Valis / Zebra / Ubik / Logos:** The negentropic vortex invading the closed system.
- **Thomas:** The "Master Self" who woke up; the real and superior personality.
- **Diana:** The tutelary spirit/guide given to PKD for "acting right."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Signal Degeneration:** The "copy" (our world) differs from the "form" (blueprints) and must be surgically corrected.
- **Ajna Eye / Metacircuits:** The tools used by the "born again" to transcend deterministic DNA programming.
- **Transubstantiation:** Valis inserting "bits of its own reality" into the "irreal copy" to repair it.

## Theological Motifs
- **Gnostic Admixture:** The world is now a mix of the "copy part" (Samael) and the "Valis part" (True God).
- **The Bridegroom:** Humans as the "female host" receiving the "male" fertilizing spirit of Valis.
- **Firebright:** The name for the new life form/progeny arising from the symbiosis.
