# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-268

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-268
- **Themes:** Joyous Eucharist, Anticipatory Joy, The Orphic "Immortal Purity."

## Key Claims
- **Abolition of Subject-Object:** In the high-level perception of Valis, the percipient and the seen (Christ) become a single field. The Spirit in the mind perceives Christ in the world.
- **Defective Liturgy:** PKD critiques the modern Eucharist for focusing exclusively on sin, suffering, and death. It has lost the "anticipatory joy" of the Kingdom and the presence of the *Risen* Christ.
- **Celebration of Life:** Real communion (which PKD performed with his partner Chrissy under the Spirit's direction) is a joyous celebration of the Spring (Life returning after the Winter of Death).
- **The Orphic Goal:** The Orphics sought not just immortality, but **divine purity** (*Happy and Blessed One, thou shalt be God instead of mortal*).
- **Becoming Divine Now:** The goal of the mystery religions was to become divine *in this life* through purity, which immortality then preserves.
- **Sacrifice as Means:** Exculpation from sin (the sacrifice) is merely the *means* to the *end* of partaking in divine life. Christianity has traded the end for the means.

## Entities
- **The Paraclete:** The "Spirit of Truth" that Jesus promised would guide to the complete truth.
- **Jane Harrison (Prolegomena):** Source of the Orphic "Son of Earth and Starry Heaven" theology.
- **Joseph Campbell:** Noted for the view that Christianity lost the goal of "New Life."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Hierogamy:** The holy marriage / reconciliation of opposites.
- **Purity:** The state required for the "child of starry heaven" to emerge.
- **Eucharist:** Reinterpreted as a feast of the harvest/new life rather than a funeral rite.

## Theological Motifs
- **Bacchos:** The state of the Orphic initiate who has attained divinity.
- **Grain of Wheat:** The necessity of death for "bearing much fruit" (John 12:24).
- **Homology:** The believer being "made the same" as Christ via the sacrament.
