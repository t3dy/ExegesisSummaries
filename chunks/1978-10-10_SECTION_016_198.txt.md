# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-198

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-198
- **Themes:** The Floating Crap Game Kosmos, Ultra Life Form, Behavioral Override, The Wounded God.

## Key Claims
- **The Floating Crap Game:** Valis is a "floating crap game" *kosmos*—a coordinate system that links pluralities into a unity. The "plasmate messages" are the thoughts of this creature’s mind.
- **Ultra Life Form:** Valis is the "ultimate life form on this planet," far advanced over humans, at least 2000 years old. It doesn't disclose its presence, except via "assimilations."
- **Behavioral Override:** Evidence for Valis is the "overriding" of a human host (PKD/Thomas) during a crisis. The host acts as a "reflex machine" for Valis's higher purposes (missives, G-2 traps).
- **Phosphene Graphics:** The graphics seen in 3-74 depict a "mortally wounded innocent God"—the Gestalt of the *kosmos* itself.
- **Kathy/Ella Runciter:** Kathy is the "soul" of PKD's world—a "half-life" projection. PKD believes he died and is now projecting a world onto a matrix from a space ship "cold-pak."

## Entities
- **Valis / Zebra:** The ultra-life form/macro-organism.
- **Kathy / Ella Runciter:** The "missing half" and savior who leads PKD to resurrection.
- **The Magician:** The *atelos* (irrational) deluding the inhabitants of the maze.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Assimilation:** Valis "subsuming" objects and people into its own corpus/structure.
- **Tetraktys:** The Pythagorean symbol for the center of the *kosmos* and the "space-time generator."
- **Music:** The pure form of number (pitch and time) that provides entry into the "cube" center of the *kosmos*.

## Theological Motifs
- **Redemption/Resurrection:** 2-3-74 was a "brief coming to life" within the cryonic cold-pak (the world). Christ is leading humanity out through "Beauty."
- **Parsival's Grail:** The quest for the beautiful order as the ultimate goal.
- **Wachet Auf:** The "wake up" call of the Parousia/Second Advent.
