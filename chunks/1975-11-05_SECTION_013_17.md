# November 5, 1975
# The Afterword / The Growth Process
## concise_summary
Dick provides a concluding "Afterword" to Section 13, reiterating that linear time is an illusion used as a "veil" for judgment. He views life as a "growth process" where humans must "choose the true from the counterfeit" to reach adulthood and receive the "Holy Spirit" (the fertilization of the soul).
## key_claims
- Reality of the Apocalypse: The "vision of the apocalpyse" is actually a vision of *authentic eternal reality* hidden behind the "linear-time world of change."
- The Veiled Game: We are in a "framed" world, veiled so we can be judged based on our choices; once judged, the veil is lifted and we are "lifted from the board forever."
- The Role of Christ: Christ came to give us "clues" (parables/riddles) to help us locate the "side of Light" in a hidden struggle; without him, the riddles are unsolvable.
- Adoptionist View / Fertilization: The Holy Spirit (the "dove") "quickens" or "fertilizes" the soul, creating an "immortal incorruptible body" (non-sarx) that "hatches" after the death of the flesh (*sarx*).
- Preparatory Lore: Preparation for the Holy Spirit involves secret rites (votive candle/golden fish) and an "altered state" (resembling the mysteries or "truth drug" effects).
- Restoration of the Kingdom: Receiving the Holy Spirit allows one to experience the "Kingdom" *now*, as a shared corporate life with the resurrected Christ.
