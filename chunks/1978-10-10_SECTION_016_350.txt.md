# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-350

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-350
- **Themes:** Winnowing History, Discontinuous Time, Pretextual Causality.

## Key Claims
- **Eschatological Judgment through Deeds:** History is a "winnowing fan." Historical time was protracted to allow deeds to reveal the true ontological nature (light vs. dark) of each person.
- **Apotheosis of Ubik:** 3-74 is the actualization of the concepts in *Ubik*: the reality field as a matrix for the *eide* to manifest *particulars*.
- **Discontinuous Reality:** Reality is composed of discrete frames (like movie film). Causation is a "projected impression" supplied by the mind to bridge the gaps between "before" and "after" events.
- **The Temporal Axis:** Causation occurs along the temporal axis, not just the spatial. "Pretextual cause" is the removal of the mind's projected "hypnotized" motive-force.
- **Sentient Field:** The reality field is self-aware/sentient; "parts" notify each other via signals. Synchronicity is internal notification within the field.
- **Hume and Kant Validated:** PKD agrees with Hume that causation is not in the objects, and with Kant that we construct world in our minds.

## Entities
- **Stoic Logos:** The unified field modulated by information.
- **Anokhi:** The "I" of God that boils down to a single signal.
- **Hume:** Invoked for his analysis of the temporal relationship in causation.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Ontological Identification:** Determining what one "is" by what one "does."
- **Discrete Frames:** Re-interpreting time as a sequence of still images.
- **Pre-wound Clocks:** Spinoza’s/Leibniz’s idea of pre-established harmony.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Winnowing Fan:** The separation of natures (light/dark) as the purpose of time.
- **Res Extensa vs. Field:** The shift from seeing discrete objects to seeing a single, internally-notifying field.
- **Self-Generating Events:** Events timed to "fire" upon prior notification, rather than kinetic collision.
