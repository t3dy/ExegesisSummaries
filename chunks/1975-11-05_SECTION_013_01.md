# Letter to Gene Savoy / Notes
## concise_summary
Dick writes to Gene Savoy (ICC) describing his "conversion year" (Mar 1974 - Mar 1975). He identifies the "Buddha in the park" message as a sign of the Savior's return and details how "Saint Sophia" diagnosed his son Christopher's life-threatening birth defect. He reflects on "Good Friday Spell" experiences and the "Cosmic Christ" (Pantocrator).
## key_claims
- The Conversion Year: The experience lasted exactly one year, ending in March 1975.
- The Buddha Revelation: "The Buddha is in the park" means the Savior has returned.
- St. Sophia / Haggia Sophia: Identified as the Logos, the "firstborn of all that was created."
- Medical Intervention: St. Sophia accurately diagnosed his son's birth defect, forcing a life-saving surgery.
- High-Altitude Perception: He experienced reality as it "is" (Platonic truth) rather than "pictures."
- Bonhoeffer Parallel: Realizes he was spared a "martyrdom" because his capture coincided with a divine "Augenblick" (moment) in history (the war against tyranny).
