# Sophia as Pantocrator / The Roman Landscape
## concise_summary
Dick identifies Sophia/Nous as the ultimate agency and goal of the universe (The Pantocrator). He argues that 1974 Calif *is* Rome 70 A.D., and that the "dokos" (illusion) is a 3-D movie designed to test humans while the "Apocalyptic Landscape" of the Bible remains the only true reality.
## key_claims
- Sophia as Pantocrator: Sophia is not just a "comforter" but the "Nous" (Mind) that runs the universe; she is the "creator goddess" of Gnosticism.
- The 3-D Movie (Dokos): Modern life (1974/76) is a "hypnotic trance" or "3-D movie" being shown to us; we are actually in Biblical times (Acts/70 A.D.).
- Anamnesis of the "Now": His knowledge of Koine Greek and Roman customs was not a "memory" of the *past* but an awakening to the *present* (Absolute Reality).
- Vertical Axis Penetration: The Parousia is an "absolute event" on an "absolute landscape" that penetrated our relative universe on a "vertical axis."
- Future Judgment: Judgment Day will be the moment the "dokos curtain" rolls up, exposing the Biblical landscape to everyone, showing exactly where they stand in the "goats-sheep struggle."
- The "Pornographic" Trigger: Notes that "porno subtracks" and radio static served to "de-hypnotize" the brain, allowing the underlying Roman reality to "leak" through.
