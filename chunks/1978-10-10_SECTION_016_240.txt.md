# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-240

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-240
- **Themes:** Miracle as Orthogonal Intervention, Seismic Retro-Causality, The "Hypnotic Dream" of Reality.

## Key Claims
- **Miracle defined:** A miracle is an intervention from outside time ("Point X"). It creates a "B" (miracle event) that sends causes backward in time to create "A" (pretextual explanation).
- **Retroactive Seismic Waves:** Event "B" generates "A" so that the miracle doesn't seem disjunctive. "A" is a "fiction" generated from the future to justify the miracle.
- **Valis as the World:** Valis is not *in* the world; Valis *is* the world. All change is its internal will.
- **The Hypnotic State:** Reality "A" is like a spell or state of hypnosis cast over the percipient. The miracle "Awakens" us from this fake causal sequence.
- **Refining the Signal:** The "dual tracking" (Forward A vs. Orthogonal B) is like a signal cooked onto another. "B" overpowers and edits out the "failure" track.

## Entities
- **Valis / God:** The entity whose will is action.
- **YHWH:** The Hebrew name identified as the source of the letters/override.
- **Spinoza:** Critiqued for seeing miracles as "weakness" rather than "love/providence."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Pretextual Cause:** The "fake" history generated retroactively by the miracle.
- **Orthogonal Cause:** Cause entering from "90 degrees" (outside the time axis).
- **Concurrent Realities:** The overlapping of the "Natural" and "Miraculous" tracks.

## Theological Motifs
- **Divine Providence:** The issue is God's love (helping the creature), not God's power.
- **Adoption:** The 3-74 event as a specific "bill of particulars" override.
- **Sui Generis Acts:** Actions with no antecedents (no causes) because they are from "B."
