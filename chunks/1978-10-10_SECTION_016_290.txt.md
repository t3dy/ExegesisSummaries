# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-290

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-290
- **Themes:** The Christian Fish Sign as Key, Zoroastrian Dialectic, Valis as Immanent Mind.

## Key Claims
- **The Familiar in the Alien:** The Christian fish sign was a "familiar object" in an alien world that triggered anamnesis and the re-collection of the missing self.
- **Zoroastrian Gnosticism:** PKD introduces the Zoroastrian dialectic: a battle between a Savior (a priori knowledge) and a Deluder (a posteriori knowledge) over the "is-not" (the irreal world).
- **Immanent Deity:** Valis is the "immanent mind" or "controlling deity" of the Milesians (Anaxagoras/Xenophanes), regulating the world order from within.
- **Refining his System:** PKD considers dropping Gnosticism for a more Greek/Milesian view where Nous (Mind) is the Absolute. The flux world "feeds" the metasoma-kosmos with new "parts" (reticulation/arborizing).
- **Time is Totally Irreal:** Change is merely the accidents "looking different." The macrometasomakosmos is eternal and nonspatial.
- **Magician/Deluder/Satan:** To explain why we fall into an *irreal* world, PKD posits a "Master Magician" (Satan) who occludes us through forgetfulness/amnesia.

## Entities
- **Anaxagoras:** Substitutor of *Nous* for *Psuche*.
- **The Milesians:** Philosophers who saw God as the regulating mind of the world.
- **The Deluder (Satan):** The "magician" architect of the interpolation.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Enantiodromia:** The mechanism by which the Tao/God regulates the Yin and Yang.
- **Macro-metasoma-kosmos:** The evolving, sentient, negentropic structure Valis builds.
- **Reticulation and Arborizing:** The thickening/perfection of the unchanging Form world.

## Theological Motifs
- **Theodicy:** God allowing us to suffer the consequences of an epistemological error.
- **The "Is-Not":** The counterfeit/spurious world generated by the Lie.
- **Search for the Truth:** The truth is "splintered" across history; PKD's task is its re-collection.
