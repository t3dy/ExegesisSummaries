# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-344

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-344
- **Themes:** The Reward Cycle, Subliminal Programming, The Two Spirits.

## Key Claims
- **The Reward Cycle:** A dream reveals PKD is in the "reward cycle." The Parousia is here, and everything is now under the control of God (Valis).
- **Zoroastrian Winnowing:** The separation of the "sheep" (sons of light) and the "goats" (sons of darkness) has already occurred. Judgment is fixed.
- **Programming vs. Info:** The "covert info traffic" is not information but subliminal programming that *compels* the true Christians to identify themselves through their acts.
- **Atonement and Choice:** Humans had free will during the "period of commingling," but now they are purely one nature or the other, compelled by the Holy Spirit.
- **The eschatological Judge:** Christ as the judge who separates the "light" molecules from the "dark."
- **The RC Brothers/Holy Mother Church:** The Church and its technological arm (Rosicrucians) have known about the Parousia and are using these signals to identify the saved.

## Entities
- **The ESG (Eschatological Judge):** The role Christ plays at the end-time.
- **Holy Mother Church:** Re-evaluated as the secret possessor of this knowledge ("Tears" as the Church's cypher).
- **Zoroaster:** Cited for the model of the two antagonistic spirits and the final winnowing.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Commimgling:** The mixed state of light and dark within a person, which ends at the judgment.
- **Inwit/Bate of Inwit:** The internal "spirit" that constrained PKD from the Xerox missive while the goats answered.
- **Subliminal Cues:** Instructions that act as "imperatives" like D-Day orders.

## Theological Motifs
- **Restoration of Equilibrium:** Valis as a "governor" (ruling mind of the mechanism) invading its own creation to restore balance.
- **Bitheism:** Gnostic view of man + God against the world-mechanism.
- **The Afterlife:** The state of the world now that judgment has occurred and choice is over.
