# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-351

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-351
- **Themes:** Hallucinating Continuity, The Form-axis Readout, Cybernetic Taoism.

## Key Claims
- **Double Hallucination:** We hallucinate *discontinuity* in space (seeing discrete objects) and *continuity* in time (seeing causal flow). In truth, space is a continuous field and time is discrete points.
- **Motion is Impossible:** Following Parmenides, PKD asserts there is no "time" or "motion," only a sequence of static point-events in a specified order (like numerical order).
- **Ubik’s Form-Axis:** The *eidos* contains all its unique, imperfect particulars. It "plays them out" and then retrieves them (cosmic resorption).
- **Causation as Faulty Syntax:** "Causation" is a faulty way of reading the syntax of information changes in the carrier (reality).
- **Linking/Relinking Wheels:** The macro-system (Valis) uses these mechanisms to convert quantity (binary digits) to quality (information/words).
- **Enantiodromia:** The binary dialectic (0-1, Yin-Yang) is the "flip-flop" that drives the system's activity.
- **Ex Nihilo Information:** Information arises spontaneously on an "as needed" basis (micro-cosmogenesis) for biological survival.

## Entities
- **Parmenides:** The ancient philosopher whose denial of motion is central to PKD’s new model.
- **Schopenhauer:** Cited for the "Will" that can create reality out of nothing.
- **Valis:** The "modulation/activity" of the carrier/field.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Transducer:** The brain's role in converting digital information into 3-D objects.
- **MMSK:** The field in which everything (source *eide* and returned reticulation) is stored.
- **Pre-set Clocks:** Logic replaces kinetic force as the ordering principle.

## Theological Motifs
- **Pantheism Denied:** The field (reality) is NOT God; God is the "music" (modulation/Valis) played *on* the instrument.
- **Spirit over Matrix:** The "Spirit of God" moves across the field (modulation).
- **The Numerical Basis:** Pythagoras was right; numbers are the *einais* (essence) of reality.
