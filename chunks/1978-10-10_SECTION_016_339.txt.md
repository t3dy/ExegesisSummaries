# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-339

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-339
- **Themes:** The Five Elements of VALIS, God's Teleological Pattern, The 5th Savior.

## Key Claims
- **VALIS as Total Synthesis:** The book *VALIS* fuses five independent threads: (1) Epistemology/10-vol meta-novel; (2) Politics; (3) Religion/Mysticism; (4) History; (5) Actual life events.
- **God's Dialectical Play:** God plays His hand in history non-logically (A to B to C to D is not the sequence). Meaning only emerges when the "final piece" (thing four) links them all together.
- **The Adversary's Defeat:** The antagonist (Satan/Empire) has only *a posteriori* knowledge; he is fooled by the teleological "subsystem method" until it is too late.
- **5th Savior vs. 60s Failure:** The political movement of the 1960s failed because its leaders were killed. The "50th/5th Savior" will lead a successful revolution to seize power.
- **Active Millenarianism:** The Kingdom will not come on its own; we must act politically. *VALIS* is a call to positive political action.
- **The Ultimate Meta-Abstraction:** The Jungian "intuitive faculty" seeing relationships between all separate thought-clusters in an instant.

## Entities
- **Che Guevara:** The model for the messianic revolutionary leader.
- **Nixon:** His downfall was a preliminary success; the next step is the actual takeover.
- **The Lord of Hosts:** The "eschatological judge" whose divine judgment is imminent.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Teleology:** The idea that reality follows a purposeful design rather than causal logic.
- **Rhipidon Society:** The secret group formed to carry out the commission of Sophia after Nixon.
- **Structure as Meaning:** Meaning is not intrinsic to events but is found in the final completed pattern.

## Theological Motifs
- **Eschatological Authority:** PKD claims "divine sanction" for his political handbook.
- **Christ as Joe Gideon:** The "Suffering Man" who is the 5th Savior (from the Britannica).
- **The Kingdom is Here:** The "invisible secret true Christians" are surfacing to take over the government.
