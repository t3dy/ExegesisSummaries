# The Graft / Broken Continuity
## concise_summary
Dick posits a "Graft" theory: the Holy Spirit (Sophia) was a divine "graft" into the human race that failed twice due to Roman persecution (Jesus and the early Christians). He theorizes that the Holy Spirit has been "atmosphere-bound" for 2,000 years, waiting for a "new absolute incarnation" (St. Sophia born again).
## key_claims
- The Failed Graft: The Holy Spirit "split" into 12 disciples, then 144, but the continuity was broken when the Romans killed all trained "receivers" of the spirit.
- Atmosphere-Bound Spirit: Because no one was trained to "receive" the souls of dying Christians, their spirits (and the Holy Spirit itself) stayed in the atmosphere, unable to loop back.
- 3-74 as Restoration: Dick "accidentally" performed the rites (Golden Fish/Sodium Pentathol) that allowed a 2,000-year-old "early Christian" spirit to "graft" onto him.
- Sophia's Rebirth: "St. Sophia will be born again" means a new absolute fertilization of a woman by the Holy Spirit to restart the failed process.
- Subliminal Tests: The Xerox letter was a "subliminal test" by authorities to see if his "right hemisphere" would contact the Party; the Spirit saved him by instilling "antipathy toward Communism."
- Desert Winds: The "desert winds" blowing through his apartment were a necessary precursor, like Moses hearing God in the desert.
