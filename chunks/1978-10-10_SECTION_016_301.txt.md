# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-301

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-301
- **Themes:** Regressus ad Originem, Backward Time Travel, Brahman as Valis.

## Key Claims
- **Regressus ad Originem:** 3-74 was a literal "regression to the origin"—witnessing the inverse process of creation (cosmic resorption).
- **Platonic Validation:** PKD only understood this "impossible" experience (moving from multiplicity back to unity) after reading Plato's metaphysics.
- **Bardo Thodol:** The phosphene graphics were a literal "Bardo Thodol trip," the most esoteric wisdom on Earth (Tibetan Buddhism).
- **Time is Irreal:** Backward travel is possible because time is an illusion (Plato/Kant); the percipient sees the primordial unity restored in the morphologically-arranged realm.
- **Pre-Fallen State:** The "lifting of the occlusion" occurred because PKD was restored to a pre-fallen state through this regression.
- **Phylogenic Axis:** PKD regressed along the "phylogenic axis of time," seeing millions of years of evolution run in reverse.

## Entities
- **Brahman:** The ultimate unity identified with Valis.
- **Mircea Eliade:** His work *Myth and Reality* provided the term "time can be overcome."
- **Plato:** The philosopher whose metaphysics proved the "how" of PKD's experience.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Cosmic Resorption:** The "implosion" of multiplicity back into the unitary absolute.
- **Anamnesis:** Not just memory, but the recovery of the original, un-shattered state of the soul.
- **Chaos before Creation:** The state reached at the end of the regression.

## Theological Motifs
- **Soteriology through Memory:** Absolute memory (omniscience) gives the power of a "Cosmocrator" and frees one from karma.
- **Moksa (Liberation):** Achieved by "burning up" memories through reliving them in reverse.
- **Chaos/Seed:** The restoration of the original pre-cosmogonic situation.
