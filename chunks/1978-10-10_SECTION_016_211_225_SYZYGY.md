# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-211-225 Summary Note

## Overview
Chunks 211-225 represent the final radical revaluation of the 1974 events from the perspective of **1980**. PKD synthesizes his Gnostic "Empire" model with a **Participatory Field Theory** (influenced by *The Tao of Physics*) and a profound **Pauline critique** of Law vs. Grace.

### Key Transitions:
1.  **From External God to syzygy:** The realization that Valis isn't just an external "Invader" but a **syzygy** (interpenetration) between the Greater Mind and the percipient. Reality is **psychomorphic**.
2.  **The Sheep Dream Resolution:** Reconciling the "Summum Bonum" of Kosmos (Art/Beauty) with the horror of individual suffering. Christ is the champion of the "Part" (Agape) against the "Whole" (Beauty).
3.  **The Schism in the Godhead:** Creation itself damaged the Godhead, causing a split between the **God of Law/Justice (Theos)** and the **God of Mercy/Agape (Christos)**.
4.  **Man as Redeemer:** The "Salvation Scheme" is flipped: the "Restoration" of man to his pre-fallen state is the only way to "lead God back to the throne."
5.  **Ontogon vs. Phylogon:** The mechanical system of the "Species" (Phylogon) using the "Individual" (Ontogon) is the "Prison." Christ is the "Phylogenic mind" placed at the service of the individual.
6.  **Pauline Superiority:** Christ is the "Living Torah" whose ethics and power to "restore" supercede the "Frozen/Ossified" Mosaic Law of Judaism.
