# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-196

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-196
- **Themes:** Epistemological Riddles, The Binary Off-Cycle, Her Second Thought, Pythagorean Cosmogony.

## Key Claims
- **The Ultimate Riddle:** PKD is fascinated by the binary proposition: "Am I alive and she (Kathy) is dead, or am I dead and she is alive?" He views this as the ultimate application of the *Ubik* puzzle to himself.
- **Breaking Karma:** The power of Karma over PKD was broken in 3-74. He is "dead" to his old way of being and "alive" to a new, free one.
- **The "Off" Side of the Dialectic:** In 3-74, his neural firing speeded up so fast he perceived the "off" part of the on-off binary cycle governing reality. This "non-being" is the subcarrier of the dialectic.
- **Her Second Thought:** St. Sophia (Wisdom) designed the universe (the First Thought). The "Second Thought" is the *telos* (Valis/Thomas) inserted into the irrational *atelos* (the artifactual world) to structure it.
- **Pythagorean Anamnesis:** 2-74 was the reacquisition of the "intact corpus of Pythagorean Gnosis." Valis is the *kosmos* (beautiful order) growing from the center by assimilating the "unlimited" (atelos) stockpile of parts.

## Entities
- **St. Sophia / The Bird Woman:** The designer of the binary computer/universe.
- **Valis / Telos:** The "rational invading the irrational."
- **Thomas / Apollonius of Tyana:** The Pythagorean "Master Self" who achieved anamnesis.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Telos vs. Atelos:** Limit vs. Unlimit; the active force of order vs. the disordered scrapheap.
- **Binary Computer:** The universe as a dialectic machine designed by Sophia and actualized by God.
- **Kosmos:** Specifically defined as structural perfection/beauty in arrangement.

## Theological Motifs
- **Shamanistic Apollonism:** The religion of Apollo/Pythagoras/Sibyl involving anamnesis and the "Second Birth" (spirit birth).
- **Transubstantiation:** Valis "devouring" the irrational to turn it into rational structure.
- **Minaean Code:** The idea that secret brotherhoods use specific signals (like the Golden Rectangle) to trigger memory.
