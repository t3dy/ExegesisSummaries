# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-191

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-191
- **Themes:** The Deep Sleep Agent, Retroactive Track Change, Time-Arcing (1971-1974), The Revolution of Sophia.

## Key Claims
- **Deep Sleep Agent:** PKD considers himself a "deep sleep agent" for a revolutionary conspiracy led by "Secret Ruled" (early Christians).
- **Time-Arcing (1971-1974):** The "Taco Stand" incident in 1971 was fixed/arced to the 1974 3-74 event. Thomas was "trained" in '71 to surface in '74.
- **The Female Ruler Returning:** Christ/Sophia is the "rightful original ruler" ousted by the "Regent" (The Empire). She is returning to overthrow the prison.
- **Retroactive Surgery:** History was modified "surgically." Track A (the world of *Tears*) was a future that was "abolished" so it never happened, replaced by Track B (our world).
- **Tachyons from the Future:** Thomas is an agent from the *future* (using tachyons) who moved backward in time to remaster the past (the "The Eagle has Landed" signal).

## Entities
- **Thomas:** An agent from the future / Secret Revolutionary / Disciple of the female Christ.
- **The Empire:** The "Regent" / Tyranny of the Past that never ended.
- **Linda:** Involved in the "dysfunction/manipulation" of the time-tracks.

## Recurring Concepts
- **BIP vs. Newness:** Moving into the future is "freedom"; moving into the past is the "congealed iron prison."
- **The "Scrambler":** A defensive screen or "mind-scramble" used by higher powers to prevent PKD from formulating the truth too clearly.
- **The Xerox Missive:** Seen in a hypnagogic vision as "red apples" (Edenic temptation) but countered by the "healers."

## Theological Motifs
- **NT as Code:** The New Testament is a political "hard-core" revolutionary code for setting up a literal Kingdom on Earth.
- **Female Christ (Sophia):** The secret identity of Jesus/Diana.
- **Apotheosis of the Womb:** The "Healers" caused 3-74 to protect the "progeny" (Thomas) hidden in PKD.
