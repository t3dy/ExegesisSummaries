# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-327

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-327
- **Themes:** 4-D Perception, Time as Space, The 3rd (Ajna) Eye.

## Key Claims
- **4th Spatial Axis:** 3-74 was an "evolutionary leap" in human perception: seeing 4 spatial axes instead of 3. In this state, **time turns into space**.
- **Living Information life form:** Valis/The Plasmate is a life form that lives in 4-D space. It is camouflaged in 3-D but visible once the "ultra-parallaxis" (4th eye) comes on.
- **Persistence of the Past:** Because time is a spatial axis (depth), the past (phylogons) does not perish but is preserved in the MMSK.
- **Depth = Age:** Age is perceived as spatial depth. Older morphemes (ASHER, FELIX) lie "deeper" along the 4th axis than contemporary text.
- **Hypnotic Command:** The 1974 experience was a "command to wake up," setting off the GABA fluid drop and the Ajna eye.
- **The 3rd Eye:** A new sense organ for human evolution that sees through the "occlusion" of 3-D time/space.

## Entities
- **Gautama (Buddha):** Experienced "waking up" (dibba-cakkhu), which PKD correlates with 4-D perception.
- **Patrice:** Pointed out that each *eidos* containing its previous forms was a "major philosophical breakthrough."
- **Connie Wheeler:** Her voice (and the Beatles' "Strawberry Fields") acted as a wake-up command.

## Recurring Concepts
- **MMSK (Macrometasomakosmos):** Our 3-D world seen from a 4-D perspective.
- **Phosphene Graphics:** Visions of prior spatiotemporal "sets" arranged along the space axis.
- **Set-Ground Discrimination:** Enabled by the 4th spatial axis, allowing one to see the "signal" (Valis).

## Theological Motifs
- **Shamanism:** Moving backward thousands of years through "wafer-thin holograms" of time.
- **Parsifal Quote:** "You see, my son, here time turns into space."
- **The "High/Low" Text:** Seeing different "depths" in the text of *Flow My Tears* based on the age of the language.
