# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his 1974 experience as a "Discernment of the Present" rather than an "Anamnesis of the Past," concluding that Imperial Rome *exists now* in a metaphysical form. He proposes that 1,500 years of "Apostasy" have been deleted, linking the present directly to the authentic Fish-Christian era (100-250 AD).
## key_claims
- 3-74 was "Discernment": seeing the true, normally obscured nature of the present (Imperial Rome/Police State).
- "It was, it is not, but yet it is": The secular evil (Rome) was defeated but remains as an invisible metaphysical prison.
- Deletion of History: God has "snipped out" 1,500 years of sterile apostasy, joining 1974 directly to the authentic period of 100 AD.
- Continuity of Truth: The "Living Water" (Logos) flows again from the far-past juxtaposition, making Attic Greek technically "present."
- Two Romes: The secular empire (historical) vs. the metaphysical counterpart (eternal evil).
- The Secret: We are in a Roman prison, and "help is on the way" (Parousia).
