# Victimhood and the Detection of Evil
## concise_summary
Dick continues his meta-analysis of "victimhood," suggesting that by serving as "bait," the victim forces evil to unmask itself for God's judgment. He explores the "orthogonal" convergence of his novels and the 3rd level of reality, where writing becomes a "replication" of the divine word.
## key_claims
- Victim as Bait: Suffering is not meaningless; the victim lures evil into the open. Once evil "acts out its nature," it is visible to the "all-seeing eye."
- The Secret Gnosis of Detection: This theory must remain secret; if the "fallen angels" knew they were being judged, they would hide their behavior.
- Reality levels (Synthesis):
    - Level 3 (The Constructed world) is becoming more like Level 1 (The Logos).
    - Thought produces thought through the "intermediary stage" of material/nature.
- Evolutionary replication: Humanity/Technology is the "intermediary stage" through which the original Logos replicates itself (The 2nd Advent).
- *Maze* model: The 1st Advent was a "conspicuous" breakthrough; 3-74 was a breakthrough of *information* directly from level 1 to level 3.
- The Landscape of Level 1: The "real" landscape is Biblical/Apocalyptic (Rome, Christians, God).
- The Power of Rollback: Discovering that time can be rolled back (as in *Maze* or 3-74) is the definitive proof that the current world is an "illusory document."
- The Electronic/Biological/Logos Loop: Level 1 (Logos) generates level 2 (nature) which generates level 3 (us/tech) which then begins to look back at level 1.
