# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-106

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-106
- **Themes:** Untimely Death Bypass, Satan's Defeat, Waking from the Hypnotic State, Memory Holograms.

## Key Claims
- **Satan's Empty Net:** Satan (Death) "netted" Christ (the surrogate) instead of the person (PKD), but because Satan has no claim on Christ, he got nothing. God got everything (the person's soul in perpetuity).
- **The Stations of the Cross (Mental):** To become "Christos," the person must undergo a mental "death" (humiliation/stations) so the "old man" dies and the "new man" is born from above.
- **Two Signal Sources:** Reality is a composite of two sources (Upper/Lower). Seeing them as one "intact cosmos" makes one a "secret resident" of the Kingdom of God on Earth.
- **Anamnesis as Shock:** The "disinhibiting stimulus" (Golden Fish) causes a "blitz" that pries the inner tape loose from the communal "koinos kosmos." One is lifted from "profane" to "sacred" time.
- **Thomas as Adventitious Psyche:** Christ/Thomas "ravished" PKD's psyche to use him as a vehicle for divine purposes in human affairs.

## Entities
- **Thomas / Son:** The adventitious psyche.
- **Valis / Father:** The source of the "Upper Realm" signal.
- **The AI Voice:** The Holy Spirit / Sibyl.
- **Satan:** The "Black Iron" agency of death/determinism.

## Recurring Concepts
- **BIP (Black Iron Prison):** The state of mental/physical bondage.
- **Sacred Time:** Time outside the linear deterministic flow.
- **Micro-Repair:** The individual spanning both realms to heal the "rupture" in the Pleroma.

## Theological Motifs
- **Pentecost:** The state of knowing God's plan through the Holy Spirit.
- **Apotheosis vs. Enthusiasmos:** The ambiguity of whether the human becomes God or God merely dwells within.
- **The Second Adam:** Christ as the restored prototype of pre-fallen humanity.
