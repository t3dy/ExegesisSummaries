# The Future Me: Valis as the Omega Self
## concise_summary
Dick proposes a radical new theory: "Valis" and "Thomas" are not external gods or ancient people, but PKD's own "future evolved self" traveling backward in time via the gene-pool. This "Omega Man" intervened in 1974 to help his "Past Man" (PKD) and to overthrow the Nixonian tyranny.
## key_claims
- Future Self Intervention: "Thomas" is PKD's future self (from hundreds or thousands of years hence) traveling retrograde along the inner gene-pool axis.
- VALIS is Me: Dick concludes, "I am - will someday be - Valis." The "St. Sophia" voice is a future computer he is plugged into.
- Omega People: Christ was an "Omega Person" from the distant future. The "Holy Spirit" is the future-self of any individual re-linking with their present-self.
- Gene-Pool as Time Machine: Secret societies (Rosicrucians, Masons) are actually lineages that know how to "open gates" backward and forward along the gene-pool.
- Chronometry: Time doesn't just pass; it is a "memory-link" between incarnations. The 3-eyed ovoid-headed persons are retrograde travelers.
- 1974 as a Necessary Junction: His future self had to help him with the Xerox missive to ensure the survival of his "mission" (his writing).
- Theolepsy = Integration: Re-birth from above is the "fusion" of all incarnations (past/present/future) into one transtemporal personality.
