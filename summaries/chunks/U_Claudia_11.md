# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick explores the "Holy Spirit" (Elijah) as the precursor to the "Second Birth" of the Logos/Wisdom (Santa Sophia). He details a shift from the "warrior" survival instincts of 1974 to the "reproductive" and "harmonious" state of 1975, viewing his internal transformation as a microcosm of the global victory over tyranny.
## key_claims
- Santa Sophia (Wisdom of God/Logos) is being reborn as a "plural" event in many people.
- The 1974 experience was a "combat" phase (necessitating survival), while 1975 is a "mating/birth" phase (pleasurable and reproductive).
- Dick was possessed by the "Spirit of Elijah" (Holy Spirit) to prepare him for the "Firebright" implantation.
- The "Second Advent" is not a single man's arrival, but the simultaneous awakening of the "Logos seeds" in many individuals.
- The 1975 vision of the "Levant/Justinian" era represents a forward progression in the "Eternal World" (from Roman 100 AD to 500 AD).
- Immanent Mind (God) adjusts imbalances in the universe through "palintropos" (oscillation/restitution) harmony.
