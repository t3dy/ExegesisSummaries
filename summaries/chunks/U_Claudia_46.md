# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick develops "Sophianism," identifying the Holy Spirit/Wisdom (St. Sophia/Ma'at) as a feminine co-ruler of the universe and the "Rightful King" (Christ/Dionysos) as the returning judge. He connects the "Scout" dream to the first begotten son and proposes that 3-74 involved the reception of the Spirit through the "Third Eye" (pineal gland).
## key_claims
- Sophianism: The universe is becoming feminine; St. Sophia (Wisdom/Ma'at) is the 4th member of the Quaternity and co-rules with God.
- Thoth-Ma'at Connection: Thoth (Egyptian God of Wisdom/Scribe) is a precursor to Sophia; "revealed writing" (Hermetic) is a divine mode.
- The Rightful King: The "Good News" is the Second Advent; the King who was "rejected" (Christ/Dionysos) is returning in power.
- Third Eye Reception: The "lost knowledge" of the early Christians was how to receive the Spirit through the Pineal Gland/Third Eye.
- Judgment by Witness: St. Sophia/Ma'at as "Ma'at-Sibyl" witnesses evil deeds; to witness is to deal out justice (Nixon's fall).
- The Living Universe: The universe is not a machine but a living, feminine entity that "holds us in her arms."
