# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-16

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-16
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 044 to 048
- **Themes:** Simon Magus, Zebra Identity, Trashy Gnosis, 2-Part Siddhartha Book.

## Key Claims
- **Zebra = Savior Messenger:** Zebra is not the Holy Spirit (internal) but the supreme deity and savior-messenger (Ubik/Runciter).
- **Simon Magus Identity:** PKD identifies the entity as Simon Magus in his "true form" (the great plasmate).
- **Homoplasmate Syzygy:** PKD is a homoplasmate: Zebra acting in syzygy with a human. His writing restores Gnosis to the world in a "trashy" form.
- **Siddhartha Dream:** A dream about a two-part book ("Siddhartha slept" vs. "Siddhartha awakes") signifies the transition from the evil age to the good age (5th Savior).
- **King Felix Cypher:** The code in *Tears* signaled Zebra to trigger anamnesis in PKD (Simon) and reunite them as planned.

## Entities
- **Simon Magus:** The 1st-century Gnostic/Savior figure.
- **Zebra:** The macro-plasmate/messenger from the Pleroma.
- **St. Sophia:** Holy Wisdom, overruling the mad creator Yaltabaoth.
- **Siddhartha/Buddha:** The 4th/5th savior archetype.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Anamnesis:** Triggered by the "Golden Fish" or "King Felix" signs.
- **Syzygy:** The partnership of human host and divine plasmate.
- **Trash-Stratum:** The humble/low-quality medium through which Gnosis is transmitted.

## Theological Motifs
- **Salvific Gnosis:** Knowledge of celestial origin that breaks "astral determinism."
- **The Pleroma:** The true home beyond the prison world.
- **Postmortem Experience:** 3-74 was a literal death and resurrection into an immortal state.
