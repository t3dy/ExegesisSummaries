# Chunk 191 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_191

## Description
PKD formalizes his "Distributed Brain" theory, viewing himself as an Old Testament-style prophet revealing God’s plan. He identifies the current state of history as an invasion of the sentient Realm I (Noös/Brain) into the deterministic Realm II (Magnet/BIP), seeking to incorporate and free humanity piece by piece.

## Key Claims
- **Prophet Role**: PKD sees his role as that of an O.T. prophet who reveals God’s plan to an evil king (Nixon/BIP), even if rarely listened to.
- **The "Floating Crap Game" Brain**: The Brain is a superior, discorporate life form that does not organize the same "cells" (humans) constantly. It perpetually incorporates and releases individuals as needed for historical interventions.
- **Ubik as True**: PKD asserts that his novel *Ubik* correctly described the brain’s "latent messages" which coordinate humanity subliminally.
- **The Magnet vs. The Brain**: The adversary (Magnet/BIP) is a static, rigid "magnet" that warps thoughts into dead circles, while the Brain is the negentropic force of growth and freedom.

## Entities
- **Zebra**: Direct electric impulses between parts of the distributed brain.
- **The Paraclete**: The Holy Spirit/Logos/Brain, identified by Christ in John as "more important than I am."
- **Thomas**: An incorporated apostolic identity that provides the Brain with historical memory.
- **The Magnet**: The criminal invader/sickness that stopped "real time" at A.D. 70.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Distributed Brain**: A physical system organizing people, objects, and processes into an interface for Noös.
- **Transtemporal Constants**: Both the Magnet and the Brain are constants existing outside linear time.
- **Memesis / Mimicry**: The Brain's use of camouflage to influence history without detection.
- **Die Messages**: Negative, enslaving signals sent by the Magnet/BIP to declare man sinful.

## Theological Motifs
- **Grace as In-breaking**: God reaches down to man (Grace) rather than man reaching up (Religion).
- **Pentecost**: The initial manifestation of the Brain as recorded in the Book of Acts.
- **Sentience vs. Determinism**: The choice between being guided by a purposeful mind or by blind cause-and-effect.
- **Salvation as Incorporation**: To be saved is to be "taken up" into the corporate body of the sentient brain.
