# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-83

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-83
- **Themes:** Direct Domain Contact, The Master Map, Miracle through Death, Resurrected Awarenees.

## Key Claims
- **Direct Domain Contact:** In 3-74, PKD bypassed substantial reality and touched the "Domain of Limit/Structure" (the Map) directly.
- **The Pliant Map:** Reality reflects a "master map" reflexively. Change the map (the domain of limit), and substantial reality "analogically" alters.
- **Vortex of Intent:** A "vortex" of divine will shaped PKD's space, time, and thoughts without "substantial interface."
- **Miracle of Return:** Being "born again" requires having literally died. Christ "surrogated" himself for PKD, dying in his place so PKD could return with "stereo vision" of both worlds.
- **Resurrection as Transmutation:** The "lowest" (trash/degraded) is made over into the "highest" in an instant—a process of "joining" pieces into a structure.

## Entities
- **VALIS / Ubik:** The living, intentional "Map" or blueprint of the cosmos.
- **The "Stranger":** God in disguise, entering the world to "deprive the ancient powers of their victim."
- **Thomas / Christ:** The surrogate self that "took the death wound."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Final Cause (Aristotle):** Awareness of "intentionality" within the laws of nature.
- **Acausal Parallel Synchronization:** The method by which the "real domain" influences the "substantial domain."
- **Entropy vs. Blueprint:** Ubik selects bits from a decaying universe to incorporate into its permanent non-physical structure.

## Theological Motifs
- **Pre-fallen State:** Restoration to the state of "hearing God's voice" through the miracle of death and return.
- **Span of Worlds:** The "born again" person exists in both the substantial realm and the "void/upper domain."
- **Divine Workshop:** The artisan (God) is concealed within the process of the world's transmutation.
