# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-121

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-121
- **Themes:** Failure of the 1st Advent, Reassembling the King, Pretextual Coincidence, Instruction Points.

## Key Claims
- **The Gospel as Incomplete:** The 1st Century account ends in failure/death; the "full story" is the gradual reassembly of the King (Ho Kyrios) over 2000 years through the Reformation, Enlightenment, and 1960s.
- **Predestination vs. Autochthons:** Salvation is not by faith or deeds but by being a genetic carrier of the "DNA packet." Valis (the narrow door) locates these carriers.
- **Pretextual Synchronization:** Valis orchestrated the sequence (Tears publication, pentathol, pharmacy girl's fish sign) to trigger anamnesis just in time for the Xerox missive. It uses "pretextual cause" (the fish) to mask the internal firing of info.
- **The Instruction Point:** Like written instructions appearing on a game hen's wrapper only when it's ready to be used, the 3-74 info was released only when the "goal point" of use was reached.
- **Partisans Behind Enemy Lines:** PKD is a partisan (agent) in the BIP, receiving "explosives" (truth) and "money" (intelligence) from "outside" (the skies/Valis) to blow up fortifications.

## Entities
- **Valis / Abba:** The strategist/sender.
- **Ho Kyrios:** The fragmented/reassembling King.
- **The Plasmate:** Back-and-forth message traffic for the distributed brain (DNA carriers).
- **Torah Feldschuh:** archetype of the Soviet/partisan resistance.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Koinos Kosmos:** The communal hypnotic reality.
- **Instruction Sheets:** Written pages seen in 3-74 were technical manuals for the disinhibited psyche.
- **Doublethink:** The ability to hold two exclusive versions of reality (Pigspurt vs. Xtian) simultaneously.

## Theological Motifs
- **Pentecost:** The arrival of the "Distributed Brain."
- **Pleroma:** The "Home Planet" or origin system.
- **Transubstantiation:** Matter turning into "Instructions."
