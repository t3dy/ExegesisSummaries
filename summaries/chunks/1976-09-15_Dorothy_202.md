# Chunk 202 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_202

## Description
PKD leans toward the "Model B" simulation theory, concluding that the world of Acts (Rome CAD 45) is not "real" in an absolute sense, but is the subjective reality of the personality Thomas. He characterizes the divine entity as a "Holy Phagocyte" (Zebra) in hot pursuit of a pathogen (the BIP/Magnet).

## Key Claims
- **Subjectivity of Acts**: Acts exists only for Thomas (+ those like him). Once Thomas "fades out," the world of Acts fades, suggesting it is a subjective projection rather than an objective Ur-reality.
- **Present Tense Presence**: PKD emphasizes that the voice said Thomas is "not *living* [present tense] in this century," proving that Thomas exists *now* in a parallel simulation, not in the chronological past.
- **World as Mask**: The "phasing in and out" of worlds in *Flow My Tears* shows that no world is more real than another—they are all masks for the underlying Zebra/Info-traffic.
- **Holy Phagocyte**: The irruption of Acts/Thomas into the 20th century is a "rescue mission" where the divine entity (Zebra) devours and engulfs the "magnet-dominated" space-time of the BIP.

## Entities
- **Thomas**: The "Master Self" who is locked into the Acts world and currently co-habits PKD's head.
- **The Holy Phagocyte (Zebra)**: The "devouring" sentient entity that seeks to liberate humanity from the pathogen of the BIP.
- **The Magnet / Pathogen**: The rigid, entropic power that dominates our space-time.
- **Abalard**: Mentioned for a "middle position" regarding the reality of universals/worlds.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Form Reversion**: The process by which a simulated world reverts to its "Ur-world" (Acts) as a way of unveiling truth.
- **Model B (Direct Brain-Stimulation)**: The theory that one brain can experience two worlds simultaneously via selective site-stimulation.
- **Syntonic Blend**: The "harmonious" fusion of Rome and California, reinforcing the idea of a plastic, multidimensional matrix.
- **Double Impression**: The simultaneous perception of the "American" and the "Roman" facets of individuals.

## Theological Motifs
- **Abandoning Linear Time**: The rejection of the "past-present-future" model in favor of a "now-living" parallel world theory.
- **The Savior as Invader**: Christ/Zebra as the adventitious mind that breaks into the "closed loop" of our simulation to rescue us.
- **Idios Kosmos as Simulation**: Each self is locked into its own subjective world, with Zebra acting as the cross-connecting master-system.
- **The First Advent as Irruption**: The arrival of Christ as the first time the "upper eternal realm" uniqueley broke into the temporal order.
