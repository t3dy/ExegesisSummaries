# The Irony of Gnosis: Confirming the Kingdom
## concise_summary
Dick identifies a "Zen" irony in his Gnosis: the secret revelation is that standard Orthodox Christian claims (the Paraclete's arrival and the Kingdom's presence) are literally true. He views himself as a witness to the Parousia occurring "now" as a hidden, mustard-seed-like invasion.
## key_claims
- Paradoxical Gnosis: The "esoteric saving gnosis" confirms that "pistis" (faith/orthodoxy) is actually correct. The Paraclete clears up all mysteries.
- Jesus as Zeus-Zagreus: A vision showed that "Jesus" is a cipher for Zeus-Zagreus, linking Christianity to the Orphic/Dionysian mystery religions.
- Sane Dionysos: In a sick cosmos (chaos), Dionysos (the "mad" god) is the only one who is actually sane.
- 3 Temporal Layers of Reality: (1) Mass-hallucinated good world, (2) Black Iron Prison (Hell), (3) Kingdom of God (Palm Tree Garden).
- The Pulling Apart (Eschatology): The two cities (God vs. World) are "pulling apart," marking the end times.
- Orthogonal Parousia: The Second Coming has already happened, but only those with "anamnesis" can see it. It is "very small" (mustard seed).
- Anamnesis as Kingdom: To remember our divine origin is to see the Kingdom. 3-74 was the "End Days" occurring within his own lifetime.
- Transubstantiation of the Cosmos: The entire world has become the "Temple" (the real presence of God).
