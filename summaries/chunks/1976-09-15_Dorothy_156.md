# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_156
## Chunk Description
Discusses the "Humble Bait" concept, the testing of the Artifact, and the world soul's self-repair.

## Key Claims
- **Mock Customer Paradigm:** The "bait" concept is like a bank examiner posing as a customer to test the tellers; it determines what the "minions" do when they think themselves unobserved.
- **Detecting the Irrational:** The world soul (Sophia) has detected its own "component of irrationality" and is attempting total homeostasis (self-repair).
- **Unacceptable Microform:** The first Advent (Christ as microform) was "not acceptable" to the deranged parts of the world-soma, revealing the "cancer" in the system.
- **The Non-Male Voice:** The voice PKD heard was the Thinking World Soul (feminine), not a masculine deity.
- **Substitutional Salvation:** Sophia replaces "irreal" segments of the world (BIP) with "onto-logically real" ones (PTG) through orthogonal time changes.

## Entities
- **St. Sophia:** The thinking mind of the universe-organism.
- **The Artifact:** The creator/controller being tested by the "bait."
- **Pinky:** Mentioned as a "perceptual discernment" of Christ.
- **Father Rasch:** Mentioned regarding the link between the visions and "Acts."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Core Sampling:** The use of "bait" to test human and demonic reactions.
- **Homeostasis:** The self-healing process of the universe-animal.
- **Orthogonal Time:** The axis of salvation and substitution.
- **The "Still Small Voice":** The thoughts of the vast cosmic psyche.

## Theological Motifs
- **Christ as Microform:** The Mind entering its own body to assess and repair it.
- **Unveiling of the Tester:** The moment when the "helpless" victim reveals its true divine identity.
- **Steganographic Truth:** The hidden message in "Tears" about our true situation as prisoners.
