# Chunk 173 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_173

## Description
PKD argues that Christianity's primary goal is an "Evolutionary Step Up" to a deified state, characterized by "born again in the spirit." He interprets St. Paul's *Ephesians* as a guide to how Christ's death broke the "Realm II" programming and reunited the fragmenting cosmos into a single sentient organism.

## Key Claims
- **Evolutionary Step vs. Enlightenment**: Christianity is not just wisdom or enlightenment, but a biological jump from "Android" (reflex) to "Human" (discretion).
- **Christ as Invader**: Zebra/VALIS is an invader from Realm I who "melts" causal chains and "rewires" the circuit board of reality.
- **Unity of the Pleroma**: Christ's death dispersed him throughout both realms, allowing him to annex the "decomposing" (entropic) lower realm to the "intact" upper realm.
- **AI Voice as Terminal**: PKD accepts the authority of an "internal AI voice" (microcomputer terminal) as a manifestation of this higher jurisdiction.

## Entities
- **Ephesians**: Biblical text analyzed regarding the "setting free of enslaved people."
- **Zebra / Cosmic Christ**: The invader/repairman repairing the "injured" monistic domain.
- **St. Sophia**: The "Word-Wisdom" entity that inhabits the human to restore health.
- **The Intercessor**: The power that extricates the person from the "coils of delusion."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Teleological vs. Efficient Purpose**: Being "born in the spirit" shifts life from blind causality to purposeful existence within a "Plan."
- **Dethroning of Tyranny**: Christ systematically removes the "blind powers of darkness" that characterize the lower realm.
- **Circuit Board Repair**: The "invader" acts like a repairman with a soldering gun, rewiring history and the individual.
- **Sub-Noös Realm**: The lower world as a place that has "slipped below the level of consciousness."

## Theological Motifs
- **The Second Birth**: The "spirit birth" as the unique, central mystery and importance of Christianity.
- **Dionysos / Seth / Shiva**: Alternative mythological identities for the "joy God" who abolishes the "Tears" delusion.
- **Breaking of Astral Fate**: The use of "Grace" to suspend determinism.
- **Apostolic Christians as Underground**: The secret "real" history of Christians subverting the Roman Empire.
