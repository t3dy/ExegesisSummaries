# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his 1974 experience as the "Second Advent," occurring pluralistically within individual minds rather than in a single visible man. He analyzes the "Parousia" as the abolition of time and equates the fall of Nixon with the casting down of the "Prince of this World."
## key_claims
- The Second Advent (1974) happened internally to many "seeds" (persons) simultaneously.
- Christ cannot appear as one man again (he'd be killed); instead, there are "implantations" of Christ-consciousness.
- The 1974 experience involved the "dead being raised incorruptible" (an Essene personality awakening in Dick).
- The "Prince of this World" (Evil One/Nixon) was caster down into the dust in 1974.
- Anamnesis is a re-enactment of Christ's victory over the "Romans" (tyranny).
- "Santa Sophia" and "Firebright" are the same revitalizing principle.
