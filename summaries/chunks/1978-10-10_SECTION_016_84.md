# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-84

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-84
- **Themes:** Subsumption of Wills, The Bardo Wake-up, Rerun Advantage, Twin Sister as Tutelary.

## Key Claims
- **Subsumption of Intention:** The "Ultra-Macro-organism" (God) incorporates individuals' wills into its own central purpose without them noticing.
- **Life is Death:** Our "life" is actually the Bardo Thodol half-life state of *Ubik*; the "next world" is life.
- **Waking by Remembering:** Recognizing "world" as one's own past memories induces anamnesis and the reversal of "the Fall."
- **The Rerun Strategy:** PKD's brain (as "Thomas") used "future memories" (the solution) to solve the 3-74 crisis that he had already lived and failed once before.
- **The "Thomas" identity:** Possibly his dead twin sister, Jane, acting as a "tutelary spirit" and "hindsight" provider.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "second run" self with hindsight.
- **Jane (Twin Sister):** The possible true identity of "Thomas."
- **The Iron Complex:** The deterministic, disorganized plural "raw material" of the universe.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Phosphene Library:** The transferred information used to "out-think" the police (Pigspurt).
- **Simulated World (Joint/Eye):** The 27-year obsession with irreal/subjective worlds is now validated.
- **Fossil Forces:** Past memories fed to the "dying soul" as environment.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Second Adam:** Being "born again" as a restoration of faculties lost in the Fall.
- **Siddhartha's Wake-up:** The sudden realization that the "external" is the "internal."
- **Karmic Breakout:** Using the "future info" to edit and successfully exit a repeating time-loop/life.
