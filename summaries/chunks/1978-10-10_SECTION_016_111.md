# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-111

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-111
- **Themes:** Orthogonal Time, Teleological Tugging, Brain as Nature, The Game of Hands.

## Key Claims
- **Orthogonal Change:** God exists in a universe larger than the "actual" (including the "potential"). He can change what seems like the past for us by "re-running the tape" with new variables.
- **The Teleological Warp:** Valis is a network of purposeful "warps" or "valences" that tug reality away from the mechanical "plumb" of efficient cause.
- **Nature is Brain:** God's body is not just "stuff" but specifically a "brain structure." Thoughts in nature are neurons/cells linking and severing.
- **The Game of Generations:** Creation is a game (like Go or Chess) where each "hand" (generation) is predicated on the prior ones. God "plunders" the antecedent total to set up the next win.
- **Elijah/Elisha Enthuasia:** PKD's experience with Thomas is compared to the transfer of the "spirit of Elijah" to Elisha.

## Entities
- **Valis:** The "Unmoved Mover" / Teleological trigger.
- **Thomas:** The "spirit of Elijah" / Jim Pike persona.
- **The Dark Counterplayer:** The entity with only hindsight/mechanical cause.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Efficient vs. Final Cause:** The "plumb" line of physics vs. the "tug" of divine intention.
- **Synchronicity Axes:** Reality as a right-hemisphere "structure" read along its horizontal connections rather than its temporal sequence.
- **0-1 Oscillation:** The binary pulse of the "Macro-brain" thinking.

## Theological Motifs
- **Divine Strategist:** God as a long-range player winning by persuasion and "laying down sequences."
- **Transubstantiation:** The literal turning of "matter" into "thought/message."
- **Pretextual Cause:** A "fictional history" laid down to make a new divine introjection look like natural law.
