# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick synthesizes the "Magdeburg hemispheres" of his 1974 experience: the external transformation of Fullerton into Rome and the internal transformation of himself into an early Christian Essene. He identifies the "Solar Spermatikos" as the triggering force for this "Firebright" second birth.
## key_claims
- Reality is a single "Form One" reflected (as "Form Two") in our perception; we must reverse the signals to see the truth.
- Fullerton becoming Rome was a reflection of the change *within* Dick: he became a member of a secret "Fish-sign" society hostile to Rome.
- The 1974 experience was a "second birth" triggered by solar seeds (Solar Spermatikos).
- Waking up (Anamnesis) is the retrieval of what has lain dormant for 2,000 to 10,000 years.
- *The Three Stigmata of Palmer Eldritch* contains clues to God if "read properly" (reversed).
