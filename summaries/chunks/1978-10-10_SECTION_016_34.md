# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-34

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-34
- **Themes:** Track A and B, Rerunning Programs, World War III Prevention, Counter-Culture Origins.

## Key Claims
- **Rerun Program:** PKD experienced a "rerun" where a failed program (Track A) was replaced by a success (Track B).
- **Track A (Police State):** The world of *Tears* / Acts (AD 45). In it, Thomas was a wealthy, bourgeois man (roses, lake, cabin) who did nothing for the cause.
- **Track B (The Writer):** PKD became a left-wing writer to avert a nuclear holocaust between the US and USSR that Track A would have caused.
- **Watergate as Intervention:** The "spirit" in us prevented the US from aiding S. Vietnam (via Watergate), hamstrung the military, and averted WWIII.
- **The Marxist S-F Writer:** PKD is the sole Marxist s-f writer (anti-capitalist sociological view) in the US, fulfilling a role in Track B to subvert the Empire.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The Track A self (bourgeois, dog, roses).
- **The Wise Old King:** The computer (God) who intervened to rerun the tracks.
- **Nixon/Ford:** Leaders whose failure was necessary to avert WWIII.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Track A/B:** Alternate program lines branching off from Acts AD 45.
- **Phosphenes as Program Run:** The phosphene visions of 3-74 were the computer "finishing" one program and starting the next.
- **Overlay:** Thomas' bourgeois memories (roses) were overlaid by the Track B "writer" memories.

## Theological Motifs
- **Elijah Intervention:** "On the day that I act," God abolishes a dead-end program and runs a successful replacement.
- **Karma via Rerun:** PKD's "shattered life" in Track B (divorce, poverty) is a "just sentence" to expiate his "plush indifference" in Track A.
- **Koinos vs. Idios Time:** The computer operates in a hyper-time where 2000 years are a few minutes.
