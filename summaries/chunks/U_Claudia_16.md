# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick analyzes the "Minkowski block universe" where all causes are present simultaneously. He identifies "Empire" (Imperial Rome) as the ontological identity behind the US (Nixon) and USSR (Soviet), concluding that "to see the game is to be released from it."
## key_claims
- Reality is a "pushrod system" or "engine" where all causes are present in the *Augenblick* (moment of vision).
- Time is merely a "lag" or "friction" (heat-loss) to be overcome, not a fundamental reality.
- The 1974 crisis (Xerox/COINTELPRO) "exposed" evil because it had too much "bulk" to remain camouflaged.
- "Imperial Rome" is the archetype for both US imperialism and Soviet tyranny (Twin branches of one tree).
- *Flow My Tears* is a "true picture" of the essence of the US as a police state (Soviet/Roman paradigm).
- "Possession" is actually "waking up" from the two-person game (Zoroastrian) into which everyone is locked.
