# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick explores the "erotic" nature of divine possession, equating the Holy Spirit with the "Yang" that impregnates the human "Yin." He redefines the Trinity as a biological reproduction process and identifies himself as the "mother" of an immortal "Light Body" (Firebright).
## key_claims
- Divine possession is a "mating" between God the Father (Yang) and Man (Yin/Mother).
- The "Second Birth" produces an immortal transpersonal offspring (Christ-consciousness/Firebright).
- The "Gifts of the Spirit" (healing, prophecy) are by-products of this biological-spiritual union.
- Christ is a "half-breed" (Half God, half man) who allows the human part to survive physical death.
- The 1974 "Rome-within-Fullerton" experience was a stripping away of temporal layers to reveal the "restored" original state.
