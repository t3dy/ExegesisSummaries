# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 235

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 235
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 22 - 069 to 077
- **Themes:** Retrograde Info-Flow, The Retromind (Postmortem Self), VALIS/Satellite, The 5th Savior, The New Phenotype.

## Key Claims
- **Retrograde Info-Flow:** PKD concludes he began "seeing the universe backward," tapping into an info-flow from the future. This explains the "precognition" in his writing (e.g., *Faith of Our Fathers*).
- **The Retromind:** The "AI voice" is actually PKD's post-mortem, retro-time self (Thomas/the 3-eyed phenotype) informing the present conscious mind.
- **VALIS/Satellite:** Reference to a satellite (VALIS) that fires information down to humans, controlling and overriding them.
- **The 5th Savior:** Horse Lover Fat (PKD's alter ego) sets out in search of the "5th Savior" (Zebra's Parousia), who will be King and Judge.
- **The Multiplex Phenotype:** A new race of men is arising with "orthogonal" or "multiplex" thinking, utilizing non-linguistic, anti-logical faculties to deal with reality.
- **Bipolar God:** God (Yahweh/Satana, Osiris/Seth) contains both good and terrible aspects. A mediator (St. Sophia/5th Savior) is needed to split the bipolarities.

## Entities
- **VALIS:** The information-firing satellite.
- **Horse Lover Fat:** The "modern-day Magi" searching for the Savior.
- **The 3-Eyed People:** The super-evolved future selves.
- **Siddhartha:** The divine one about to awaken.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Double Helix / DNA:** Subliminal designs of the "Gene-pool memory" appearing in the world (the film "VALIS").
- **Hindsight as Foresight:** The mechanism of the retromind.
- **The 2-Source Hologram:** The universe as a product of two info-processing sources (A and A).

## Theological Motifs
- **Searching for the Savior:** The "mild yoke" and "burden light" of searching for the Parousia.
- **Splitting the Bipolarities:** The need for Wisdom (Sophia) to emerge as a unitary thing from the binary struggle.
- **End of the Age of Iron:** The transition to the new era heralded by Zebra.
