# Transmutation and the Divine Marriage
## concise_summary
Dick discusses the Urgrund’s intervention to transmute the projected reality into a stable divine kingdom. He introduces the "divine marriage" between humanity and Christ/Logos as the final synthesis of the material and divine realms.
## key_claims
- Transmutation of Reality: The artifact is not being destroyed, but "converted" into divine substance.
- Divine Marriage: A mystical union where the human soul is "married" to the Logos, merging their natures.
- Omega Man Realization: The goal of history is the "Omega Man," the hybrid of human and divine that can inhabit the transuted world.
