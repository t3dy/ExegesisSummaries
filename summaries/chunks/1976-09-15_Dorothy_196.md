# Chunk 196 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_196

## Description
PKD identifies himself as a member of a "secret early Christian underground" and theorizes that *Flow My Tears* serves as the informational interface connecting USA 1974 with the absolute reality of Acts (Rome CAD 45). He explores the concept of the "Time Rollback," where the 1900-year ellipsis of "spurious time" is being stripped away.

## Key Claims
- **Esoteric Doctrine (1A)**: Christ is the psyche of a true, hidden church possessing supernatural powers. PKD is a "micro bit" of this Pantocrator.
- **"Tears" as Doorway**: *Flow My Tears* is a nexus between worlds. Reading it leads the reader across the "form-axis" from current tyranny (Nixon) to the absolute world of Acts.
- **The Defeat of the Paraclete**: Sometime in the 1st or 2nd century, the Paraclete was "defeated" by Rome, initiating a 1900-year period of "spurious world and time."
- **Acts = Brain = Information**: The Book of Acts is the "verbal analog" of the Brain; it is the Brain converted into information, capable of triggering anamnesis in the reader.

## Entities
- **Christos Pantocrator**: The ruler and judge who is not just "up there" but located "down here" for good.
- **The Apostolic Christians**: The initial "members" of the brain who were incorporated at Pentecost and are now re-emerging.
- **The BIP**: The latent evil in the world that was made manifest in the 1974 tyranny.
- **Luke**: The author of Acts, noted for not knowing about the "inner dream level" of Paul's conversion.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Form-Axis**: The non-linear dimension along which different historical periods (like Rome and USA) are immediately tangent to one another.
- **Onion Rings of Reality**: The layers of latency that must be stripped back to reveal the final "King-Felix" kernel of truth.
- **Time Rollback**: The process (depicted in *Ubik*) of reality reverting to its original, absolute form (Acts).
- **Delayed Action Micro-Systems**: Humans are seen as systems "rooted" in Acts, waiting for the informational trigger of anamnesis.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Irruption of the Supralunar**: The divine has broken into the sublunar world to "break the lower realm powers."
- **Apostasy**: The belief that the historical church was absorbed by Rome, leading to a long period of spiritual darkness.
- **The Secret Church**: An unbroken, covert chain of true Christians linked back to the Apostles.
- **Victory of the Paraclete**: The "Second Comforter" cannot be defeated; its 20th-century re-emergence proves its ultimate victory over "seeming" time.
