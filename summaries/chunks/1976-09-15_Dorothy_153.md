# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_153
## Chunk Description
Explores the "Magdeburg Hemispheres" of the brain, the "Timaeus" cosmology, and the persuasive nature of the Demiurge.

## Key Claims
- **Magdeburg Hemisphere Brain:** The brain's hemispheres are "yoked in tandem" to different space-time continua (A.D. 70 vs. 1974).
- **Plato’s Timaeus as Key:** The true cosmology is that of *Timaeus*: a living animal universe with a soul (Psyche) that has an "irrational element" (Ananke/Chance).
- **The Persuading Demiurge:** The Demiurge (Noös/Reason) cannot *compel* the universe but must *persuade* it into order.
- **St. Sophia as Mediatress:** Divine Wisdom (Sophia/Logos) is the penetration of Reason into the microcosm.
- **YHWH as the Irrational World Soul:** The God of the O.T. (YHWH) is the "irrational world soul"—capricious, erratic, and powerful—while Christ is the rational Noös sent to rectify YHWH's errors.

## Entities
- **St. Sophia/Hagia Sophia:** The feminine principle of divine wisdom.
- **Demiurge (Noös):** The divine artisan who organizes the pre-existing chaos.
- **YHWH:** The irrational, "ranting" God of the O.T.
- **Cornford:** Cited for his interpretation of *Timaeus*.
- **Anaxagoras:** Criticized by Plato for a "deistic" Noös.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Ananke:** Chance/Necessity, the irrational constant in the universe.
- **Persuasion vs. Force:** The divine M.O. is organizing the random (trash) into a "good" pattern.
- **Platonistic Christianity:** Rejecting Jewish elements (YHWH) in favor of the rational Greek Noös.
- **Magdeburg Hemispheres:** Metaphor for yoking diverse realities.

## Theological Motifs
- **Gnostic Theodicy:** The creator of this world (YHWH) is the "wrong God to worship."
- **The "Father":** Christ's "Abba" is the Demiurge/Artisan, not YHWH.
- **Stealth Seed:** The "mustard seed" of Noös is planted where YHWH cannot see it.
