# Albemuth and the Alien Hideout
## concise_summary
Dick integrates his ETI theory, suggesting that humans are "escaped prisoners" from another star system (Albemuth) who are hiding on Earth behind a "veil" (Maya). He posits that the Nixon gang and other "pursuers" were onto his secret nature, leading to the 3-74 confrontation.
## key_claims
- Albemuth Refugees: We (the authentic Christians) are escaped prisoners from the star system Albemuth, hiding on Earth from our "fallen" pursuers.
- The Veil as Protection: The "Dokos" (veil/Maya) was devised by the refugees to obscure themselves, but it "leaked" through Dick's writing.
- Pursuit and Assassination: Figures like the Kennedys and Dr. King were likely refugees or sympathizers hunted by the "Black Iron Prison" authorities.
- Re-activation: The "pharmacy girl" was sent to reawaken Dick's memory because *Flow My Tears* had exposed him to both sides.
- Mimicry/Brahman: Equates the "Brahman stuff" with a "mimicking living stuff" (Zebra) that assumes the form of phenomenal objects to hide and act.
- Steganography: The universe is alive with coded messages ("Golden threads") intended for the refugees (Zebra's parts).
- Toppling Rulers: The fall of Nixon, and regimes in Spain/Greece/Portugal, are signs of the refugees' successful counter-attack.
