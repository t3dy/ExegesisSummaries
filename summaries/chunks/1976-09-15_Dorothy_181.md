# Chunk 181 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_181

## Description
PKD develops the "Great Assize" interpretation of *Flow My Tears*, where Jason Taverner's trial becomes a cosmic model for Vicarious Atonement. He identifies Zebra as the "Cosmic Christ" who has annexed the decomposing Form II and rendered it irreal through his return.

## Key Claims
- **Vicarious Atonement as Legal Verdict**: The trial of Jason Taverner represents the "Great Assize" (Final Judgment), but instead of the "serious old king's" verdict (death), a supernatural verdict of "innocent" is delivered.
- **Trial as Salvation**: The "spotless son" (Taverner/Christ) stands in the place of judgment for humanity, resulting in the "justification" and "saving from hell/nemesis" of all.
- **Ubique (Omnipresent) Christ**: The Cosmic Christ (Zebra) does not possess a humanoid body but interpenetrates the entire universe. PKD believes both the external Cosmic Christ and an internal Paraclete (Holy Spirit) were present in 3-74.
- **Age of Innocence Restored**: The current historical shift from guilt to innocence is a restoration of the "primordial lost state."

## Entities
- **Jason Taverner**: The "innocent, spotless son" whose trial saves mankind.
- **The Serious White-Haired King**: A polyform of YHWH or the "Ancient of Days" whose harsh judgment is overruled by Love.
- **Zebra**: The Cosmic Christ / Pantocrator.
- **Peter**: Reference to a TV drama about a boy rescued by a "savior polyform."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Vicarious Atonement**: The idea that one entity's innocence secures another's acquittal.
- **Nemesis = Hell**: The deterministic "punishment too great" from which Christ saves us.
- **Paraclete**: The Holy Spirit that enables the perception of the Cosmic Christ.
- **Age of Guilt vs. Age of Innocence**: The transition from the Roman prison to the Palm Tree Garden.

## Theological Motifs
- **Free Gift of Grace**: Salvation is unconnected to merit—it is an infinite Love that fills in human deficiency.
- **Mercy Above Justice**: The victory of love over the "judicial verdict of death."
- **The Holy Mystery**: The transformation of absolute grief (Tears) into salvific love and joy.
- **Exculpation (Absolution)**: The "decree" that wipes away the curse of sin.
