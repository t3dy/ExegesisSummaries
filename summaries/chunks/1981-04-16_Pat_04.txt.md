# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_04
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_04
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Babylonian Determinism, Paul's Captivity Letters, Christ Consciousness, Second Advent
- **Key Entities**: Paul, Capra, Koestler

## Key Claims
- Dick roots mechanical causation in Babylonian astral determinism (astrology). These "planetary powers" are the very Thrones and Authorities that Paul claimed Christ came to defeat.
- The invasion of VALIS (the Cosmic Christ) is specifically an invasion of, and interference with, the mechanism of causality, transforming it into volitional decision-making.
- Christ operates in two camouflaged forms: as the governing entity interfering with the causal machinery, and as the "living information" (plasmate/blood) interwoven with human message traffic.
- At the Second Advent, the situation is reversed from the First: the ruling angels now *know* Christ is here and are trying to occlude humans from this knowledge.
- Dick fuses Pauline Christian mysticism with modern quantum mechanics (unified field theory, indeterminacy) and Taoism, claiming they all describe the exact same reality.

## Entities
- **Paul**: Highly central here; his Captivity Letters (Ephesians, Colossians) describing cosmic spiritual warfare are literalized by Dick to explain the 1974 event.
- **Fritjof Capra / Arthur Koestler**: Mentioned for equating post-Newtonian physics with Taoism, prompting Dick to further equate both with Pauline mysticism.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Secret Stolen**: In this context, the secret is knowledge that the Cosmic Christ has returned and is actively dismantling the oppressive causal mechanism.
- **Christ Consciousness**: A cognitive leap that allows human beings to discard the modem of causality and see the binary, choosing nature of reality for the first time in history.

## Theological Motifs
- **Cosmic Christ**: A discorporate, systemic presence replacing strict karma/cause-and-effect with grace/decision-making.
- **The Dethroning of the Archons**: Christ actively unseating the angelic powers that enforce rigid determinism.
