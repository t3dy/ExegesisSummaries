# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-160

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-160
- **Themes:** Interspecies Symbiosis, The Winnowing of the Sons of Light, Retrograde Influence, Individual Parousia.

## Key Claims
- **Interspecies Symbiosis:** The "Sons of Light" are the progeny of "fallen angels" (extraterrestrials/higher beings) and humans. They are "blended" into the race and must be "winnowed" back out.
- **The Observation Period is Over:** God has been observing humanity to "reverse the symbiosis." The selection process ("winnowing") is complete; the "books are closed."
- **Adopted and Decoupled:** PKD was "adopted" and "taken out of the system" in 3-74. He is no longer subject to the "laws of the prison."
- **Retrograde Influence:** The helping help sent in '77 or '78 *retroactively* changed the '74 result. Valis moves "backward/retrograde" in time to remaster events.
- **Private Parousia:** The 3-74 experience was a "private" Apocalypse and Kingdom. The intervention "triggered" PK's future stages to "fire prematurely."

## Entities
- **Sons of Light:** The genetic/divine remnant (interspecies offspring).
- **Thomas / Elias:** The identity of the "Evolved Human" who was "host" for the intervention.
- **Asher / Belial:** The archetypes of "Sleeping/Non-being" vs. "Waking/Being."

## Recurring Concepts
- **The "Rasp" from Outside:** Reality touches the "Dreamer" in a way that "cannot be worked into the dream." This "dysyntonic" contact causes the second birth.
- **Winnowing:** Separating the "wheat" (Sons of Light) from the "tares" (humans).
- **Synthowomb:** This world as a place where the soul is "before true birth."

## Theological Motifs
- **The Adopting God:** A man (PKD) is chosen and "becomes God" (reabsorbed into the Godhead).
- **The Remnant:** The small minority hidden underground who display signs of "massive giving."
- **Legend of the Pearl:** The soul as a prince who has forgotten his origin and must be awakened by a "letter" (Xerox missive).
