# Chunk 167 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_167

## Description
PKD focuses on the "Esse" (Being) over "Doing" in the fulfillment of a divine plan. He posits that a person doesn't just *perform* a task for God, but is *incarnated as* the task itself—a lived embodiment of structure and purpose.

## Key Claims
- **The Person as the Note**: A man utilized by God is "the animated embodiment of structure and purpose"; his "self-knowledge" is the understanding of the plan.
- **The Last Unveiling**: The final secret of the "Psychopomp" or guide is the recognition that "It is you + it was you back at the start." The self informs the self.
- **The Three-Source Reality**: Reality is an amalgam of **Chance** (Ananke), **Determinism** (Cause/Effect), and **Sentient Overrule** (Noös).
- ***Flow My Tears* Revelation**: The novel reveals the "bedrock of reality" beneath spurious layers: that we are living in A.D. 70 "apostolic times."

## Entities
- **The Pomp / Guide**: The blurred figure at the end of the "cave of quietness" who is revealed to be the self/God.
- **Christ / Holy Spirit / St. Sophia**: The mediating entities that lead the "incarnated task" (the person).
- **Thomas**: The "ur-person" or apostolic Christian identity of PKD.
- **Buckman**: The character in *Tears* who breaks the "rigid iron framework" through grief and love.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bait vs. Opportunity**: Bait identifies evil (caught regardless of ignorance); Opportunities identify goodness (missed through ignorance but repeatable).
- **The Cowboy Tune (1933-1974)**: An example of a "vast tapestry" plan where a stimulus from childhood (1933) is recycled in middle age (1974) to fulfill a goal.
- **Apostolic Times**: The claim that 1974 is actually A.D. 70 Rome, specifically the time of *Acts of the Apostles*.

## Theological Motifs
- **Incarnation of Task**: The "Word come to life" (Logos) applied to the individual's role in history.
- **Divine Mimicry**: God disguises himself through "mimicry" (mimesis) to appraisal and reward individuals.
- **Agape as the Solution**: The solution to the "Roman Prison" reality is revealed in *Tears* as *agape* (sacrificial love).
