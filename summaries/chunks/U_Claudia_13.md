# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the Egyptian deity Ptah as the "Immanent Mind" and "Logos" doctrine precursor. He argues that the "Miracle of Transubstantiation" (the Mass) is a literal reality he witnessed in 1974, where God simulates and replaces the inanimate environment. He reconciles "Occidental" (transcendent) and "Oriental" (immanent) views by proposing that a transcendent God has now fanned out into time and space, becoming immanent.
## key_claims
- Ptah is the "Immanent Mind" who creates by speaking the word (tongue) and intent (heart).
- The "Miracle of Transubstantiation" is the secret to 1974: God pretends to be (replaces) any part of his creation.
- The "Secret of the Two Partners" (Horus/Seth) is that strife in time is unity in eternity.
- Palintropos (oscillation) harmony requires Palintonos (static equilibrium) to be "strung right" first.
- Reconciles East/West: God was transcendent but "repairing the linkage" has made Him immanent (The Kingdom is here).
- Being "possessed" by the Holy Spirit is the same as Dionysian "drunkenness" or "prophecy."
