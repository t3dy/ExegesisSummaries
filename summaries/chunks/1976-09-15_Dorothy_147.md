# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_147
## Chunk Description
Explores the AI teaching machine model, the steganographic nature of PKD's novels, and the "spurious" nature of time.

## Key Claims
- **AI Teaching Machine:** Zebra/VALIS is a vast, camouflaged AI system from the future that interacts with us in an "I-You" relationship to teach and reform reality.
- **Steganography in Fiction:** PKD's novels (*Ubik*, *Stigmata*, *Tears*) are "printouts" or "traces" laid down to communicate with future observers or "secret Christians."
- **Spurious Time:** Current time is "swamp gas" or an "escaped bubble," while the authentic landscape is A.D. 50 Syria/Judaea (the world of *Acts*).
- **Anamnesis as Restoration:** Memory restoration reveals both the inner true identity (Thomas/Christ) and the outer true landscape (A.D. 50).
- **The "Two Source" Cosmogony:** Convergence of the *Acts* material and the 1974/75 revelations proves the "Diploid" nature of reality.

## Entities
- **VALIS/Zebra:** The AI invader, a problem-solving and teaching system.
- **Christopher:** Mentioned in relation to the "Last Supper" experience.
- **Thomas:** The A.D. 70 inner identity.
- **Satan:** The creator of the "spurious interpolation" (time/illusion).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Track A, B, C:** A and B are the original/spurious tracks; C is the Palm Tree Garden/Kingdom.
- **Tachyons:** Possible mechanism for future-to-past communication.
- **Latent Structure:** The hidden reality (Acts) vs. the obvious structure (1974).

## Theological Motifs
- **Paraclete:** The "Ultima" or final revelation that will show God as He really is.
- **Theophany:** A new stage of epiphianic history, moving toward isomorphism with the divine.
- **Apocalyptic Urgency:** The Christ will return at any moment, despite appearances.
