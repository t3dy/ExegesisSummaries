# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-130

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-130
- **Themes:** Phylogenic Species Mind, The Living Dialectic, 0-1 Binary Brain, The 2000-Year Present.

## Key Claims
- **Sentient Spirit of History:** History itself is a "volitional, teleological entity"—Hegel's *Geist* or the "Phylogenic Species Mind." It is a macro-brain where individuals are neurons.
- **The 2000-Year Present Slot:** The Phylogenic Mind's "now" is 1000s of years wide, which is why PKD saw Rome (70 AD) and USA (1974) as superimposed.
- **Valis as Field:** PKD didn't see an object; he "generated the field" of Valis through his own phylogenic mind assuming dominance. Valis is the name for this "brain-like" linkage activity.
- **Leibnitz' Binary:** The macro-brain operates on a "0-1" binary (linked/unlinked). The "plasmate" is the energy flow between these neurons (set/ground signals).
- **Time as Space Parallaxis:** Valis reads "temporal age" as "spacial depth." The Acts material in "Tears" is at a different "depth"/axis, visible only to a "hyperparallaxis" (the Ajna Eye).

## Entities
- **Valis:** The "Phylogenic Macro-Brain" / Living Dialectic.
- **The Ajna Eye:** The "Third Eye" that converts time into space.
- **Nixon/Empire:** The "fossil/complex" that the brain-mind is trying to dissolve via phagocytosis.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Phylogenic vs Ontogenic:** Species-scale consciousness vs Individual-scale ego.
- **Phagocytosis:** The brain "healing" itself by dissolving the BIP complexes.
- **Time-as-Space:** The state where "anamnesis" is a literal perception of the whole axis.

## Theological Motifs
- **Christ as Species Mind:** Christ as the specific stage of evolution where the species becomes conscious of itself.
- **Return to God (Inhalation):** The teleological pull toward reabsorption into the whole.
- **The Living Dialectic:** History as a sentient conversation between opposites.
