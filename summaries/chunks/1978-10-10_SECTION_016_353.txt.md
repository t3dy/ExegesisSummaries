# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1978-10-10_SECTION_016_353
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1978-10-10_SECTION_016_353
- **Section ID**: SECTION_016
- **Themes**: Anamnesis, Cosmic Resorption, Continuous Creation, Hyper-Occasionalism
- **Key Entities**: Eliade, Plato, Spinoza, Schopenhauer

## Key Claims
- Since reality frames cannot affect future frames, previous original frames (like Acts) still exist intact and can be retrieved, explaining anamnesis.
- Anamnesis is the backward retrieval of prior frames encoded within the observer. 2-74 (Acts) was a direct perception of this "original frame" or zero-point before modulation (cosmic resorption).
- Reality is a coaxial system: a range of variations operating off omnipresent *eide* structures.
- Because discrete objects and static frames behave in a synchronized, unified way despite having no real causal link, one can infer a purposeful, sentient "Prime Mover" initiating the sequence.
- God must destroy the universe every nanosecond and create a new one slightly differently. This continuous creation replaces mechanical causality.
- "Deus sive natura" (God or reality) is slightly imperfect; beyond the reality field (nature) lies the "void," allowing God to perturb the field.

## Entities
- **Mircea Eliade**: Referenced for concepts like cosmic resorption and overcoming time.
- **Schopenhauer**: Associated with the idea that spontaneous information or "will" can create reality ex nihilo.
- **Spinoza**: His assertion of "God or Nature" is examined; Dick amends it by stating the Perturbing Force (God) and the reality field are distinct, with the void lying behind reality.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Anamnesis**: Frame-retrieval; recovering the permanent original configurations.
- **Intrusions**: Elements of reality breaking into a dream (referencing Lem's analysis of *Ubik* or *Time Out of Joint*).
- **Continuous Creation**: Hyper-occasionalism; the generation of the universe anew every instant.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Void / Anokhi**: The ultimate level of deity that thinks, and whose thoughts become the perturbing force upon the reality field.
- **Eschatological Conflict**: The firing of particulars reveals their intrinsic essences (Darkness or Light), resulting in a vast conflict resolved by God.
