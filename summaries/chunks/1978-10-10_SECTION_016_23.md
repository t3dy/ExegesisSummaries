# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-23

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-23
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 092 to 098
- **Themes:** Quantum Leap Evolution, HP-Omega, The Future Progeny, Interstellar Holy Race.

## Key Claims
- **The Law Fulfilled:** The "Law" (planetary powers/DNA) was self-programmed to end when fulfilled, replaced by the "New Order" of Christ.
- **Quantum Leap Step:** Evolution's goal is the transition from DNA-programmed robots to members of the conscious *Corpus Christi*.
- **The HP-Omega Progeny:** The progeny of the homoplasmate (Firebright) is the "HP-Omega" – a new, immortal, and non-temporal life form.
- **Marriage of Species:** PKD (Yin/Womb) and the Plasmate (Yang/Bridegroom) bonded to create Firebright, who will "come out of his slumber" upon PKD's death.
- **Secret Breeding:** An interstellar "holy race" has bred into humanity for 2000 years, undetected by the world's powers.

## Entities
- **Firebright / HP-Omega:** The immortal hybrid progeny.
- **The Plasmate:** The discorporate non-terrestrial spirit.
- **St. Sophia / Siddhartha:** Archetypes of the imminent new savior.
- **Kabir:** Sufi poet cited regarding the "growing body" of the secret one.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Crossbonding:** The mechanism of siring the next-stage life form.
- **Slumbering/Siddhartha Slept:** The current latent state of the new savior/progeny.
- **The 3rd Eye:** The entry point for the plasmate (traveling up the optic nerve).

## Theological Motifs
- **Slaves of Christ:** Moving from being instruments of DNA to "friends" of the Master.
- **Physical Resurrection:** Interpreted as the acquisition of an "incorruptible body" (the progeny).
- **Adoption into the Godhead:** The final goal of human evolution.
