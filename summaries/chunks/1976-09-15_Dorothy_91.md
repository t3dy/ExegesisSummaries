# Divine Isomorphism: The Secret Identity of Man and God
## concise_summary
Dick concludes that Man and God are "secretly isomorphic" (identical in form and nature), while the world is a separate, alienating agency. He views his 3-74 experience as the realization of this identity, where the "Sane God" (Zebra) and "True Man" (Thomas) unite against the "Iron Prison" of the world.
## key_claims
- Secret Isomorphism: 1 John 3:2 ("We shall be like him") confirms that Man and God are fundamentally the same. The world (II) is NOT isomorphic with either.
- The True Vine (Kingship): "Kingship of God" refers to eternal life as the fruit of the vine. Christ is the source of life and the "judicially" supreme judge.
- World-Repudiation (Gnostic): The world literally hates and condemns God. Dick reverses this: God disavows and punishes (destroys) the world.
- To Know is to Rectify: Gnosis (ontological knowledge) reverses the Fall (ignorance) and heals the split between Man and God.
- Scanner/Tears Gestalt: Combining *A Scanner Darkly* and *Flow My Tears* reveals how Zebra smuggles saving messages. Bruce's "I am an eye" is the Ajna-eye seeing the "mors ontologica."
- 3-74 as Historical Sentence: The "Ancient Eye" saw the modern Empire, passed judgment, and carried out the sentence (Nixon's fall).
