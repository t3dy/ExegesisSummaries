# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-07

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-07
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 20 - 040 to 045
- **Themes:** Christ as Judge, The Beetle Redux, Evolutionary Leap vs. Restore, Toxicity.

## Key Claims
- **Christ Pantocrator:** PKD experiences Christ in three roles: Judge, Lord, and Savior. Adjudication of his life was rendered final and perfect in 3-74.
- **The Beetle Radar:** PKD's act of sparing a beetle as a child "circumnavigated the universe" and returned as his salvation. "I:beetle, Christ:me."
- **The Great Assize:** The universe is a "teaching machine" that ultimately judges whether we have learned the lessons (Agape).
- **Metacircuits & Evolution:** Homoplasmates represent a quantum leap forward (up) in evolution, but also a recovery of lost (pre-fall) faculties.
- **Neurological Toxification:** Following "Scanner," PKD views our occlusion as a literal poisoning ("bichlorides" / "mercury"). Salvation is a "detox."

## Entities
- **The Beetle:** The micro-creature whose sparing saved its savior.
- **Ma'at:** Goddess of judgment (invoked for the "closing of the books").
- **Christopher:** St. Christopher / PKD as the carrier of Christ.
- **William Burroughs:** Cited regarding the "original curse" or "criminal virus."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bichromated Lights:** The signaling system of the macrobrain.
- **Karmic Determinism:** Broken by the act of divine mercy.
- **Mustard Seed:** The growth of the divine nature from a small initial act.

## Theological Motifs
- **Inductive Reasoning:** Reasoning from the "reminder clues" back to the original situation.
- **Vicarious Sacrifice:** Understanding how Christ's sacrifice exculpates the human.
- **The Closing of the Books:** Final judgment occurring within this life.
