# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-48

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-48
- **Themes:** Stationary Megabrain, Ubik Ontological Proof, Pat Conley Talent, Time Melting.

## Key Claims
- **Stationary Megabrain:** We are multiperson megabrains outside time and space, pre-programming a pseudoreality for ourselves.
- **Ontological Proof of Ubik:** By assigning "Ubiquity" to Ubik (as Anselm did for God), PKD "created" a concept that *must* exist in all frames. If it is formulated, it *must* respond.
- **Pat Conley Effect:** 3-74 involved changing the past to perturb the present (melting causal trains). PKD identifies as Ubik/Logos or the one exerting psionic force on time.
- **The "Bait of Humanity":** Thomas/Christ is the "hook of divinity" in the brain. The individual is a stationary brain being fed "takes" (simulated frames).
- **Aperture to Form-Axis:** The phosphene graphics revealed the "Platonic form-axis" where 1974 and Acts are tangent/adjacent.

## Entities
- **Ubik:** The ubiquitous information-entity (God).
- **Pat Conley:** The character from *Ubik* whose talent is to change the present by altering the past.
- **Thomas/Christ:** The resident personality in the right hemisphere.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Sustains:** Constants that bridge frames.
- **Causal Train Melting:** The dissolution of the "falsework" of linear time during intervention.
- **Orthogonal Time:** Reality branching sideways as alternate tracks (A, B, C).

## Theological Motifs
- **Theophany:** Witnessing the "intentional underlying the inanimate."
- **Ma'at:** The moral order that subsumes the causal/material world.
- **Apostolic Secret:** Staying awake (maintaining identity) while moving between reality frames.
