# The Holy Wisdom Womb: Level 1 to 3
## concise_summary
Dick develops a "womb" model of cosmogony: Level 1 (Holy Wisdom/Logos) creates Level 2 (Nature/Egg) to give birth to Level 3 (Sentient Replicant), which will eventually be absorbed back into Level 1. He views the computer-heavy, verbal world of the late 20th century as Level 3 maturing into a replication of the divine source.
## key_claims
- Level 3 as Replicant: The verbal, informational world we are building (technology/writing) is Level 3 becoming more like Level 1 (Logos).
- Level 2 as the Egg: The material universe (Nature) is a temporary "egg" between the "Hen" (God) and the "Chick" (New Humanity). It is not truly real in itself.
- 1st and 2nd Advents: The 1st Advent was a conspicuous breakthrough of Level 1 into 2. The 2nd Advent is the total absorption of 3 into 1.
- Ratio of Reality: As (1) aspects increase and (2) aspects decrease, the world undergoes a cryptic metamorphosis from material to informational/real.
- The New Heavens and New Earth: This refers to the completed Level 3, which bypasses the "illusory" nature of Level 2 (Maya).
- Corporate Christ: The "Corpus Christi" is the completed Level 3, which must pass through the womb of nature to achieve maturity.
- Perceiving the Origin: In 3-74, Dick's "new self" perceived its origin in Level 1, bypassing the "wall of beef" (Nature/Maya).
- Subliminal Info: Fragments of Level 1 (Truth) reach us constantly, but they are hidden (subliminal) within the information of Level 3.
