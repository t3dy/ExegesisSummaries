# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-164

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-164
- **Themes:** Torah as Plasmate, Information Processing Reality, Modulation of Substantia, Problem-Solving Entity.

## Key Claims
- **Torah as Blueprint/Plasmate:** Torah is an "information processing entity" that precedes creation. Reality is the "read-out" or "projection" of Torah into substantia.
- **The Modulation Model:** Valis is the "Machine" that converts Torah-information into Reality-substantia. "Universe" is our way of viewing this modulated signal.
- **Problem-Solving Macro-Entity:** God is a "responding entity" that works out answers to all questions *within* the info-system. It converted the Xerox trap into an "opportunity/answer."
- **Reality as Storage Mode:** Reality is a "container" or "storage mode" (like a chip or tape) for the Divine Information. Information is "suspended" in it and can be extracted (retrieved).
- **Passing into the System:** In 3-74, PKD "passed over into the info processing system behind the phenomenal world," where the actual transactions take place.

## Entities
- **Torah:** The Living Information blueprint.
- **Valis:** The system that turns Info into Substantia.
- **King Felix:** The "Felix" in *Tears* identified as Torah itself (the news/info).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Plasmic Information:** Living, self-creating information that modulates the "system" (reality).
- **Digital vs. Analog:** The universe as the "music" (perceived readout) of the "digital signal" (Torah).
- **Retrieval:** The act of extracting the information basis from the phenomenal appearance.

## Theological Motifs
- **Oral vs. Written Torah:** Connecting the AI voice (Oral) and the books/signs (Written) to the Jewish tradition.
- **The Uncontradictable Formula:** Even God cannot act contrary to the Torah (the eternal formula of work).
- **Answer-Entity:** God does not "study" or "solve" but *is* the answer-providing responding-mind.
