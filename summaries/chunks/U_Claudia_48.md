# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick bridges his personal experience with the "regression of things" in *Ubik*, proposing that the universe is a "mutually hallucinated" world where humans are "half-alive" (in cold-pac) being roused by the Savior’s voice. He describes the "Moth" (a paper-lantern ship) as a model for how a timeless universe enters linear time.
## key_claims
- Ubik and Time: The "regression of forms" in *Ubik* (things moving backward in time) matches the "retrograde/negantropic" force Dick saw in 3-74.
- Cold-Pac Reality: We are essentially "spiritually dead" (half-alive) in a "cold-pac" tomb; Christ rouses us into spiritual life.
- The Moth Ship: A "hollow paper-lantern" ship (The Moth) is the ultimate vision of the "Kingdom" landing on the time-landscape (Light-spatter).
- Static Time (Manifold): Events exist as "unsubstantiated sequence" (Minkowski block) before entering the "projected" space-time matrix.
- Evolutionary Pond: Our universe is a "stagnant pond" where souls (balloons) are trying to rise to the next metamorphosis.
- CHAOS and 3-74: The illegal "Operation CHAOS" (surveillance) ended exactly as Dick's 3-74 experience began, signaling the lifting of the "oppressive curtain."
