# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-28

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-28
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - Page 8 to end (Valis/Ubik connection)
- **Themes:** Time into Space, Lamination Model, Ubik as Matrix.

## Key Claims
- **Time as Matrix Error:** The modern world is in error; Space, not Time, is the matrix of being. Enlightenment (Buddha) is converting time into space.
- **Lamination Model:** Reality is not a sequence of replaced moments, but an "onion" of superimposed, laminated layers (Past + Present + Future = Bip).
- **Ubik as Reality Model:** *Ubik* correctly depicts time spatially (the forms within forms). Valis is the same entity as Ubik.
- **Zebra = Future Self:** Zebra/Valis is the "ultimate multiplex life form" of PKD's own self from the future, reaching back to organize his past.
- **Constants in the Graphics:** The phosphene visions showed "constants" emerging from the flux, indicating the assembly of an entelechy.

## Entities
- **VALIS/ZEBRA/UBIK:** The unitary informational entity.
- **Thomas:** The past psyche (Acts world).
- **Parsifal:** Cited for the phrase "There, my son, time changes into space."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Entelechy:** The self-assembling informational structure using the universe as parts.
- **Laminations:** The "onion model" where the past remains within the present.
- **Dibba Cakkhu:** The divine eye of discernment.

## Theological Motifs
- **Oscillation/Exhalation:** The Godhead breathing out individual identities and then inhaling them back.
- **Acosmism:** The view that reality is informational and non-material.
- **Inner Space:** The "inner firmament" of Paracelsus / R-C Brotherhood.
