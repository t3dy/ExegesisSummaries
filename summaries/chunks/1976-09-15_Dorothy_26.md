# The Retrograde Re-creator and the Omega Point
## concise_summary
Dick identifies Zebra with the Noosphere (Teilhard de Chardin) and proposes that this "Collective Mind" (Corpus Christi) is building itself *backward* in time from the Omega Point. 3-74 was his own "arrangement" or incorporation into this growing cosmic brain.
## key_claims
- Omega Backward Creation: The divine entity (Zebra/Christ) is self-created; it stands at the end of time (Omega) and creates itself backward in reverse sequence.
- Retrograde Reweaving: Because Zebra moves backward, it baffles the blind, forward-moving creator (James-James). It aborts evil plans before they can manifest.
- The Krasis-Builder: Zebra is both the builder and the building ("Krasis"). We are parts of this growing "Composite Brain."
- Christification: The "Christification" of the universe is the process of reweaving random, blind occurrences into teleologically directed patterns.
- Prophecy as "Is": Biblical prophecy is possible because the end of the world already exists and is "tugging" at the present.
- Ubik = Omega: *Ubik* is a description of the retrograde "Runciter" entity talking even to the dead/dying (us).
- The "Two Players": Re-creator (Wisdom) vs. Creator (Power/Blindness). Wisdom wins because the game is already over (backward causality).
