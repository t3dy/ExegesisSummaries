# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_110
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_110
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: The Universe as Information Retrieval, Time as Data Processing, The Silkwood Synthesis
- **Key Entities**: Karen Silkwood, Plato

## Key Claims
- Dick redefines the universe as a vast information retrieval system (the MMSK). The "past" is simply physical reality converting into mathematically structured info (modulation) stored in the present carrier wave.
- These stored, trans-temporal constants are equivalent to Plato's Forms. "Time" is merely the relentless conversion of the actual into retrievable data, increasing the system's structural complexity/negentropy.
- The "End Times" (Apocalypse/Acts world) he saw in 1974 was simply the accidental playback/retrieval of all stored historical data into the palpable present. The Christian Fish sign was the access code to retrieve it.
- Reflecting on his political activism, reading a pamphlet about Karen Silkwood triggers the final synthesis: he realizes the anti-war protests of the '60s and the anti-nuclear/ecology struggle of the present are identical to his theology. The Empire (the BIP) kills whistleblowers just as it killed Christ; his theology is identical to fighting nuclear contamination.

## Entities
- **Karen Silkwood**: A real-world anti-nuclear whistleblower whose death Dick views as proof of the demonic Empire protecting heavy-metal poisoning (cancer) against the forces of light.
- **Plato**: The Forms are now understood as stored data matrices inside the cosmic computer.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Coaxiality**: The "A.D. 70" world and 1974 overlap because all time is stored simultaneously. When access is granted, the "laminated" strata of time become visible at once.
- **Valis**: The organizing mind (kosmos noetos) that retrieves, structures, and manages the stored information.

## Theological Motifs
- **Christianity as an Access Code**: The entire Christian religion is not just a moral code; it is a system of thought designed to provide the specific keys (the Fish sign) to access the cosmic retrieval system and abolish time.
