## Key Claims
- Dick discovers the mechanical purpose behind human occlusion (the Fall): Humans are kept disconnected from the macro-mind so they can generate their own independent, autonomous messages (Message A).
- When a human's Message A interacts with the world's deterministic reality (Message B), it forces a synthesis, creating a totally new, supervenient output (Message C).
- This is how the Macro-Entity (Valis) keeps itself alive and prevents thermodynamic heat death. It absorbs negative entropy (new information) generated by billions of isolated, struggling sub-units (humans) engaging in dialectical combat with their environment. 
- While humans are strictly bound by the Karma/necessity of the Message C they help create, Valis curtails its own omniscience specifically to allow this unpredictable novelty to occur, thus keeping itself young and viable in a closed system.

## Entities
- **Hegel**: His dialectic (Thesis + Antithesis = Synthesis) is adopted as the exact mechanism by which new information (negative entropy) is generated in the cosmic system.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Information as Life**: The ultimate definition of life—whether for humans or the Cosmic Christ—is the continuous generation of novel, anti-entropic recombinant information.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Illusion of Predestination**: Despite the overarching structure of the macro-entity, human free will is absolutely fundamentally required. Without our autonomous choices, Valis would stagnate and die of entropy.
