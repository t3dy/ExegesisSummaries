# The Inner Tape: Victory over DNA Programming
## concise_summary
Dick analyzes the deterministic "inner tape" (DNA/Ancestral programming) that controls humans like "robots." He argues that 3-74 was a "volitional override" of this biological script by a "conscious salvific entity" (God), triggered by the Fish sign to save him from physical death.
## key_claims
- Automated Existence: Humans normally act like robots, enslaved by "inner tapes" (DNA/DNA programming) that serve the "gene pool," not the individual.
- The Script Analysis: Dick applies Berne's script theory to DNA; our biological programming eventually orders us to "execute ourselves" (Death/Entropy).
- Override via 3-74: In 3-74, Dick's volitional center was "displaced" by an entity (Thomas/Zebra) that overrode his DNA script.
- The Fish Sign as Trigger: The Golden Fish sign (2-74) triggered the firing of previously "inhibited" neural circuits, initiated by a GABA drop.
- Saving from Death: Dick identifies "Fate" as physical death itself. The "DNA programming" had led him to doom (The Xerox letter/Suicide potential), and God introjected a "sua generis" (historyless) phenomenon to save him.
- Noos over Ananke: The victory was not over "evil" but over "Necessity" (Ananke/Determinism).
- Continuous Creation: Thomas was "literally created" ex nihilo in 3-74, serving as an example of how God modualtes history by adding "neg-entropy" to a closed system.
- Ma'at as Regulatory Justice: Dick posits a universal moral order (Ma'at) that regulates the "struggle" between the two demiurges (James-James and the Intercessor).
