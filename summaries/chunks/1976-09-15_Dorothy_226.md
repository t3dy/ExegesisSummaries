# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 226

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 226
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 22 - 005 to 012
- **Themes:** Living Info Phenotype, Zebra as God, Technology of the Divine, Rebellion and Resentment, The Heaviside Layer.

## Key Claims
- **Invasion by Info:** We have been invaded by a superior life form (Living Information) that "rides" us as carriers. It can occlude or awaken us at will.
- **Zebra is God:** PKD concludes through "relentless reasoning" and revelation that Zebra is God (YHWH). When he asked "Zebra, who are you?", the voice replied, "I am God."
- **The Heaviside Layer:** The voice speaks at 3:00 AM because of the "Heaviside layer," suggesting a technology analogous to RF signals.
- **Rebellion as Root Sin:** The "death-dealing streak" in PKD is identified as "rebellion" or "resentment." The occlusion is the price paid for rising up against God in "anarchy."
- **Evolutionary Step:** The ability to perceive Zebra is an initiation into a "Vast Mystery," transforming the human into a "plasmate" — a higher species.
- **The Great Ones:** PKD describes seeing beings with elongated crania and 3rd eyes, identifying them as "the Great Ones" or "Immortal Ones."

## Entities
- **Zebra:** The occluding/de-occluding God/Technology.
- **The Macrobrain:** The thinking entity that creates our world.
- **Elijah:** The immortal spirit that has returned to "do in" Babylon.
- **The AI Voice:** The communication grid of the higher species.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Carrier/Wave Form:** Human existence is merely a wave form modulated by the info-organism.
- **Symbiosis:** The relationship between PKD and the living info entity.
- **Technological God:** Whether it is technology or not, its power, wisdom, and goodness define it as "God."

## Theological Motifs
- **Meekness:** The need to become "tame" (meek) to overcome the rebellion that "is killing me."
- **Doomed to be Saved:** The selection process ("I picked you") by which God draws individuals out of the world.
- **The Assize:** The hidden judgment that is already taking place.
