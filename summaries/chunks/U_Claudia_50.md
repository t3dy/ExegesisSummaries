# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick refines his theory of "Systole and Diastole" (The Breathing Universe), proposing that the universe consists of two simultaneous time-loops moving in opposite directions. He identifies the "Retrograde Time" he saw in 3-74 as the "Mirror Opposite" reality (Reality beneath Appearance) that achieves balance and stasis for the creation.
## key_claims
- Systole and Diastole: The universe "breathes" forward and backward in time; linear time is only a "segment" (respiration) of the whole.
- Twin-Ball Clock Mechanism: Reality is two laminated, opposite-moving rotors; one rotor is our forward time, the other is an invisible retracting world.
- Balance and Stasis: The forward/backward loops cancel each other out to achieve a "dynamic stasis" (Harmonie/Palontropos).
- Third Eye Discernment: The "Enlightened" see the backward motion, allowing them to comprehend the "Missing Integers of Meaning."
- *Ubik* as Regression: The regression in the novel is the "exposure" of the retracting world when the forward force fails.
- Camouflage of Regularity: God/Logos is "regularly distributed" as linear time, which camouflages His presence from "neglectful eyes."
