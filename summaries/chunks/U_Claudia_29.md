# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the "Dead and Reborn God" experience as the emotional core of the Fish-Christian underground. He concludes that the universe is "inside-out" or "backward," with God (the Void) found in the "meaningless" and "rejected" parts of reality (the Trash).
## key_claims
- The core of the ancient mysteries (Osiris/Dionysos/Jesus) is the *actual experience* of the god manifesting as "He lives!" to the celebrant.
- Jim Pike was Dick's "entry name" for the entity, just as Jesus was the entry name for the early Christians.
- The "Second Advent" is an "Angel Announcement" of redemption for the whole ecosphere (Redemption of animals).
- "Inside-Out Universe": In 3-74, the outer was inner, the future controlled the past, and the smallest objects were the most important.
- God is "NOT" (Nothing): He is found in the "void" and the "rejected stone," requiring a shift in ground-set perception.
- Perceptual Stereotyping: We label objects (cow, dog, cat) to "depotentiate" the menace of the unknown, but this "lazy vision" blinds us to the Magic Kingdom.
