# The Infiltrator Mission: Waking to the Real 70 AD
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the "linear world" (1974) as a communal dream or "hallucination" (*Maze of Death*) that we are "doomed to repeat" until we wake up. He argues that 3-74 was his "waking" into the "authentic reality" of 70 AD, where he remains as an "inner agent" for the coming 2nd Advent.
## key_claims
- Reality as Repetitious Loop: Linear time is a "shared hallucination" or "polyencephalic fusion" where we repeat the same script (Karmic wheel).
- 1974 is a Dream: Waking up in 1974 is like waking from a dream of being in Berkeley to the reality of Santa Ana. 1974 is the "dream future"; 70 AD is the "real present."
- Waking vs. Memory: Anamnesis is not remembering a "past" life but realizing "I AM really in 70 AD" (Present Tense).
- Deterministic Snipe: The "Dark Counterplayer" keeps us in a state of sleep/amnesia to ensure we play our roles in the script.
- The Deceiver's Purpose: The "long extended time" (2000 years) is a trick to make the "fallen ones" (tares) feel safe enough to reveal their evil natures.
- The Omphalos of 70 AD: 70 AD is the "punched-through omphalos" (center) of reality. All "history" is just a circular warp around this center.
- Orthogonal Voiding: Once an orthogonal event (Advent) occurs, linear time is "voided" and becomes a repeating "vortex."
- The 2nd Advent as Completion: The 2nd Advent (1974) is not a separate event but the "Omega stage" completion of the 1st Advent.
