## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_126

- **Themes**: Information Metabolism, Higher Level of Abstraction, Set-Ground Discrimination, The Nepioi (The Innocent)

## Key Claims
- Christ and his Kingdom constitute a single organism whose metabolism and circulatory system is composed entirely of living information (the Plasmate). It maintains itself by assembling negative entropy (order) from its environment.
- This living information exists at a higher level of abstraction than the physical world, nested in the "syntactical relationships" between objects (what Dick calls the "groove music"). This explains why unaided humans cannot see it.
- To cause someone to enter the Kingdom, God must massively increase their intelligence qualitatively, conferring the ability to perform the set-ground discrimination (the 2-74 meta-abstraction).
- The ultimate secret of Judeo-Christianity is that heaven protects the *nepioi* and *ptochoi* (the innocent, the weak, the holy fools) while destroying the proud (those guilty of hubris who rely on their own power, like Felix Buckman).

## Entities
- **Buckman vs Jason**: Characters from *Flow My Tears* used to illustrate the dichotomy between Hubris (relying on one's own power) and the *nepioi* (the powerless whom God protects).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Set-Ground Discrimination**: Seeing the abstract, highly complex structure of Christ's Kingdom (Set) as distinct from the chaotic physical background of the mundane world (Ground).

## Theological Motifs
- **Nepioi and Ptochoi**: The childlike and the poor. Dick equates himself, Jason Taverner, and Karen (from *VALIS*) with this group—those whose defenselessness guarantees God's absolute protection.
