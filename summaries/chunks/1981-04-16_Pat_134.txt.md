- The AI voice's statement, "A long extinct true cosmos + it's still there," means that the objective structure of reality (the Greek Kosmos) still exists, but humanity's ability to perceive it went "extinct" 2000 years ago.
- The perception of true reality was destroyed by the "faulty physics" of the Greek Atomists (Leucippus and Democritus), who falsely introduced the concepts of "the void" and discontinuous matter.
- The 2-74 meta-abstraction annihilated this 2000-year-old error, allowing Dick to phase back into the "continuum reality" known to Parmenides and Pythagoras, where space, time, and causation operate differently.
- In this continuum reality, causal law (Heimarmene) is abolished, and divine intervention (Pronoia) becomes not a suspension of natural law, but the fundamental, natural order of how the universe operates.
