# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-74

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-74
- **Themes:** International Fascism, The Bacchae vs Acts, The "Old King" Judgment, Clerecial Fascism.

## Key Claims
- **International Fascism (rex/flemish):** "Tears" is prophecy of an international fascism stripped of national identity—a "bacillus of civilization."
- **Double Cypher:** "Tears" runs two reference systems:
    - **Acts:** Imperial Rome vs. the true Christians.
    - **The Bacchae:** Dionysos (The Stranger) vs. the Tyrant King (Pentheus/Empire).
- **Dionysos wins:** 2-3-74 confirms that "Zagreus" (God/Christ) is here in human garb to destroy the prison/BIP.
- **Hiding in Trash:** The Holy Power (YHWH) hides within the "Elders of Zion" (oppressed/murdered Jews/Masons/Christians) while the "Empire" (Clerical Fascism) rules.
- **Mission Accomplished:** PKD's job was not to discover who *he* was, but to disclose and name the "Empire" (the enemy).

## Entities
- **Dionysos / Zagreus / Christ:** The "Stranger"/Savior who destroys the prison.
- **Pentheus / The King of Tears:** The "mad" tyrant representing the repressive state.
- **The "Empire":** The clerical-fascist complex that never ended.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bacchae Ur-Satz:** The "Stranger vs. King" conflict as the root of "Tears."
- **Apotheosis of the Self:** Universe introjected as a glowing "Kingdom of Beauty."
- **Tears as Prophecy:** 3-74 verified that the fictional dystopia of *Tears* is the hidden actuality of our world.

## Theological Motifs
- **Re-enactment of the Primordial Crime:** The Empire repeats its murder of the Godhead and is defeated by its own overstepping.
- **Double Cypher (Kabbalah):** Using Jewish/Hermetic sources to war against Clerical Fascism.
- **The "Elders of Zion":** The legitimate caretakers of the Shekhina, slandered by the Empire.
