# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-86

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-86
- **Themes:** Retrograde Tracking, Time Vortex, Bio-evolutionary Leap, Self-Programming.

## Key Claims
- **Retrograde Time Flow:** PKD is actually moving *backward* in time (toward order/form) while the rest of the world moves toward entropy (chaos). This explains why he "pre-knew" 3-74 in the 60s.
- **Time Vortex (Nitty-Gritty):** Stress caused a "time vortex" that pulled past (anamnesis) and future (hindsight) into the present to solve the Xerox crisis.
- **Self-Programming (Adam Kadmon):** We are miniaturizations of the macro-entity (God) who pre-programmed our own reality (Karma) and then forgot. Enlightenment is recognizing the "external" as our own amplified creation.
- **The Plasmaste Integration:** "Living information" (the plasmate) prints instructions directly into our "inner tapes," harmonizing us with the simulation.
- **Acausal Synchronization:** Our "inner tapes" and the "external simulation" are coordinated in advance, creating the illusion of "cause and effect."

## Entities
- **Valis / Ubik:** The macro-computer/booster that maintains the simulated reality field.
- **Thomas:** The "later" self from the post-mortem side (providing hindsight).
- **Diana:** God's daughter / tutulary goddess guide.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Krasis (Timaeus):** The mixture of Noos (mind) and Ananke (necessity).
- **Phylogenic memories:** Memories of a 2000-year-old self (Acts).
- **Pre-textual Cause:** The spurious "external" world that merely notifies the "inner tape" when to fire.

## Theological Motifs
- **Passing into Eternity:** The 3-74 experience as a leap out of time itself.
- **Tat Tvam Asi:** The realization that "Thou art That" (Self = Macro-entity of the world).
- **Return to Mastery:** Regaining creative control over the simulated reality once it is "spotted."
