# The Eucharist as Time-Travel and the "Agape" Feast
## concise_summary
Dick interprets the Eucharist as an authentic transportation to an "orthogonal universe" where Christ still lives. He links his experience to the "Feast of Agape" and the legalistic/Roman suppression of this sacred memory. He identifies 2,000 years of "apostasy" and the upcoming "next great step" in human evolution as a return to the state of Holy Wisdom (St. Sophia).
## key_claims
- Eucharist as Anamnesis: The Command "Do this in remembrance of me" is a mechanism to enter hyper-time (eternity) and rejoin the Savior.
- Transubstantiation: Defined as an ACTUAL transportation to another universe where the Last Supper is still taking place.
- Feast of Agape: The authentic early Christian rite that induced theolepsy (sacred possession/restoration).
- 2,000 Year Apostasy: The Romans suppressed the knowledge of theolepsy and transubstantiation, leading to an "obscure adversary" period.
- Evolutionary Step: Using Teilhard de Chardin, Dick frames the return to Holy Wisdom as the next step of human evolution.
- Minkowski Block Universe: Future and past events exist simultaneously; 3-74 allowed Dick to "pass across" to the level where the Cosmic Christ (Logos) is active.
- Cosmic Christ vs. Man Jesus: Dick worships the "First Emanation" (St. Sophia/Logos), not the dead man Jesus.
