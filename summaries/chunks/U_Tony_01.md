# U_Tony_01
# SECTION_003
# [Undated, early 1975]
# Tony Hiss
## concise_summary
Draft of a memorial piece for Tony Boucher. Dick explores Boucher's fear of the universe, his role as a mentor, and the startling claim that his cat Pinky was Boucher's reincarnation.
## key_claims
- Tony Boucher was "terrified all the time" despite his brilliance.
- Timidity in people often stems from "loving the universe too much."
- Pinky (Dick's cat) was a reincarnation of Tony Boucher.
- The "Roog" motif in Dick's early work was a warning from the "Dreamer."
