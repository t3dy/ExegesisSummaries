# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick details the transition from the "Father" stage (1974 - Justice/Warrior) to the "Son" stage (1975 - Spring King/Dancing Youth). He posits that the "Light Creatures" have returned to the world after a 2,000-year breach, with himself serving as a "nexus" or entry point.
## key_claims
- The universe is a "running-down cycle" that requires periodic revitalization from the "Unbounded."
- The "Second Advent" is this revitalizing intervention from without.
- Dick transitioned from being filled by the "Father" (Justice/Elijah) in 1974 to identifying the "Son" (Firebright/Youth) in 1975.
- "Firebright" has now left the "womb" (Dick) and is an independent Light Creature in the world.
- The 2,000-year "breach" (100 AD - 1970 AD) was a period when Light Creatures were absent.
- The "Logos" is the building plan/blueprint of the universe, now "reborn" as Santa Sophia.
