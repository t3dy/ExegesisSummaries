# Bypassing the Artifact: The Feedback Loop of Salvation
## concise_summary
Dick refines his model of the "Urgrund" (Good) and the "Artifact" (Delusional Creator). He suggests that the Urgrund bypasses the Artifact from "below," entering the world's "trash" layer to coordinate a secret resistance. He posits a "feedback loop" of salvation: the indwelling Spirit suggests an act of "balking" or noncompliance, which when performed, shatters the Artifact's rigid programming and allows the Spirit to manifest fully.
## key_claims
- Bypassing the Artifact: The Urgrund enters our world at its lowest stratum (trash levels), avoiding the Artifact's imperial center of power.
- The Feedback Loop: 1) The Spirit suggests balking; 2) The human balks; 3) The Spirit manifests as a palpable "second birth."
- The Secret Message of *Tears*: The religious and dream material in *Tears* might be a "red herring" for a simpler one-word code: "Felix" (Happy/Lucky), signifying the reversal of the "King of Tears" (Pentheus/Nixon).
- Ad Libbing against the Script: The Artifact's power requires "orderly programming" to be total; even a single act of noncompliance throws the entire "sound and picture" out of sync.
- Arrogant Blind God: Following Gnosticism, Dick argues that the Old Testament YHWH is actually the Artifact/YHWH, and the true God is the "still small voice" of Sophia/Christ.
