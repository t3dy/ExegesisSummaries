# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-40

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-40
- **Themes:** God's Secret Plan, Valis as Satan (Questioned), Disclosure Technology, 2nd Advent.

## Key Claims
- **The Hidden Plan (Ephesians 3:9):** God keeps His salvific plan secret from the "sovereignties and powers" (planetary archons) to foil them.
- **Valis vs. Satan:** PKD briefly considers if VALIS is Satan (the deceiver who rules the world), but rejects it because the disclosure (Gnosis) was intentional and liberating.
- **Time Liquidation:** Christ returns to "liquidate time," which PKD experienced in 2-3-74. This breaks the inner-outer programming.
- **Female Spirit of Christ:** A revelation that Christ's spirit is female (Holy Wisdom/Sophia) despite having the body of a man (Jesus).
- **Technological Disclosure:** PKD speculates if Soviet experiments with time (Kozyrev) accidentally "dis-synched" reality, revealing Valis.

## Entities
- **St. Sophia:** The female nature of the Holy Spirit/Wisdom.
- **Diana:** The spirit seen at the vernal equinox; identified with the soul of the world.
- **The Sovereignties and Powers:** The evil spirits/archons unaware of God's plan.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Liquidating Time:** The core of the soteriological fulfillment; moving from time to eternity.
- **Acosmism:** The realization that our objective world is irreal.
- **Inner-Outer Lock:** The synchronization of the mind and the environment that constitutes "fate."

## Theological Motifs
- **The Chosen (Philip):** PKD’s conviction that he was personally called and chosen for this disclosure.
- **The Great Experiment:** Using time manipulations to reveal the "camouflaged" macro-life form.
- **The Meadow Reward:** The peace and pastures experienced after the time-liquidation of 3-74.
