# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-128

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-128
- **Themes:** Steganographic Opposite, The Protecting Spirit, Individual Salvation vs. Species DNA, Mind Transfer.

## Key Claims
- **The True Narrative:** *Tears* contains one ostensible story (police paranoia) and a steganographic "Opposite" (the police are right, Taverner is the revolution). 2-3-74 confirms the "Opposite" is the true one.
- **Self-Protection of Info:** Supernatural help in 3-74 was given to protect the *information* in "Tears," not PKD personally. Thomas stepped in to ensure the "true story" wasn't detected by G2.
- **Salvation as Individuation:** Jesus combats Schopenhauer's "irrational will" (DNA) which treats individuals as expendable tools for the species. Salvation makes the individual "safe" from the genetic cycle.
- **The Universal Logic of "Dying for the Friend":** Christ "died for PKD's sins" in 3-74 by taking the "death blow" (the Xerox probe/G2 threat) himself, allowing the "mask-self" to survive and be reborn.
- **The Secret Underground Hierarchy:** The underground members don't know each other; they are unified by the Holy Spirit (a single composite entity/distributed mind).

## Entities
- **The Holy Spirit / Paraclete:** The "distributed mind" unifying the underground.
- **The Xerox Missive:** A "G2 Probe" or "test" of the author's true identity.
- **Thomas:** The "Reconstituted Kyrios" in the brain.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Steganographic Opposite:** A text whose true meaning is the inversion of its surface.
- **Archon / Dark Counterplayer:** The entity duped by the "bait" of Christ's humanity.
- **Bait and Hook:** Christ offering his humanity for the "Powers of Darkness" to strike, only to be "hooked" by his divinity.

## Theological Motifs
- **Transubstantiation:** Christ as a "man pressed onto the cosmos" at an ontological level.
- **Individual Immortality:** The retrieval (anamnesis) of the specific person from the "erased tape" of history.
- **The "Friend" who dies:** The literal substitution of one psyche for another during a life-threat.
