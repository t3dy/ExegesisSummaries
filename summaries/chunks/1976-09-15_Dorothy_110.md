# The Urgrund’s Search for Self-Awareness
## concise_summary
Dick suggests that the artifact (Zebra) projects our reality so that the Urgrund can achieve self-awareness. By observing the "shadows" it projects onto the material plane, the primordial ground learns about its own nature through the agency of conscious beings.
## key_claims
- Mirror of the Divine: The universe is a "mirror" or "screen" for the Urgrund's self-reflection.
- Projector Experiment: Zebra projects the artifact as a way for the infinite Urgrund to "view" itself in a finite form.
- Human as Observer: Humans are the "sensors" by which the Urgrund gains experience in the simulation.
