# The Ham Sandwich Model: Reality as Hidden Pedagogy
## concise_summary
Dick formalizes his "Ham Sandwich" model of reality: a triune structure of Teacher (Zebra) / Artifact World (The "Ham") / Pupils (Humans). He concludes that the empirical world is a "slave labor camp" or "prison" from which we are being roused by help penetrating from the "outside" (*Ubik*).
## key_claims
- The Triune Model: (1) Sentient Teacher (Zebra), (2) The World as Artifact/Hologram (The Ham), (3) Pupils. The "world" hides the teacher to create a "guessing game" (Deus Ludens).
- 3-74 as Apocalypse: For Dick, 3-74 was the "lock-in payoff" (graduation) where the Teacher bypassed the "Teaching Machine" and took direct control.
- Enslaving Programming: The world is a mental "prison" sustained by a web of lies ("hysteresis"). The "in-power liars" and the "oppressed" are both corrupted by this.
- Subconscious Interaction: Humans are in constant dialog with the Teacher via the right brain, but only becoming "changed" slowly through repeat lessons.
- Help from Outside: Positive information (like the "Sophia" message in *Tears* or *Ubik*'s manifestations) penetrates the world from the "outside" to wake us.
- Withdrawal of Assent: True freedom involves a mental "denial" of the empirical world. Only then does the "Black Iron Prison" become visible so we can align with God.
- *Scanner* as Indictment: Dick views *A Scanner Darkly* as a logic progression from *Tears*, showing "New Path" (Rehab centers) as literal slave labor camps and the Feds as the source of the drugs (5D).
- The Dealer/Cop Syzygy: The cover of *Scanner* (showing dealer and cop as two halves of one face) represents the "syzygy" or "two sides of the same coin" in the Empire's strategy.
