# The Koinos Kosmos: The Single Map of All Novels
## concise_summary
Dick realizes that all his novels (1952-1976) are actually chapters of a single "Ur-Geschicht" (Original Story) that maps the "Koinos Kosmos" (Shared Reality) breaking through the "Idios Kosmoi" (Private Delusions). He views his writing and 3-74 as two halves of a single discovery process.
## key_claims
- All Novels are One Story: From *Time Out of Joint* to *Scanner*, every book is an account of another world (World II) thrusting into our illusory one (World I).
- The Ur-Geschicht (Quelle): The "original story" involves a "wisdom machine" (Zebra/Christ) that leaves clues to help humans wake up.
- Koinos vs. Idios: The "Shared World" (Rome 70 AD / Koinos) is the "bottom real world" that the "Private Delusive worlds" (*Ubik*, *Maze*, *Tears*) are hiding.
- The 5th Chakra Payload: 3-74 was the "payoff" or "finding" that proved the maps in his books were accurate. Without 3-74, the books are "maps of nothing."
- Orthogonal Matrix Intensification: The "orthogonal matrix" (the 2nd Advent) doesn't move forward in time; it "intensifies" and "emerges" through the linear strip.
- Trash/Cheap Matter: The 2nd Advent breaks through in "low" places: science fiction novels, trash, and rock lyrics, rather than grand cathedrals.
- The Window to Heaven: This is the first time in 2000 years that a "window" has opened to reveal that it is still the 1st Century A.D.
- Superimposing the Novels: By superimposing *Stigmata*, *Ubik*, *Tears*, and *Maze*, a single "true message" emerges (Eschatological/Gnostic).
