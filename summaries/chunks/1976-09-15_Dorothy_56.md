# Pinky's Passion: The Surrogate Sacrifice
## concise_summary
In one of his most affecting claims, Dick identifies his deceased cat, Pinky, as a "surrogate sacrifice" and manifestation of Zebra who took the "blow" (death) intended for Dick in 3-74. He explores the concept of "adoptionism" and "salvation from time" (death).
## key_claims
- Pinky as Christ-Figure: Pinky was a "surrogate sacrifice" who took the "blow" of the astral powers' script. His death allowed Dick to be "spared" and "given freedom."
- Zebra's Transsubstantiation: Zebra (the plasmic energy) entered and "transsubstantiated" Pinky, just as it entered Dick. Pinky became a "vessel" of the Savior.
- The Blow of Darkness: On the night of the exploding votive candle, "death/wrath" entered the room; Pinky offered himself voluntarily to absorb it.
- Salvation as Release from Time: Based on the EB, Dick defines salvation as "bondage to time/death." 3-74 was literal salvation from a script and a deadline for death.
- Adoptionism via Theolepsy: Jesus/Thomas was a man who "today became the Son" through the theolepsy of the Holy Spirit (possession/inspiration).
- Planetary powers: Deliverance is sought from the "planetary powers" (Heimarmene) that control destiny in the sublunar world.
- N.T. as Supercession: Dick considers if the New Testament (Grace) renders the Old Testament (Law/Creation) null and void.
- The 2 Contesting Powers: (1) Evil deluding astral powers, (2) Good intervening mimicking deity (Zebra) inserting itself like a "cosmos" into "anomie."
