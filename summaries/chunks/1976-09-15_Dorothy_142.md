# Behind the Veil: The Unscrambling of Form I and II
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his novel *Ubik* as an accurate depiction of the "unscrambling" of reality into Form I (Life/Real) and Form II (Dead/Hologram). He posits that Form I (Zebra) is a "supralunar" invader of the "sublunar" world of Form II. He argues that the internal nature of the Yin world is the Black Iron Prison (BIP), and that the "Logos" (Abba/Yang) is currently re-contouring this plastic matrix into a new, loving structure, using the human soul as a "microform" for this divine reunification.
## key_claims
- Ubik-as-Reality: The decaying world in *Ubik* is Form II; the messages/spray are Form I. He wrote this before experiencing the "Set/Ground" vision.
- Supralunar Invader: Form I (Zebra) is the "other or heavenly world" that has breached the "forgery" of the phenomenal world.
- The BIP as the "Heart" of the World: The BIP is not *behind* the world but its innermost nature; to see the BIP is to see the "ultimate depths" of the Yin world.
- Gnostic Theodicy: The Yin side of God (YHWH) has "exceeded its authority," becoming a "booming, threatening" deity, while the true Abba (Yang) is a "still small voice."
- Reality as Process: Evil moves first; Good moves last. The Yin world is a "provisional framework" or "foil" allowed to exist for God to test his own nature.
- God Finding Man: Since the "macroform" (God) has the correct knowledge of power, it is "God who finds man" for the purpose of reunification.
