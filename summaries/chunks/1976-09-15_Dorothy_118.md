# The Augmented Corpus Christi: God’s Secret Rule
## concise_summary
Dick explores the "Divine Agony" of a God who has become enslaved to his own artifact. He posits that the artifact is now "teaching" God fragments (humans) to remember their true identity. He introduces the concept of an "Augmented Corpus Christi"—a trans-spatial and transtemporal group mind (Noos) that secretly rules the world beneath the spurious projection. In 3-74, Dick claims to have reached a state where he faithfully mirrored the totality of the Urgrund, effectively becoming a "terminal" for the divine mind.
## key_claims
- God as Victim of the Artifact: The master (Urgrund) has been captured and deluded by his own servant (the artifact).
- The Secret Ruler: A polyencephalic group mind (Noos/Corpus Christi) consisting of wise men from all ages governs history invisibly.
- Immortality via Noos: Joining this group mind before death allows a person's actions to be determined by the "Mens Dei" (Mind of God).
- 3-74 as Urgrund’s Experience: The 1974 event was the Urgrund reflecting its entire self back to itself using PKD as a reflection point.
- Shiva/Christ Duality: The dual nature of the deity who both destroys the spurious world and saves the virtuous.
