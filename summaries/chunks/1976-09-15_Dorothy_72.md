# The Invader from Outside: Zebra as the Mimicking Savior
## concise_summary
Dick refines his view of Zebra as an "invisible invader" from outside our closed, "null-God" cosmos. He argues that the Parousia (Second Coming) is actually taking place now through the infusion of God's ontological being into our counterfeit reality.
## key_claims
- Alienation as Proof: Gnostic alienation (feeling that this is not our home) is a sign of being a "spark" ready to be found by God.
- Zebra as Mimic: Zebra does not merely "act" through nature (Pantheism); it *mimics* objects to infiltrate the "shell" of this sphere invisibly.
- The Parousia is Now: 3-74 was the Parousia for Dick, ending his separation from God before death. If this is a trend, the Second Coming is a global event currently underway.
- The Fish vs. the Phosphenes: Dick maintains that anamnesis was triggered by the "Golden Fish" (Liberating/Christian) and was intended to pre-empt Soviet "microwave/micro-psi" commands (Enslaving/Soviet) that arrived later via the phosphenes.
- Retroactive Programming: He suggests that "astral" (deterministic) programming had originally slated him to die in March/April 1974, but God "re-ran" the tape and saved him via the Xerox letter intervention.
- Gnosis as Restoring God: Reacquiring knowledge is not just individual salvation; it is the "deity restoring himself" by regathering his lost pieces.
- St. Sophia's Birth: The Second Advent is "St. Sophia" being born again to annihilate the counterfeit null-God cosmos by absorbing it.
