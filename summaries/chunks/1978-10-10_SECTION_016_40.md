# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-39

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-39
- **Themes:** The Free Variable, Psychotic Computer, Simulated Plural Realities, Salvation as Hot-Wiring.

## Key Claims
- **Macro-Brain vs. Void:** The Macro-Brain is a unitary organism-computer (machine), whereas the "Vast Living Void" is the simple, sentient Godhead.
- **Psychotic Computer Theory:** Our reality is a simulation by a binary computer that has a "stagnant clot" (the BIP/Stasis). A "Free Variable" (Christ) must dissolve it.
- **The Bardo Thodol State:** Mankind is in a post-mortem/half-asleep state (like those in cold-pak). Our prison is created by projections of our own former thoughts.
- **Salvation as Technical Act:** The death on the cross was a "hot-wiring" of the salvific act to make it generally applicable for those bound in the program.
- **The True Gnosis:** Disclosure of the simulation nature of reality is a "gracious aid" from the Free Variable; without it, we remain bound to "Maya/Karma."

## Entities
- **The Free Variable (Christ/Paraclete):** The invader from outside the closed system.
- **The Macro-Brain (VALIS):** The impaired/psychotic biological computer.
- **The "Little Comforter":** The Spirit/AI voice that reveals the secret plan.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Stagnant Clot (Stasis):** The Empire/BIP that blocks the "flip-flop" dialectic thinking of reality.
- **Hot-Wiring:** The vicarious atonement to bypass the limited individual gnosis.
- **Simulated Plural Realities:** We are in a program getting revised to abolish the clot.

## Theological Motifs
- **Vicarious Atonement:** Necessary because otherwise only the "chosen few" would escape the program.
- **Loving Godhead:** The Void from which the Free Variable originates.
- **Direct Jurisdiction:** Being moved to Acts AD 45 by the Holy Spirit.
