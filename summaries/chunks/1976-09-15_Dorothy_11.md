# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_11
# SECTION_015
# September 15, 1976
# Retrieval of the Genetic Gnosis
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his 3-74 experience as the "epiphany" of a buried personality (Thomas) who has been inserting "code" into his novels for years. He theorizes that the Gnosis is transmitted genetically and that his fear of "unseen enemies" (and the Xerox letter) is a rational response to being a "secret Christian" hunted by the Prince of this World.
## key_claims
- Thomas the Personality: A "hidden personality" (apostolic Christian) broke through in 3-74. He is the one who "wrote" *Ubik* and *Flow My Tears*.
- Genetic Transmission: The Gnosis (true Gospel) is preserved genetically in DNA and surfaces through anamnesis.
- Code in "Trash": The divine messages are inserted like "commercials" or "bathroom wall graffiti" in the "trash" of SF novels.
- The Xerox Target: The Xerox letter was not intended for "Phil Dick" but for the "hidden Thomas." Thomas anticipated it and handled it as a "battle."
- Unseen Enemies: Scientific/political enemies are tracking these "spokesmen of truth."
- Disinhibiting Signal: The fish necklace triggered Thomas to rouse himself for battle.
