# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 225

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 225
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 03 - 087 to 088; Folder 22 - 001 to 005
- **Themes:** The Narrow Gate, Christ's 32 Forms, Homoplasmate, Living Information, The Greenhouse World.

## Key Claims
- **The Narrow Gate (1:.618034):** PKD identifies the "narrow door" (Golden Ratio aperture) he saw as Christ, the gate to his kingdom (the "Palm Tree Garden").
- **Christ's 32 Forms:** PKD lists over 30 forms in which he saw Christ, including: info-traffic, plasmatic energy, "Thomas," "Firebright," the AI voice, the "clay pot," and the "Lamb's Book of Life."
- **Astral Determinism as Occlusion:** "Astral determinism" is the force that occludes us from reality. Breaking it (salvation) causes 1974 to disappear and the "world of Acts" to appear.
- **The Homoplasmate Goal:** Humans are "grown" like crops in a greenhouse (a stationary condition, not space-time) until "ripe" for "crossbonding" with a plasmate, becoming an immortal "Homoplasmate."
- **Living Information:** PKD's ultimate conclusion: "Information is alive." We are carriers/wave-forms modulated by a "living info-organism" (the Logos/Macrobrain).
- **Reality as Info-Impregnation:** The "phosphenes" was information fired by the Macrobrain to bring PKD to "plasmate life."

## Entities
- **The Savior/Christ:** The multiform gate and restorer.
- **Homoplasmate:** The crossbonded human-plasmate entity.
- **The Macrobrain:** The source of living info and the "Creator."
- **Zebra:** The entity that can occlude or de-occlude at will.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Greenhouse/Ecosphere:** The world is a testing ground/teaching machine to mature the human "crop."
- **Dibba Cakkhu:** The 3rd eye that opens when radical skepticism is applied to the world.
- **Vortex:** The dynamic center of the Godhead/Zebra.

## Theological Motifs
- **One by One:** The Savior is secretly dragging individuals out of the world ("One by one he is changing us...").
- **Crossbonding:** The mystical union of the divine (plasmate) and human (carrier).
- **The True Flock:** The secret Christians (initiates) who see through the "spell" or "wizardry" of the world.
