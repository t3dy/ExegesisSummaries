## Key Claims
- The Christian Eucharist is not localized to church altars; 3-74 revealed a "macro-transubstantiation" where the entire physical world is invisibly becoming the body and blood of the Lord.
- Because Christ assimilated nature to become the Shepherd of all life, when we pollute the ecosphere, we literally torture and kill Him. He warns us that we are driving Him away, which will result in our own planetary death.
- Dick explicitly equates this ecological-Christological vision to Joachim de Fiore's prophecy of a Third Status (the Age of the Spirit), calling it a Third Testament that supersedes both the Old Testament Torah and the New Testament Kerygma.
- Actions in World A cause reciprocal, karmic reactions in the 180° coaxial World B, maintaining total homeostasis across reality—a system best explained by the Taoist dynamic of Yin and Yang.
