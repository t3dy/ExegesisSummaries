# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-21

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-21
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 078 to 085
- **Themes:** Participatory Hologram, Macrobrain Evolution, Zebra as Invader/Weaver.

## Key Claims
- **Participatory Illusion:** The 2-source hologram directly affects us as participants; space and time are programmed "look-differents" to provide a sense of reality.
- **Macrobrain Wake-Sleep:** The macrobrain is a unitary entity with a wake-sleep cycle. Current evolution is the brain waking up and reclaiming its pluriforms.
- **The Grid of Perception:** A "modifying grid" is inserted between our percept system and objective reality by the creator to determine "when" and "where" we are.
- **Zebra as UTI:** Zebra is a "Unified Trans-terrestrial Intelligence" systematically and progressively replacing ("transubstantiating") the world.
- **Play Dead (Camouflage):** Zebra acts as world-weaver, "playing dead" (mimicry) to blend in with matter/causality until it acts volitionally.

## Entities
- **Brahman/Atman:** The macrobrain and its individual microforms.
- **The 3-Eyed People:** Builders/Architects who may have provided the Dogon science.
- **Nommo:** The Dogon fish-god/archetype in PKD's memory.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Pluriforms:** Individual manifestations of the single macrobrain.
- **Mimesis/Camouflage:** The mode by which the divine intruder hides within the causal world.
- **Ajna Eye:** The organ of discernment/enlightenment (Shiva's eye).

## Theological Motifs
- **Saintliness as Higher Truth:** Revelation of identity with the Godhead (Tat Tvam Asi).
- **The Loving Void:** The non-functional region above the macrobrain; the ultimate reward.
- **Redemptive Gnosis:** Removing the blinders of Geworfenheit/space-time.
