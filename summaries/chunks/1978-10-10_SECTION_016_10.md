# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-10

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-10
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - Page 1 to 6
- **Themes:** 4-Pronged Deformation, Inauthentic Existence, Grace, The Beetle Logic.

## Key Claims
- **The 4-Pronged Deformation (Detailed):** 1) Irreal world, 2) Perceptual occlusion, 3) Deterministic enslavement, 4) Amnesia.
- **Self-Perpetuating System:** The "prison" is built to be self-reinforcing; we cannot know we are occluded or enslaved until after the deformity is removed.
- **Role of the Holy Spirit:** The Spirit lifts the irreal world, reveals our true nature (microisomorphism), and breaks the "inner-outer tape" determinism.
- **Inauthentic Existence:** Everything in the fallen state (world, perception, choice, identity) is a "forgery" calculated by a deluding power.
- **Selective Salvation:** God/Christ chooses his "Little Flock." PKD was "doomed to be saved" since the foundation of the world.

## Entities
- **The Holy Spirit:** The syzygy/symbiotic partner who rouses us from sleep.
- **The Little Flock:** The selected/predestined group of the saved.
- **Ma'at / Osiris:** Egyptian templates for the "closing of the books."
- **Philip:** The specific name called out by the Savior.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Microisomorphism:** The human self as a microform of the macrobrain/God.
- **Two-Track Determinism:** The reflex-machine state of the fallen human.
- **The Great Branching Vine:** A vision of infinite causal paths/teaching.

## Theological Motifs
- **Grace over Merit:** Salvation is a gift (grace) from a perceive-able Savior.
- **Adoption into the Godhead:** The second birth into a homoplasmate.
- **Planery Powers:** The celestial/deterministic forces that enslave.
