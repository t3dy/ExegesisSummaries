# The Urwelt and the Ubiquitous Christ
## concise_summary
Dick discusses the transformation of the "Urwelt" (supralunar reality) and his experience of being a "hollow vessel" for a divine purpose. He distinguishes between the localized Christ of the 1st Advent and the "ubiquitous" (plasmatic) Christ of the 2nd Advent, which he witnessed in 3-74.
## key_claims
- Urwelt Transformation: The intervention in 3-74 took place in the "Urwelt" (supralunar template reality), which then affected our world.
- The Hollow Vessel: Dick feels like a "hollow vessel" used for a transcendent purpose that elevations his meager life into an apotheosis.
- Ubiquity of Christ: 3-74 was a pre-experience of the 2nd Advent, characterized by Christ's ubiquity (being everywhere like lightning) rather than a localized human form.
- Plasmatic Light: He saw Christ everywhere as "thinking plasmatic light."
- The Secret Church: He believes he was chosen for the "secret invisible real church" (Corpus Christi).
- Intercessor vs. Creator: God as creator (James-James) drives the aggregate, but as intercessor (Christ), he intervenes on an individual basis.
