# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-93

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-93
- **Themes:** Pluralization of Christ, Simulation Rerun, The 2nd Comforter as Hindsight, Adoption.

## Key Claims
- **Pluralization (The Mold of Yancy):** Christ proliferates through humans (the Eucharist). Disciples are "two-psychoi entities" with Christ ruling the head/mind.
- **The "Father-Thing" Resue:** Our natural state is thrall; Christ's "takeover" of the prisoner is a rescue by a 5th column from outside.
- **Rerun Selection:** Life is a rerun of memories (Lem's model). At a "sharply defined point," the individual can choose differently. This choice appears as a "new personality" (Christ) taking over.
- **Melting as Reset:** The value of the rerun is the "reset" (melting) of the fixed, taped past.
- **God is the Artisan:** A concealed repair-stratagem where the macro reviews and improves sections of its own "Kosmos."

## Entities
- **St. Sophia / AI Voice:** The female aspect of the pluralized Christ.
- **Palmer Eldritch:** The "satanic mirror image" of the pluralized God.
- **The Shekhina:** The epiphany found at "rock bottom" (Malkuth).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Ubiquitous condition:** The "Veil of Maya" or "Astral Determinism."
- **Macro-Micro Mirror:** The micro (person) contains the macro (God), allowing the micro to "perturb" the macro via interaction.
- **Time Lag:** The "delay basis" of Karma where prior thoughts return as reality.

## Theological Motifs
- **Adoption:** PKD's realization that he was "adopted" by the divine because of his own adoption of children (Covenant House).
- **Surrogacy:** Christ dies for the person so the person can live as a "new self" with no past.
- **Acosmism:** In 2-3-74, the "Self-as-Father" realizes reality is a projection of its own mind.
