# September 15, 1976
# Prophecy, Politics, and the Iron Empire
## concise_summary
Dick interprets his 3-74 experience through the lens of the Book of Daniel, identifying Richard Nixon with the "harsh and grim king" (the Master of Stratagems) whose "Iron Empire" (BIP) was broken by the "Uncut Stone" of God. He sees the "Watergate fall" as a cosmic event he was programmed to witness and help facilitate.
## key_claims
- Daniel's Prophecy Fulfilled: Nixon is identified as the king from Daniel who "succeeds in whatever he does" but is "broken, but not by human hands" (the Uncut Stone).
- The Iron Empire: The Black Iron Prison (BIP) is the 4th Kingdom of Daniel (Rome revived), manifesting as the "USA/USSR as one great prison."
- 3-74 as Breach: The intervention in 3-74 was a "breach" from without, where the "Palm Tree people" (Christ/Heavenly Host) entered the prison to provide "medical assistance" to the innocent.
- Medical Assistance for Violence: Redefines the "medical assistance" requested in his dream; it was not for sickness but for "violent injury done to the innocent" by the demiurgic J-J.
- The Witness: Dick's role was to stand up ("stand up for Jesus' sake") and see the evil historic processes, which made him a target (the Xerox letter/pursuers).
- Victory over the Conspirators: The fall of the "Watergate gang" was the destruction of the "absolute evil incarnate" by the "absolute good" of the Christian underground.
- Initiated Eyesight: Seeing Zebra was a temporary "grace" (initiation) allowed for the duration of the final confrontation between Nixon and the divine.
