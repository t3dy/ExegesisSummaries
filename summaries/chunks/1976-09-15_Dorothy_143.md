# The Joker God and the Trash Heap of Clues
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the "Yang" principle (Zebra/Christ) as a "persuading" force that redirects the outcomes of the rigid, "Yin" world of cause and effect. He describes himself as a "sharp-eyed crow" or a "childish" naive seeker who finds the divine in the "trash" and "junk jewels" of reality. He posits that salvation involves "wresting loose" the soul from the Artifact's (Yin) possession and bonding it to the authentic Yang reality.
## key_claims
- Persuasion vs. Coercion: Yang does not destroy Yin but "persuades" it (re-contours its outcomes). Yin represents the "immutable world of cause and effect."
- The Toymaker Gnosis: God is a "Joker God" (Dionysos/Hephaistos) who hides in the trash; a naive, "childish" mind is best suited to find Him "hidden in plain sight."
- Two Laminated Worlds: We don't have one world with two sources, but "two worlds laminated together" which must be unscrambled.
- Salvation as Transfer of Possession: To be "saved" is to be pried loose from the Yin and "bonded" (laminated) to the Yang.
- Pattern Recognition as Method: PKD's "try it all" technique (shaking coin-operated doors and God) is a high-speed search for the one correct "pattern" hidden in the mercurial flux of reality.
