# The Secret Feminine Nature of Christ
## concise_summary
Dick analyzes a "Medieval Diptych" dream revealing that Christ's inner nature is feminine ("SHE" / "SECRET" / "St. Sophia"). He treats the human Jesus as a "puppet" (Pinocchio) animated by this female spirit and discusses the "sacred secret" of esoteric Christianity.
## key_claims
- The Diptych Dream:
    - Right panel: Delphic Sibyl (Inner nature) labeled "SHE" and "SECRET."
    - Left panel: Pinocchio/Puppet (model of human) worked from above.
    - Bottom: Trigram Ch'ien (Creative Masculinity).
- Christ as Syzygy: A binary entity consisting of a feminine divine animating spirit (St. Sophia) and a masculine human "puppet" (Jesus).
- Secret of the Logos: The Spirit of God (Paraclete) is Haggia Sophia. This insight is to be kept SECRET (injunction from the dream).
- Quaternity: Proposes a quaternity (Trinity + 4th person: man Jesus).
- Immortality of the Man: If the spirit conferred immortality on Jesus (resurrection), then the syzygy is eternal and lives on in the "supralunar world."
