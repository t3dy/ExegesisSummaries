# The Star Wars Revelation: Jedi Knights in Fullerton
## concise_summary
In a profound pivot, Dick interprets his entire life and 3-74 experience through the lens of George Lucas's *Star Wars*. He identifies Christ as "Ben Kenobi" (a dead but omnipresent guide), himself as a "Jedi Knight," and the Nixon administration as the "Empire/Death Star."
## key_claims
- Star Wars as Non-Fiction: Dick asserts *Star Wars* is "definitive" and "fully and accurately explicates the actual situation underlying our world."
- Christ/Ben Kenobi: Christ is the "dead" Jedi Knight who speaks into the minds of the rebels ("Remember the Force"). He is the "all-pervasive energy field" (Zebra).
- The Empire/Nixon: The Nixon administration rotted the "Old Republic" from within, exactly as Palpatine did. The Vietnam War and police state identify the Empire.
- Darth Vader/Palmer Eldritch: The evil super-entity (Vader) is the same "Black Iron" presence Dick saw as Palmer Eldritch.
- Jedi Knights = Rebel Christians: The "illegal Christians" of 70 AD and the "Rebels" of Star Wars are the same group of "Sons of Light."
- Anamnesis of the Blowup: Dick recalls a "success" where "we" (the Knights) blew up the Iron Citadel (Death Star). 3-74 was the culmination of this victory.
- Force as Brahma: The "Force" is the Brahman/Zebra energy that "envelopes and controls" reality.
- The 22-Year Mission: Dick's novels (1951-1974) were "John the Baptist" tasks: negative preparation (questioning reality) to make way for the "Force."
