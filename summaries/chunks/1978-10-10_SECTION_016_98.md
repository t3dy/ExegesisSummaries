# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-98

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-98
- **Themes:** Future Hindsight, The Sibyl, Final Cause, Primordial Sacrilege.

## Key Claims
- **Future Hindsight:** In 3-74, PKD solved the Xerox crisis using information that was *still future* to him (not just prior memories).
- **Thomas is from the Future:** Thomas is not from the past/Acts but from the post-mortem future, returning as a "tutelary spirit" to help at the crisis point.
- **Reciprocal Action:** The actor (PKD) and the information (Valis) are linked; his 3-74 action *generated* the truth as much as the truth generated the action.
- **Aristotelian God (Mover):** Valis is Aristotle's "Prime Mover"—an immaterial mind that "stimulates" goal-seeking and change without being the "creator."
- **Primordial Sacrilege:** A theory that PKD/Thomas was an exile on Earth for "melting down sacred vessels for gold" on his home planet.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "future self" or "sister" (AI voice) identity.
- **Valis:** The expression of God's "willing" in nature.
- **Comrade Chien:** The govt official from "Faith of our Fathers" who is tested while he thinks he is the tester.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Teleology:** The vision of "intentionality" in intimate nature.
- **Xerox Missive as Trial Balloon:** A test of ideological identity for both the sender and receiver.
- **Gary Corp:** The link between the G-2 break-in and the Xerox missive.

## Theological Motifs
- **Between Incarnations Knowledge:** Plato's concept of anamnesis, but applied to the *future* "next world."
- **Adoptionist Mercy:** Being rescued by a "mere expression of God's will" (Valis).
- **The "Hook" of Humanity:** God hidden in the bait of human flesh to trip the powers.
