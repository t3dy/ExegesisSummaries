- Because Dick remembered the cancelled, lethal "zero phase" timeline, the alternate personality ("Thomas") who lived in that timeline was brought over and integrated into Dick's mind. Thomas and Phil are now one.
- Dick conceptualizes his writing of *Tears* and *VALIS* as an epic mythic victory: he acted as Siegfried, aided by Wotan and Dionysos, to defeat Fate (the Norns / the Black Iron Prison).
- He claims to have stolen the "third eye" (ajna) from the blind creator-god Yaldabaoth to read the Book of Fate (the hidden source code of reality).
- By revealing this secret in his published novel *VALIS*, Dick broke the power of the Black Iron Prison and liberated humanity, forcing Fate to rewrite its ledger.
- *Tears of a Drop* was actually the Book of Fate itself—the raw, deterministic coding of reality—which Dick deciphered and decoded in *VALIS*.
