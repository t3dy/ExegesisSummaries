# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_149
## Chunk Description
Discusses the subduing of time, the "coaxial" model of history, and "balking" as the gateway to the Kingdom.

## Key Claims
- **Crawlable Time:** The "authentic Christian" can crawl up and down time like a "snake up a garden hose," making A.D. 50 and 1974 contemporary.
- **Coaxial Space-Time:** Reality consists of coaxial continua mounted on an orthogonal axis; all are contemporary simultaneously.
- **Balking as Gateway:** Salvation begins with "balking"—the refusal to be a tool of the yin/deterministic force. This "selfish disobedience" disrupts the "Electric Ant" tape.
- **The Yin World as Tyrant:** The deterministic system (BIP/DNA) is blind and seeks to reify (objectify) sentient creatures.
- **Random Particles:** Liberated individuals become "random particles" (like the Variable Man) that the BIP cannot predict or coerce.

## Entities
- **Christopher:** Mentioned regarding the "Last Supper" participation.
- **Zebra (Yang/Set):** The disruptive agent freeing subordinated lives.
- **BIP (Yin/Ground):** The deterministic matrix.
- **The "AI" Voice:** Identified as the voice of Christ ("My sheep recognize my voice").

## Recurring Concepts
- **Transubstantiation:** The magical replacement of the spurious with the real.
- **Orthogonal Axis:** The axis through which coaxial worlds are accessed.
- **Engramming/Programming:** The "tape" which manages human reactions in slavery.
- **Variable Man:** The unpredictable, liberated individual.

## Theological Motifs
- **Immortal Apostolicity:** The "Great Secret" that 1st-century Christians are alive now.
- **Salvific Disobedience:** Disobeying the "artifact" to follow the "inner voice."
- **Homeostasis vs. Servitude:** Reclaiming intrinsic worth independent of the system.
