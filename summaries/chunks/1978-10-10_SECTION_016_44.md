# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-43

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-43
- **Themes:** Evolution of the Will, Ontogeny vs Phylogeny, Brahman Awake, The Holographic memory.

## Key Claims
- **The Miracle of 3-74:** The impersonal will (phylogeny) became bonded to the conscious ego (ontogeny) to defend it. This is "Christ" - making the will conscious.
- **Brahman Awakened:** This is not Atman realizing it is Brahman, but Brahman (the world-soul) itself waking up. "Siddhartha no longer sleeps."
- **Self-Cancelling Reality:** Reality is so irrational and self-contradictory that no rational analysis holds; it pulsates in oscillations we don't normally see.
- **Anamnesis as Frame-Bridge:** Thomas and PKD co-habited one brain because they were "just an instant before" one another; anamnesis opened the bridge between these presents.
- **The "Staying Awake" Trick:** Immortality is achieved by the early Christian "trick" of staying awake (maintaining identity-continuity) as the mind moves from frame to frame.

## Entities
- **Siddhartha (Thomas):** The awakened avatara/world-soul.
- **Atman/Brahman:** The individual soul and the universal spirit.
- **The Ursein Individual:** The constant that is reconstituted in every "playing card" (nanosecond flip-flop) of reality.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Ontogeny/Phylogeny:** The individual life vs. the species/spirit life.
- **Flip-flop Frames:** The nanosecond units of reality that we normally forget.
- **The 1.618034 Constant:** The "Golden Section" that represents the recurring real.

## Theological Motifs
- **Birth from Above:** The fusion of the personal and impersonal as a victory over death.
- **The Risen Pantocrator:** The Christ who spans all frames and is disclosed through anamnesis.
- **The Bardo Thodol:** Our world as an after-life/dream state where we are "half dead" and deluded by our own thought-projections.
