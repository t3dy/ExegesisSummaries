# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_25
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_25
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Divine Illumination, The Recovery of the Eide, Augustine and Plato, The Hermetic Transform
- **Key Entities**: Plato, Augustine, Pythagoras

## Key Claims
- Dick finally understands what he meant when he cried "I am no longer blind!" in 1974: he experienced the **Divine Illumination** originally described by Plato and baptized by Augustine.
- Under this Divine Light, the temporal accidents of reality vanish, and the eternal, intelligible structure of the universe (Plato's *eide* / Archetypal Forms) becomes directly visible in the mind.
- This represents a restoration of the cognitive faculties mankind lost at the Fall (referencing *The First Book of Adam and Eve*). Anamnesis is not remembering the past, but recovering the unfallen state of direct spiritual perception.
- He links Malebranche's "vision in God," Plato's *anamnesis*, and Augustine's "Divine Illumination" into a single unified event triggered by the indwelling Christ/Logos.
- He compares this state to the Hermetic/Alchemical transform (the birth of the Golden Flower in the mind), where the perceiver assimilates the world conceptually, resting outside of space and time like *Adam Kadmon*.

## Entities
- **Plato**: His concept of *anamnesis* is reframed as a recovery of unfallen cognitive abilities.
- **Augustine**: Crucial for equating Plato's Divine Light with the indwelling Christ/Logos that illuminates the mind.
- **Pythagoras**: Referenced for defining reality as pure mathematical/conceptual structure ("the harmonious fitting-together of the beautiful") rather than material substance.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Eide (Forms)**: The permanent, timeless conceptual arrangements of reality that Dick perceived, replacing the flux of phenomenal causality.
- **Adam Kadmon**: The cosmic human integrating all of macrocosmic reality into their own conceptual mind.

## Theological Motifs
- **Simultaneous Locality**: Because the Forms are outside time and space, seeing the eternal essence means one is simultaneously seeing 1974 USA and AD 70 Acts—they are the same Form.
