# The Aggregate Message: Becoming the Ubiquitous God
## concise_summary
Dick synthesizes the messages of his core novels (*Ubik*, *Stigmata*, *Maze*) into a single Gnostic revelation: we are in a mass-hallucinated prison, and a ubiquitous deity (Ubik/Zebra) is "invading" us through information to turn us into Himself (Theolepsy).
## key_claims
- The Aggregate Message: (1) *Maze*: Prison world hidden by amnesia. (2) *Stigmata*: Invasion by a ubiquitous deity via the host (Theolepsy). (3) *Ubik*: Salvific information penetrating world-walls.
- Theolepsy as Invasion: *Stigmata* describes how consuming the "host" makes the imbiber *become* the deity. We are currently undergoing a "mass theolepsy" where Christ (Zebra) is invading the species collectively.
- Ubiquity: Ubik, Zebra, and Palmer Eldritch are all names for the same ubiquitous, reality-supporting force.
- Altering History: The one element missing from his books but present in 3-74 is the "explicit altering of the historical process" (Nixon's fall).
- Zebra as External Presence: Zebra was not just a mental state; it was visible externally in his room, ruling out "Soviet microwave" theories.
- The Mimicking Entity: The higher entity uses camouflage to remain invisible. Viewing it required "Thomas" (the ETI-lens) inside him.
- Gnostic Christianity: The "Gnosis" revealed to him is that *Ortho-Christian* prophecies (Paraclete/Return) are actually true, but in an esoteric way (Zen-governed).
