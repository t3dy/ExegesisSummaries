# Chunk 215 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_215

## Description
PKD synthesizes the "Inner Space" experience of 3-74 with the Hermetic theories of Paracelsus and the "Push-Pull" binary mirror model of reality. He concludes that the the human microcosm and the macrocosm are "Isomorphic Twins" capable of perfect dialogue.

## Key Claims
- **Subjective Space-Binding**: PKD’s agoraphobia and "difficulty moving through space" are seen as a result of his space being primarily a "subjective reality" that retracts when he is anxious.
- **The Two Mirrors**: Reality is a binary system where the Psyche ("Push") projects a world and the Matrix ("Pull") mirrors it back enhanced. This forms a closed loop of "belief vs. effect."
- **Isomorphism**: Any micro-bit of the macro-organism recapitulates the totality. Man can retrieve the entire wisdom of the macro-mind because he is "isomorphic" with it.
- **The Contraction of God**: As man (the microform) expands himself through wisdom, God (the macroform) contracts himself to become a human figure (often seen as a woman), allowing them to meet as equals.

## Entities
- **Paracelsus / Thomas**: The "Older and Wiser" mind that replaced PKD's "puny" worldview in 3-74 and provided the "vast vistas of inner space."
- **The Void / Abyss / Urgrund**: The final non-being to which we return once Karma ends; the level beyond even "God" and "Process."
- **Zebra**: The plasmatic energy that "de-substantiates" the physical world to show it was never real.
- **Krishna**: Cited for the realization: "I am not here. Neither am I elsewhere."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Inner Firmament**: The "magic mirror" of the macrocosm situated inside the human mind.
- **Push-Pull System**: The non-causal synchronization of "Inner and Outer Tapes" coordinated by a clock (Time).
- **Syntonic Superimposition**: The ability of all places and all times to exist as aspects of the omnitemporal/omnispacial macro-brain.
- **The Hour You’ve Waited For**: The realization that Christ is latent ("sleeping") in all of us and is now aroused through the phylogenic layers.

## Theological Motifs
- **Hand-in-Hand with God**: The mystical goal of the microcosm and macrocosm meeting as harmonized equals.
- **Acosmic Gnosticism**: The belief that the physical world is "not here" and that substantiality is an illusion.
- **The Great Void**: Level 9 of reality—the "not-is" which is incomparably better than the layers of existence.
- **Enlightenment through Assent**: The "Poof" moment where the world disappears when the psyche withdraws its belief in the simulation.
