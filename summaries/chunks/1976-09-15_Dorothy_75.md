# The Universal Reconstitution: From Cells to Macro-Organism
## concise_summary
Dick proposes a "holographic" or "fractal" model of divinity: just as a single cell contains the blueprint for the whole human, each human psyche contains the information to reconstitute the "macro proto-organism" (Christ/Vine). Evolution is the process of these "cuttings" growing back into identity with their parent source.
## key_claims
- Microanalogs of the Macro: Every human psyche is "topologically congruent" with the macro proto-organism. We are "stations" in a vast incorporeal energy-body.
- The Re-constitutable God: If the macro-organism were destroyed, it could be "reconstituted" from any single "micro-station" (individual psyche) containing its coded information.
- Pruned Cuttings (Replication): Humans were "pruned" from the Great Vine (Christ) as seedlings to grow on their own. Evolution is the successful development of these cuttings into counterparts of the parent.
- Teleological Firing: Neural "meta-circuits" (activated in 3-74) allow the mind to perceive its "end-state" (Omega Point). This "summons" the individual forward from the future.
- ISOMORPHISM: Salvation is achieving "isomorphic parity" (shape-identity) with the Divine Progenitor (The Vine).
- Deprogramming from the Past: 3-74 was a "quantum jump" in homeostasis, erasing engramming from the past (3 billion years of evolution) and replacing it with "tropisms" from the future.
- The Universe as Teaching Machine: Reality is a didactic construct designed to educate the "pupils" (humans). In 3-74, Dick met the "Teacher" (Zebra) and "graduated" to a state of direct control bypassing the machine.
- The Goal of Learning: The universe is an "artifact of reality" (made by the Teacher). Our sense of the world's "irreality" is a sign that we are searching for the Teacher outside it.
