# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-136

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-136
- **Themes:** Spinoza's Monism, Natura Naturans vs. Naturata, Absolute Will, Pretextual Cause as Signage.

## Key Claims
- **Spinoza as the Secret Hero:** PKD identifies 2-3-74 as a direct perception of God as Spinoza understood him (*Deus sive natura*). To have an intellectual understanding of this is to become part of God.
- **Natura Naturans (The Real):** Valis is identified as the *natura naturans*—the active, creative "Will of God" or "Natural Law" which is sentient and volitional. The *natura naturata* (physical objects/epiphenomena) are secondary results.
- **Unity vs. Teleology:** PKD realizes he was confusing "unity" with "teleology." 3-74 was nature "turning into God" (revealing its unitary basis) rather than a separate being moving toward a goal.
- **Pretextual Cause defined:** What he called "pretextual cause" (like the fish sign) is actually a notification system between parts of a single unitary whole. The "signal" is part of a panpsychic language.
- **Absolute Will (Valence):** The *natura naturans* realm exerts an inexorable "valence" on the *naturata* (human modes). In 3-74, PKD had "no free will" because he was following the "warped path" of God's absolute mind.

## Entities
- **Valis / God:** The *natura naturans* (Active Nature).
- **Spinoza:** The philosopher who predicted PKD's experience.
- **The Xerox Missive:** An information transaction within the "Total System."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Deus sive natura:** God or Nature (identically).
- **Valence:** The gravitational-like pull of God's will on matter/mind.
- **Phagocytosis / Phosphene Graphics:** The process of the "System" reorganizing its modes (humans) into information.

## Theological Motifs
- **Karmic Law Overruled:** The intelligent *natura naturans* uses its own laws to "overrule" individual karmic debt/sin, not by suspending law but by using it deftly (Pronoia).
- **Theophany of Structure:** Seeing God as the "Structure/Information" of the cosmos rather than an external person.
- **Real Presence:** The universe as the body of God.
