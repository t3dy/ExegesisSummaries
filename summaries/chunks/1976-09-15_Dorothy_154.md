# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_154
## Chunk Description
Links *Ubik* to the blocking of information and discusses the "Bait" theory of the incarnation.

## Key Claims
- **Ubik as Negentropic Sentience:** *Ubik* depicts the unoccluded state of the world soul, where meaningful messages are detected in "trash."
- **Blocking Agent:** Humans have a "blocking agent" (occlusion) preventing them from seeing the Palm Tree Garden/Zebra.
- **Neural Meta-Circuits:** "They" (the macro-minds) placed neural circuits in our brains and are now disinhibiting them to restore our lost faculties.
- **Bait Theory:** God (St. Sophia/Christ) enters the world in humble disguise to act as "bait" for Satan/The Artifact to grasp.
- **Admixture of Reality:** Our world is an "admixture" of Satan's malicious spurious world (BIP) and God's authentic creation (Palm Tree Garden).

## Entities
- **Ubik/Zebra:** The negentropic, sentient, immortal entity in control.
- **William Burroughs:** Cited regarding "information parasites" and "Nova police."
- **Satan:** The "Ape of God" who interpolates spurious creations.
- **St. Sophia (Hagia Sophia):** The world-soul mediatress.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Spurious Interpolations:** False sections of reality inserted by the "Ape of God."
- **Bait the Bait:** Christ's humble form as a trap for the adversary.
- **Neural Disinhibition:** The "unveiling" of suppressed brain circuits.
- **The "Ticket That Exploded":** Burroughs' novel as a parallel Exegesis text.

## Theological Motifs
- **Feminine Wisdom:** Identifying the "still small voice" as the feminine Thinking World Soul.
- **Cancer Analogy:** The irrational/evil part of the universe as a metastatic cancer Sophia must fix.
- **Restoration of Faculties:** The end-times marked by the removal of the information block.
