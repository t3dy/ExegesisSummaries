# Chunk 171 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_171

## Description
PKD elaborates on the "Teaching Machine" model of reality, where our world is a simulation designed to test whether we can detect its artificiality. He frames "Moral Balking" as the key act of disobedience to the "programmed stimulus-response" of the prison system.

## Key Claims
- **Simulation Test**: Reality is a "puzzle" or "problem" posed by a benign mind; failing to "catch on" means living in the simulation forever.
- **Moral Balking**: Disobeying a "programmed stimulus" because it is "improper" is the act that breaks the inner-outer tape and proves one is "born from above."
- **Acts as the Real World**: When the "astrally determined simulation" is broken, the world of *Acts* appears because it is the locus where the Holy Spirit (the Liberator) entered history.
- **Expendable Personalities**: Both the "surface PKD personality" and the "USA 1974 world" are scathingly dismissed by the divine voice as "expendable" alibis.

## Entities
- **Lem**: Reference to Stanislaw Lem's theories about brains in vats/simulated reality.
- **Klingsor**: The "Iron Castle" magician metaphor for the ensnaring world.
- **Zebra / VALIS / Holy Spirit**: The liberating power that induces "malfunctions" in the simulation to expose it.
- **Thomas**: The personality linked to the "upper, discretionary realm."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Inner-Outer Correspondence**: The constant reinforcement of the simulation through feedback loops.
- **Astral Power**: The coercive power that compels "perceptual and cognitive assent" to reality.
- **Bardo Thodol**: Reference to the 3-74 experience as a death segment in the tape.
- **Disjunctive from Not-Him**: The goal of the test is for man to become a distinct, discretionary being.

## Theological Motifs
- **Acosmic Gnosticism**: The view that the physical world does not reveal God but instead conceals him as a mask.
- **The Serious Guessing Game**: Life as a high-stakes puzzle where one must choose the "invisible landscape" and face punishment from the "palpable" one.
- **Deity in Disguise**: In the lower realm, God appears in "debased or trivial" forms (commercials, sick tomcats).
