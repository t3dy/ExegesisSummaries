# The Micro-Cosmos Seed: Rebuilding Reality from Within
## concise_summary
Dick concludes that 3-74 was the planting of a "Micro-Cosmos Seed" (Thomas/Telos) within him to replace the "Scramble World" of the Empire. He views his writing and experiences as "world-creation," where the "Acts" world is the staging ground for the final overthrow of Satan/Nixon.
## key_claims
- Micro-Cosmos Seed: Thomas is a "telos seed" placed in Dick (atelos) to grow into a microcosmic version of Valis.
- World-Creation: Melting the causal trains in 3-74 was an act of "world-creation." Dick's perception of "Acts" is the creation of a "domain of beauty" that replaces the "Scramble."
- Satan is Overthrown: Dick asserts that in 1974, "we Christs" (The Rebels) overthrew the "obscure dominion of Satan" (Nixon/Empire).
- The Workshop of Acts: "Acts" (The Biblical world) is the "workshop" where true Christians perform their transactions with evil. It is where the two sides "engage each other openly."
- Future-Ancient Hybrid: Reality is a technologically sophisticated "future slave state" (The Empire) that the seed (Valis) is devouring.
- Plasmate as Christ's Blood: The "Plasmate" (Living Information) is the blood of the cosmic Christ/Organism growing as it feeds on the "helpless atelos" (The irrational Empire).
- We Control the World: Dick claims that "We [the Rebels] control this world now." St. Sophia will emerge everywhere, in everyone.
- Departure from Empirical Reality: Truth departs from empirical "copy" reality for long enough to observe the "correction" by the original Logos (Blueprint).
