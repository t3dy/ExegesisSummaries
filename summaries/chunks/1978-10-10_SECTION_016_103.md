# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-103

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-103
- **Themes:** Future Mind Intervention, The Binary Flip-Flop, Computer Program Reset, The Gray Corporation.

## Key Claims
- **Future Self to the Rescue:** The "Paraclete" is the person's own "future mind" coming back from the real world to the "archaic memory-world" to ensure the right choice (Salvation).
- **Rerun with Variables:** We are on a computer program being "rerun and reset" with new variables (Info). 3-74 was a "change of variables" introduced from outside.
- **The Gray Corporation:** A dream symbol representing the "G-2/Gary Powers" intelligence operation that "broken in" (the 1971 hit) to plant evidence.
- **Valis as Verification:** The existence of Valis *is* the proof that the program has been "broken open" by new information.
- **Memory Recirculation:** The brain (God) recirculates its memories (Karma) but has the power to alter its own past by changing choices at "nexi."

## Entities
- **The Paraclete:** The "divine brother" / future version of the self.
- **Ensof / Shekhina:** The absolute and its indwelling presence.
- **Gary Powers:** The pilot as a cipher for the "simulacrum/forgery" of the Xerox missive.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Phagocyte Agent:** The mind in microform attacking the "BIP" (ossified thoughts).
- **Two-Tape Programming:** The deterministic nature of the "archaic" taped past.
- **Binary Flip-Flop:** The infinite logic generated by the computer-like system.

## Theological Motifs
- **Refining Creation:** God correcting the error of the universe from "outside time."
- **Zoroastrian Battle:** The confrontation with the "Dru" (Lye) where the person now wins with divine help.
- **Reset:** The introduction of "new info" (Christ) into a closed program.
