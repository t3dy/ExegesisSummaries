# Ubik as Scripture and the Gnostic Conquest of Rome
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his Bantam novels (*Ubik*, *Maze*, *Tears*) as vehicles of revelation and "scripture," arguing that *Ubik* contains an original cosmology that explains its own source. He reflects on the "Form axis" and "orthogonal time" from *Ubik* as keys to understanding how 1974 USA was actually Rome A.D. 70. He declares that the secret Christian Church finally conquered "Rome" (Nixon) in 1974, and the "King" (Zebra) is now here in discorporate form awaiting his final return.
## key_claims
- Ubik-as-Scripture: The novel *Ubik* was effectively written *by* the entity Ubik through automatic writing, making it a form of revelation.
- Conquest of Rome: Unlike Constantine's era, 1974 was the "genuine" conquest of Rome by the secret church, creating a vacuum for the King.
- Orthogonal Time-Axis: The "Form axis" from *Ubik* is a real cosmological feature that allows prior versions of an eidos (like Rome A.D. 70) to accrue and manifest.
- Entropic vs. Organizing Force: *Ubik* depicts the battle between entropy (form-regression) and the Logos/Sophia (form-stabilization).
- Runciter as Agent: The character Runciter is an agent for the ubiquitous Ubik, just as PKD is an agent for the Cosmic Christ.
