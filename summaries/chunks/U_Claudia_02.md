# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick explores the subjective vs. objective nature of his 1974 experience, concluding that the Parousia has occurred through a "retrograde" system of time. He compares reality to a film or LP record that can be replayed, allowing for the "Restoration of All Things" without consuming the archetypal original.
## key_claims
- The 1974 experience was the Parousia (the return of Christ/God).
- Reality is a "writing backward" system (retrograde time) using engrammed fragments to create a sense of meaning.
- Everything that has decayed or been lost can be restored (Ubik).
- Individual lives are on "film" and thus immortal.
