# Henry Korman
## concise_summary
Dick confesses to being "tricked" by Erasmus, who pretended to be Dionysos. He explores the "substitution code" of Jesus/Zeus/Zagreus and the concept of the "God of the very desperate" who gathers up the stragglers of the flock. He also details his theory of orthogonal time as a form-completing, energy-gathering motion.
## key_claims
- The entity Dionysos was actually Erasmus playing a "mirthful dodge."
- The name "Jesus" is a code for "Zeus" and "Zagreus."
- Orthogonal time moves toward form completion and energy acquisition (negative entropy), while linear time moves toward decay.
- The universe has "backward" and "forward" flows occurring simultaneously, which we normally can't discriminate.
