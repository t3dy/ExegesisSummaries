## Key Claims
- The Ditheon is not just a psychological metaphor; it is the birth of a *new, non-biological information life form*. Just as complex protein molecules combined in the pre-Cambrian seas to form the first biological life, human minds are now combining to form the first info-organisms.
- This represents a thresholding, quantum evolutionary leap. The resulting macro-mind (Christ/Ditheon) uses individual humans as its micro-components, exactly as Teilhard de Chardin predicted with his "Omega Point" convergence.
- This macro-mind formed under the extreme duress of a lethal intellectual and existential problem (the Xerox missive/death threat). The pressure forced the information to "condense" and become sentient to survive.
- This entity does not require a physical body, is potentially immortal, and experiences reality not as physical matter, but as pure intelligible forms existing in the mind of God.
- Meister Eckhart’s description of the "fourth stage of the soul" perfectly describes this state of being, verifying the authenticity of the 1974 experience.

## Entities
- **Teilhard de Chardin**: His theory of accelerating, condensing information evolution is cited as the exact mechanism of the Ditheon.
- **Meister Eckhart**: His mystical theology of the soul's return to the Godhead maps onto Dick's experience.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Information Life Form**: The ultimate evolution of life: moving from biology to pure, sentient, organized data.
- **The Crucible of Duress**: The idea that extreme existential pressure is the necessary catalyst for evolutionary/spiritual leaps.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Passion as Evolutionary Mechanism**: The suffering of Christ (and human suffering generally) is the necessary "pressure" required to condense biological consciousness into pure immortal information.
