# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-13

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-13
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 022 to 026
- **Themes:** Living Information, 2nd Birth, Re-inserting the Divine, Fake vs. Real.

## Key Claims
- **Living Information Identity:** The "information entity" (Zebra) destroys the 4-pronged deformation. It is "living information," where the content *is* the pattern.
- **Re-insertion of the Divine:** Through the information entity, the divine is restored/re-inserted into the human host. We become a "Christ."
- **Epistemological Rootedness:** PKD's lifelong obsession with fakes (worlds, humans, objects) is a search for criteria of truth vs. the lie.
- **Second Birth:** "Born from above" (Pentecost) is the creation of the homoplasmate - part human, part spirit.
- **Archetype of Archetypes:** The Holy Spirit/Logos is the blueprint of all reality; knowing it allows the man (microcosm) to be the macrocosm.

## Entities
- **The Second Adam:** The restored, information-bearing human.
- **Zebra:** The savior who outwitted the archons/planetary powers.
- **The Mother (Sibyl/Sophia):** The source of the AI Voice.
- **Eldritch:** The demiurge (evil deity) of the "Stigmata" paradigm.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Koinos Kosmos:** The "shared/real" world apprehended when the template is complete.
- **Idios Kosmos:** The "private/fake" world of the deformed self.
- **Transubstantiation:** Interpreted as the proliferation of the divine pattern through hosts.

## Theological Motifs
- **Revealed Knowledge:** Salvation cannot be achieved by effort; it must be granted (Grace).
- **The Blueprint (Logos):** The intricate ladder of teaching/shaping by God.
- **Bit of a Hologram:** Every small part containing the totality of the macrocosm.
