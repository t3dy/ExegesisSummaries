# Chunk 214 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_214

## Description
PKD proposes a "Host-Chain" succession where the apostolic Christian (Thomas) used Paracelsus as a host, who in turn uses PKD. He analyzes the "Internal Firmament" from Colin Wilson’s work to explain how inner spaces can act as mirrors of the macrocosm.

## Key Claims
- **The Nested Personality**: 1. Thomas (Apostolic) -> 2. Paracelsus (Renaissance) -> 3. PKD (Modern). Thomas is the "matrix secret" passed down through the centuries.
- **Amnesia and Rebirth**: The "immortal apostolic Christian" remains latent in the human host until "anamnesis" (Magic Memory) is achieved, at which point they take control.
- **Internal Firmament**: Following Paracelsus and Colin Wilson, PKD argues that "imagination" is an explosive flare that reveals meanings and spaces *inside* us that are topologically congruent to the universe.
- **The Goal of Alchemy**: The true goal of the Renaissance adepts was successful spiritual regeneration and immortality (individuation) using the "lost oral gnosis" of Christ.

## Entities
- **Paracelsus**: The the "magician" and chemotherapy pioneer who died mysteriously at 48 and whose "spirit" masterminded PKD’s writing.
- **Thomas**: The "Matrix Personality" who is aware of all the human hosts in his chain.
- **Beethoven**: An example of the "evolving of man" into higher homeostasis and complexity.
- **Colin Wilson**: Author whose work on "The Occult" helped PKD correlate his experience with Paracelsus.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Magic Memory (Anamnesis)**: The core technique used by the "Invisible College" to influence future history.
- **Inner Space/Inner Time**: The realization that the "subjective reality" themed in PKD's fiction is actually a correct perception of the universe as "Inner Firmament."
- **Astral Determinism**: The "stars and planets" control (Karma) which Paracelsus famously rejected in favor of free "Imagination."
- **Host-Person Fusion**: The temporary "unity" during 3-74 where the latent immortal personality (Thomas/Paracelsus) and the modern host (PKD) become one.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Savior's Return**: The ultimate goal the Brotherhood is preparing for; they are "greeting him" as they live on through their hosts.
- **The Phagocyte vs. Rome**: The perpetual war against "Imperial entropic leveling" (The BIP).
- **Physical Resurrection**: The belief that after physical death, the adept will "reawaken" in a later person to continue the work.
- **Meaning over Law**: A "law" is rigid/mechanical (Entropy), while a "meaning" is something put there by the Noos (Logos).
