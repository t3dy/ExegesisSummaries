# Magic Memory and the Pons Dei
## concise_summary
Dick discusses "magic memory" (anamnesis) as the key to immortality and divine wisdom. He introduces the "Pons Dei" (Bridge of God) as the connection between the human mind and the divine mind, activated by the recovery of lost or suppressed knowledge.
## key_claims
- Magic Memory: A type of memory that transcends time and personal biography, connecting the individual to the "total knowledge" of God.
- Pons Dei: An ontological bridge that allows the human to cross over from the simulation to the divine realm.
- Christ as Microform: Christ is the "microform" of the divine wisdom that fits into the human mind to trigger this bridge.
- Recovery of Truth: Immortality is achieved by remembering one's true divine origin.
