# Chunk 200 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_200

## Description
PKD summarizes his exegesis as the record of a "Deliberate Irruption" by God into human history. He frames the events of 1974 as a "Glorious War" where the divine "Phagocyte" (mystical body of Christ) attacked the "Pathogen" (Satan/BIP) to restore absolute time.

## Key Claims
- **Historical Irruption**: The 1974 events were a planned "raid" on USA history by God, using secret, immortal apostolic Christians latent in certain humans.
- **The Phagocyte vs. The Pathogen**: The "mystical body of Christ" (Brain/Phagocyte) is an active, militant force replacing the universe of blind determinism.
- **Acts as Eternity**: The world of Acts signifies "eternal" or "transtemporal" time. Absolute time on Earth "stopped" in A.D. 45 and resumed in 1974 when the two realms "joined" again.
- **USA 1974 as Rome CAD 45**: There is only one real world (Acts), and USA 1974 is merely a "way of viewing" it during the period of illusion.

## Entities
- **The Phagocyte**: The divine collective (Apostolic Christians/Brain) that "eats" or dismantles the entropic BIP.
- **The Pathogen**: Satan / The Black Iron Prison / The Nixon Tyranny.
- **Thomas**: The agent "installed" in PKD to facilitate his role in the 1974 irruption.
- **Dante**: Cited for the concept that "time in heaven is A.D. 45," while time on Earth is a relative construct.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Absolute Time**: The timeline that "stops" and "starts" based on the divine presence; the 1900-year gap is not recognized by the divine.
- **Syntonic Melding**: The two realms (Heaven/Earth or Acts/USA) superimposed as a single composite reality.
- **Obsessive Paradigm**: PKD recognizes his lifelong writing about "World X hiding World Y" as a preparation for this unfolding revelation.
- **The End-Time Things**: The realization that he is a "witness" to the 2nd Advent or the final "inbreaking" of the divine.

## Theological Motifs
- **The 2nd Incarnation**: The entry of God into the temporal order through "noble men" (apostolic agents).
- **The Inbreaking of Reality**: Reality (Acts) "irrupts" into the delusion (1974), much like a theophany.
- **The Lamb's Book of Life**: The conviction of divine selection and the "promise" of being taken into the absolute realm upon death.
- **Lost Secret**: The "Return" has happened, but it is a "secret" only witnessed by the enhanced eyes of the apostolic agents.
