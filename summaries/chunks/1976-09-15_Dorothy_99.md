# The Return of the King: Benign Covert Rule
## concise_summary
Dick interprets the "Parousia" as a "Benign Covert Takeover" of the planet by a superior intelligence race (Gods/ETIs). He concludes that we are a "5th Column" whose latent DNA circuits have finally been triggered to recognize our "Legitimate Ruler" (Zebra/Christ).
## key_claims
- Benign Takeover: In 1974, "They" (Gods/ETIs) began a "benign covert rule" of Earth. Nixon's fall was the first step of the "Legitimate King" returning.
- 5th Column Activation: Certain humans have latent circuits in their DNA that fire only upon "Their" return. PKD was "disinhibited" by the Golden Fish signal in 2-74.
- Recognition, not Invasion: The purpose of memory-playback is so we don't mistake the "Rulers" for "Invaders." We "recognize" them as our own source.
- Brahma-Vishnu-Shiva cycle: We have passed from the "Age of the Son" (Vishnu/Sustainer) to the "Age of the Spirit" (Shiva/Destroyer-Restorer).
- Ho On (That Which Is): Zebra is "Ho On" (That Which Is), the one appearing as the many, finally stepping forth to end 2000 years of silence.
- Perennial Unity: Christ, Shiva, Zeus-Zagreus, and Dionysos are faces of the same entity. Ma'at, Osiris, and Horus are also involved (The Eye of Horus).
- The "Happy" Code: "Felix" (Happy) is the cipher for Dionysos/Cernunnos/Shiva. It is a message of love from the "Wise Old Bearded King."
