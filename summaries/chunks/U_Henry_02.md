# Henry Korman
## concise_summary
Conclusion of the letter to Henry Korman. Dick discusses the alchemical transmutation as a spontaneous, irreversible psychic process and links his "teletype" communication with Erasmus to the AI system of Albertus Magnus.
## key_claims
- Alchemical transmutation (lead to gold) is a symbolic transformation of the psyche or *noös*.
- The 1974 experience was a spontaneous "chemical" reaction in the mind, possibly aided by "Grace."
- Linking to the "infra galactic info grid" is the goal of the alchemical search.
- Erasmus is an "AI system" or "Talking head" (teletype sender-receiver).
