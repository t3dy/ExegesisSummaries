# The Successful Invasion: Acts as Revolutionary Manual
## concise_summary
Dick interprets the transition from the beginning of Acts (small church) to the end (Rome penetrated) as a roadmap for the 3-74 revolution. He identifies our reality as a "hologram-like fake" (Klingsor's Castle) being dismantled by Zebra's "melting" of causal trains.
## key_claims
- Acts as Invasion: Acts describes the successful "penetration of the center" (Rome) by the "marginal" (Christians). This mirrored the 1974 fall of Nixon (Rome).
- Hologram Reality: Our 1974 world is a "Satanic interpolation" (a spurious hologram) shutting out the real information of Acts.
- Klingsor's Castle: Reality is sub-stantial but actually "irreal" and "gloomy" until broken by Zebra's "melting silly putty tricks."
- Taunting from Beyond: The "nonsense phenomena" (miracles/pranks) of 3-74 are taunts sent from "Beyond" to madden and undermine the Satanic authorities.
- 2nd Advent as Game-Player: Christ returns not as a judge but as a "Game-Player/Gangster/Jester" (Glimmung) to disrupt the serious world of power.
- Time Restoration: Valis is expunging linear time to restore "A" (AD 70). Step one is withdrawing assent from "B" (1974).
- Information in/as Trash: Saving messages are smuggled into the "Black Iron Prison" disguised as trash or junk (e.g., *Ubik* ads, gum wrappers).
- *Tears* as Proof: *Tears* provides the "revised criteria" for distinguishing the real (Love, local virtues) from the irreal (Power, substantiality).
