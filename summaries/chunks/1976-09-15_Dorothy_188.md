# Chunk 188 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_188

## Description
PKD expands his "Distributed Brain" model, theorizing that humanity consists of "nerve fibers" within a vast, cosmic thinking system. He analyzes the "Leaderless Revolution" of the 60s and 70s as a physical expression of this divine brain's intervention in history.

## Key Claims
- **The Distributed Brain**: Zebra is a "distributed brain" that lives in 2000-year present fields. We are the "physical brain components" or "nerve fibers" linked together with Christ as the psyche.
- **The Leaderless Revolution**: The anti-Vietnam War movement was a "miracle" where the brain coordinated its parts (protesters) without central leaders. It was a "quantum leap" in human consciousness.
- **BIP as Fossilized Reflex**: The Black Iron Prison is "dead" because it has no new thoughts—it is a closed, recirculating circuit that repeats the same psychotic signals forever.
- **Freedom = Self-Programming**: Human evolution is the progression toward "homeostasis of the individual" (self-programming/freedom), which the BIP tries to roll back.

## Entities
- **Beethoven**: Cited as a contributor to the idea of the "homeostasis of the individual."
- **Nixon Period**: A "deadly roll back" towards a fossil BIP state, which the brain intervened to overthrow.
- **The Sibyl**: The divine power represented as the one who overthrows the establishment.
- **U.S. / USSR**: Both seen as "fronts" or "incarnations" that the two transtemporal entities (Rome/Zebra) can "wear like gloves."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Plenary Override**: The absolute control a superior thinking system (the Brain) can exert over its components (humans) in times of peril.
- **Floating Crap Game**: Metaphor for the brain as a mobile, organizing principle that re-links constituents.
- **Balking**: The act of "fighting free" of the BIP's inner/outer reflex-arc determinism.
- **Negentropy**: The growth and freedom of the brain (Form I) vs. the static entrapment of the BIP.

## Theological Motifs
- **Corpus Christi as Organism**: The "mystical body of Christ" is a physical, distributive brain whose parts include both the living and the "saintly dead" (e.g., Jim Pike).
- **Ubik as Mind**: PKD identifies the "Ubik" of his novel with the vast brain (Logos/St. Sophia) he encountered in 3-74.
- **Hegelian Dialectic**: The view of history as the unfolding operation of Divine Purpose, which PKD sees as the brain becoming actualized.
- **The "Great Secrets" of Esotericism**: The belief that we were "devolving" and needed divine interference to resume the axis of true evolution.
