# The Onion Layers of Time: Acts as the Present Reality
## concise_summary
Dick argues that the Book of Acts is not a historical text but a "code" for our current reality. He views 3-74 as the moment he "woke up" from a fake memory-veneer into the real landscape of 70 AD Rome, which is currently "breaking through" our 20th-century dream.
## key_claims
- Acts is Now: Ontologically, we have been living in 70 AD. 1974 is a "spurious interpolation."
- Onion Layers: Time is stacked in layers. No real time passes; the world is a permanent "modern slave state" (Empire).
- The Camouflaged Moth: Dick identifies Valis as a "timid, harmful, ultra life-form" with ocelli (eyes) that mimics objects for protection. The "Third Eye" (Ajna) is the defense mechanism he triggered.
- Thomas as ETI: Thomas is not just a "higher self" but an Extraterrestrial Intelligence (ETI) in symbiosis with Dick.
- The Re-papering of the Past: God "re-papered" the past in 1974, switching the linear track to a more viable one. This explains the "acosmism" (lack of world-reality) in his novels.
- The Glass-Gazer / Guard Dog: In *Tears*, the "guard dog" (Thomas/Savior) mingles with the sheep (humans) until the predator (Empire) attacks.
