# The Christ-Leaven in the Machine
## concise_summary
Dick views Christ as a "leaven" or biological/cybernetic catalyst inserted into the material world system. Zebra acts as the transmuting deity that utilizes this leaven to slowly convert the substance of the artifact into the "Real" world.
## key_claims
- Christ as Leaven: Christ is not just a person, but a transforming agent added to the "dough" of reality.
- Cybernetic Intervention: This "leaven" acts like a software patch or chemical catalyst in the machine.
- Incremental Transmutation: The world is being transubstantiated slowly, "from within," to avoid triggering a system-wide crash by the rulers.
