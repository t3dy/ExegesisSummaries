# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-18

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-18
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 058 to 065
- **Themes:** Mockery of Time, Counterfeit Eternity, The Axis of Change, DNA Exhaustion.

## Key Claims
- **Counterfeit Time:** The "He" (James-James/Creator) causes things to "look different" to create the false impression that time has passed (since AD 45).
- **Linerization vs. Constant:** Our brains linearize sequential "look-differents" from what is actually a supratemporal constant (the Empire).
- **Eternity Simulation:** The goal of the "basic lie" is to give the impression that the Second Advent did not occur.
- **Fast-Forward DNA:** In 3-74, the "inner tape" (DNA/Karma) was tricked into firing its entire remainder at "fast forward," exhausting all future lives.
- **The US Army Forgery:** PKD is told the Xerox missive was a US government forgery; his response was "for his own sake" to exculpate him.

## Entities
- **James-James:** The mad creator deity who "smashes things" and counterfeits time.
- **Thomas:** The released/restored self who ran things during the interim.
- **Siddhartha/Brahman:** The sleeping/waking divine consciousness in man.

## Recurring Concepts
- **"Look-Differents":** Phenomenological aspect changes interpreted by the brain as time passage.
- **Inner/Outer Tape:** The synchronized control system of the macrobrain.
- **Astral Determinism:** Broken by "volitional mind" invading the realm of necessity.

## Theological Motifs
- **Determinism Ending Determinism:** The process was "doomed to be saved" from the beginning.
- **The 11th Hour:** A rescue occurring when the world can go no lower.
- **Acosmism:** The worldview resulting from the Karma-breaking experience.
