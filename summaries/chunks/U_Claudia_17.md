# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick develops the "Steuben Cup" myth: humans are "creating angels" who were given the "draught of Lethe" and are now laboring to recreate the divine model (Christ) from memory. He identifies his 1974 experience as the "Daimon of Socrates" (verified by Rollo May) and equates this entity with "Eros" (Life/Joy).
## key_claims
- We (humans) are "creating angels" who have forgotten our origin but are fashioning ourselves from the inside out to reach the "Christ-point."
- God conceals the "Steuben Cup" (Christ/Ideal) so we don't merely "copy" (print) it, but instead create a "million paths" of diverse ontology.
- Dick's failure/dead-end in 1974 allowed God's "Grace" to replace his "faulty sketch" with the "Light Being."
- The "Voice" in his head is the "Daimon of Socrates" (Eros), which handles the future and protects autonomy.
- Attic Greek is the language of the Daimon because it is the "matrix" of the highest human culture-forms.
- The "Creative Person" (maimed like Jacob) is the vanguard of society, building values for the future.
