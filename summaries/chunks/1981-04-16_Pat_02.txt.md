# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_02
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_02
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Anamnesis of the Discarded Choice, Coaxial Alternate Realities, The Fall, Parallel Worlds
- **Key Entities**: A.N. Whitehead, Calvin, William Burroughs

## Key Claims
- The "in-breaking" of 3-74 required Dick to experience anamnesis—not of the past, but of the *contemporary missing half* of reality: the discarded "zero" track.
- The alternate world Dick saw in 1974 (AD 70 / Acts) and the dark world of *Tears* were perceptions of this discarded, zero-phase timeline (Track A).
- He posits three coaxial realities: Inferno/Track A (The Black Iron Prison), Purgatorio/Track B (our current reality), and Paradiso/Track C (The Kingdom/Palm Tree Garden).
- Track C (The Kingdom) is currently breaking into Track B.
- Calvin was right that humanity is under a severe perceptual and cognitive occlusion, locking us out of the Kingdom.

## Entities
- **Calvin**: Validated regarding human occlusion and exile from the Kingdom (total depravity/blindness).
- **William Burroughs**: Discussed for his idea that "living information" acts as an invading virus causing occlusion, though Dick argues the occlusion is from the Empire, while the living information (plasmate) tries to cure it.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Coaxial Worlds / Orthogonal Time**: Alternate presents stacked alongside each other.
- **The Zero Phase**: The discarded possibilities the universe rejects in order to optimize reality.
- **Occlusion**: The theological blindness imposed on humanity that prevents seeing the Kingdom.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Restoration of Memory**: To perceive God's mechanism, one must remember the alternate choices God prevented.
- **The Plasmate (Living Information)**: Identified with the Logos / Christ attempting to bypass the occlusion and reach humanity.
