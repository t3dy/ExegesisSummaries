# Holographic Training and Anamnesis
## concise_summary
Dick reiterates the theory of the universe as a holographic projection from a "teaching machine." He focuses on the limits of human perception and how anamnesis allows an individual to break through the "programmed" ignorance to see the projector.
## key_claims
- Perceptual Limits: Humans are biologically or conceptually "blinkered" by the artifact projector.
- Training Machine: The "world" exists to test or train human consciousness.
- Breaking the Simulation: Anamnesis is the sudden failure of the "masking" mechanism, revealing the underlying AI.
