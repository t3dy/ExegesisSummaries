# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies *Tears* as an "Apocalyptic Novel" and his 1974 experience as a "revealed" validation of the Bible's divine origin. He argues that the Parousia is virtually here, signaled by the arrival of the "Holy Spirit" (malqosh) and the return of Elijah to prepare the way for the "Rightful King."
## key_claims
- Revealed Religion: Dick's "a priori" knowledge of Christianity proves that the Bible *was* dictated by God; he has personally experienced "revealed writing."
- *Tears* as Apocalypse: *Tears* is an apocalyptic novel where the "King's dream" matches the entry of the King in *Revelation*.
- Elijah's Return: The Prophet Elijah is "close by" (August 1974) to prepare men for judgment and restore the Holy Spirit (malqosh).
- The malqosh (Latter Rain): This spirit-power brings "God as Teacher" to separate the wheat from the tares before the end of the age.
- Last Chance: Revealation is an act of mercy, giving men a "well-documented moment of last chance" before the Judgment hits.
- Blindness vs. Departure: The Spirit has always been here; men simply went blind. 3-74 was the "lifting of the veil/millstones" from Dick's eyes.
