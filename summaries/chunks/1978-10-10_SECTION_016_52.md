# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-52

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-52
- **Themes:** Macro-Feedback Circuit, Camouflage Invader, King Felix and Acts, Revisionist Scripture.

- **Macro-Feedback:** Zebra/VALIS is a macro-feedback circuit responding to PKD's micro-conception of the deity. "Above as below."

- **Roles Reversed:** In the Second Advent, Christ judges and slays; Rome (the Empire) is judged and slain.
