# SECTION_006
# March 4, 1975
# Dr. Robert Ornstein
## concise_summary
Dick writes to Dr. Robert Ornstein, framing his mystical experience as the clinical activation of his brain's right hemisphere. He outlines the premise of his novel *TO SCARE THE DEAD* (VALIS), featuring an ordinary man (Nicholas Brady) who awakens a dormant "Essene" personality within himself.
## key_claims
- PKD's mystical experience was a "bilateral brain hemispheric parity" triggered by vitamins and meditation.
- Inside him has awakened a dormant Essene personality from 45 A.D. (named "Thomas").
- "Jesus" is a cipher for Zagreus/Dionysos, who is reclaiming his followers.
- The world is the "City of Iron" (Rome/Washington).
- A station in the noösphere ("Erasmus") is providing a "Sea of Knowledge."
