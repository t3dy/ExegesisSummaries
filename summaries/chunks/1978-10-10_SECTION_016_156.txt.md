# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-156

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-156
- **Themes:** The Deposed King, Belial's Mock-Reality, Retracking Alternate Pasts, Concentric Ascent.

## Key Claims
- **The Deposed King Model:** An "impostor" (Palmer Eldritch/Satan) rules as if he were God, while the "True King" (YHWH/Ubik) has been deposed and relegated to the periphery (the "trash layer").
- **Ubik as YHWH:** Ubik is the "reality support" because it is the "He who causes to exist" (YHWH). Belial spins out irreal worlds; YHWH transubstantiates them into the real.
- **Retracking (The 2nd Run):** PKD realized he had "lived through this all before" (deja vu/anamnesis). *Ubik*, *Tears*, and *Faith* are reports from the "first track," which PKD is now replaying and "overriding."
- **Concentric Worlds:** Hell (BIP), Purgatory (mixed), and Paradise (Garden) are not spatial places but "rings of emanation" (Plotinus). Ascent occurs through anamnesis of the lower level.
- **The 2nd Attempt Success:** The 1st Advent (70 AD) failed because "She was not acceptable"; the 2nd is successful because YHWH has "tinkered" with the past to ensure his "transplant" survives.

## Entities
- **Palmer Eldritch / Belial:** The impostor-king who spins "irreal worlds."
- **Ubik / YHWH:** The "Reality Support" fighting against entropy.
- **Elijah:** The spirit/prophet whose return heralds the Messiah's advent.

## Recurring Concepts
- **BIP (Black Iron Prison):** The "Track A" reality where Christianity lost.
- **The Garden:** The highest level of Purgatorio/Paradise where the "work is completed."
- **Time Displacement:** The experience of worlds superimposing as the "Spinner" (Valis) changes memory and track.

## Theological Motifs
- **Gnostic Inversion:** The God worshipped in churches is the "Evil Impostor"; the true God is found outside.
- **The Second Signal:** The "Real (Sentient) Future" fighting against the "Past (Mechanical Entropy)."
- **Moksa / Satori:** The "terrifying realization" of the total dependency of creation on the Mind of God.
