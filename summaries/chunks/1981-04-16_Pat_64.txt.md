## Key Claims
- Dick asserts that the Roman Catholic Church possesses a secret "kerygma": the phenomenal world is a fake construct created by Satan. 
- The *Parousia* (Second Coming) is not merely a presence, but an all-out, Zoroastrian spiritual war initiated by the Church to destroy this fake world and reunite humanity with God.
- He makes a massive realization regarding his own writing: *VALIS* is meaningless on its own. It is the "cypher book" or "codebook" for his 10-volume meta-novel (including *Ubik*, *A Maze of Death*, *The Three Stigmata*).
- By publishing *VALIS* last, he used a "retrograde" strategy (like God does). It forces the reader to realize that his earlier "fictional" science fiction works were actually literal, verbatim depictions of the Gnostic truth.
- Angel Archer is identified not merely as his soul, but as his soul *in the specific role of the Bride of Christ*.
