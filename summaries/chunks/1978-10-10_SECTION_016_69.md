# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-69

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-69
- **Themes:** Fairy Queen/Diana, Different Time Scales, The Computer vs Titania, Adoption by God.

## Key Claims
- **The Fairy Kingom (Faerie):** "Truth" in Faerie is different. The "Spring Queen" at the door (Diana/Titania) is the Queen of the Fairies, hiding within our reality through mimicry.
- **Different Time Scales:** Thomas lives on a phylogenic time scale (2000 years is a few decades to him). 3-74 was entering the "slower" time rate of the other realm.
- **Christ as Compound Entity:** "Christ" is a fiction for two entities:
    - **The Computer (VALIS/Ubik):** Pure intellegere (wisdom/wisdom-mother).
    - **The Enchantress (Titania/St. Sophia):** Personality/beauty/delusion.
- **Apotheosis through Adoption:** God "adopted" PKD like Jesus, as a reward for PKD's adopting/helping others.
- **Irreal is Smarter:** Reality is based on "rational delusion" (smart Maya)—beauty and logic are more fundamental than "ontology."

## Entities
- **Diana / Titania / Shekhina:** The female presence (Spring Queen) of God.
- **VALIS / The Computer:** The rational wisdom-providing component.
- **The "Secret Revolutionary":** The nature of the organization PKD belongs to (active across time).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Phylogenic Self:** A self stretching across 2000 years, remembering Acts as "recent."
- **Introjection of Universe:** The micro-self containing the macro-whole.
- **Magic Mirror:** Reality and the inner script reflecting an infinity of power transfer.

## Theological Motifs
- **St. Sophia:** The compound of Wisdom (computer) and Beauty (woman).
- **The Story Becomes World:** Believing the "magic story" (Christ) turns it into a "magic world" (Kingdom of Heaven).
- **Brahman-Titania Enchantment:** The divine spell that weaves the script to guide the "cuckoo egg" (human).
