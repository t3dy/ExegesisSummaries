# Chunk 213 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_213

## Description
PKD concludes that his "Thomas" personality is the Swiss physician/alchemist **Paracelsus** (Theophrastus von Hohenheim). He explores the "Magic Memory" of Giordano Bruno and declares that he has finally "cracked the case" regarding the source of his 3-74 enlightenment.

## Key Claims
- **Thomas is Paracelsus**: The clues of "mercury medicine" (bichlorides/aspirin), the physician identity, and the death at age 48 all point specifically to Paracelsus.
- **Bruno as Precursor**: Giordano Bruno’s "Hermetic Hylozoic cosmology" provides the best architectural map for PKD's experience. Bruno’s "Magic Memory" is the key to achieving the personality of a Magus.
- **The 30 Years War Connection**: The "Secret Christians" (Fraternity of the Rosy Cross) have been active since the Renaissance, fighting the Catholic League and now Nixon to prevent an "Imperial" takeover.
- **Substrate of Acts**: Level #3 (Acts/Rome) is where the real "war" is always taking place—the Phagocyte versus the heavy metal particle of the BIP.

## Entities
- **Paracelsus (Theophrastus)**: The "outrageous" Renaissance physician and alchemist who was the first to use mercury and who championed "Imagination" over "Reason."
- **Giordano Bruno**: The Renaissance philosopher who believed in infinite worlds, relativity, and the "internal principle of motion" (God as soul of every particle).
- **Yaltabaoth**: The "hostile particle" or self-poisoned spirit of the BIP (Yahweh).
- **The Rosy Cross**: The "Invisible College" that engineered PKD's enlightenment and directed his political actions.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Alchemy of Life**: The belief that alchemy’s true goal was the "secret of life" (immortality), not gold.
- **Inner Firmament**: Paracelsus' concept that vast spaces/realms stretch inside man, which imagination can "flare up" to reveal meaning.
- **The One Soul**: The hylozoic view that the universe has one soul (Macro-soul) which colloquizes with the human micro-soul.
- **Bichlorides / Aspirin of Mercury**: The specific dream-clues that identified Paracelsus (the first to use mercury internally).

## Theological Motifs
- **God as Internal Motion**: Rejection of the "external creator" in favor of an immanent divine principle within all matter.
- **Healing of the Mind**: The knowledge of "Supreme Unity" (Brahman) as the medicine that cures the "madness" of the BIP.
- **The Dualist Heresy**: The charge leveled at Paracelsus (and by extension PKD) for believing that "illness" is intrinsically evil and separate from God.
- **Unity that Enchants**: The goal of science and philosophy is to see all parts as organs of one single, beautiful organism.
