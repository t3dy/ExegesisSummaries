# The Form-Axis and the Return to Archetype
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the "Form-Axis" (from *Ubik*) as an orthogonal time-axis along which he regressed to AD 70. He argues that this regression was not just subjective but a "reversion to essence," revealing that our modern police state is a temporal recurrence of the archetypal Rome. He posits that ETIs (Zebra/St. Sophia) are time-travelers providing "information overrides" to human agents to prepare them for the King's return. The human race is currently "putting on immortality" in preparation for this union.
## key_claims
- Form-Axis Regression: Time travel is possible along an axis of "form" rather than linear progression. This is the "movie film" nature of the universe.
- AD 70 as Archetypal Prototype: AD 70 is not the past, but the social/spiritual prototype of the current state.
- St. Sophia / ETI Overrides: "Zebra" works through technical information overrides (as described by Carl Sagan), aiding human agents who have forgotten their origins.
- Putting on Immortality: We are currently in a state of "ascent" to meet the descending God (St. Sophia), as circuits in our brains are disinhibited by news of the return.
- Ubik’s Cosmology Refined: Evolution is the forward-driving organizing force (Cosmos), but entropy is the "undoing of form" via regressive time.
- Half-Life Perception: The reversion to AD 70 was a "waking up" from the half-life/half-sleep delusion of linear 1974.
