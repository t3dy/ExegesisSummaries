## Key Claims
- The experience of 2-3-74 revealed God not primarily as Creator, but as Sustainer. God overpowers the world (Heimarmene) to sustain the *nepioi* (His adopted sons).
- The "Second Coming" (Christ's Return in Glory) is not a future historical event, but an instantaneous, cognitive disclosure of the true state of affairs: that the powerless are actually invincible because God secretly rules.
- This Parousia is kept completely secret from the many, revealed only to the few. This secrecy is a logical necessity: if everyone knew that self-sacrifice brought the absolute protection of God, people would perform good acts out of selfish ambition rather than genuine morality.
- Therefore, the apparent failure of Jesus, the seeming irrelevance of his ethics, and the apparent non-occurrence of his return in glory are necessary illusions (steganography) designed to test true moral character.

## Entities
- **Karen**: Dick's friend whose innocent, defenseless nature helped him crystallize his understanding of the *nepioi* and God's role as protector.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Christ Returning in Glory**: The sudden apprehension that the hidden, 180° reversed coaxial world is the true reality, and that Christ is actively ruling and judging it right now.

## Theological Motifs
- **God Contra World**: The Gnostic-leaning view that God actively intervenes against the physical, causal laws of the universe to rescue the elect.
