# 1975-03-05_Ursula_02
# SECTION_005
# March 5, 1975
# Ursula K. Le Guin
## concise_summary
Continuation and conclusion of the letter to Le Guin. Dick iterates on the literal nature of St. Paul's harvest parables, linking the end of linear "Winter" to his dream of Bach's Cantata 4.
## key_claims
- St. Paul's parables of the "first fruits of the harvest" were meant literally.
- The command to "Wake up!" is being issued through dreams (the "Active Dreamer").
- The "Bonds of death" (Cantata 4) are relaxing as the Christos/Dionysos awakens the human seed.
