# Retroactive Mending and the Man from the Future
## concise_summary
Dick explores the "Man from the Future" theory, suggesting that his 74 experience was a "retroactive mending" of the past by a sentient entity traveling backward from the end of time. He considers that the "3-eyed people" are our future descendents or an ETI race (Albemuth) from whom we originally degenerated.
## key_claims
- Retroactive Mending: God/VALIS is a sentient entity traveling *backward* from the end of time to direct causal events.
- Time Travel Theory: 3-74 was a "tinkering" by people from the future (using "Bathosphere technology") to ensure the Palm Tree Garden prevails over the Black Iron Prison.
- The 3-Eyed People: Visions of large-brained, telepathic, 3-eyed beings represent evolved humans or ETIs.
- ETI/Albemuth: We are "deformed descendents" of a colonizing parent race from the stars (Albemuth). Christ was sent from this race to "restore" us.
- The Tachyon Theory: The "not-me" personality (Thomas) came from the future, which is why he knew the Xerox letter was coming.
- The "James Father" Voice: Identifies a voice calling itself "Father" which links him to his stellar origins.
- Occam's Razor: The "Future Man" theory is more economical than the "Past Man" theory because it doesn't require positing a unique historical individual.
