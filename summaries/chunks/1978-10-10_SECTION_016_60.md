# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-60

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-60
- **Themes:** Binary On-Off Logic, Ultra-thought Formation, Reality as Information, Pythagorean Cosmos.

## Key Claims
- **The Forming Thought:** Reality is a "forming thought" or "ultra-thought" within a macro-brain. "On" = Junction/Connection; "Off" = Disjunction/Disorder.
- **VALIS as Thought-Pattern:** VALIS is not just an entity but the *connective pattern* itself. The goal is to incorporate all "neurons" (us) into the ultra-thought.
- **Reality as Knowledge:** In 3-74, physical reality went through a transform and became knowledge/information.
- **Pythagorean Telos:** Creation is a "seed" (limited structure/telos) inserted into the "boundless" (unlimited/breath). It grows by assimilating the outside into ordered numerical structure.
- **Apotheosis of the Species:** All creatures suffer like Christ and are thus transfigured into Godhood. Jesus showed the end result (transfiguration) of our innocent suffering.

## Entities
- **The Ultra-thought:** The total all-parts interaction emerging from the binary system.
- **Pythagoras / Empedocles:** The philosophical precursors to the shamanistic/magical view of 3-74.
- **The Slain/Dying God:** The tragic beauty that underlies the burden of tribility.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Form Axis Morphological Tangency:** 1st Century and 1974 are adjacent because they sharing the same "form."
- **Amanuenis:** PKD's role in encoding "Tears" (Acts) as a record for the macro-brain.
- **Palintropus Harmony:** The oscillation of opposites that creates the hologram.

## Theological Motifs
- **Sacred Blood:** The thinking process/information flow of the universe.
- **Kosmos as Beauty:** Order and limit as the highest degree of divine realization.
- **Resurrected Faculties:** The ability to see the world as a 3-D "halo cube" (hologram).
