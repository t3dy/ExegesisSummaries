# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick proposes a "Light Body" theory of immortality: a 2,000-year-old collective "Bioplasmic" entity (the fellowship of Christ) re-enters human hosts through the 3rd eye/pineal gland. He identifies the "Fall" as the loss of communication with the Immanent God and argues that reality is alive, evolving, and perfecting itself.
## key_claims
- Immortality is achieved via a "Light Body" (released by Jesus' death) that re-enters suitable human hosts through the 3rd eye.
- The "Second Comforter" (Holy Spirit) is this aggregate Light Body, with Christ as "the head."
- The 2,000-year "dry period" occurred because the last memories of this community were of 100 AD Rome (Greek-speaking).
- God is immanent ("Break a stick... there I am"), but humans have "fallen away" from the perception of Him.
- The "Logos" is the "Meaningful Structure of Reality" and is born as a man to lead us back to relatedness.
- Reality is an "Entelechy" (evolving toward completion) and we are within its "breathing" cycles (*Palintropos*).
