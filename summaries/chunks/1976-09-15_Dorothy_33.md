# The Override of Astral Determinism
## concise_summary
Dick refines his "Signal Chamber" theory, arguing that God (via Grace) "overrode" his pre-programmed deterministic response to the Xerox letter. He sees 3-74 as a breakdown in "synchronization" that revealed the "fake" nature of world-programming and allowed for a new, "orthogonal" track to emerge.
## key_claims
- Override of Programming: 3-74 was a divine intervention to prevent a catastrophic response to the Xerox letter by overriding Dick's "astral determinism."
- Synchronization Breakdown: We are normally "pre-synchronized" to react to external stimuli (like the phone ringing); 3-74 broke this "synch," revealing the "Black Iron Prison" of mechanism.
- Orthogonal Grace: God's intervention is "orthogonal" (coming from outside the system) and institutes a new "future track" that doesn't follow the old "take."
- Time Regression/Advancement: Dick "regressed" to Rome AD 70 and then "advanced" to the Millennial Kingdom, experiencing the whole history of man in 11 months.
- The Zebra Principle: Zebra's purposefulness is only visible when the internal clock is accelerated; he is an "inference from the direction of the flux."
- Future Memory: "Perceptual memory" can bring the future into view just as it does the past, indicating that the future already exists as "traces" in the programming.
- Truth Serum/Pentothal: The drug he took for the Xerox letter (combined with the fish) was the "truth serum" that dissolved the barrier and allowed the real memory to fire.
