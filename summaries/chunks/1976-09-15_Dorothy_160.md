# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_160
## Chunk Description
Discusses the steganographic message in *Tears*, the "Xerox Missive" as a forgery, and the "Bait" effect on the evil power.

## Key Claims
- **Tears as Steganography:** *Flow My Tears* carries the "latent (true) message" that we are in Rome C. A.D. 70 and help is imminent.
- **Smoking out the Network:** The "Xerox Missive" was a attempt by a network that knew the truth (and was suppressing it) to silence PKD.
- **The Disinhibition Stimulus in Tears:** The dream sequence + the "Felix" command + the Black man scene constitute a trigger for unveiling reality.
- **Xerox Missive as Forgery:** PKD is positive the "Xerox Thing" was a forgery and not from the entity it claimed ("the party").
- **Breaking the Astral Template:** God (Sophia/Noös) intervenes to deflect "bad luck" or "astral fate" (Ananke), which is a higher purpose than mere philosophy can offer.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** Used PKD's writing to move an encoded message through time.
- **Felix:** A key "word" (not just a name) in the steganographic code.
- **St. Sophia/Noös:** The power that breaks the "deterministic script."
- **Will Durant:** Criticized again for seeing religion only as "spiritual comfort."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Steganography:** Hidden truths in popular media (novels, rock music).
- **Astral Fate (Moira):** The deterministic template God breaks.
- **Disinhibition:** The "unveiling" of the true situaton (Prisoners of Rome).
- **The "Right Persons":** Those who knew the truth and felt threatened by PKD's "printout."

## Theological Motifs
- **The Living God:** A God of vast knowledge and personality who acts on human history as His material.
- **Apostolic Mystery:** Christianity as a means to put man in touch with the God who intervenes.
- **Unearned Suffering Transmuted:** Suffering as a channel for catalytic transformation (fusing with Christ).
