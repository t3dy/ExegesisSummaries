# Programmed to Love the Fish
## concise_summary
Dick explores the idea that 3-74 was an "override" of his regular social/political programming. He theorizes that he was "switched" from an alternate track (Black Iron Prison/Soviet agent) to a new one (Christian) by a "disinhibiting signal" (The Gold Fish) to prevent him from following the Xerox letter's instructions.
## key_claims
- Override of Programming: 3-74 was an "interruption" or "override" of the normal deterministic programming of his life.
- Alternate Track Swapping: Dick believes he was "switched" from a "dreadful alternate present" (BIP) where he was perhaps a "CP trained" agent, to the current "Christian" track.
- The Gold Fish Disinhibitor: The fish necklace was a pre-arranged signal that triggered a "Christian engram" which overrode the instructions from the Xerox letter.
- Ubik as Steganography: Reconfirms *Ubik* and *Flow My Tears* as containing "half-messages" that require an internal brain-analog to "link up."
- Zebra the Time Traveler: Zebra is a "time traveling entity" that rewove the past specifically to create a better future.
- The Great White Fish: Zebra is the "great white fish" that gives its body (the mimicking world) to save us from astral determinism.
- Post-Hypnotic Amnesia: He suspect he was given a "you will forget" command (likely by the Soviets/CP) that was broken by God.
