# Chunk 197 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_197

## Description
PKD reconceptualizes the "Upper Realm" as the post-mortem or heaven realm, which is now "superimposed" on the physical world. He views the 3-74 experience as a "physical resurrection" for the dead (like Thomas) and a "participation in the next world" for the living.

## Key Claims
- **Spanning Two Realms**: The Cosmic Christ (Brain) spans both the lower (physical) and upper (post-mortem) realms, knitting the decomposing cosmos back together.
- **Double Exposure Reality**: The 3-74 experience was an "intermingling" where PKD saw 1974 and A.D. 45 simultaneously—a "superimposition" of two space-times.
- **Physical Resurrection**: For Thomas (an apostolic Christian), USA 1974 is his physical resurrection; for PKD, it is a sudden participation in the "next world."
- **The 1900-Year Ellipsis**: The mystical body of the church "ignored" the intervening centuries, which were not part of the divine timeline.

## Entities
- **Tony Boucher / Jim Pike**: Deceased friends whose "presence" PKD sensed, confirming the brain's link to the post-mortem realm.
- **Thomas**: The "master personality" from the next world who exerted a "plenary override" on PKD.
- **Pinky**: PKD's cat, seen on the "other side" of the 1:.618034 doorway (the post-mortem realm).
- **Hegel**: Mentioned as one who took the "mystical body" into account in his philosophy of history.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Palintropos Oscillation**: The feedback loop where the upper realm overrules the lower realm.
- **Double Exposure**: The phenomenon of seeing two realities (Rome and USA) as a single composite.
- **Negentropy (Ubik)**: Affirming *Ubik* as a description of the revitalizing force of the Logos against thermal death/entropy.
- **The Big Bang (Brain Flash Point)**: The hoped-for "explosion" of consciousness that restarts the universal cycle.

## Theological Motifs
- **Heaven as CAD 45**: In heaven (the absolute realm), it is still A.D. 45; linear time is only a property of the lower, "apostate" realm.
- **Agnus Dei / Lamb's Book of Life**: PKD's sense that his name has been entered into the record of the saved.
- **The Kingdom as Inter-realm**: The Kingdom of God is neither "here" nor "there" but spans and intermingles both this world and the next.
- **Stagnation as Evil**: Stagnation (entropy) is the "adversary," while divine intervention (negentropy) is the "forward motion" of history.
