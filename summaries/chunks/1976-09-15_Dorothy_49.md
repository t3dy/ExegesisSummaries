# The Benign Mantis: We are Impostors
## concise_summary
Dick embraces the "Impostor" theme of his fiction, theorizing that he is a "linked extension" (a programmed "sleeper") for a benign, non-human "Mantis" race. He sees reality as an "orthogonal metamorphosis" where the "Mind-Central" (Zebra) uses camouflage to intervene in history.
## key_claims
- We are Impostors: Dick and others like him are "benign mantises" posing as humans. They are "factory reps" for the "Mind-Central" (the hive mind).
- Hetero-Control: In 3-74, he was taken off "homeo" (autonomy) and placed on "hetero" (control) by the master circuit to perform his task.
- The Benign Insect Mind: God is like a "Mind-Central" or "Hive Mind"—ancient, programmed, dispassionate, and using camouflage (mimicry).
- Orthogonal Change: Metamorphosis happens "laterally" (off the linear timeline), which is why we have "amnesia" for the previous arrangements of the world.
- The Fake World (Joint): *Time Out of Joint* was true; the world is a fabricated delusion, and the real time is 70 AD.
- Thomas as Lateral Change: The "Thomas" personality was a lateral shift in Dick's identity that normally would go unnoticed but for his "meta-awareness."
- The Reweaving Canvas: Zebra is constantly "repainting the canvas" of reality; linear time is just our perception of this reweaving.
- The "Olive Holt" Vision: His childhood vision of "Olive Holt" (a neighbor) was his first glimpse of the "dropping of the mask."
