# Ubik as the 3rd Testament
## concise_summary
Dick elevates his novel *Ubik* to a "3rd Testament," viewing it as a prophetic account of Zebra's "re-animation" and "re-arrangement" of the universe. He argues that Zebra's reconstruction of the world is an "intervention" that has been ongoing for 1900 years, visible only through "inwardness."
## key_claims
- Ubik = Zebra = Christ: The "messages on the trash" in *Ubik* are precisely how the Cosmic Christ communicates by rearranging the "stockpile" of reality.
- The 3rd Testament: *Ubik* (along with *Tears* and *Deus Irae*) is proof that 3-74 was real; the books themselves were arranged by Zebra.
- 1900-Year Intervention: Zebra's insertion/assimilation is not new but has been secretly progressing since the 1st Advent.
- Memory and Insertion: Dick's "Thomas" memories (AD 70) are actually Zebra's own memories of its long insertion process.
- Re-animating the Stockpile: Zebra rebuilds the universe out of the "pile of parts" (antecedent matter), animating "lifeless" matter into a "Corpus Christi" (Body of Christ).
- Inwardness vs. Outwardness: Our "unaided eyes" see only the outer aspect (matter); 3-74 provided a view of the "inwardness" (Noos/Spirit) that animates it.
- Autonomy Restored: Dick believes the "Spirit" (St. Sophia) withdrew to restore his autonomy, but the "Assembly" (the new cosmos) is still here, invisibly.
