# Reweaving the Past: Alpha and Omega
## concise_summary
Dick proposes that God is "reweaving" history backward from the Omega Point to improve the human lot. He defines the "Black Iron Prison" (BIP) as the original, un-intervened track (Alpha/Hell) and the "Palm Tree Garden" as the final, corrected track (Omega/Heaven). 3-74 was the "juncture point" where these two alternate worlds branched.
## key_claims
- Retrograde Reweaving: The Logos (God) is reweaving the historical past from the temporal end of the universe backward to the beginning.
- Three Alternate Worlds:
    1. Black Iron Prison (Hell/Original track/Nixonian tyranny).
    2. Our World (Partially improved track).
    3. Palm Tree Garden (Perfect track actualized in 74).
- The Dream Message of "Felix": *Flow My Tears* contains the message that the original "Track 1" (BIP) was destroyed by God and replaced.
- Absolute Erasure: The reweaving is so thorough that the evils of the original track "never existed" (Lethe/forgetfulness). 
- First vs. Second Advent:
    - 1st Advent: Tinkering in Roman times (set up our track).
    - 2nd Advent: Tinkering in 1974 (starts the Palm Tree track).
- The Urwelt: The BIP and Palm Tree worlds are "templates" or "urwelts" underlying the thin skin of our daily reality. 
- Christ the Weaver: Christ penetrates the "core and heart" of the demiurge's creation to fashion a "New Heaven and New Earth."
