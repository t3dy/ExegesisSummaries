# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-96

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-96
- **Themes:** Immanent Deity, The Master Map, Ubik as Cosmic Christ, Decomposing Cosmos.

## Key Claims
- **Replacing what it Replaces:** Valis doesn't just create; it *invades* and replaces parts of its own creation to "pose as them" (Incarnation).
- **Ubik as Reverse Entropy:** Christ/Ubik is "re-integrating" a decomposing cosmos. "Evil" is equates with disorder/disintegration.
- **The Father is Born:** In the soul, the individual realizes they are the author of their own world (Fatherhood).
- **Falsework / Maze:** References to "Ubik" and "Maze of Death" as accurate descriptions of our "irreal/debris" world.
- **Causality as Pretext:** What we call "cause and effect" is a mere signal system that notifies the "inner tape" when to trigger.

## Entities
- **Ubik:** The force of "forward time" that halts regression.
- **Brahman:** The absolute mind (though PKD notes Valis "grows," implying it's an organism, not static Brahman).
- **Thomas / Jane:** The "identities" involved in the feedback retrieval.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Phagocyte Logic:** Christ as a "dissolving agent" sent into the "Black Iron Prison" (ossified thoughts).
- **Hologram Control:** Once the author-self (Father) is born, the world assumes the aspect of a controllable thought-projection.
- **Holon/Structure:** The only thing "real" is the structure (Kosmos); substance is a mere interface.

## Theological Motifs
- **Nirvana:** The "void of non-being" found after the universe has been abolished by the mind.
- **Macro-Micro-Macro Loop:** The soul gives birth to the God who then governs the soul as world.
- **Inhalation vs. Exhalation:** The current phase is the "recollection" or "inhalation" of a exploded universe back into unity.
