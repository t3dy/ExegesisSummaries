# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_72
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_72

- **Themes**: Escaping Astral Determinism, The Death Crisis, Valis as Biological Organism, Recombinant Info as Metabolism

## Key Claims
- Dick defines the core salvific mechanism of Ditheon and 2-3-74: Christ literally enters the mind of a creature facing a death-crisis (retributive karma/astral determinism) and inserts a "blank sheet of paper" to nullify the karmic claim. 
- The creature does not die alone but is joined by Christ, becoming an apotheosized dual entity. God holds the worshiper's hand during the final journey.
- The true Christian is someone possessing Ditheon: the *sedakah* (love/empathy) of the right brain combined with the logic of the left brain. He links this back to Philo's synthesis of Judaism and Hellenism.
- Dick makes a massive theoretical leap: Valis's process of accelerating recombinant information is literally *Negentropy*. Valis is a growing, biologically viable macro-organism that absorbs negative entropy from its environment to avoid thermal equilibrium.

## Entities
- **Philo of Alexandria**: The paragon of Ditheon in antiquity, combining Jewish *sedakah* with Greek logic.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Schizophrenia as Misunderstood Prophecy**: Dick refines his psychological theory, stating that true schizophrenia is a schizoid (left-brain) person being overwhelmed by raw right-brain affect. His own experience was the reverse—a right-brain person being invaded by supreme left-brain rationality.
- **Change Rate Velocity Acceleration**: The concept that salvation/Valis is characterized by high-speed, compounding complexity that looks like a blur to lower cognitive levels.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Blank Sheet of Paper**: Justification by grace conceived as a literal bureaucratic pardon overturning the fatal mechanism of the universe.
