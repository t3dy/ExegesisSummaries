# The Punched Tape Logos: Reality as Information
## concise_summary
Dick proposes a "3-level" model of reality: (1) The Logos/Bible as a "punched tape" of ideas, (2) The World of Flesh/Nature, and (3) The Artificial/Technological World. He argues that our world (3) is totally obscured from its source (1) by the opaque layer of nature (2).
## key_claims
- Reality as Program: Following *A Maze of Death*, Dick sees the world as a simulation generated by a "punched tape" of verbal/informational thoughts (the Logos).
- 3 Levels of Reality:
    - Level 1: The Logos (Biblical/Informational). The "source code."
    - Level 2: Nature/Flesh (The "Opaque Surface"). Obscures the source.
    - Level 3: Artificial/Constructed (Our daily environment). Walled off from source.
- Christianity as the Tape: The Bible and its archetypes are the literal "bits" of the machine code that generate our shared reality.
- The Wall of Beef (Dream): A massive wall of "Sarx" (flesh) hides the layer of "crazy" religious information/writing beneath it.
- DNA as Information Carrier: Human beings are primarily carriers of the divine DNA/informational coding.
- Time Rollback as Diagnostic: If time can be rolled back, it proves the world is "illusory" (Maze/Maya).
- The Immanent Word: Human history is the "story" the immanent deity (Brahman/Zebra) is thinking; the thinking *is* the enacting.
- Salvation as Information: *Ubik* correctly identified level 1 (info) rising through level 2 (nature) to level 3 (us).
