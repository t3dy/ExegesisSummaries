## Key Claims
- The "di" in Ditheon does not mean "sundered" but "two" or "stereo." It is the biological/evolutionary advance of having two perception/cognition fields operating simultaneously, exactly analogous to how two eyes provide spatial depth perception.
- Because one psyche is native to the present (1974) and the other is a resurrected psyche from the ancient past, there is a built-in "time-lag factor." This lag converts time into visible, spatial-like depth ("temporal depth").
- Only what shows up in *both* fields is regarded as truly real (Plato's eide / archetypal constants). What only shows up in one field is discarded as epiphenomenal background (Maya).
- The adventitious psyche (the Holy Spirit / Thomas) is introduced by God. This is the true meaning of "Baptism in the Spirit" or being "born from above." It is the implantation of a second psyche.

## Entities
- **Plato**: Dick finally believes he has fully explained Plato's recovery of the Forms (eide): they are the overlapping constants perceived by the stereo operation of two dysphasic brain hemispheres.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Firebright**: The immortal "offspring" created by the union of the two psyches (the Golden Flower of Alchemy). It survives physical death to return and possess someone else, perpetuating the chain of Christs.

## Theological Motifs
- **Necessity of the Crucifixion**: Jesus had to be crucified and resurrected so his psyche could return as the "Second Comforter" to fuse with the disciples at Pentecost, creating the first generation of Ditheons.
