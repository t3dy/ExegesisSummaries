# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-30

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-30
- **Themes:** Macro-Brain as Mind, Binary Language of 0-1, The Maze as Test.

## Key Claims
- **Rational Intelligence as Invader:** Rational mind is not the creator but an invader/product that "makes war" on the irrational, mechanistic universe.
- **The 0-1 Language:** The structure of reality is not *using* a language but *is* a 0-1 binary language (Living Information).
- **Time as Mind's Activity:** Flux and change are the literal thoughts/thinking activity of the macro-brain.
- **Maze as Training:** The world is a "maze" built by higher life forms (3-eyed people) to test/train/study humans (pond life).
- **Sabotage of the Maze:** The secret early Christians (Valis faction) are revolutionaries sabotaging the "Black Iron Prison" maze to free us.

## Entities
- **VALIS/ZEBRA/UBIK:** The unitary entity with no fixed corpus, patterning "the given" to form a brain.
- **3-Eyed People:** The surgeons/controllers outside the hologram, surgeons of the "cold-pac."
- **Palmer Eldritch:** The vision in the sky representing the "Old Order" of the maze.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Entelechy:** The self-creating, negentropic vortex.
- **Integer 1 vs. Rest 0:** The binary mode of incorporation into the entelechy.
- **Biosphere:** The idea that we are in a "owned" environment (biosphere) or cold-pac simulation.

## Theological Motifs
- **Mystical Body of Christ:** Paul's description of a macro-brain using humans as constituents.
- **Antithetical Dialectic:** The "furious flux" used in the biosphere's experiment.
- **Parousia:** The in-breaking of the Savior (Valis) as a sabotage of the maze.
