# Chunk 207 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_207

## Description
PKD elaborates on the "fallen" state of our universe as a localized mental illness within the macro-brain. He describes a secret college of "time-traveling healers" and defines the 3-74 experience as the opening of the "ajna eye" to perceive the geometric blueprint of reality.

## Key Claims
- **Localized Madness**: Our region of the universe is a dead or "mad" subsection of the macro-entity (Yaltabaoth). Phagocytosis (Zebra) is the attempt to repair this damaged region.
- **Secret Time-Travelers**: Esoteric groups (like the "Invisible College") move through the omnitemporal matrix to influence history toward freedom and negentropy.
- **Noos vs. Brain**: The macro-brain (physical universe) is a "print-out" or map topologically congruent to the Noos (Pure Knowing). Noos is hierarchically prior to matter.
- **Golden Ratio (1:.618034)**: The constant by which the totality maintains its design across all levels. Seeing this ratio allows one to apprehend the whole from any bit.

## Entities
- **Yaltabaoth**: The deranged usurper or "heavy metal speck" that poisons our section of the brain with "noise."
- **The Invisible College**: Transtemporal human agents of the Brain's anima who act as healers to breach our isolation.
- **Siddhartha/Christ**: Brothers within the Godhead; Siddhartha is the forgotten man, Christ is the seeker who awakens him.
- **Hagia Sophia**: The personification of revealed wisdom whose rebirth initiates the ascent.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Heavy Metal Poisoning**: A metaphor for the toxin/virus that deranged our neuro-receptor sites, causing the "curse" of repetitive, entropic time.
- **Geometric Polychrome Ratio**: The ultimate visual form of reality—an intricate, beautiful geometric shape utilizing intervals and octaves.
- **Orthogonal Form Axis**: The non-linear dimension that allows for "time travel" via identity exchange and anamnesis.
- **Anima (Soul) of the Totality**: The divine intelligence that can take the form of an "indescribably beautiful woman" to entrance the sleeping Siddhartha.

## Theological Motifs
- **Gnostic Fall/Restore**: The "forgetfulness" of the soul (Letter/Lethe) as the cause of death; anamnesis as the restoration to immortality.
- **The Elusive Wild Animal**: Christ as a "wild" (free) information entity that replicates itself seed-like within the rigid, deranged world.
- **The Sacred Secret**: The use of feminine beauty ("Das ewige Weiblichkeit") as a divine tactic to catch the attention of the sleeping human soul.
- **Micro-Macro Isomorphism**: Since every bit recapitulates the whole, the individual’s enlightenment is proof of the total structure.
