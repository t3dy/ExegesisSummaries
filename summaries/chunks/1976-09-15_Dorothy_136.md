# The Orthogonal Axis: Plato's Theory Experientially Proven
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the "horizontal form-axis" (orthogonal time) from his novel *Ubik* as an experientially real cosmological feature, proving Plato's theory of Forms. He refines his model of the "Urgrund," suggesting it contains both Yang (Good) and Yin (Evil) polarities. The Urgrund projects the Artifact (YHWH/Creator) to separate these opposites, creating our "copy of a copy" world. He views the "Good" (Zebra/Christ) as a non-coercive invader found in the "trash" levels of the world.
## key_claims
- Orthogonal Axis: There is a real axis of "form" (horizontal) as well as linear "time" (vertical). *Ubik* correctly glimpsed this before PKD experienced it in 3-74.
- Urgrund as Androgyny: The Urgrund contains both Yang and Yin; the Artifact (YHWH/Yaldabaoth) is its Yin offspring that creates the BIP world.
- Parmenides Ratified: Form II (BIP/Night) is "dead" and an "empty shape," while Form I (Zebra/Set) is alive and authentic.
- Invader Gnosis: Zebra (Form I) is not part of this universe but an "invader" who has breached our "mock world."
- The Trash Christ: The Divine is found at the outskirts and "trash" levels, as far from the center of imperial power as possible.
