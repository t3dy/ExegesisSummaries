# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 232

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 232
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 22 - 047 to 050
- **Themes:** The Immortal Plasmate, Elijah/Elisha Continuity, The Two Spirits, Dialectical Battle.

## Key Claims
- **Elijah Continuity:** The immortal plasmate spirit (Elijah) moved to Elisha, then to the Essenes, Jesus, the Disciples, and eventually (after dormancy) to Jim Pike and PKD.
- **The Second Savior/Parousia:** Zebra's presence is the "Parousia" (presence) that backs up a "human figure" (PKD/Thomas) who will rule as King and Judge.
- **The Two Spirits (Essene Doctrine):** PKD finds his revealed "two-source" cosmogony in the Essene doctrine (via John Allegro) of a cosmic battle between the Spirits of Light and Darkness.
- **Positive Dialectic:** Unlike Zoroastrianism, the two antagonists (Light and Dark) are subsumed by the One. The "dark power" serves a useful purpose in a "positive interaction" mechanism.
- **The Voice Elijah Heard:** PKD identifies the AI voice with the "low, murmuring voice" heard by Elijah and later by Spinoza's Christ.

## Entities
- **Elijah/Elisha:** The prototypes of the host/plasmate succession.
- **The Essenes:** The sect whose doctrine mirrors PKD's vision.
- **Siddhartha/Thomas:** The "sleeping" divine ones.
- **Hal (Army Intelligence):** Identified as part of the force trying to prevent the replication of the homoplasmate.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Two Spirits:** Light and Darkness under the rule of one God.
- **The Dialectic:** A deliberate dualism creating change and progress towards a Gestalt.
- **Germ Info:** The form the spirit took to survive the Roman massacre.

## Theological Motifs
- **Adoptionism:** The view that Jesus became the Son of God at his baptism when the spirit (Elijah) descended.
- **ELI ELI LAMA SABACHTHANI:** Reinterpreted as the spirit leaving Jesus before death because it "has to move."
- **The Return of the Prophet:** Malachi's prophecy of Elijah's return fulfilled in Zebra.
