# The Eternal Now of the Advent
## concise_summary
Dick concludes his massive day of theorizing by realizing the 1st Advent is an "Eternal Now" (always occurring at the vernal equinox) and the "Rome" memories were incidentals of an "Internal Epiphany." He argues that he was essentially "reborn" from above, with a new personality (Thomas) grafted on to handle a terminal spiritual ordeal.
## key_claims
- Eternal Renewal: The 1st Advent is a timeless mythic event that re-occurs "every spring" (Vernal Equinox). It is the fundamental "Urwelt" of reality.
- Rebirth from Above: Dick's old personality disintegrated, and a "trans-mundane" person (Thomas) was "incised" or "grafted" onto him from above.
- Authentic Rebirth: God did not give him "false memories" of Rome; He provided a *legitimate* historic personality who actually had those memories.
- Lateral Axis: Christianity is not linear in time but intensifies "laterally" or "orthogonally" along an axis of ontological depth.
- Ubik = Eternal Now: *Ubik* represents the "timelessness" (Boethius' present) of the divine realm breaking through.
- The 2nd Advent: While the 1st Advent occurred in a hostile world (BIP), the 2nd Advent will totally absorb reality into the Palm Tree Garden.
- Judgment Passed: Dick feels he has been "judged" in his lifetime and found worthy of release (Nirvana/Kingdom).
