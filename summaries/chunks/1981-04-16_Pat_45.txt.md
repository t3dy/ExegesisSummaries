## Key Claims
- Dick defines the ultimate core of the "meta-abstraction": it is the sudden realization that the world's *power to compel our assent to it as real* is broken. We realize we are being fed information, like dreamers waking up.
- This represents a built-in "acosmism" where the physical world is seen as irreal, and the realization itself constitutes the "Saving Gnosis" that frees the mind from Heimarmene (the Machine).
- He returns to the Ditheon concept, realizing that Thomas + PKD do not "create" Christ by linking up; rather, they are both outgrowths of Christ, who exists *prior* to them as their eternal "Phylogenic" self.
- The Parousia (Second Coming) is happening organically via the incremental growth of this trans-temporal "body." As more human particulars link up across time, the Resurrected Body grows in complexity and power, stealthily infiltrating the universe.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Compelled Assent**: The mechanism by which the Torah/Machine forces human consciousness to accept the physical simulation as the ultimate reality. Overcoming this is the true Gnosis.
- **The Phylogenic Self**: The overarching, immortal identity (Christ) from which individual, mortal human personalities branch off.

## Theological Motifs
