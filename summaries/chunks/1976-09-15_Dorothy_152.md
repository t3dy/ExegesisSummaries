# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_152
## Chunk Description
Refines the metaphorical model of the "Radius Bar" and "Styli" for tracking multiple time-tracks.

## Key Claims
- **The Record Groove Metaphor:** Linear time is a spiral groove; human lives are "styli" tracking it. The "Radius Bar" (macro-mind) links these styli orthogonally.
- **Composite Identity:** The "radius bar" blends the signals from many grooves, creating a composite consciousness of past, present, and future.
- **Script Breaking:** The macro-entity can "bleed assistance" from earlier or later lives to "break the script" of a current life (as in 3-74).
- **Tears as the Master Novel:** *Flow My Tears* is the "most pregnant" novel because it accurately depicts the "latent reality" of A.D. 70 Syria/Rome beneath the surface.
- **Invisibility of Salvation:** The "penetration and rescue" by the macro-entity must remain invisible to the "artifact" (adversary).

## Entities
- **Joe Chip:** Cited from *Ubik* ("the man contains not the boy but the former man").
- **Zebra (The Radius Bar):** The orthogonal intelligence.
- **The Artifact:** The adversary/adversarial power-structure.
- **AMORC:** Mentioned as a model for "mystical political radicals."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Orthogonal Axis:** The 90-degree axis to linear time where macro-entities reside.
- **Form Regression:** The "Ubik" concept of returning to the "rock bottom stratum" (A.D. 70).
- **C.E. 70:** The "authentic" time-track.
- **BIP:** The underlying field of linear time, currently under attack by Set/Zebra.

## Theological Motifs
- **New Covenant:** The person who is "injected" becomes part of a corporate, transtemporal body (the corpus Christi).
- **Conquest of Time:** Time is "nothing" to Zebra; the Holy Spirit makes humans supratemporal.
- **Secret Brotherhood:** A fraternity of "injectees" spanning thousands of years.
