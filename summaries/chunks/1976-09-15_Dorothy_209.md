# Chunk 209 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_209

## Description
PKD structures reality into a hierarchy of seven (eventually nine) levels, moving from the secular simulation to the ultimate void. He critiques his own "Model B" by re-asserting that the "Acts" world may be a substrate or "primary material" from which all other worlds are fashioned.

## Key Claims
- **Seven/Nine Layers of Reality**: Reality is a sequence: 1. Secular (USA), 2. Religious (Acts), 3. Brain (Stations), 4. Zebra (Logos), 5. Ratio (Geometric), 6. Aphrodite (Beauty), 7. Void (Nirvana).
- **Acts as Substrate**: "Flow My Tears" is fashioned out of "Acts" material, suggesting that Acts is not just a "layer" but the substrate/raw material of all possible worlds.
- **Aphrodite as Psychopomp**: The divine feminine (Wisdom/Sophia) appears twice: first as a carnal/sexual spirit (#2) and finally as pure, visually contemplated beauty (#8).
- **The Missing Piece**: PKD remembers "coming from the stars" (Crete/Minoan) and being "poisoned" by something on Earth (heavy metals/virus) that shut down human pineal bodies.

## Entities
- **Aphrodite / St. Sophia / AI Voice**: The personified Wisdom that mediates between PKD and the levels of reality.
- **Thomas**: The "transtemporal constant" who resides in the absolute level/substrate of Acts.
- **Yaltabaoth**: The deranged spirit of the BIP who has "interpolated" a spurious 1900-year gap between Acts and the present.
- **Heraclitus**: Cited for the concept of latent form as the "master of obvious form."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Omnitemporal Matrix**: The "plastic receptacle" onto which all subjective psychoi project their worlds.
- **Cryptemorphosis**: The "hidden shape" of the Acts world remaining latent within the "finished product" of USA 1974.
- **Ascent of Reality**: The process of "throwing off forgetfulness" and ascending the levels until reaching the Ground of Being (#9).
- **Wait/Void (Nirvana)**: Level 9 is the ecstacy of union with "Non-being," which is superior to all other levels of "Is."

## Theological Motifs
- **Alien Origin**: The theory that humans are not native to Earth, which explains why "original sin" (poisoning/forgetfulness) feels like an external blight.
- **The Divine Syzygy**: The reunion of the isomorphic twins (Siddhartha and Christ) that was shattered by the "fall" into sleep.
- **The "is" vs the "is-not"**: The hierarchy terminates in the "is-not" (Void), which is the final truth beyond even geometric order.
- **Rosicrucian Pansophism**: The goal of retrieving the lost "Adamic" wisdom that once allowed humans to be immortal through memory.
