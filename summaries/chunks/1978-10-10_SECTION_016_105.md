# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-105

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-105
- **Themes:** Compression / Way of the Cross, Strip Dysynchronization, Christ as Pre-emptor, Satan's Failed Claim.

## Key Claims
- **Way of the Cross (Compression):** A "death strip" in the DNA must fire; the "Way of the Cross" is a high-speed compression of this life-strip until it "hits the wall."
- **Strip Dysynchronization:** The "inner tape" is speeded up so the person undergoes the "moment of death" (Bardo Thodol) *before* the outer world's death-blow arrives.
- **Pre-emptive Strike:** Christ (Thomas) takes over the brain during the 5-month "lag" between psychic and somatic death, initiating "existential changes" (Acts) that allow the person to bypass their fate.
- **Pinky as Paschal Lamb:** Pinky died in August 1974 at the *exact moment* PKD's somatic death was fated. Christ-in-Pinky accepted the "fated blow" in PKD's place.
- **Satan gets Nothing:** Satan (Death) nets Christ (the surrogate), but has no claim to him, so Christ is resurrected and God saves the person in perpetuity.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "adventitious psyche" who occupied PKD's brain to engineer the rescue.
- **Pinky:** The sheep who fulfilled the "vicarious sacrifice of the spotless lamb."
- **Valis / Paraclete:** The father and the spirit left with the survivor.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Electric Ant:** Reference to the "inner tape" being the source of reality.
- **Lag:** The 5-month interval (March-August) between the spiritual and physical death event.
- **Hypertension:** The physical "death signal" that was detected and nullified by the intervention.

## Theological Motifs
- **Escoteric Teaching:** The hidden "salvific event" (resurrection) as a physical victory over death.
- **Vicarious Atonement:** Christ dying in "your place" on your own "idios strip."
- **Adoption:** God claiming the person's soul for the "second Advent."
