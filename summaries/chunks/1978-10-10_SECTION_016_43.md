# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-42

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-42
- **Themes:** Fascist Epistemology, Power of the Will, Shamanic Subversion, The Ruling Class.

## Key Claims
- **Fascist Epistemology:** PKD defines his logic as "fascist" in the Mussolinian sense: "There is no truth. We make truth." The deed and the will create reality; what we believe becomes objectively true.
- **Shamanic Subversion:** His writing hands over "secrets of power" (weapons) to the disenfranchised (misfits/schizophrenics) to subvert the plutocracy.
- **Schizophrenia as Tactic:** Mimicking schizophrenia (null-logic, self-cancelling logic) is a political tactic to "vitiate" those in power.
- **The Gene Pool Victory:** Moving his daughter into the ruling class (Stanford) is his "ultimate victory" over his father and the plutocratic enemy.
- **De-ontologizing Reality:** Reducing reality to "mere stuff" on which the will acts; knowledge is defined in terms of shamanistic power-techniques.

## Entities
- **Hitler/Mussolini:** Cited as archetypes of the "will over reason" and the rise of the disenfranchised.
- **Jason Taverner:** The stripped-of-power man who destroys his authority foe.
- **The Plutocracy:** The "true enemy" (specifically identified with Jews in a brief, provocative passage regarding his daughter's views).

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Will to Deed:** The impersonal will (Brahman) taking control over the thinking ego.
- **Null-Logic (y = -y):** The self-cancelling logic used as a revolutionary weapon.
- **Ma'at:** The cosmic balance/justice achieved through his daughter's success.

## Theological Motifs
- **Impersonal Will:** The force that perturbed reality in 3-74.
- **Post-Rationalism:** The move to 20th-century mythic and spiritual magic because "it works."
- **Nihilism as Tactic:** Values are generated as mere moves in the game of power.
