# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-31

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-31
- **Themes:** The Maze, 3-Eyed People, Humane Saboteurs, Valis Vortex.

## Key Claims
- **The Maze as Project:** Our reality is an irreal "maze" or "project" observed by 3-eyed crab-people from outside.
- **Humane Saboteurs:** The early Christians (Valis faction) are revolutionaries/saboteurs fighting the "old order" of the maze to liberate us.
- **Valis Vortex Evolution:** The project (world) has evolved into a "Valis vortex" which is now consuming/subsuming the irreal world and transubstantiating it into the real.
- **Rational Invading Irrational:** The world was created by an irrational designer; Valis is the "rational" introduced to convert irreal to real and irrational to rational.
- **Unitary Brain:** Humans are becoming part of one unified brain (Valis) as it self-constructs using our reality as stockpile/field.

## Entities
- **3-Eyed Crab People:** The dispassionate observers/controllers outside the maze.
- **The Master Designer:** The deranged/irrational creator of the maze.
- **VALIS/ZEBRA/UBIK:** The real, rational vortex/information center.

## Recurring Concepts
- **BIP (Black Iron Prison):** The rigid shell of the project/maze.
- **Transubstantiation:** The process of the irreal being made real by the Valis pattern.
- **Binary Dialectic:** The 0-1 flip-flop principle used by the computer/maze.

## Theological Motifs
- **Christ as Seed:** Valis was injected into the project in seed-form as Christ to fertilize human hosts.
- **Point Omega:** The final state of total consciousness in a rotating perfect sphere of noos.
- **Platonic Eidos:** The pattern (Valis) is real, while the things patterned (world) are irreal.
