## Key Claims
- The "pulley" vision represents Dick's method of lateral/heuristic thinking: moving orthogonally along a 4th axis (converting time into space) to acquire information *in advance* of normal linear deduction.
- VALIS is the externalized, macrocosmic version of the heuristic feedback loop normally operating between Dick's two brain hemispheres (left vs right, time vs space).
- The syzygy (union) of the two hemispheres creates the "Chemical Wedding" (Ditheon/Firebright), an immortal, bilateral entity capable of surviving death.
- The overarching conclusion of the *VALIS* trilogy is that Christ and the Buddha are homologized as the Maitreya, and that this savior is ultimately YHWH (ABBA), whose core nature is *Agape* (loving-kindness).

## Entities
- **Firebright/Ditheon**: The immortal "mysterious embryo" generated by the alchemical synthesis of Dick's bilateral mind.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Atman-Brahman**: The realization that the internal micro-structure (the two hemispheres of the brain) is perfectly reflective of the external macro-structure (VALIS/The Universe).

## Theological Motifs
- **The Third Dispensation**: Joachim of Fiore's concept. Dick posits that YHWH of the O.T. evolved into ABBA of the N.T., and has now evolved further into the unified ecosphere itself.
