# SECTION_015
# September 15, 1976
# The Conglomerate vs. The King
## concise_summary
Dick synthesizes investigative journalism (*Playboy*, *Rolling Stone*) to conclude that Nixon was a mere "puppet" of a larger technocratic/financial conglomerate. He argues that only a divine force (the "King" from *Flow My Tears*) is strong enough to confront this "Beast" and identifies 3-74 as a prophetic revelation rather than a personal conversion.
## key_claims
- The Conglomerate Conspiracy: Nixon was a figurehead for an industrial/financial/police conglomerate.
- Divine Battle: The current era (Armageddon) features a battle between this conglomerate and divine forces led by Christ (the King).
- Jimmy Carter: Speculates that Carter’s evangelical Christianity might be a factor in this "Armageddon."
- Revelation vs. Mysticism: 3-74 was literal "revelation" like that of the prophets, commissioned to witness the divine counter-attack.
- Justice as Immortal: Cites wisdom literature about the "just man" snatched away from a wicked world by God.
- Haggia Sophia's Return: The "Great Important Books" seen in dreams contain the returning wisdom (*Haggia Sophia*) previously rejected by humanity.
