# Steganography, "Felix," and Rome Two
## concise_summary
Dick reveals a "steganographic" reading of his own work, specifically the "Felix" message hidden within *Flow My Tears*. He argues that the Parousia required a "topological superimposition" (Rome Two) over 1st Century Rome to defeat the Beast (Nixon). He introduces a Right-Hemisphere theory where time flows backward toward a terminal goal.
## key_claims
- Steganography ("Felix"): The dream in TEARS contains a secret message ("Felix" = Live/Prosper) embedded in a "die" message.
- Esoteric vs. Exoteric: Only the "sheep" (flock of Christ) receive the "live" message; the evil ones receive "die."
- Rome Two (Superimposition): 1974 USA was topologically superimposed over 1st Century Rome. Nixon identified as "666".
- Timing is Critical: The "King" (Christ) could only strike when the 2,000-year cycle produced a perfect reprise of Rome.
- Right-Hemisphere Time: Extinguishing the left-brain ego allowed the right brain to see time "flowing backward" toward total assembly (Gestalt).
- The Logos as Architect: The Logos uses the "dialectic of strife" (Empedocles) to built constructs that lie in our linear future.
- Brahman / Atman identity: Dick discovers that Hinduism (Brahmanism) provides the best explanation for his experience: the inner "Atman" is the outer "Brahman."
