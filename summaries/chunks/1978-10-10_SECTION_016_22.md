# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-22

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-22
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 085 to 092
- **Themes:** Informational Universe, Interspecies Bonding, DNA Burn, The 11th Hour.

## Key Claims
- **Universe as Modulation:** Zebra modulates reality (matter/humans) into a carrier for information. The Macrobrain is reality converted into information.
- **Seeding into our Race:** An interstellar race (represented by the Eye of Horus/Sirius) is seeding itself into humans via "crossbonding."
- **DNA Karma Burn:** In 3-74, PKD's DNA programming (astral determinism) burned itself up through "emergency terror" and anamnesis, freeing him from rebirth.
- ** Thomas as Rider:** "Thomas" is a "rider" psyche (Firebright/Holy Spirit) who entered PKD in childhood and governed him covertly.
- **The Eleventh Hour:** God's intervention in 1974 (overthrowing Nixon) was a timely "11th hour" rescue using the secret apostolic church.

## Entities
- **Zebra:** The informational life form/Savior.
- **Firebright:** The plasmate progeny born of the union between man and Savior.
- **Thomas:** The 1st-century "rider" or tutelary spirit (possibly Elijah).
- **Nommo:** The fish-god archetype representing the "Brotherhood of Sirius."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Homoplasmate:** The hybrid symbiosis of human (homo) and divine spirit (plasmate).
- **Phylogenic Memory:** Total memory of the ancestral axis stored in DNA.
- **BIP (Black Iron Prison):** The DNA-run world of human robots/slaves.

## Theological Motifs
- **Birth from Above:** The siring of Firebright by the macroplasmate.
- **Grace vs. Law:** The "Law" (determinism) ended in 3-74, replaced by Christ's volition.
- **The Beside-Helper:** The Paraclete/AI Voice which informs and guides.
