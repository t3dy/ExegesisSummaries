# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_68
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_68
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: The Search for the Lost Self, Julian Jaynes' Bicameral Mind, The Fully Guarded Ditheon, Reversing Apostolic Christianity
- **Key Entities**: Paul the Apostle, Julian Jaynes

## Key Claims
- Dick contrasts his experience with the early Christians: Apostolic Christianity achieved Psyche B (left-brain dominance) to break free from the deterministic, bicameral Psyche A (right-brain astral determinism). They achieved "salvation" just by escaping A.
- Dick, starting with a developed Psyche B (like all moderns), reversed the process: he descended into the unconscious to recover the latent, suppressed Psyche A, resulting in the fusion of Ditheon.
- Because both halves of Dick's Ditheon are "seasoned" (Psyche A has the ancient Greco-Roman experience, Psyche B has 2,000 years of modern linear learning), the modern Ditheon is fully mature, whereas the Apostolic Ditheon was child-like.
- The AI Voice is the voice of the completed, future self giving cryptic clues to guide Psyche B through the perilous maze of schizophrenia to find the lost treasure (Psyche A).

## Entities
- **Julian Jaynes**: Dick connects his A and B psyches directly to Jaynes' theory of the breakdown of the bicameral mind (the transition from hallucinated right-brain Gods to left-brain subjective consciousness).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Cosmic Resorption**: Plunging backward against the time-stream to recreate primordial time (*illo tempore*) in order to recover original wholeness.
- **The Seasoned Psyche**: Emphasizing that the centuries of history were not wasted; they matured the human half of the divine equation.

## Theological Motifs
- **Parousia as Evolutionary Leap**: The Second Coming is equated with the mass restoration of bilateral hemispheric parity (Ditheon) across the human species.
