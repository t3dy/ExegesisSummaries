# The Delusion of 2000 Years: The Palimpsest Present
## concise_summary
Dick proposes a staggering "solution" to his experiences: it is actually still ~90 AD, and the last 2000 years of history is a "lie" or "delusion" generated by Satan/the Empire to break human faith. He views 3-74 as a "rolling back" of this counterfeit time to reveal the underlying apostolic reality.
## key_claims
- The Time Delusion: History is a "great lie" to convince us that Jesus's promise to return "soon" has failed. In reality, very little time has passed.
- It is 90 AD: Dick empirically "saw" Rome c. 70-100 AD in Fullerton; his companion K.W. arrived at this same conclusion a priori.
- Salvation as Victory over Time: Salvation is defined as the "triumph over the planetary powers" (Time/Fate). Dick's 3-74 rollback was literal salvation.
- Time as Palimpsest: Time is not linear but "circular-accruing." The past is "inside" the present, like layers on a parchment that can be exposed.
- Advent Proximity: The 1st and 2nd Advents are "tangent" to every layer of linear time. They are closer to us than they appear.
- The Female Principle: St. Sophia is the "female principle of Holy Wisdom" whose incarnation (in Dick and the world) marks the 2nd Advent.
- Thomas/PKD Duality: Thomas is the "real" identity from 70 AD; PKD is the "mask" from the 1974 delusion.
- Jesus as Permanent Form: Christ was not an image (*eikos*) but a permanent *edolon* (form) that entered our world. He cannot be destroyed, only "hidden."
