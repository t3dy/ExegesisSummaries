# Chunk 185 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_185

## Description
PKD synthesizes his "Broadcasting Circuit" role, realizing that his science fiction (especially *Ubik*, *Maze*, *Tears*, and *Deus Irae*) served as a progressive "print-out" for the Holy Spirit to overtly state the Good News. He identifies the 1974 political events as the "first stage of the apocalypse."

## Key Claims
- **The Author as Transmitter**: PKD views himself as a "machine," "receiver," and "broadcasting circuit" for the Holy Spirit's teleological efforts to communicate.
- **Progressive Disclosure**: The Spirit moved from covert hints (*Ubik*) to steganographic messages (*Tears*) to the overt Kerygma in *Deus Irae*.
- **The "Road to Damascus" in *Tears***: Buckman's conversion in *Tears* is a recap of Saul/Paul's conversion on the road to Damascus. This "Agape conversion" is the solution to the BIP and the "magic" that abolishes its warpage.
- **True Time Resumed**: In 1974, linear time "resumed" where it left off in A.D. 70. The 1974 overthrow of the government represents the historic victory over Rome.

## Entities
- **Buckman**: The official/magistrate whose conversion by a "dream of God" triggers the novel's *Agape* ending.
- **Taverner**: Identified as Christ (the innocent one persecuted by Saul/Buckman).
- **Felix (Roman official)**: Citing the *Jerusalem Bible*, PKD notes the overlap between the historical Felix and the "Felix" in *Tears*.
- **Holy Spirit**: The "Gospel of the Spirit" (Acts) which provides the memory banks for PKD's work.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Agape as Solvent**: The true power issued out of grief that "melts" the BIP's warping signals.
- **Latent vs. Obvious Form**: "Tears" shows how Rome is "latent" within the modern world, and how Advent II brings acquittal instead of condemnation.
- **Teleological Writing**: The idea that the Holy Spirit worked "backward" from the 3-74 experience to "authenticate" the messages in earlier books.
- **The Dismantler (Zebra)**: The Cosmic Christ reweaving the "stockpile" of Form II into the beautiful Form I.

## Theological Motifs
- **Advent II**: Characterized by the "exculpation" and "acquittal" of man, replacing the "Age of Tears."
- **The Road to Damascus**: The central "in-breaking" event for both the early church and PKD's own life.
- **Book of Life / Beatic Vision**: PKD claims to have seen his name in the "Book of Life," confirming his "adoption" into the divine plan.
- **Unified Church of Living and Dead**: The "Corpus Christi" as an undivided living organism that transcends space and time.
