# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-67

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-67
- **Themes:** Puzzle Logic, The "Unfair" Puzzle, Apotheosis through Problem Solving, Retrieval of Identity.

## Key Claims
- **The "Closed" Puzzle:** The Xerox Missive was a puzzle where the answer could not be derived from the puzzle itself. You had to have the answer (the inner solution) years in advance.
- **Solution Preceding Puzzle:** Possession of the solution years prior (in *Faith of* and *MITHC*) proves a mind not bound by time was informing PKD.
- **Apotheosis of the Self:** Intellectually solving the supreme cosmic puzzle forces the "macromind" (God) within the human to regain its forgotten powers and know itself.
- **The "Mirror" Response:** PKD responded "als ob" (as if) he were the deity, suggesting he and the macromind became/are one.
- **Writing as Reality's Blueprint:** The inner script/template came first; world unfolds in accordance to it. PKD is the "author of the world" trapped in his own creation.

## Entities
- **Ubik / Valis:** The vast benign info-processing thinking entity.
- **The Dark-Haired Girl:** The "messenger" of the solution who triggers the analog comparison.
- **The "Great Mind":** The timeless computer-like intelligence (Logos).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Anamnesis:** Memory retrieval that dissolves the I-World dichotomy.
- **Inner Analog Template:** A priori knowledge that is a replica of outer reality (micro = macro).
- **Mimicry:** Either PKD mimicked the entity, or the entity mimicked PKD.

## Theological Motifs
- **God Posing Problems to Himself:** The universe as a divine ritual drama where God rediscovers his identity by solving challenges.
- **Return to Primordial State:** The "anamnesis" is the self-recognition of the Godhead.
- **Bardo Thodol Trip:** Gaining the "Kingdom" by gazing at the precious metal/jewelry.
