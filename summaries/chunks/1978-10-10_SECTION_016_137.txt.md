# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-137

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-137
- **Themes:** Surrogation of Christ, Advanced Foresight, The Ancient Underground, Invading Logos.

## Key Claims
- **Surrogation Necessity:** God cannot "suspend" his own law (*natura naturans*), so he must "surrogate" himself. He substitutes his "Wise Mind" for the human victim at the moment of crisis. This is the "Vicarious Sacrifice."
- **Atman-Brahman Fusion:** Surrogation creates the "Microform of God" within the person (Atman) which acts in conjunction with the "Macroform" (Brahman/Valis).
- **Advanced Synchronization:** If miracles are just "deft use of natural law," then Valis must arrange long sequences of synchronizations years in advance. This is "Pronoia" via foresight.
- **The 52-Year Engram:** PKD spent 52 years (his life) cracking the code of "Tears." The engram points to a revolutionary underground dating back to Moses, Akhnaton, and the fight against Egyptian slavery.
- **Valis as Invader:** Contrasting Spinoza, PKD suggests God is not just immanent but has *invaded* a fallen/artifactual creation to revitalize it. This is the "Zoroastrian Dialectic" (Noos vs. Ananke).

## Entities
- **Christ / Valis:** The "Risen Lord" as a cosmic figure re-entering creation.
- **The Underground:** A body of "Xtian Supermen" (3-eyed people) living in a different universe (Paradise) whose messages ride our media.
- **Moses / Akhnaton:** Founders of the anti-slavery monotheistic revolution.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Engramming:** Information stored in the human species mind (Phylogenic) to be triggered by signs.
- **Kerygma:** The news that Christ died but is alive and returning through the Spirit.
- **Dual Dialectic:** The struggle between Authoritarianism (BIP) and Revolution (Holy Spirit).

## Theological Motifs
- **Transubstantiation as Restoration:** Passing to a "Sacred Time/Space" where the Parousia has already occurred.
- **Passover / Exodus:** The paradigm of the "Escape from the Prison" as the central theme of religion.
- **Logos-Sophia:** The wisdom entity in symbiosis with human "participants."
