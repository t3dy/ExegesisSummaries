# The Synthetic Cosmology: A Seven-Part Fusion
## concise_summary
Dick formally merges seven distinct philosophical and religious traditions into a "Synthetic Cosmology" aimed at defining the 2-source struggle between Zebra (Yang) and the Black Iron Prison (Yin). He defines salvation as "liberation from deterministic programming" and identifies himself as a "re-contoured" being whose history and memories are periodically "edited" by the Logos to replace "worse" tracks of existence.
## key_claims
- The Seven-Part Fusion: PKD's system merges: 1) 3-74 vision, 2) Taoism, 3) Zoroastrianism, 4) Gnosticism, 5) Christianity, 6) Greek philosophy (Timaeus/Empedocles), and 7) Mystery Religions.
- Salvation as De-programming: "Salvation" means the Yang entity (Christ) breaking the Yin world's deterministic "astral" power over the individual.
- Subjugation of Yin: The ultimate goal is the total subordination of the Yin world (Satan/BIP) back into its "proper servant status."
- Jettisoning the Spurious: Our world is a "spurious interpolation"; the "Kingdom of God" is real and will replace it selectively using the Yin world as a "stockpile."
- Trans-Temporal Immortality: Form I (Yang) is changeless and encompasses all time (Crete, A.D. 70, 1974 are all "now").
- Strategic Insult: To deny the existence of the Yin world (Parmenides' route) is a "strategic" move to break its power, even if it has "value" as a secret partner.
