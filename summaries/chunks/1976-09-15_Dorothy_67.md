# The Intercessor: Reweaving the Deterministic Strip
## concise_summary
Dick explores the mechanism of the "Intercessor" (Zebra/Christ) as an "orthogonal programmer" who enters the linear strip to "re-weave" situations. He argues that he was "re-programmed" in 3-74 to handle the Xerox letter, breaking the "back" of his deterministic fate.
## key_claims
- Victory over Determinism: 3-74 was a "colossal victory" where God intruded from a "right angle" to break the causal chain of Dick's life.
- Thomas as Non-Linear: Thomas has "no linear antecedents." He was a "sui generis" (out of nothing) intervention from the "Urwelt" (Original World).
- Reweaving vs. Programming: Zebra is not the "Programmer" (Brahman/Laws of Nature) but the "Reweaver" (Logos/Intercessor) who fixes errors.
- Pre-planned Blocking Moves: God engages in "blocking-moves" against the "Dark Counterplayer," using humans as pieces on a game board.
- Anamnesis as First Step: Before the reweaving could occur, Dick had to be "unhooked" from linear time via anamnesis (restored memory of Christ).
- Participation in the Urwelt: Dick was "plunged into the ancient real-world matrix," freeing him from the "planetary powers" (Heimarmene).
- Mimicking Objects: Zebra "mimics" objects in our world (Zebra-camouflage) to modulate outcomes invisibly.
- The Star Wars Parallel: The rebels blowing up the Death Star is the "archetypal memory" of the Christians defeating the Empire. Nixon's fall is the local manifestation.
