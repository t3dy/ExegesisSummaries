# The Ham Sandwich Revisited: Ubik as Gnosis
## concise_summary
Dick formalizes the "Ham Sandwich" model of reality: Man - World - God. He concludes that his entire writing career was a "preparatory dissolving" of the world-slice, which allowed the "God-slice" (*Ubik*) to break through in 3-74.
## key_claims
- Ham Sandwich Model: Reality is a triune structure. The "World" (Ham) is an "alienating, divisive agency" that separates Man from God (the two bread slices).
- *Ubik* as the Blueprint: *Ubik* accurately depicts this triune division. Runciter (God) sends messages into the "regressed world" (Hallucination) to save the "inertials" (Man).
- 3 Levels of Reality: (1) Hallucinated Good, (2) Prison World (The Iron Fortress), (3) Divine Ground/Kingdom.
- Information from "Behind": Information penetrates reality not from "above" but from "behind" (the other slice of the sandwich).
- *Tears* as the Climax: Dick identifies a "latent/cryptic message" in *Tears* (the dream section) that was revealed to him by the Holy Spirit in 3-74.
- Gnostic Epistemology: Repudiation of the world (Step 1) is the necessary condition for the affirmation of God (Step 2).
- The Xerox Missive: He views his handling of the Xerox letter (passing it to the FBI immediately) as a form of "balking" or "non-cooperation" with a hot artifact of the Empire.
