# The Bible as a Supratemporal Description
## concise_summary
Dick identifies "salvation" as the abolition of linear time, merging Gnostic Christianity with Brahmanism. He argues that by "withdrawing assent" from the phenomenal world, one can pass into a timeless, underlying reality where Rome 70 AD and Fullerton 1974 are a single event.
## key_claims
- Salvation = Death of Time: Since time brings death, salvation must lift us "out of time." 3-74 was the literal abolition of time's dominion.
- The Bible as Map: The Bible is a "supratemporal description" of the timeless, underlying reality (the "Is"), not a book of historical "was."
- Consigning Existence: Dick didn't just "believe" in 70 AD; he "conferred actual physical existence" on it, which broke the power of the fake present.
- Ignorance as the Fall: Following Hinduism, Dick sees the Fall not as moral sin but as the "intellectual error" of taking the phenomenal world as real.
- The Paraclete's assistance: The Holy Spirit (Paraclete) begins the "divinizing" of a person *in this life*, providing palpable help against the astral powers.
- Form Axis Reversion (Ubik): Time can revert backward thousands of years along a "form axis" (orthogonal) rather than a linear axis.
- Washington DC as Rome: Linear time between manifestations (Rome -> US) has no significance; the "jump" is a metamorphosis of the form itself.
- Rollback vs. Decay: Challenges the Western idea of forward-linear-progress. Linear motion = entropy/decay. Orthogonal rollback = entelechy/negentropy (Salvation).
