# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-71

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-71
- **Themes:** Overstepping and Adjustment, The Baited Hook, Sacred Ritual Drama, Acosmic Panentheism.

## Key Claims
- **God as Baited Hook:** PKD was a "carefully fabricated baited hook." Evil (the Empire) disclosed itself by "overstepping" to trap the "garbage" (PKD), only to find it had trapped the Godhead.
- **The Ritual Drama:** 3-74 was a timeless re-enactment of the Christ drama (Zagreus/Christ vs. Pentheus/Rome). The legitimate King enters his Kingdom in humble disguise to expose the pretender tyrant.
- **Valis as Adjustment:** 3-74 was the "adjustment back to plumb" of a disturbed cosmic balance. Valis adjusted reality to conform to the Logos (blueprint).
- **Inversion of Roles:** The "stranger" (God) is degraded into garbage, then lightning-swiftly transmutes into gold/victory.
- **Dreamer Waking Up:** PKD is the "dreamer" in a state of partial wakefulness. VALIS is the dreamer's mind melting the causal trains of the "pseudo-world."

## Entities
- **The Iron Complex (Nixon/The Empire):** The "evil power" that was duped into disclosing itself.
- **Zagreus / Christ:** The "stranger"/God in human garb.
- **Logos / Ubik:** The blueprint/protonarrative that invading "trash" to realign it.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Causal Train Melting:** Adjusting the "bent" universe back to the plumb/Logos.
- **Morphe/Camouflage:** The deceptive appearance of God as "garbage" or "trash."
- **Tetraktys / Ma'at:** The cosmic balance/harmonia that governs the reversal.

## Theological Motifs
- **Panentheism:** The world is the idea of the divine mind; the dreamer contains the dream.
- **Dying to Regain Identity:** The "sacred ritual" of death and resurrection as the only way to reveal the truth.
- **Overstepping (Hybris):** The error of the Empire that leads to its downfall.
