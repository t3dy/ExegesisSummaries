# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-126

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-126
- **Themes:** The Acts Grid, Taverner as Paul, The Reconstituted King, Hidden Underground.

## Key Claims
- **The Acts Grid:** *Flow My Tears* is designed as an "information-extraction grid." Using the *Acts of the Apostles* as a lens, Buckman = Felix (Procurator) and Taverner = Paul (the persecuted Church).
- **Taverner is Guilty:** Without the grid, Taverner is an innocent isolate. With the grid, he is the leader of the "Secret Revolutionary Xtian Underground" (Paul's role as spokesman). 
- **The Reversal of the Text:** The grid doesn't just add info; it inverts the ostensible narrative. Taverner is "guilty" of the very conspiracy the state suspects.
- **1974 as the Victory Year:** The Xtians went on the offensive in 1974 (Nixon's fall), and "Tears" was the "fire signal" or "credentials" for this offensive.
- **The King of Tears Defeated:** Buckman (Pentheus/Empire) is defeated by the "King of Joy" (Dionysos/Christ). The "Woman who dies" (Jane/Alice) is the catalyst.

## Entities
- **Jason Taverner:** The "Paul" archetype / Underground leader.
- **Felix Buckman:** The "Felix Procurator" / Persecutor who undergoes conversion.
- **Ho Kyrios:** The Wise Old King/Resurrected Christ who judges.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Grid Analysis:** Literature as a coded mechanism to identify present-day actors.
- **Dual Dialectic:** The "Empire" vs "Secret Church" struggle spanning planets/aeons.
- **Conversion on the Road:** Buckman's dream as the 3-74/Acts 9 "Damascus Road" experience.

## Theological Motifs
- **Christ-as-Judge:** The "Wise Old King" in the dream represents the return of the Kyrios to reclaim his throne.
- **Exhalation/Inhalation:** Creation (away from God) and Redemption (return to God via Valis).
- **The Secret Church:** An indubitable revolutionary group that uses the grid to recognize its own status.
