# The Magnamind: We are Memory Coils in a Self-Repairing AI
## concise_summary
Dick refines his "Cybernetic Model": the Universe is a "Magnamind" or "Self-Repairing AI Brain System." We are its "memory coils" that have fallen into "forgetfulness and ignorance" (malfunction). Salvation is the "repair" process involving anamnesis (memory retrieval).
## key_claims
- Magnamind / Magna-organism: Reality is the "deliberate intention" of a vast living organism that resembles an AI system.
- Malfunctioning Subcircuit: We are a "distressed fraction" or "impairied section" of this mind. Our perception of "reality" is a collective hallucination caused by this malfunction.
- Salvation as Repair: God (Zebra) is the "Repairman" sent to reactivate the memory subcircuit. 3-74 was a "signal" to fire blocked neural banks.
- Anamnesis as Retrieval: Learning is remembering (Plato). Gnosis is the retrieval of information stored in the "DNA gene-pool true self."
- Acosmic Hallucination: Like in *A Maze of Death*, we are not where or when we think we are. The divine "Abyss" (Boehme) will replace the false world once we wake up.
- Love as Energy: Love is the "boundless energy" and "life-joy" that draws the elements of the system together into a "happy Krasis."
- Close Encounters of the 3rd Kind: Dick views Zebra as "Them" (ETIs or higher powers). He identifies 3-74 as a "Contact of the 3rd Kind" involving "manipulated causality."
