## Key Claims
- Dick asserts that his withholding of taxes was not merely a "good" or culturally relative "moral" action, but a scientifically measurable engagement in reality testing. By verifying that the universe responds to Justice, he achieved empirical proof where others rely on faith.
- He concludes that Injustice actually cannot exist—because Justice (Ma'at) is the ultimate foundation of reality. When mechanical/natural law (astral determinism) clashes with moral law (providence/Ma'at), the moral law overrides and suspends the natural.
- In 3-74, Dick witnessed this exact event: the natural causal order of the Hologram was suspended and altered by providence to protect him as a direct consequence of his just act.
- This transition from mechanical determinism to intentional providence is the ultimate return to the maternal: Ma'at is identified as the Great Mother (Ubik, Zebra, VALIS). Aligning with her brings you home to a safe, volitional cosmos.

## Entities
- **Ma'at**: Equated with Zebra and VALIS. She is the structural order created by Re and is female, representing the universe operating intentionally rather than mechanically.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Hologram Override**: The artificial, deterministic lower realm (the Black Iron Prison) is overridden by the upper volitional realm whenever true cosmic Justice is invoked.

## Theological Motifs
- **Volitional Providence**: The universe operates as a blind, mechanical cause-and-effect system (Karma/Heimarmene) *until* a human introduces true Ma'at, at which point the universe wakes up and displays sentience and providence.
