## Key Claims
- Replacing the discontinuous matter model with the continuum model (which quantum mechanics is beginning to do) will usher in an axial age, returning divine providentia and teleology to the realm of rational, empirical science rather than supernatural religion.
- True cosmos, as Pythagoras defined it, is a highly ordered, living organism possessing a high degree of information (negentropy). Therefore, experiencing thoughts as physical arrangements of objects is precisely what "cosmos" literally means.
- Plotinus' concentric rings of emanation map perfectly to this: the atomized material world is simply the lowest, fallen ring of reality, while the unitary continuum (cosmos) is a higher, more real ring existing in the exact same space.
- The Atomist model signifies the Fall of man's perception. Plato was the last rational man attempting to reverse this fall utilizing pure intelligible reason (noesis and anamnesis).

## Entities
- **Pythagoras**: Credited with coining the term "cosmos" to mean a perfectly ordered, living, proportionate structural totality.
- **Plotinus**: His Neo-Platonist model explains how the fallen, atomized world and the unified, divine continuum can co-exist simultaneously.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Universe as Information**: Redefined here not just as a metaphor, but as the literal structural requirement for the universe to be a negentropic "cosmos."

## Theological Motifs
- **Anamnesis as Ascent**: Because the fall was an epistemological error (losing the continuum), remembering (anamnesis) the intelligible forms immediately reverses the fall and restores the soul to the higher realm.
