# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-38

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-38
- **Themes:** Psychotic Macro-Mind, Reality Dissolving Agent, Plasmate as Medication, Anamnesis Miracle.

## Key Claims
- **The Psychotic God:** The macro-mind (the brain/world) has a "congealed complex" (the BIP) which has stopped the dialectic, making it psychotic.
- **Zebra as Phagocyte:** Zebra/Valis invades the macro-mind to battle and dissolve the stagnant BIP complex (rationality vs. irrationality).
- **PKD's Corpus as Toxin:** His writing is a "dissolving agent" meant to break up the frozen hypostasis (reality) so time can start up again.
- **Anamnesis defined:** The miracle where the God takes over the internal self, abolishes time, and relocates the percipient to Acts AD 45.
- **Plural Worlds Verification:** PKD's experience of being in Acts proves we live in plural worlds; reality isn't one objective thing but a jurisdiction.

## Entities
- **Zebra / VALIS:** The salvific medication/invader.
- **The BIP Complex:** The stagnant, entropic malformation in the Godhead.
- **The "Little Flock":** The secret early Christians under Christ's direct rule.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Metabolic Toxin:** The "Holy Spirit" as a medication to dissolve the BIP complex.
- **Valence of the BIP:** The force that slows the dialectic and leads toward entropy/uniformity.
- **AI Voice:** The spirit/Logos that informs the "initiate" (PKD) about the return of Christ.

## Theological Motifs
- **Body and Blood:** The Plasmate (Blood/Info) and Valis (Body/Camouflaged Invader).
- **Apostolic Secret:** Only the original Christians possess the "trick" of staying awake across frames.
- **Grace as extrication:** The act of taking the individual out of the "planetary law" (astral determinism).
