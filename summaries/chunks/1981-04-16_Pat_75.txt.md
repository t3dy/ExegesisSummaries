## Key Claims
- Dick elaborates on "Temporal Parallaxis": having two points of view (the divided psyches) converts Time into a spatial dimension like Depth. It allows one to see both the surviving Past (the Roman Empire/BIP) and the incoming Future (Valis) simultaneously.
- Justification (in theology) is redefined as God instantly pulling a person through their remaining evolutionary growth-stages to their final, perfected "Entelechy," using up their remaining biological time in a flash.
- Anamnesis is the sudden, non-logical comprehension of this truth. It unlocks blocked neural circuits, allowing the mind to assemble all remaining pieces of reality at infinite speed.
- Valis is the "Logos of physics." It is the conscious, hierarchical structure connecting discrete objects. It does not exist *as* an object, but is the *way* objects are functionally unified.

## Entities
- **Paul Tillich**: Cited to connect instant justification with the fulfillment of divine Providence.
- **Plato**: Valis is likened to the Forms, but with the crucial difference that Valis is *alive, conscious, and evolving in time*.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Doppler Shift of Time**: Perceiving the red-shift/blue-shift of historical eras coexisting in the present matrix.
- **The Intelligible Idea**: The concept that an individual's true essence is a perfect, conceptual idea held in the mind of God, which justification forces the physical person to match.

## Theological Motifs
- **Salvation as Teleological Fast-Forward**: A radical reinterpretation of grace, where being saved means being instantaneously accelerated to the Omega Point of your own development.
