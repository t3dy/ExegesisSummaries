# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-99

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-99
- **Themes:** Aristotelian Entelechy, Information Feedback, Ubik as Truth, The Gray Corporation.

## Key Claims
- **Valis as Entelechy:** Valis is the "organism of organisms" (Kosmos) that structures the disparate pieces of the universe into a rational unity.
- **Ubik situation verified:** The fact that world consists of "forgotten memories fed back" (Lem's model) is the "crucial clue" that the "Ubik" situation is the literal truth.
- **Homeostasis = Monitoring:** Valis is a highly homeostatic control system that feeds info back to its earlier self to correct errors.
- **The Gary Corporation:** A dream symbol for US-G2 (Gary Powers) planting evidence (the Xerox missive) to "smoke out" suspects.
- **Winning by Being Adopted:** The "Interventive Remastered" part of our life is a "reward program" where we triumph via divine adoption.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "second time around" psyche who survived the crisis.
- **Gary Powers:** The pilot who had forged Soviet documents (analogous to the Xerox missive).
- **The Workman:** The immanent deity "concealed" in the workshop of the world.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Pronoia:** Divine foresight preparing for the crisis years in advance (via PKD's books).
- **Memory Feedback loop:** The program is being "rerun and improved" each time.
- **Isomorphism:** The individual (PKD) is a "micro-template" of the macro-booster (Valis).

## Theological Motifs
- **Doctrine of Grace:** The choice of the "monitoring system" to adopt/save a specific component.
- **Covenant House:** The "price" or "merit" that led to PKD's adoption by the divine.
- **Ich bin der Retter:** The recurring "I am the Savior" thought while in altered states.
