## concise_summary
Dick continues analyzing the "reweaving" of reality in 3-74, suggesting it was part of a global "extrication" from the Black Iron Prison. He discusses the "personality switch" from PKD to Thomas and the idea that history is a repeating "symphony" being rewritten from the "Omega Point" backward.
## key_claims
- Global Extrication: 3-74 wasn't just for Dick; it was a "general release" of humanity from the B.I. Prison (Watergate, falls of juntas in Greece/Portugal).
- Landscape Switch: It wasn't just a subjective change; the "background" (landscape) was switched or rewoven orthogonally.
- Thomas as Waking Self: Thomas is not a "memory" but the "restored real personality" matched to the "real 70 AD world."
- Repetition with Variations: We relive segments of linear time (like the period leading to the Xerox letter) with God's support so that we "succeed" this time.
- The 2nd Advent Visibility: Christ (Zebra) has no physical body; he uses "theolepsy" to speak/act through the "poor in spirit" (e.g., poor humans, SF novels).
- Linear Time as Gnostic Gutter: 3-74 dreams from a "vantage point" looking down into the "stagnant pond" of lower, linear life forms.
- Re-playing for Judgement: Spurious long time exists so the "deceivers" (Satan's agents) reveal themselves, thinking God's promise has failed.
- The 3-5 Gate: Zebra/Valis entered through a specific "gate" (the 3:5 window) to complete the 1st Advent.
