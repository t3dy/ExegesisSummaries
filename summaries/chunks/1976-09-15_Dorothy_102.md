# Shifting Reality Tracks
## concise_summary
Dick discusses the concept of "tracks" or parallel versions of reality. He suggests that his 3-74 experience involved a literal shift from one version of history to another, orchestrated by the Noos (Logos) to correct a defective reality.
## key_claims
- Multiple Tracks: Reality consists of multiple branching tracks of time and history.
- Reality Shifting: Conscious beings can be moved between tracks by a higher intelligence.
- Logos Weaver: The Logos (Noos) acts as a re-weaver of reality, mending broken or "spurious" timelines.
- 3-74 as Correction: The 1974 experience was a transition to a "better" track where the Nixonian state was dismantled.
