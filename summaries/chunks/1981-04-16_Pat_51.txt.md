# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_51
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_51
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Waking Up from the Dream, The Return of the Waking Self, Pretextual Cause as Final Cause, Teleology from the Future
- **Key Entities**: Aristotle, Albert Einstein, Angel Archer (Sophia)

## Key Claims
- Dick revises the "invasion" model of the Ditheon: the adventitious psyche (Thomas) did not invade; rather, Thomas is the true *waking self* returning to a dreamer (PKD). This return is the ultimate "uncanny one-way intrusion from outside."
- Normal sleep is defined by the absence of the waking self; when the waking self returns, it shatters the dream. 2-3-74 was exactly this: the genuine conscious ego returning and saying "I Am" (Anokhi).
- The "Person" (in the Christological sense of "two natures, one person") is synthetically projected by the two complementary halves (Thomas and PKD). This new, divine entity is Angel Archer/Sophia.
- He solves the mystery of "pretextual cause" (effects preceding causes) using Aristotle's concept of *teleological/final cause*: Valis is an entity from the *future* acting retroactively on the present, tugging reality toward itself.
- He experienced temporal displacement—time flowing backward—because the organizing locus of Valis exists in the highly evolved future, not the past.

## Entities
- **Aristotle**: His concept of "Final Cause" (the Prime Mover pulling everything toward its perfected end state) is used to explain how Valis operates retroactively from the future.
- **Albert Einstein**: Mentioned regarding spatial vs. temporal displacement (bending light).

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Dreamer Awakes**: Re-framing the 1974 experience not as acquiring a second mind, but as the true mind returning from sleep.
- **Retrograde Time**: The perception of effects preceding their causes because the true cause originates in the future.

## Theological Motifs
- **Two Natures, One Person**: Applied directly to his own mind; the human/irrational and the saintly/rational combine to project a singular, divine "Person" (Angel Archer/Sophia).
