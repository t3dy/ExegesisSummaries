# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-06

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-06
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 20 - 032 to 039
- **Themes:** Taoist Gnosticism, The 4-pronged Deformation, Predestination, The "Wurm".

## Key Claims
- **Taoist Overlay:** PKD reconciles Gnosticism with Taoism: Yin sinks because it is "thick, unthinking" (soma); Yang rises because it is Noos.
- **牺牲 (Sacrifice):** Yang violates its own nature to descend and save Yin. This is the "highest law": helping the part that doesn't know by abandoning one's own knowing.
- **The 4-pronged Deformation:** The fallen state consists of: 1) Irreal world, 2) Perceptual occlusion, 3) Deterministic enslavement, 4) Amnesia.
- **The "Wurm" Slain:** Salvation is the removal of the original curse ("the Wurm") from the nervous system. Something evil in PKD died in 3-74.
- **Predestination:** PKD accepts he was "doomed to be saved." Christ (the Good Shepherd) chooses his flock; it is not based on merit but on calling.

## Entities
- **The Good Shepherd:** Christ as the selective savior.
- **James-James:** A name for the bungling/insane creator deity.
- **The Wurm:** The deterministic "curse" or toxin.
- **Philip (The Raft Boy):** A symbol of the invading divine self in disguise.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Krasis:** The unity of love (Empedocles).
- **Bilateral Parity:** The need for right-brain functioning to achieve truth.
- **Red-Herringed:** Outwitting the intelligence agencies through superior (divine) tech.

## Theological Motifs
- **Divine Justice:** The "Beetle" act of mercy (micro) triggering a macro response (the Savior's return).
- **Adoptionism:** "You did not choose me; I chose you."
- **The 11th Hour:** The rescue of history before total decomposition.
