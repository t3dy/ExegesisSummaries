# Claudia
## concise_summary
Conclusion of Section 1. Dick summarizes the "Great Cycle" of the Earth: a seasonal move from Greco-Roman "Spring" to 2000 years of "Winter" (100 AD to 1970 AD), followed by the current "Rebirth" or return to life.
## key_claims
- Jesus and Zagreus are the "root of the vine" slumbering under the snow of Winter.
- The 2000 years from 100 AD to 1970 AD were the "Dead Winter" of our species.
- The current Rebirth involves the "snow" (world of appearances) melting to reveal the true Earth beneath.
- We share an "endless rebirth" with the Christos/Dionysos.
