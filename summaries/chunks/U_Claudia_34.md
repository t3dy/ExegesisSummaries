# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his experience as a literal "Passover Epiphany" occurring in August 1974, coinciding with Nixon’s resignation. He concludes that God used "plagues" to pull down the modern Egypt (Nixon's administration) and that Christ's role as a voluntary sacrifice reversed the logic of the first Passover.
## key_claims
- The "Passover Epiphany" (August 1974) was an actual event in space-time, not a head-trip.
- Nixon's Resignation = The fall of Pharaoh; Nixon's phlebitis/hospitalization = The "plagues."
- The First Passover (Exodus): God spared the innocent and killed the guilty (Egyptians).
- The Second Passover (Crucifixion): The ultimately good/innocent man died; the guilty were spared (Reversed logic).
- Voluntary Sacrifice: Christ (God) died *in our stead* as a surrogate, which is a higher moral evolution than the animal sacrifice of the First Passover.
- Third Passover/Covenant: Recent events (Pinky's death) might signal a new covenant concerning the "ecosphere and animals."
