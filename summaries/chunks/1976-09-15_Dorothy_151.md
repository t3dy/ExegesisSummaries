# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_151
## Chunk Description
Discusses the "Summit Conflict" of 3-74, the triple exposure of lives, and the macro-mind's injection into temporal forms.

## Key Claims
- **Summit Conflict:** 3-74 was a "summit conflict" between sophisticated present technology (USSR psychotronics) and a superior future technology (Zebra/VALIS).
- **Triple Exposure:** PKD experienced a "superimposition" of three selves: Thomas (A.D. 70), PKD (1974), and the Future Self (3-eyed human/Macro-self).
- **The Macro Mind as Christ:** The macro-mind (the "Injection Macro-entity") is identified as Christ or the Holy Spirit.
- **Coaxial Worlds Strategy:** These multiple lives are "mounted along an axis orthogonal to linear time," all contemporary and superimposed.
- **Thomas as "First-Fruits":** The "Thomas" personality is the primary "injection" into history, linking the apostolic age to the future.

## Entities
- **Thomas (A.D. 70):** The primary temporal injection.
- **Zebra/VALIS:** The macro-mind, an UTI (Ultra-Terrestrial Intelligence).
- **Ted Sturgeon:** Refereed regarding the "Circle of Selves" theory.
- **USSR Psychotronics:** The "microwave psycho-energetics" beam countered by the future self.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Orthogonal Time:** The axis of simultaneous lives.
- **Track Transfer:** Moving between alternative tracks (A, B, C) with associated amnesia.
- **Tachyons:** Possible carrier for future-to-past communication.
- **Symbiosis:** The relationship between humans (animals?) and the Holy Spirit.

## Theological Motifs
- **Holy Spirit as Macro-Mind:** The plasmatic energy for whom all things are one.
- **Theolepsy:** The divine takeover that breaks the "script" of a given life.
- **Eternal Apostolicity:** Apostolic Christians as supratemporal entities spanning millennia.
