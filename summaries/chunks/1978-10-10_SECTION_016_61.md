# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-61

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-61
- **Themes:** Pythagorean Shamanism, Kosmos as Beauty, The Morphological Dialectic, Thomas as Phylogenic Self.

## Key Claims
- **Pythagorean Reality:** Reality is based on form and number, not substance. "Kosmos" means ultimate beauty through order.
- **Living Gnosis:** Teachings (Gnosis) grow as part of character and possess life/volition. They "know" whom to visit and when to depart (e.g., Ubik). 
- **The "Sustain" in Dialectic:** Individual creatures are "frames." The "sustain" is the phylogenic entity (the lineage/phylum). 
- **Phylogenic Memory:** Thomas is not a former life but the "sustain" of PKD's individual form-axis—a superself that remembers Acts because the phylum was there.
- **The Miracle as Pointer:** 3-74 was a "chink in the [curtain]" designed to point to the existence (esse) of God, not his attributes.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The phylogenic/species-memory self.
- **Ubik:** The selective, antidotal life-form.
- **Apollo / Delphic Sibyl:** Pythagorean/Shamanistic regulators.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Anamnesis:** Penetrating to the form-axis depth of the human phylum.
- **Bilateral Mind:** Fusion of hemispheres creating a "Micro-Macro-cosm mirroring."
- **Kosmos:** Order/limit giving numerical structure to the "unlimited outside."

## Theological Motifss
- **Theophany Once Removed:** The miracle indirectly proves God's existence by being unexplainable.
- **Pistis Sophia:** A blitz of certitude that creates "kosmos" within, allowing one to see it without.
- **Adoptive Grace:** God using "Marie [Laupe]" or other agents to save the soul from "Satan/K-W."
