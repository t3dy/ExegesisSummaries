# Chunk 183 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_183

## Description
PKD views his life and writing as a "Technological Spy Mission" where he acts as an unaware "sleeper agent" for the divine. He recognizes the "split-brain bipolarization" in his work, such as Bob Arctor in *Scanner*, as an analog of his own two identities (PKD and Thomas).

## Key Claims
- **The Sleeper Agent (Thomas)**: PKD is two people—a " surface" personality (PKD) and a "hidden" one (Thomas). Thomas is a "cop with amnesia" sent by superiors (the Divine) to monitor the terrestrial BIP.
- **Mission Accomplished**: The publication of *Tears* and *Scanner* was like "Bruce (Arctor) reporting back with the evidence" of the BIP's slave-labor system.
- **Victim as Savior**: Only by being a "degraded drug-destroyed freak" (Arctor/PKD) could the divine spy penetrate the BIP undetected and witness the "ugly secret truth."
- **Secrecy as Protection**: Knowledge of his identity was suppressed ("amnesia") so he wouldn't "tell them" (the BIP) under interrogation.

## Entities
- **Bob Arctor / Bruce / Fred**: The split-personality protagonist of *A Scanner Darkly* used as an analog for PKD.
- **New-Path**: The "deceiving apparatus" in *Scanner* that masquerades as moral while creating slave labor.
- **The Nova Police (Burroughs)**: A model for the higher authorities that "programmed" PKD/Thomas.
- **Thomas**: The hidden apostolic personality with amnesia.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Disinhibition / Trigger Stimulus**: The "Golden Fish" sign served as the "little blue flower" to trigger the sleeper agent's memory.
- **Bipolarization of the Brain**: Using the "split brain" model to explain the separation of the "unaware cop" from the "suffering victim."
- **Slave-Labor Basis**: The true secret of the BIP (Rome/USA) as disclosed through Arctor's infiltration.
- **Victim as Savior/ Savior as Victim**: The central paradox of the Christ story applied to the spy mission.

## Theological Motifs
- **Divine Predestination**: PKD feels his life was planned "from the start of eternity" (including his infancy) to participate in this mission.
- **The Unmerited Grace of Choice**: Christ chose PKD to follow him, rather than PKD choosing Christ.
- **The Corporate Body of Christ**: The church is a "real and sacramental" organism containing both the living and the dead unified by the Risen Lord.
- **The Alchemy of Redemption**: Going "all the way downhill" (degradation/death) is the necessary path for the birth of Christ within the person.
