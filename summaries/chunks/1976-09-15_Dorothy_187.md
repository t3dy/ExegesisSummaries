# Chunk 187 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_187

## Description
PKD investigates the "Satanic Substratum" of reality, concluding that Rome A.D. 70 is the "true and only" world, currently concealed by our lack of memory (anamnesis). He argues that *Flow My Tears* is a "textbook" showing how the ancient reality is gilded over with a modern facade.

## Key Claims
- **The Substantial Core**: Rome/Acts is the -not a- world. It has been there all along, but humans lack the "long-term memory" (template) to recognize it.
- **Anamnesis as Restoration**: The "Golden Fish" sign did not just enhance memory but "restored" it, allowing PKD to see the "antithetical confrontation" between Rome and the secret Christians.
- **"Tears" as the Vital Information**: The information in *Flow My Tears* (written in 1970) is more important than the 3-74 experience because it establishes the Acts stratum (Felix/King/the conversion).
- **The Scrambler Theory**: The Satanic power "scrambles" or "deludes" our perception, while Zebra (Christ) acts as the "signal unscrambler."

## Entities
- **Rome (A.D. 70)**: The "temporal constant" and personification of Satanic power.
- **Buckman / Saul**: The official whose conversion in *Tears* mirrors Paul's conversion on the road to Damascus.
- **The Black Eunuch**: Reference to the conversion in Acts 8:26-40, which PKD sees "melded" into the Felix character.
- **Felix (Happy) King**: The hidden proclamation that the "King/God" is here.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Signal Unscrambler**: The divine process that pulls the "template" of Rome forward as the visual foreground (set) instead of background (ground).
- **Temporal constant**: Higher reality entities like Rome and Zebra that "penetrate" the "onion rings" of history like electricity through iron.
- **Replicating Virus (Burroughs)**: The idea that Rome is a self-shaping, functional organism (BIP).
- **Apostolic Christians**: The "early secret Christians" who use the fish sign and undermine Rome.

## Theological Motifs
- **Victory Over Rome**: The victory occurs in the "real time of Realm I," not in the linear time of history.
- **The Two Disinhibitions**: The cessation of "spurious data" and the "augmentation" of genuine data (Rome) to allow for pattern recognition.
- **Satanic Interpolation**: The belief that we have been "made to forget" our true situation in the first century.
- **The "Us" of the Underground**: PKD's realization that "I am one of them"—an apostolic Christian fighting the BIP.
