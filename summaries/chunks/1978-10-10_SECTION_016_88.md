# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-88

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-88
- **Themes:** Pleroma Memories, Super-Race Technology, Resurrection as Workshop, The Divine Transmutation.

## Key Claims
- **Pleroma Memories:** 3-74 "Between-incarnation" memories of the Pleroma (the divine fullness) flooding the self.
- **Knowledge of ETI/Super-Race:** The "Plasmate" info-traffic belongs to a superior "super race" or "ETI" technology embedded within our own.
- **Ubik as Memory Trace:** The original idea for *Ubik* came from a memory of a prior post-mortem/Pleroma state.
- **The Workshop of God:** "Resurrection" is a process in a workshop where the "shavings" (the degraded) are transmuted into the "highest." The workman (God) is concealed within the process.
- **Final Cause (Teleology):** The most important proof of God is the "final cause"—the intentional fitting together of everything into a structure.

## Entities
- **The Workman:** The immanent deity concealed in the world-process.
- **Ubik:** The force of "forward time" and "vitality" that negates our "dying soul" condition.
- **Bhodasattva:** PKD's role as someone returning from the Pleroma with advanced knowledge to "do battle."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Idios vs. Koinos:** The individual "dying" tape vs. the restorative Universal Logos.
- **Steganography:** The past is cryptically contained within the present (fossils).
- **Phylogenic Strata:** Layers of historical memory (Acts) synchronizing with the world.

## Theological Motifs
- **Alchemical Transmutation:** The N.T. as a code for the "degradation-compression" process leading to resurrection.
- **Pleroma to Malkuth:** The soul's "descent" as a journey of forgetting, and its return as a "re-animation" by the divine.
- **Universal Restoration:** The eventual unique complex where the "picture" finally equals the "map/blueprint."
