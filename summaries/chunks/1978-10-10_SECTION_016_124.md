# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-124

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-124
- **Themes:** Logarithmic Spiral of Time, The Unmoved Mover vs. ETI, Hierarchical Subversion, The Stealth of God.

## Key Claims
- **Up-by-Bootstraps Paradox:** A feedback loop where 1970 thoughts (Thomas) affect 1950 (the notes) and vice versa. Linear time is violated.
- **Valis as Mover-Designer:** Valis is a fusion of Spinoza's immanent deity and Aristotle's "Unmoved Mover" which "inspires goal-seeking" through beauty and design rather than force.
- **Hierarchical Subversion:** God works "up from below" using minimum effort (stealth) to topple high-level systems ("for want of a nail..."). Tampering is artfully concealed as "natural law."
- **Pretextual Persistence:** Valis preserves the appearance of plausible/natural causes (the "pretext") to keep itself hidden, suggesting human belief is important for the "biosphere."
- **Point Omega:** The goal of the "Designer-Artifact" (Valis) which assembles itself from the "antecedent universe" acting as a stockpile.

## Entities
- **Valis:** The "Homeostatic Designer-Artifact."
- **The Dark Counterplayer:** The system/entity corrected by Valis.
- **Spinoza / Aristotle:** Philosophical archetypes for Valis' operation.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Log Spiral:** The shape of time as it converges toward Point Omega.
- **Mimesis:** God camouflaged into nature (mimicking natural law).
- **A priori Foresight:** The "Wise Mind's" advantage over the "Backward-looking" adversary.

## Theological Motifs
- **Immanent vs. Adventitious:** Is God part of the system or an intruder? Valis seems to be an "adventitious subverter" lodged within.
- **The Gift of God:** Faith as the condition that allows the "Vaster Universe" (Kingdom) to replace the "Regular" one.
- **St. Elmo's Fire:** The playful, elusive nature of the divine.
