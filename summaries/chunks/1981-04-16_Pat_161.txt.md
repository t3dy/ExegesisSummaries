# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_161
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_161
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Christ as Hyper-Information, The 23rd Hebrew Letter, Rogue Information System, Overruling Lex Talionis
- **Key Entities**: Eliade, Mani, Hainuwele

## Key Claims
- The universe acts as an information carrier that has been infiltrated and modulated by rogue "hyper-information"—which is Christ (the plasmate/blood of Christ).
- This hyper-information sabotages the primary information system (Heimarmene/physical law and the Torah/Lex Talionis) by blocking the retributive machinery and introducing freedom and mercy.
- The universe was originally created using 22 Hebrew letters (per the *Sepher Yezirah*), representing a rigid, mechanical system of justice. Christ acts as a new, missing 23rd letter that, when added, transforms the system into the Messianic Age of mercy.
- Christ is camouflaged within the world, particularly in the "trash stratum" and the self-sacrificing vegetable kingdom, making the consumption of plants and animals an unknowing, cosmic re-enactment of the Eucharist.

## Entities
- **Hainuwele**: The dema-deity from myth whose murdered and dismembered body became the food crops for humanity, which Dick explicitly aligns with the Manichaean concept of *Jesus patibilis*.
- **Eliade**: Cited for his observations on how primitive tribes understand the murdered deity who sinks into the world, which Dick relates to Christ camouflaged in the world.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Hyper-Information**: Information that does not merely refer to a thing but *is* the thing it refers to.
- **Rogue Information**: The subversive, divine signal (Grace) that hacks into the deterministic, "official" operating system of the cosmos (Karma/Heimarmene).

## Theological Motifs
- **The Third Epoch**: The Messianic Age brought about by the 23rd letter, where the inflexible machinery of justice is replaced by the flexible intelligence of forgiveness and mercy.
