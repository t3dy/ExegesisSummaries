# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-159

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-159
- **Themes:** Relativity and Time-Binding, The 4 Propositions, Re-absorption of Creation, Sein vs. das Nichts.

## Key Claims
- **The 4 Fundamental Propositions:** 1) Reality is epiphenomenal. 2) It depends on a unitary sentient being (Valis). 3) Valis is YHWH. 4) YHWH has been "successfully transplanted" and is here.
- **Time-Binding Paradox:** Valis is both hyper-fast (covers centuries in minutes) and hyper-slow (congealed in terms of change). This resolves "relativity" issues between the control and the system.
- **Respiration of God:** Creation as an "exhalation" (extrusion into epiphenomena) and "inhalation" (re-absorption into absolute ontology).
- **The Essential Issue:** The issue is not Good/Bad but "Being vs. Non-Being" (*Sein vs. das Nichts*). God is the "guarantor of Being," and his theodicy is the act of "causing Being per se."
- **Search for the Real:** PKD's lifelong search was for the "Real" (Epistemology), and he found it in YHWH, whose presence makes reality "actual" rather than "mock."

## Entities
- **Valis / YHWH:** The underlying generator of reality and "guarantor of substantia."
- **Elijah:** The prophet whose "low murmuring voice" identified the situation.
- **Alto Carmel:** The dream location (Mount Carmel) where the Fire was called down.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Phylogenic Memory:** The "expanded present" of the macro-organism (Valis) which sees 70 AD to 1974 AD as one moment.
- **Scanning:** The "speed" at which the divine entity perceives and acts upon the temporal system.
- **Bimodal Time:** Time as both Space (transversed) and Energy (consumed).

## Theological Motifs
- **Adopted by God:** The "man who becomes God" (Christ/Thomas) upon being "let out of prison."
- **Emmanuel:** "God with us"—the realization that the transcendent has entered the immanent world.
- **Deus Sive Nature:** Spinoza's God re-evaluated as the *Substantia* or *Esse* of reality.
