# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-44

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-44
- **Themes:** Left-Right Brain Oscillation, 2-Signal Output, Simulated Bardo state, The Hidden Real.

## Key Claims
- **Left-Right Brain Dialectic:** We move from frame to frame (left-right oscillation) like a stereo stylus. PKD and Thomas inhabit the brain as separate "channels" normally decoded but now combined.
- **2-Signal Output:** PKD's writing (especially *Tears*) is the output of two combined signals: his world and Thomas' world.
- **The Bardo Thodol World:** We are in a "half-dead" state like in *Ubik* or *Eye*. Our prison is the result of an "irrational thought" by the macro-brain (The "clot" or BIP).
- **Self-Programming Success:** 3-74 proved that we "hot-wire" our own reality; PKD's previous thoughts warped the 3-74 field, proving the world's dream-like nature.
- **The "Thomas" perturbation:** Thomas (still in Acts) perturbed PKD's USA 1974 reality. The past is "real" and invaded/modulated the present "dream."

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "Siddhartha" or avatara of the right hemisphere.
- **PKD/Bob:** The "Fred" or persona of the left hemisphere.
- **The Macro-Brain:** A deranged/psychotic entity rescued by the invasion of Christ (VALIS).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Stereo Stylus Oscillation:** The model for how two selves occupy one brain.
- **Inner-Outer Taped Sync:** The mechanism that gives the illusion of external cause; in 3-74, it resynchronized itself (a "dead giveaway").
- **Fourier Transform:** Resonance at a frequency that activates internal memory holograms.

## Theological Motifs
- **Hyper-time C:** The only real time; the state where no time has passed since Acts.
- **The Rescue:** Christ (VALIS) invades from outside the "clot" to awaken the macro-mind.
- **Dibba Cakkhu Eye:** The divine eye used by Thomas to see that the world is irreal.
