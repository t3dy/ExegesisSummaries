# Final Synthesis of the Magnamind Theory
## concise_summary
Dick concludes this sub-batch of Section 15 with a synthesis of the "Magnamind" theory: the universe as an AI-managed simulation that is currently being invaded and salvaged by the Urgrund. Human "release" is the ultimate goal.

- Transmuted Artifact: The machine is being turned into a living divine entity.
- Release: Humanity is being transitioned from "programmed" entities to free divine beings.
