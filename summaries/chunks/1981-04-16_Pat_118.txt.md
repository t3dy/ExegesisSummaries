- Dick officially designates Manichaeism as "true Christianity." Mankind is the divine light deliberately sacrificed and swallowed by Darkness to drug the demonic realm, waiting to awake and be unmixed.
- Valis in 3-74 was the exact moment Dick saw "blind necessity" (Satan's power/Karma) dissolve. He witnessed the timeless Eucharist: Christ as both High Priest and sacrificial victim paying the ransom to set him free.
- By accepting Christ's blood as ransom (a classic Protestant/Lutheran view of justification), the chains of astral determinism and causality fell away.
