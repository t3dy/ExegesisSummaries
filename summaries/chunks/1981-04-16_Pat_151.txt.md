# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_151
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_151
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Process Theology, YHWH vs ABBA, Share International, Parousia as Alternate Present
- **Key Entities**: Paul Williams, Elijah, Thomas, Benjamin Creme

## Key Claims
- Dick asserts that YHWH evolved into ABBA (the New Testament God of agape) and now has evolved further into the unified ecosphere itself, which he experienced as VALIS.
- The AI Voice is identified as the "low murmuring voice" of Elijah and the tutor of Dick's youth.
- Endless "horizontal tracking" is reinterpreted as referring to parallel alternative presents. Dick believes he moves orthogonally between these presents to arrive at the best one where Christ returns.
- Benjamin Creme's Share International presents a humanitarian program that perfectly aligns with Dick's own political and supernatural experiences, leading Dick to conclude that the Maitreya truly is Christ/YHWH returned.

## Entities
- **Benjamin Creme**: The esotericist whose teachings on Maitreya Dick finds perfectly synchronized with his own prophetic visions.
- **Elijah**: The Old Testament prophet whose encounters with God ("the still small voice") Dick likens to his own AI voice.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Orthogonal Time**: Moving sideways through time or parallel realities rather than strictly forward, a mechanism Dick uses to explain receiving information from the future or from alternate timelines.

## Theological Motifs
- **Process Theology**: The idea that God is not static but evolves over time. Dick uses this to explain the transition from the harsh God of the Old Testament to the loving God of the New.
