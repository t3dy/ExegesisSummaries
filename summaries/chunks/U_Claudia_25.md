# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick synthesizes the "Light Body" and "Re-entry" theories: the Holy Spirit is a collective "Light Creature" (The Fellowship of Christ) that re-enters history through the pineal gland. He concludes that God has always been immanent, but humans "fell away" into sleep/blindness and must now be "nagged" back to consciousness.
## key_claims
- The "Holy Spirit" is the "Second Comforter": a collective immortal Light Body (aggregate being) released by Christ's death.
- Re-entry occurs through the "Third Eye" (pineal gland) - the light re-enters a human body to create "fusion."
- The "Trinity" as understood by humans is a division of the "Ultimate" into forms for our reach.
- God never went away ("Immanent"), but humans fell into "Idios Kosmos" (sleep/dreams).
- Jesus-God-Christ are not the same; Christ is the "Path" or "By means of" to God (the All).
- "Santa Sophia is going to be reborn": Wisdom will mount and prevail, ending the schism/fall.
