# The Slumbering ETI Agent: Albemuth and the Discriminated Vision
## concise_summary
Dick concludes that he is a "slumbering agent" for an ETI intelligence from the star system Albemuth. He posits that a "beam" hit him in childhood, imprinting a personality (Thomas) that acted to thwart a Soviet takeover in 1974. He views his writing as prophetic, aimed at demonstrating the holographic nature of the world and disclosing the two hyper-realities beneath: the Palm Tree Garden (Heaven) and the Black Iron Prison (Hell). At the end of time, these two real sources will pull apart, a process he believes he witnessed in 3-74.
## key_claims
- ETI Slumbering Agent: PKD is an "avatar" for an ETI intelligence (from Albemuth) who has been under control since childhood.
- The Albemuth Beam: An instantaneous, information-rich beam from another star system is and was the source of his "rebranding" as an agent.
- Thwarting the Soviets: The "Thomas" personality was a defensive ETI intervention to prevent a Soviet psychotronic takeover of PKD's mind.
- Prophetic Writing: His novels serve the "theological-eschatological" purpose of intellectually rejecting the irreal world and preparing for the disclosure of heaven/hell.
- The Two-Source Theory: Under the projected "mixture" world lie the real sources: Heaven (PTG) and Hell (BIP). These pull apart at the end.
- Set-Ground Discrimination: The 3-74 experience allowed him to discriminate the PTG as the "Set" against the BIP as the "Ground."
