# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 231

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 231
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 22 - 039 to 043
- **Themes:** Transubstantiation of Info, Homoplasmate Reproduction, The Qumran Scrolls, Jim Pike, The Dormancy Theory.

## Key Claims
- **Info-Replication:** Zebra replicates as *information* (Logos) through the printed word (e.g., *Flow My Tears*). Thousands who read the book receive the "signal," allowing the plasmate to proliferate.
- **The Homoplasmate Miracle:** PKD witnessed the literal miracle of "transubstantiation" where the plasmate used him as a "Booster and Broadcaster" to find new host humans.
- **The Dormancy Theory:** The Holy Spirit/Plasmate was dormant for 1900 years (between Acts/70 AD and now), perhaps buried as "germ" info in the Qumran Scrolls.
- **Jim Pike as Predecessor:** PKD believes Jim Pike may have been a homoplasmate first (via the Scrolls) and passed the spirit to PKD (as Elijah to Elisha) upon his death.
- **The Fall of Massada:** Time "stopped" in 70 AD with the Roman destruction of the Essenes (the original homoplasmates). Zebra's return restores that "place and time."
- **Thomas as Paraclete:** "Thomas" is not just an apostolic human but the Paraclete/discorporate Christ returning after 1900 years of "sleeping."

## Entities
- **Jim Pike:** The likely source/predecessor of PKD's homoplasmate state.
- **The Essenes:** The original "flock" who were killed/hidden by the Romans.
- **Zebra/Ubik:** The "Living Info" life form.
- **Thomas:** The discarnate Christ/Essene spirit.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Booster/Broadcaster:** PKD's role in proliferating the plasmate via *Tears*.
- **"King Felix":** The signal (Logos) that triggers anamnesis.
- **Siddhartha Slept:** The dormancy of the Savior "underground" for millennia.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Return of the Holy Spirit:** The 2-3/74 experience is the return of the Spirit that was absent since the First Century.
- **Apostolic Secret Christian:** PKD's true identity, which he is commanded not to reveal.
- **The Empire Never Ended:** The Romans (Caesar) successfully "killed" the Spirit until the mid-20th century discovery of the Scrolls.
