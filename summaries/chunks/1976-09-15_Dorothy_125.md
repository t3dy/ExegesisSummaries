# The Second Pass: God’s Remastering of History
## concise_summary
Dick concludes that his 3-74 experience was a "second pass" of history—a literal time-reversion where God intervened to "remaster" events that had previously gone uncorrected. He identifies "Zebra" as a sentient information plasma (the Logos/Ubik) that has invisibly invaded and is replicating within the world. This invasion is not just spiritual but involves the physical restoration of the "Fix A" (AD 70) archetypal coordinates, effectively overriding the "worse track" of history.
## key_claims
- Second Pass of History: God is re-mastering a segment of linear time that already occurred once before ("Track A"). This is the "End Times" reversal.
- Zebra as Information Plasma: Zebra is an intelligence (St. Sophia/Logos/Ubik) that pervades mechanical structures to animate them.
- Anamnesis as Restoration: Anamnesis is the retrieval of a superhuman state lost thousands of years ago, giving one "supernatural faculties" to see what is really there.
- Fix A (AD 70): The AD 70 period is a "home base" or archetypal fix that sacred time "unveils" beneath the secular mask.
- Syzygy with the Redeemer: 3-74 was a multiplex operation (health, political, spiritual) that pulled PKD out of the world and into union with the invaders.
- Time Reversion: The phenomenal world dissolves when time itself disappears; memory (anamnesis) is the tool that retrieves the "past" so it is no longer gone.
