# September 12, 1976
# Letter to Dorothy (Part 1)
## concise_summary
Dick writes to Dorothy summarizing his 3-74 experience (the "conversion year"). He introduces the term "VALIS" (Vast Active Living Intelligence System), details his role in toppling Nixon through letters to his Congressman, and reveals that the entity saved him from a 15-year prison sentence for anti-war tax protests.
## key_claims
- VALIS: Acronym for "Vast Active Living Intelligence System."
- The Watergate Mission: The entity informed him of a conspiracy (Kennedy/King/Watergate) and dictated letters to Congressman Charles Wiggens to help depose Nixon.
- Tax Protest / Civil Disobedience: Dick confesses he was a tax protester during the Vietnam War (signing the *Ramparts* manifesto).
- FBI Commendation: The entity’s "eloquence" earned Dick a letter of commendation from William Sullivan (FBI Asst Director) during a potential investigation.
- Vernal Equinox: Claims March 1974 coincided with the "actual birthdate of Christ."
- The Golden Fish Trigger: Anamnesis (3,000-year memory restoration) and Koine Greek speech were triggered.
- Spiratus Veritas: The entity identified as the "Spirit of Truth" promised by Christ.
