# The Hologram of Two Lasers: Engaging the McKennas
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the McKenna brothers' "Hologram Universe" theory (from *The Invisible Landscape*) as the most accurate model for his 3-74 experiences. He identifies "PTG I" (Palm Tree Garden) and "BIP II" (Black Iron Prison) as the two "hyper-universes" whose interaction creates our irreal reality.
## key_claims
- The Two-Laser Model: Our universe is a hologram created by the interaction of two hyper-universes (lasers): (I) PTG (Palm Tree Garden - The Is) and (II) BIP (Black Iron Prison - The Is-Not).
- 2012 Climax: Dick notes the McKennas' I Ching-based computer prediction of a "Grand Climax" in AD 2012, which matches his own sense of accelerating time.
- 3-74 as Acceleration: The first stages of 3-74 were an experience of this "acceleration of acceleration," where events moved so fast that only Platonic forms remained visible.
- Cuckoo's Egg: God (I) placed Dick as a "cuckoo's egg" or "secret agent" in BIP (II). His 3-74 anamnesis was the restoration of this "agent" identity.
- Xerox Letter as Loyalty Test: The Xerox missive was a test of "determining loyalty": would he react from his "II" conditioning (fear) or his "I" origin (Thomas/Love)?
- Zebra as the Steering Tao: Zebra/Valis stands "transcendent" to both hyper-universes, like the Tao, but steers history toward the victory of PTG I.
- Backward Time Travel: The "Future" (PTG I) is modulating the "Past/Now" (BIP II) to actualize itself.
- B.I.P. as Parmenides' Form II: The Prison world corresponds to Parmenides' "seeming" reality (The Is-Not). It only "seems" to exist through Satan's interpolations.
