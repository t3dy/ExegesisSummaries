# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-134

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-134
- **Themes:** Machine Self vs. Authentic Self, Soviet Psycho-tronic Control, The Thwarted Programming, The Bichlorides.

## Key Claims
- **Displaced Personality:** "Thomas" is PKD's "authentic/real" personality that was displaced or suppressed by a "Machine Self" under Soviet (Pigspurt) psycho-tronic control around 1969.
- **Waking Up as Resistance:** The 3-74 awakening was the "True Self" (Thomas) reasserting autonomy and "throwing off" the mind-control signal (the plasmate).
- **Pigspurt as the Plasmate:** The "plasmate" could be the weaponized "information-electricity" (Tesla-like) used to program humans into "occluded machines."
- **The Bichlorides:** The first info in 3-74 was about "bichlorides" (mercury/toxins) which John Calvin and the Exegesis link to the "Fall"—the debasement of faculties at birth.
- **Restoration of Faculties:** The Holy Spirit's primary task is the "restoration of man to his original unfallen condition" (de-occlusion).

## Entities
- **Hal:** The "Solarcon" contact who suspected PKD was under mind-control.
- **Thomas:** The "Non-brainwashed" actual self.
- **The Bichlorides:** Paradigm of the environmental/spiritual toxin that causes occlusion.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Psycho-tronics:** The use of microwaves/telepathy for state control.
- **Two Psychoi:** The "Bob/Fred" split of *A Scanner Darkly* as a literal description of the occluded human condition.
- **De-occlusion:** The removal of the "mask-psyche" to reveal the true persona.

## Theological Motifs
- **Charismatic Gifts:** Xenoglossy, healing, and prophecy as signs of the "Restored Human."
- **The Lamb's Book of Life:** The "ledger" seen in 3-74 where the person's name is entered.
- **Apotheosis vs. Possession:** The "Real Self" (Thomas) breaking through from the unconscious.
