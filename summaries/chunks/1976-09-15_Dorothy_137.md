# Disobedience as the Gateway to the Kingdom
## concise_summary
Dick argues that an existential act of "disobedience" toward the Artifact (BIP) is the necessary prerequisite for liberation and the perception of the "Palm Tree Garden" (PTG). He posits that the Artifact has been "dead" since A.D. 70, and we live in its "frozen image." Zebra/Christ "cooks" or interferes with this hologram to transmit messages and salvage souls, leading them one-by-one into the already-present Kingdom.
## key_claims
- The Gravity of Rome: The BIP exerts a "black hole hypergravity" at the imperial center; one must resist this "tug" to be free.
- The Dying Hologram: The world is a hologram; 1) it is an interference pattern ("cooked" by Zebra), and 2) it disappears when one withdraws "assent."
- Success of the First Advent: Christ effectively slew the Artifact in A.D. 70, but we remain in its "frozen template" (Rome) until we wake up individually.
- The 3-74 "Faith in Rome": PKD saw A.D. 70 not by doubting 1974, but by having "total absolute faith" (*Pistis*) in the reality of A.D. 70 Rome.
- Transubstantiation of the Causal Train: Zebra "melts" or modulates the world-hologram by replacing parts of it with itself (an "imitation of an imitation") to alter outcomes.
