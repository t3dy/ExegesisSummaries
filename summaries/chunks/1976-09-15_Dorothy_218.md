# Chunk 218 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_218

## Description
PKD interprets a dream of a magazine titled **"Beyond Lies the Wug"** as a revelation of "Wub-like" (benign) alien possession. He identifies the the "Secret of the Rosy Cross" as moving in shared **Inner Space** and clarifies the mechanics of the "Inner-Outer Tape" simulation.

## Key Claims
- **Wubization**: Like his story "The Beyond Lies the Wub," PKD believes a benign, super-wise alien entity (Thomas/Christ) has replaced his mental contents while using his body as a host.
- **The Koinos of Inner Space**: There is only one vast "inner spacial universe" in which all adepts share. The "Immortal Man" moves from person to person through this shared interiority.
- **Inside Out Universe**: Human perception is "backward"—we are on the surface of a "balloon" looking *into* the actual world (which is interior), while projecting our "outer" world.
- **Pretextual Stimuli**: External events (like the Golden Fish) are "pretextual" causes. The psyche is already programmed by an "inner tape and clock" to fire, and the universe merely provides a matching cue.

## Entities
- **The Wug (Wub/Vug)**: A dream-entity representing the benign "alien" takeover of the human psyche. Thomas is "Wub-like."
- **The Wub/Human Is**: Specific PKD stories used as "models" for how a superior consciousness replaces a human one for benevolent purposes.
- **Zebra**: The plasmatic energy that exists "behind" the objects of our projected simulation.
- **God/Christ/Thomas/Paracelsus/PKD**: A nested hierarchy of "hosts" or "fronts" for the same "Sole Immortal Man."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Revoke/Recant**: The idea that the Brotherhood can "call back to memory" or "revoke" events, effectively changing the past and creating alternate histories.
- **Shared Subjectivity**: The "Subjective Reality" theme in PKD's work (e.g., *Eye in the Sky*) is confirmed as the correct view that "World is located in our minds."
- **Mirror Dialectic**: Psyche and World are two mirrors. In 3-74, PKD’s vision of Rome was a result of his psyche matching an "Inner Tape" that the "Omni-Matrix" then enhanced.
- **Teaching Machine**: The universe is a simulated "instrument" designed to provide meaningful goals (not just pleasure) to the "stations" (humans).

## Theological Motifs
- **Elijah’s Voice**: The "Still Small Voice" heard within the inner spaces of the mind, not in the external wind/fire.
- **The Sole Immortal Man**: The belief that there is only one "Man" (Christ) who is progressively taking over more and more "sheep" (hosts) to encompass them in his immortality.
- **Pluriform Presence**: Christ's "Return" is happening *now* through his multi-personal presence in various human agents.
- **The Station Paradigm**: Humans are stationary "sites" in a macro-brain who do not actually move through outer space.
