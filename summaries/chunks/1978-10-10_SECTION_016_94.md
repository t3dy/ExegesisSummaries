# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-94

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-94
- **Themes:** Rectification of "Sin," The DNA Death Strip, Bi-Brain Relativism, The Digital/Analog Stall.

## Key Claims
- **"Sin" as Error:** "Sin" refers to a wrong turn at a "sharply defined point" on the tape, now rectified by the Christ-insertion.
- **DNA Death Strip:** Destiny/Karma is a programmed "death strip" in the DNA. Christ (the physician) absorbs the firing of this strip, allowing the person to survive their fated death.
- **Hook in the Bait:** The "hook of divinity" hidden in the "bait of humanity" tricks the planetary powers into killing the wrong victim (Christ).
- **Epistemological Relativism:** The 2-brain mind has no "truth" but selects reality based on preference, dissolving its own hypostases.
- **Cybernetic Stall:** A "uni-brain" (linear) mind stalls when encountering the paradox "Dope is Bad/Dope is Good." The 2nd brain handles it by maintaining the dialectic.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "second time around" personality exempt from the death strip.
- **Fred/Bob Arctor:** References to "A Scanner Darkly" as the real picture of the 2-brained race.
- **Valis / Brahman:** The entity that synchronizes the two brains into a macro-dialectic.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bardo Thodol:** Reliving one's life as an information-based simulation.
- **Tat Tvam Asi:** Identity with the "total thought" (Kosmos) being reviewd.
- **Ananke:** Necessity/Fate represented by the deterministic tape.

## Theological Motifs
- **Birth from Above:** The moment a "new ad hoc self" (Christ) is injected into the rerun.
- **The Paraclete:** The "wise presence" who remains with the survivor past the death point.
- **Ma'at:** The ultimate principle of justice that the ethical/binary mind plugs into.
