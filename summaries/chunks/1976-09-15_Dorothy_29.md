# Zebra and the Tao of Stasis
## concise_summary
Dick refines his dualistic model using Taoist terms (Yang/Yin) and identifies Zebra with the "gentle" female aspect of the divine (Sophia). He argues that 3-74 was not just a personal experience but a global shift—the "rolling of the uncut stone"—marking the return of the King in an invisible, ubiquitous form.
## key_claims
- Taoist Duality: J-J is Yang (Creator), Zebra is Yin (Gentle/Interventive), and Brahman is the Tao (Stasis/Unity above them).
- The Gentle Female: Zebra is "female" (a secret) and acts lovingly, shielding the "flock" like a shepherd.
- 2nd Advent as Unseen Event: The Parousia has already happened; the "King has returned" in an invisible, ubiquitous form, and only his "sheep" (those with renewed faculties) can see him.
- Ontological Reality: Christ (the "uncut stone") breathes true life into the "feeble copy" of the demiurge's world, transforming it by stages.
- The Afterlife while Alive: Dick feels he "died" in March 1974 and moved through the Bardo-like afterlife events while a new personality took over his body.
- Successive Prefiguring: Zebra "programs" humans through a series of "dim to clear" signals (the Shark Tooth -> Watchtower -> Gold Fish chain) so the final revelation matches a prepared engram.
- The Secret of the Cosmos: Dick believes he was shown not just *that* it works, but *how* it works: at-advance teleological preparation.
