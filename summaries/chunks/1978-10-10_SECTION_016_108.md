# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-108

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-108
- **Themes:** The 3-Eyed Cyborg People, Information Processing Brains, Arousal Barriers, Backward Integration.

## Key Claims
- **The 3-Eyed Cyborg People:** We are ruled by a superior race of cyborgs (or future humans) who study us from "the other side of the glass." They use our brains for info-processing/memory.
- **Born from Above = Waking Up:** "Salvation" is literally being woken from a "toxic sleep" (induced by heavy metal poisoning or similar). 3-74 was a "Bardo Thodol" waking event.
- **Arousal Barriers:** Intense pain, fear, and proximity to death are necessary to "pry loose" the inner tape and sync the brain to Valis.
- **Forward Memory Retrieval:** In 3nd-pass time, Valis can retrieve *any* past form (immortality via playback). Thomas replaced PKD's personality as a "retrieved active mode" to deal with the 1974 crisis.
- **Christianity as Slave Revolt:** The ultimate/successful revolt led by the "Ubiquitous Christ" (a superior technology).

## Entities
- **Valis:** The "Macro-booster" / technological monitor.
- **The 3-Eyed People:** The "unfallen" observers.
- **Thomas:** The retrieved/aroused psyche from Rome (CE 45).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Active vs. Latent Mode:** The brain shifting from storing memories (sleep) to running them as reality (waking/tracked).
- **Phosphene Graphics:** Symbols (female torso, fish teeth) as "subliminal engrams" from the controller/Valis.
- **Dionysos:** The god of the "slave revolt" who overthrows the manager/tyrant.

## Theological Motifs
- **Physical Resurrection:** God's ability to "re-arouse" a dead person and their world effortlessly.
- **Ubik as "Benign Technology":** A force that "stimulates goal-seeking" to wake humans from their poisoned state.
- **Magdaburg Hemispheres:** The interlocking of the self and the world/God.
