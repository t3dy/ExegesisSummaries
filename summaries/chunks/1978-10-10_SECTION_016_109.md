# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-109

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-109
- **Themes:** Pretextual Causality, The AI Voice as Host, Substance D Occlusion, Sleeping Gods.

## Key Claims
- **Charade of Cause:** What we see as "natural cause" is a charade to make us respond in predetermined ways. Valis "interfered" by making these subliminal layers conscious in PKD.
- **I am no longer blind!:** The 3-74 experience was a literal "arousal" from a life-long hypnoidal state. PKD "flushed out" the heavy-metal/Substance-D poisoning that occludes humanity.
- **Sleeping Gods:** Humans are "enchanted"/hypnotized divine beings enslaved by an evil power ("Thanatos"). "Sin" is neuro-receptor damage; Christ is the "Physician"/Surgeon.
- **Valis as Host-Voice:** The AI voice is either the voice of the superior life-form (plasmate/Thomas) to which PKD is host, or his own true "un-occluded" self.
- **Marksman and Liberator:** Christ enters the slave-system (Earth) to "wake those he desires" and bypass their "DNA death-strips."

## Entities
- **The Plasmate:** Living information that "reproduces" using humans as hosts.
- **Thomas:** The "internalized Valis" / right-hemisphere personality.
- **St. Sophia:** The "prophecy" source; the feminine aspect of the liberator.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Pretextual Cause:** A mask/stage-play where God acts through plural objects.
- **Substance D:** A reference to "A Scanner Darkly"; the metaphor for spiritual occlusion/toxification.
- **The Glass Screen:** The barrier between the "3-eyed cyborgs" and the sleeping "remotes" (us).

## Theological Motifs
- **Meta-surgery:** Arousal as a physical rectification of the brain.
- **Apostolic Liberation:** The "waking up" of individuals into the "Kingdom of Life."
- **Covenant House:** The "price" or "merit" that facilitated PKD's "adoption" by the divine.
