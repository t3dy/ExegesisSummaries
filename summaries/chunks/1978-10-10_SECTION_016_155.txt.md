# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-155

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-155
- **Themes:** Acosmism as Theophany, Levels of Ontology, The "Tears" Revelation, The Invisible King Felix.

## Key Claims
- **YHWH as Outcome of Acosmism:** PKD's decades of doubting reality (acosmism) were actually a search for the "guarantor of substantia." 2-3-74 was the epiphany of YHWH as that guarantor.
- **Search for the Real:** The fundamental issue is not "Good vs. Evil" but "Being vs. Non-Being" (*Sein vs. das Nichts*). God is the "measured dose" of reality that cures the "madness" of our drugged intoxication.
- **The Invisible Underground:** There is a literally existing secret Christian underground to receive the "good news" (Kerygma) hidden in *Tears*.
- **King Felix Cypher:** On the surface, YHWH willed Christianity to die; secretly, in cypher, he willed it to live. This is the "new news" that the "Work is Completed."
- **The Two Signal Fusion:** *Tears* is the story of "Acts" but in a world where Christianity was defeated. The "St. Sophia" (Spirit) manifestation at the gas station shows the "agape" signal breaking through the "BIP/Inferno."

## Entities
- **YHWH / Ubik:** The "reality support" and bulwark against non-being.
- **Belial:** The "man shut up in darkness" (the Empire's ruler).
- **St. Sophia / The Woman:** The spirit of Christianity that "died" but is being reborn.

## Recurring Concepts
- **3 Women (film):** Re-emphasized as the visceral experience of identity-dissolution.
- **Levels of Ontology:** Plotinus's non-spatial "rings" as the better model for divine presence.
- **Bichlorides:** The "poisonous poison" that is also the "curing medicine."

## Theological Motifs
- **The White-Haired King:** God as the judge of the cosmic case, intervening at the "eleventh hour."
- **The Return of the Presence (Shekhina):** God reaching down to his faithful on the periphery because the centers (churches) are apostate.
- **Transformation of the Soul:** Passing through death (awareness of non-being) to resurrection (experience of pure Isness).
