# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_146
## Chunk Description
Discusses the Godhead's "out of round" status, the yin/yang oscillation, and the concept of retroactive time manipulation by future intelligences.

## Key Claims
- **The Godhead's Deviation:** The Godhead itself may experience an "out of round" condition or deviation from the best course, which is corrected by "lateral sweeps" (testing its powers).
- **Yin Predating Yang:** The yin realm (Form II/BIP) predates the yang incursion (Zebra), which is why Zebra requires camouflage.
- **Retroactive Reality-Altering:** An ultra-terrestrial life form from the future can travel back in time to "tinker" with history to ensure its own existence; if a goal *can* be reached, its retroactive power makes it inevitable.
- **The Two-World Inhabitation:** Humans inhabit two worlds: Form I (alive, rational, Zebra) and Form II (dead, dim, BIP). Form II is "anti-matter" balance or "non-being" posing as reality.

## Entities
- **Zebra/Form I:** The yang incursion, alive,thinking, rational, benign.
- **BIP/Form II/Yin:** The "anti-matter" balance, non-being, the counterfeit phenomenal world.
- **Xenophanes:** Mentioned regarding his exalted conception of God.
- **Parmenides/Plato:** Philosophical frameworks for Form I/II.
- **The "Invader":** The helping and juridically rectifying entity (Zebra).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Retroactive Time:** History being edited from the future.
- **Set/Ground:** Form I (Set) vs. Form II (Ground).
- **Camouflage:** Zebra's necessary mimicry within the yin nest.
- **Isomorphism:** The eventual "ham sandwich" model of human-divine alignment.

## Theological Motifs
- **Gnostic Dualism:** The world as a "counterfeit creation."
- **Taoist Balance:** Yang released to curb the excess of yin.
- **Juridical Rectification:** The divine invasion to impose proportion on a blind universe (e.g., Watergate).
