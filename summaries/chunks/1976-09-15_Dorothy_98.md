# Transubstantiating the Universe: The Shiva Side of Zebra
## concise_summary
Dick explores the "Annihilating Side" of Zebra, equating the entity with Shiva and his 3rd eye. He argues that Zebra is not just healing individuals but "transubstantiating" the entire universe, replacing the "mortified corpse" of the BIP with his own immortal, plasmic being.
## key_claims
- Shiva-Christ: Christ has returned as "Shiva," the destroyer-restorer. He has a 3rd eye capable of "inward vision" and "outward destruction."
- Transubstantiation of Reality: Zebra is an "immense transtemporal ubiquitous life form" that is transubstantiating (transforming the substance of) the universe.
- Annihilation of the Goats: 3-74 was a judgment. Zebra blessed the "sheep" (Dick) but "annihilated" the "goats" (the conspirators/Empire).
- Supratemporal Presence: Christ/Zebra is simultaneously in 70 AD and 1974 AD. He has linked the two advents into one "now."
- Post-Mortem Life: Dick believes he was "programmed to die" in early 1974, but Zebra made an "orthogonal adjustment." For a year, he experienced "post-mortem life" until it was re-normalized.
- The 2nd Birth: Memory restoration is the secret of immortality. By joining the "Corpus Christi," we transcend the "DNA death-determinism" of James-James.
- The Intercessor as Paradox: Zebra is both Everywhere and Nowhere, All Things and "Mere St. Elmo's Fire."
