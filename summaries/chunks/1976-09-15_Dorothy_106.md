# Fiction as Fragmented Gnosis
## concise_summary
Dick analyzes his own novels, specifically *The Three Stigmata of Palmer Eldritch* and *A Maze of Death*, as accidental or subconscious conduits for gnostic truth. He believes these stories were "encoded" with insights about the nature of the artifact and divine intervention long before his 1974 awakening.
## key_claims
- Subconscious Encoding: His fiction was being "written through him" by a higher intelligence (Logos).
- Fragmented Truth: Novels like *Maze* contain the blueprint of the simulated reality he later experienced.
- Pre-Gnosis: He had access to the truth "in code" for years before the "key" was turned.
