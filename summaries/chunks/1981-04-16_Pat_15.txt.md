# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_15
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_15
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: The Mechanics of the Dialectic, Discharging Karmic Energy, The Role of the Zero Firing
- **Key Entities**: Zoroaster, Lao-Tzu, Heraclitus, Jakob Boehme

## Key Claims
- Dick finally solves the mystery of *why* the system must fire a "wrong" (zero) move before choosing the correct "one" move.
- The "zero" firing acts as a lightning rod to discharge the accumulated karmic energy (antecedent cause/karmic pressure) built up from the past.
- This "might" (blind force) must be expended. By letting it fire into the void, the system drains off the deterministic pressure harmlessly.
- Once the karmic pressure is dissipated, the Mind of the system (Noos) is completely free to make an un-coerced, wise choice (the "one" move) to construct the next frame.
- Right does not fight Might using force; Right (Wisdom) allows Might to exhaust itself, then uses the resulting raw energy as the clay to shape a new, good reality.

## Entities
- **Zoroaster / Boehme**: Praised for understanding that a wise mind actively utilizes and converts evil/negative stages to achieve a higher good.
- **Lao-Tzu / Heraclitus**: Criticized for believing that extremes *automatically* revert or turn into their opposites (enantiodromia). Dick argues it requires the active intervention of sentient Wisdom.
- **David Hume**: Referenced for pointing out that reasoning from "effect" back to "cause" is custom rather than logic.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Noos vs Ananke**: Sentient Mind (Wisdom) persuading and discharging blind Necessity/Fate.
- **The Modem of Causality**: The human mind's fatal error of linking discrete temporal frames together conceptually, causing us to see rigid causality instead of divine, disjunctive decision-making.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Good Refining the Evil**: The theological necessity of the zero-phase. God requires the raw, unshaped energy of the deterministic discharge in order to have material to sculpt the new, optimal frame.
