# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick links the "Spirit of Truth" (the inner voice) to his high school physics test, concluding that it provides "absolute for sure intervention." He interprets his novel *Flow My Tears* as a revealed message about the "Police State" using the KKK as a vigilante arm, and explores the Logos as a "two-person game" (dialectic).
## key_claims
- The "Inner Voice" (Comforter/Spirit of Truth) provides correct info and "Handlungstreue" (action based on truth) during acute despair.
- Four "Absolute for sure" interventions: High school physics, 1971 "Fullerton/Mexico" dream, *Tears* dream, and 3-74.
- *Flow My Tears* reveals the US as a police state where the KKK (posse) is the militant arm of the police.
- The "Logos" is a "two-person game" or "dialectical saw" (oscillation) that moves backward in time from the completion-end.
- Entering the Kingdom of God is a matter of "Anamnesis" (returning/remembering) rather than moving forward in time.
- The "Colgate Pincers" (Xerox/COINTELPRO) were a trap that evil used to expose itself.
