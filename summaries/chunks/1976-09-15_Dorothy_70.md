# The Omphalos of Reality: Waking from the Repeat Script
## concise_summary
Dick concludes this set of entries by asserting that 70 AD is the "omphalos" (navel) of reality around which all "linear" history circularity warps. He argues that Gnostic "awakening" is the only escape from the "repeated computer tape" of a circular, deterministic universe.
## key_claims
- 70 AD as Omphalos: The first century (40-100 AD) is the fixed center of reality due to the "Unique Breaking-Through" (Advent).
- Circular Warp: Linear time isn't straight; it's warped into a circle by the "gravity" of the 1st Advent. We never truly left the 1st Century.
- The Repeat Script: Like *A Maze of Death*, we are trapped on a "repeated computer tape" (Idios Kosmos) until a Gnostic revelation (Koinos) breaks the loop.
- The Deceiver's Trap: The appearance of 2000 years of time is a "snare and delusion" by the Dark Foe to make us forget the imminent Second Coming.
- Awakening as Emancipation: To wake up is to realize "I am in 70 AD" (Present Tense), which instantly breaks the deterministic power of the 1974 dream.
- Transtemporal Sacraments: Christ provided these (Baptism/Eucharist) as ways to bridge the "circular warp" and reach the Omphalos.
- 1974 as Omega Stage: The events of 1974 (Nixon's fall) are the "Omega Stage" completion of the 1st Advent's war against the Empire.
- The Fish Sign as EIE: Like the Persus 9 tattoo, the Fish sign is an "Extrinsic Informational Entry" that disinhibits the brain's internal computer.
