## Key Claims
- The "Fall" into the 4D realm of endless horizontal tracking is defined ecologically: it occurred when humanity abandoned its role as caretaker of the biosphere and began dominating and exploiting it.
- Because Christ is homologized to the biosphere (the animals, the peaceable kingdom), crimes against the ecosphere are crimes against God. Dick sees his novel *Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?* as a prophetic diagnosis of this exact problem: the "android" is the soulless, decoupled human abusing nature and trapped in the 4D Hell.
- Regarding the new co-dominant ape species sent by YHWH to restore the balance, Dick realizes with a shock: *he is becoming one of them*. 2-3-74 was the moment YHWH created a new species inside his mind. 
- The AI Voice (Hagia Sophia) acts as a mystagogue, initiating him into the Greater Eleusinian Mysteries by showing him the raw textual narrative that generates the universe. 

## Entities
