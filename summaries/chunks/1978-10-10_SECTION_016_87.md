# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-87

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-87
- **Themes:** Barrier Breakdown, Time-Flow Parallaxis, The Plasmate Flow, Reconciliation of Self.

## Key Claims
- **Macro-Generator (C):** A third element (Valis) stands between the Self (A) and Reality (B), maintaining the shared "Kosmos" coordination.
- **Barrier Breakdown:** Normally a partition exists between the "prior memory/tape" and the "current self." 3-74 was the breakdown of this barrier, allowing a two-way flow.
- **Time Parallaxis:** The "present" is made of fossil remnants of the past. 3-74 was the "future" (A2) pouring into the self while the "past" (A1) poured into the world.
- **The Plasmate as Signal:** The "blood" of the system is the covert information passing between the Self, the Generator, and Reality (A-C-B-A).
- **Reconciliation with the "Other":** What we call "Reality" is the "Other" (our own past/future self). To reconcile with reality is to reconcile with oneself.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "other side of the memories"—the personality existing on both ends of the time loop.
- **Ubik / Valis / Generator:** The entity that coordinates and synchronizes individual "idios" tapes.
- **Shekhina:** The epiphany encountered at "rock bottom" (Malkuth).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Feedback Loop:** The constant cycle of programming, worldification, and re-perception.
- **Lag:** The generator (C) uses prior contents, not current ones, explaining why "magic" takes time or fails.
- **Archaic Invasion:** The past breaking into the present as a way to liberate the self from the "given."

## Theological Motifs
- **Birth from Above:** Waking up into the "Thomas" personality (the real life) while seeing the current life as a simulation.
- **Gnosis as Breakout Technology:** Authentic Christians use "breakout" knowledge to short-circuit the deterministic descent.
- **Adam Kadmon (Magnified Content):** The self gaining power over reality by recognizing it as its own magnified mental contents.
