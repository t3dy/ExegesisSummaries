# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-47

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-47
- **Themes:** Truth Modulations, Idios vs Koinos, Shiva the Inhaler, Post-mortem 1st Century.

## Key Claims
- **Plastic Truth:** Reality is totally plastic. The question is not "What is true?" but "What modulations shall I imprint on the stuff around me?"
- **Ho On / Diana:** The deity (Ho On) took the form of the spring queen Diana to protect PKD. He "pre-programmed" her, but she is real.
- **The Feedback Loop:** Reality is a push-pull oscillation between the human brain and externalized thought-formations. Ubik exists because he wrote it, and he wrote it because it exists (timeless loop).
- **The Shiva Cycle:** We are in the 3rd stage of the cosmic cycle (Inhalation). Shiva/Holy Spirit has "killed" the world and is withdrawing the sparks (souls) to God.
- **We Died in 70 A.D.:** Real time stopped at Acts. We have been dead "half-lifers" in a Bardo state for 2000 spurious years, only now being "born" into real life by the Paraclete.

## Entities
- **Shiva / The Paraclete:** The "Herdsman of Souls" and "Psychopomp" (Runciter figure).
- **Diana (Ho On):** The beautiful young woman representing the soul of religion and the goal of the return.
- **Jim Pike / Thomas / PKD:** The layers of identity merging signals to reveal the truth to "half-lifers."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Inhalation/Exhalation:** The Brahma breath model of creation and return.
- **Bardo Thodol Jurisdiction:** The state we entered at the time of Acts, forgetting our true lives.
- **Indeterminate Truth:** Truth where conflicting themes are true in pulses or sequences.

## Theological Motifs
- **Birth from 2-3-74:** PKD did not die in 3-74; he was "born" then after being dead for 2000 years.
- **The Body and Blood:** The only true reality is the thinking body and information-blood of God.
- **Satori:** The realization that the "Instructions to the Dead" in the Bardo Thodol are for us, now.
