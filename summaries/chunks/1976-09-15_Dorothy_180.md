# Chunk 180 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_180

## Description
PKD analyzes *A Scanner Darkly* as a study of how the BIP punishes "Play" (intoxication/Dionysos) with extreme severity. He identifies his own "conscience" as a form of "BIP die-messages" (guilt) and celebrates Zebra's rescue of him from this "sin-punishment" system.

## Key Claims
- ***Scanner* as Sequel to *Tears***: *A Scanner Darkly* logically follows *Tears* as a study of the "punishment too great" for those who chose to "play" instead of "toil."
- **Slave Labor via Substance D**: The "authorities" produce Substance D to create a "slave labor force" out of those who just wanted to have a good time.
- **Conscience as Warped Message**: PKD views his feelings of guilt (re: his wealth vs. his friends' suffering) as "die messages" from the BIP designed to keep him in "masochistic" servitude.
- **The End of Punishment**: The crucifixion proves there is NO connection between sin and punishment; Christ's mission is to "destroy the Tears world" and its "sin-punishment" equation.

## Entities
- **Substance D**: The "poisoned candy" used to enslave those who "play."
- **Zebra / Christ / Dionysos**: The power that "bursts" the BIP and its "theodicy" of suffering.
- **Nancy / Tessa / Joan / Doris**: Figures in PKD's life whose suffering triggers his "BIP-warped" guilt.
- **King Felix (The Happy One)**: The notification that "Play and Happiness" are the true state of the liberated soul.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Masochism**: The psychological result of the BIP's "sin-punishment" programming.
- **Theodicy**: The false teaching that suffering is "natural" or "tied to sin."
- **Lamination of the Two Advents**: The 1st and 2nd Advents "laminated" together to change "Tears/Crucifixion" into "Exculpation/Joy."
- **The Fenmen / Partisans**: The "secret church" operating within the BIP.

## Theological Motifs
- **Vicarious Atonement as Liberation**: Christ didn't suffer so we would imitate him, but to free us from the belief that suffering is necessary.
- **Joy as Signal**: "Hosanna" is the shout of joy indicating the BIP has been burst.
- **God of the Desperate**: Christ found where the world "pulverizes" the individual.
- **The End of the World**: The "annexing" of Form II (Rome/Darkness) into Form I (God/Light).
