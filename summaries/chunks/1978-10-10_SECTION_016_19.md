# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-19

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-19
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 065 to 071
- **Themes:** Messenger Role, Valis as Opus, Elijah/Ahab, Elastic Time.

## Key Claims
- **Unconscious to Conscious Messenger:** PKD sees his role as a messenger using "trashy" subliminal clues to prepare for the 5th Savior.
- **Valis Proposal:** *Valis* will be a consciously formulated depiction of "restored man" and the "metamorphosis" process.
- **Elijah Identity:** PKD returns to the idea that Elijah (as Thomas) took him over to scourge the land (Nixon/Ahab) and prepare for the Lord.
- **Breaking Time = Breaking Fate:** Victory over astral determinism is victory over time; for PKD, time dissolved in 3-74.
- **The Regulator of Time:** Time perception is solely regulated by the rate of "things looking different" (observable change). If there were no change, there would be no time.

## Entities
- **The 5th Savior:** The imminent "Lord and Judge" (Pantocrator).
- **Ahab/Nixon:** The modern tyrant scourge by Elijah.
- **Apollo:** Identified with the Christian God in *Deus Irae*.
- **Elijah:** The prophet who "restores all things" and prepares the way.

## Recurring Concepts
- **"Look-Differents":** Successive changes of appearance used to deceive us into believing time has passed.
- **Astral Determinism:** The power of the archons (Fate) tied to the causality of time.
- **Moksa:** The abolition of the division between microcosm (inner) and macrocosm (outer).

## Theological Motifs
- **Road to the Pleroma:** Salvation is the re-ascent of the spark to its home.
- **Interspecies Symiosis:** Potential for the 5th savior to be an incarnation involving this.
- **The Sun of Righteousness:** The healing and release resulting from fearing the "name."
