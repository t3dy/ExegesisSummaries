# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the pineal gland as the "third eye" and the biological receptor for the "Other Sun" (Sun of Righteousness). He links this to the 1974 chromatic phosphene experience and argues that Christian healing is "pre-natal care" for the divine seed within the human "womb."
## key_claims
- The pineal gland is a light-receiving organ that responds to non-physical "fire-like" light.
- The 1974 experience was a deliberate signal from the "Sun of Righteousness" to the pineal gland, bypassing the brain's "Aurora" display.
- Human beings serve as "mothers" (Yin) for the implantation of "solar spermatika" (Yang) from God the Father.
- Healing the physical body is necessary because it acts as the "womb" or "launch platform" for the immortal Light Body.
- Re-interprets the biblical "darkness at midday" as a time dysfunction/light going out, which he and his daughter Laura witnessed in 1962.
