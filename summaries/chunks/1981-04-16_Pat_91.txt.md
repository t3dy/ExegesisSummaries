# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_91
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_91
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Time as Change-Rate Velocity, Coaxial Worlds, Forward/Vertical Motion in Time, Reality as a Textual Hologram
- **Key Entities**: Teilhard de Chardin, Joachim of Fiore (implicit)

## Key Claims
- The acceleration of the macro-entity's (Christ/Valis) change-rate confers greater flexibility and freedom. As evolution progresses, the system moves from biological rigidity (death) to absolute, flexible life.
- Time is redefined entirely as "change-rate velocity." Being "earlier in time" simply means existing at a slower change rate. 
- In 1974, Dick's "meta-abstraction" caused his own biological clock (neural firing rate) to speed up. Because his velocity was faster, the surrounding world appeared to move slower, hence the world regressing to the appearance of AD 70.
- This leads to the startling conclusion that Dick didn't travel back to the past; instead, he accelerated into the *future*, proving that the ancient past (The Black Iron Prison/Hemarmene) is always slower/lower on the ontological axis.
- The universe is an information-processing entity that simulates a material world (Maya); the true basis of the universe is a textual hologram (recombinant scripture).

## Entities
- **Teilhard de Chardin**: The macro-system evolving towards the Omega Point underpins Dick's theories on the accelerating change-rate of the universe.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Coaxial Worlds**: Hell is the past, Purgatory is the present, Paradise is the future. Because change-rates differ among individuals and structural strata, these realms exist simultaneously (coaxially).
- **The Golden Fish Sign**: Identified as a piece of data from outside the baseline "present," serving as a marker of a slower-rate component persisting into the present, thus allowing the perception of "temporal depth."

## Theological Motifs
- **Yoga vs. Anti-Yoga**: Dick compares ordinary mystical practice (slowing down the metabolism to reach Heaven/Nirvana) to his experience (speeding up neural firing so fast it put him in Hell/the past).
