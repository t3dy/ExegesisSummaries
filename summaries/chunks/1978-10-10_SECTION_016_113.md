# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-113

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-113
- **Themes:** Heavy Metal Poisoning, The DNA meta-packet, Pigspurt Dissociation, The 3-Eyed Adopters.

## Key Claims
- **Sin as Heavy Metal Poisoning:** Humanity's "occlusion" (Sin) is compared to neuro-receptor damage or intoxication. Christ is the "Physician" who uses "Meta-surgery" to flush out the brain.
- **The DNA Meta-Packet:** "Salvation" is a specific high-priority DNA packet firing to override the "lesser" genetic programming (Karma/death-strip).
- **Pigspurt Hypnotism:** PKD suspects he was under "Pigspurt" (G2/military) hypnotic discipline, and the pentathol in 3-74 caused him to "remember" and break the post-hypnotic programming.
- **The 3-Eyed People as Adopters:** PKD is one of the 3-eyed people, "adopted" by them to help overthrow the "Empire" (BIP) and restore the "Garden/PTG."
- **The Secret Sacrament:** The "Lord's Supper" is a way of "reconstituting" the slain King (Kyrios) who lives on as information in his followers.

## Entities
- **The 3-Eyed Cyborgs:** The superior civilization.
- **Thomas:** The "reconstituted Kyrios" in PKD's brain.
- **Ho Kyrios:** The Great King / Lord.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Anamnesis:** Recovering memories of a "Missing Year" (1971) and a "Home Planet."
- **Set/Ground Cypher:** Using media (like the Xerox missive or "Tears") as "instruction-release" signals for the secret group.
- **Compression:** The alchemical transmutation resulting from "humiliation and death" (Running the other way).

## Theological Motifs
- **The Man Who Knew How to Lose:** Christ/Dionysos as a being who triumphs through a "transmutation into opposites."
- **Ich bin der Retter:** The recurring automatic thought of being "The Savior."
- **Lord's Supper:** A genetic/informational "eating" to preserve a culture across stars.
