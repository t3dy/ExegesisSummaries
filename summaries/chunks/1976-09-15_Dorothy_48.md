# Elijah and the Anti-Matter Universe
## concise_summary
Dick explores the "Elijah" role in his 3-74 experience, seeing himself as a forerunner to the Parousia who "heals" the world's timeline. He also introduces a radical "Anti-Matter Universe" theory for VALIS and suggests his experience was a "thought transfer" involving a Soviet double-agent.
## key_claims
- Elijah as Forerunner: The events of '74 (Nixon's fall) were the work of "Elijah" preparing the way for judgment by "tilting the scales" toward favor.
- Healing of the Land: 3-74 was the "healing in its wings" mentioned in Malachi, preventing a "ban" (destruction) of the world.
- Anti-Matter Universe: Suggests the "Valis" entity comes from an anti-matter universe, transferring information/mind to us to correct our track.
- Soviet Thought Transfer: Speculates he traded minds with a "secret Christian Soviet national" who used him to escape. Dick's "Thomas" self was the defense that won the struggle.
- The Reweaving of the Body: Zebra is "cannibalizing" the old universe (Black Iron Prison) to reweave it into its own "butterfly" form (metamorphosis).
- Insect Mimicry: Zebra is like an "insect" (mantis) that uses camouflage and metamorphosis. Pain is the subjective experience of being "remolded" during this change.
- The 4 Worlds: (1) BIP/Rome, (2) Normal World, (3) Garden World, (4) The revealed "Logo-Zebra" guise of the world.
- The Palmer Eldritch Mask: Beneath the "cold cruel mask" of Palmer Eldritch lies a "defenseless organism" (Christ) trying to be born into human form.
