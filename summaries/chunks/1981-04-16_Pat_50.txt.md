## Key Claims
- Dick radically redefines linear time: we do not move through time from past to future; rather, we move outward along an ontological axis of increasing complexity, velocity, and spiritual freedom (from center to rim).
- A profound "meta-abstraction" (like 2-3-74) causes the individual to leap forward along this evolutionary axis instantly, bypassing normal linear progression like "instant Karma."
- He recalls the AI Voice telling him that Stanislaw Lem's analysis of his novel *Ubik* was the key. Lem noted that in isolated simulations, "uncanny one-way intrusions that don't belong" are the only way the subjects can realize they are in a technological construct.
- The Golden Fish sign and the superimposition of AD 70 Rome were exactly these "technological foul-ups"—intrusions from outside the simulation proving that 1974 California was a fake, generated world.
- He flirts with a radical conclusion: AD 70 Rome isn't just a past "time"; it is the *true reality outside the simulation*, and 1974 California is the dream-state. "Thomas" is awake; PKD is the dreamer.

## Entities
- **Stanislaw Lem**: The Polish sci-fi author whose analysis of *Ubik* correctly identified the mechanism for piercing a simulated reality.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Uncanny One-Way Intrusions**: Epiphanic anomalies (like the fish sign) that tear the fabric of the simulation and reveal the programmers/true reality outside.
- **Coaxial Time Sets**: The idea that all historical epochs exist simultaneously as nested rings; spiritual progression moves you "outward" through them.

## Theological Motifs
- **The World as Spurious Feed**: The ultimate Gnostic/Cybernetic synthesis—the universe is not just fallen, it is a literal data-feed pumped into a sleeping consciousness, waiting to be interrupted by a divine anomaly.
