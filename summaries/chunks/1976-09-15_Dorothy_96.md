# The Voice of Agapē: The "Glossa" of Love
## concise_summary
Dick interprets several dreams and "ciphers" (Felix, Glossa) as a single, overwhelming message from Zebra: "God loves us." He concludes that his novel *Flow My Tears* was "completed" by a divine insertion to show the absolute pinnacle of love (Agapē).
## key_claims
- The Message is Love: The "hidden message" in Dick's writing is "I love you." Zebra inserted this via the "Felix" glossa in *Tears*.
- Agapē as Pinnacle: *Tears* is a study of all types of love, culminating in "Agapē" (divine love) shown between Buckman and the black stranger.
- Zebra as Cosmic Christ: Dick distinguishes between the "Holy Spirit" (Inside) and "Zebra" (Outside). He saw Zebra because the Spirit made clear the mystery of Christ.
- Cryptomorphosis: "Born from above" is the awakening of the immortal transtemporal personality ("Thomas") that transcends death and time.
- Notification to the Prince: The miracle of 3-74 was a "notification" to the "Prince of this World" (Satan/Empire) that Christ is here, everywhere, and arrayed against him.
- The 2nd Advocate: St. Sophia is the "2nd Advocate" (Comforter) sent to guide those who have forgotten their original home (The Pearl).
