# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick contrasts Gnosticism (external grace) with Neoplatonism (self-attainment), concluding that the 1974 experience was a "zygote" formation between the human "egg" and cosmic "spermatika." He reinterprets time as a loss of perception and "anamnesis" as the retrieval of the past from within the present.
## key_claims
- True immortality requires a "union" between the human and the divine (Gnostic "Grace").
- Time is a 4th dimensional space that we perceive imperfectly; being "freed from time" means seeing the past as still present.
- The "past" is not gone, but "contained" within things like onion rings or a palimpsest.
- Prophets possess "second sight," which is the ability to see into the past and future rings.
- The "Autofac" factory represents the immortal seed that replicates itself to escape destruction.
- Future reality consists of "larger rings" reaching down to assist the present.
