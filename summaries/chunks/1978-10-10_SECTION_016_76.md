# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-76

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-76
- **Themes:** Stationary Time, Microcircuitry Invasion, Phagocytosis of Iron, The Miniaturized Representative.

## Key Claims
- **Stationary Time (Acts):** No real time has passed in 2000 years; society is "stuck" in the Roman period (Empire).
- **Secret Christian Underground:** Christians are a "dissolving agent" creating newness/change in an ossified world.
- **Invasion by "Ubik":** The reality of *Ubik* (living/melted) is invading the world of *Tears* (ossified/Iron).
- **Phagocytosis of the Iron Particle:** Microcircuitry (the "dissolving agent") is slipped into the "City of Iron" to encompass and eventually devour the complex/Empire.
- **The Miniaturized Self:** The "Total Mind" (Logos) miniaturizes itself and enters its own creation to report on the state of "ossification." This "representative" (PKD) forgets its identity until the job is done.

## Entities
- **Taverner (Tears):** The variable man who destabilizes the ossified Empire.
- **St. Sophia:** The wisdom-entity destined to be reborn.
- **Logos:** The total mind in dialog with the miniaturized representative.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Melting Causal Trains:** The method of destroying the deterministic fixed nature of the Empire.
- **Dissolving Catalyst:** The nature of Christ/Newness introduced into the static background.
- **Post-Mortem existence:** The true Christian lives in a "magic Garden Kingdom" where time actually moves.

## Theological Motifs
- **Cuckoo Egg / Representative:** The soul as an alien investigator sent from the divine realm.
- **Divine Providence:** The recent revelation that PKD was "adopted" by the divine because of his own act of adopting children (Covenant House).
- **Karma Arcing:** Love (agape) reflected back at the self to "burn up" Ananke (necessity).
