# 1975-02-27_Claudia_02
# SECTION_001
# February 27, 1975
# Claudia

## concise_summary
Dick continues writing to Claudia, further elaborating on the spiritual significance of the rat's death. He connects this act to broader cosmological ideas from Empedocles about the necessity of strife and death balancing love and harmony. He explores the concept of judgment, relating it to the fall of Richard Nixon (the "beast", code 666). Finally, he posits that orthogonal time leads backward to childhood ("about 3 or 4 years old"), where humanity's true, uncorrupted perception of the universe resides, before the "World" limited our vision.

## key_claims
- Death is a necessary component of the universe's structure; strife is required to balance love (drawing on Empedocles).
- The author was "spared the Wrath" of Judgment Day and was of use against "the tyranny" (Nixon) by writing *FLOW MY TEARS*.
- The number 666 in Revelation refers to Richard Nixon.
- Orthogonal time completion throws the participant backwards into linear time, effectively restoring them to the perceptual state of a young child ("3 or 4 years old, maybe up to 6").

## tensions_contradictions
- The author recognizes he is filled with flawless the Holy Spirit/completed entelechy, but admits he is still "wicked and think[s] wicked thoughts" (lusting after Victoria Principal).
- He claims 1974 was the completion of his entelechy (which usually implies time ending/perfection naturally), but immediately corrects himself to say it was an "intrusion into time" (God's grace saving him *despite* being imperfect/incomplete).

## evidence_quotes

## uncertainty_flags
- The author struggles to reconcile his newfound spiritual completion with his persistent earthly desires and imperfections, actively questioning how both can coexist.
- Numerical decoding of Nixon's name (IX from nIXon, IC from dICk = 666 or 9-599) is highly speculative and mathematically playful, changing mid-thought.
