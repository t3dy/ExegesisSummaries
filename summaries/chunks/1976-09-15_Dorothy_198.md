# Chunk 198 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_198

## Description
PKD explores the idea of the Brain as a "Self-Monitoring Feedback Loop" communicating from the future to the past. He discusses the battle between Eidos (form/information) and Entropy (congealing/scrambling), painting the 3-74 event as a cosmic "rectification of the signal."

## Key Claims
- **Brain as Self-Monitoring Circuit**: (Refining a theory from K.W. Jeter) The Brain in the future used PKD and *Flow My Tears* to monitor information degradation along the time-axis and adjust "the signal" (history).
- **Retrograde Time Motion**: The Brain may be an entity from the future moving backward in time and using electronically boosted telepathic signals.
- **Information Degradation = Entropy**: The BIP (entropy) seeks to congeal reality and suppress new data; the Brain (negentropy) provides a "breaking of the inflexible state" through divine intervention.
- **Flash Point / Pure Fire**: The Brain's growth moves toward a critical mass ("Fire") which will eventually blast all "congealed" (entropic) opposites.

## Entities
- **K.W. / K.W. Jeter**: Friend and fellow writer who provided the theory of the "self-monitoring circuit."
- **Parmenides**: Greek philosopher cited for the concept of God as pure "fire/heat/light."
- **The 3-Eyed People**: A vision of the future human-evolutionary stage—mute, telepathic, and consciously part of the Brain.
- **Nixon Residency**: A "rectification" point where the Brain intervened to fix a "dysfunction" in the timeline.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Eidos (Platonic Form)**: The "intelligent plasma" that incises purpose and knowledge onto objects and history.
- **Evolved Homeostasis**: The ultimate goal of the Brain, reaching a state of total wakefulness for all its parts.
- **Information Scrambler**: The BIP’s primary function—degrading information as a method of entropic control.
- **The Final Goal (Journey's End)**: PKD feels his 4+ years of analysis have finally "unmasked time" and explained his life's work.

## Theological Motifs
- **Holy Wisdom (Logos)**: The active psyche of the Brain, which is not merely a collective of humans but a distinct divine intelligence.
- **Form II vs. Form I Override**: The belief that without divine (Form I) interference, the universe (Form II) would simply run down into entropy.
- **The 2nd Advent as Revelation**: The final stage when the Brain's camouflage is dropped, revealing its ubiquitous control over reality.
- **The Word as Blueprint**: The "Word" (Logos) precedes reality like a blueprint precedes a building.
