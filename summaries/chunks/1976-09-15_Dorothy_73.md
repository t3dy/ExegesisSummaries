# The Secret Partnership: Harmonizing Christianity and Taoism
## concise_summary
Dick proposes a trinitarian model that harmonizes Gnostic dualism with Taoism. He identifies "James-James" (the Creator/Yang) and "Zebra/Sophia" (the Arranger/Yin) as secret partners under the ultimate Tao (Love), ensuring the final victory of the Savior through the "invincible advocate" of the Holy Spirit.
## key_claims
- James-James as Creator (Yang): The Demiurge is a "sick genius" who churns out chaotic, unrelated creations (Blind nature/efficient cause).
- Zebra as Arranger (Yin): Holy Wisdom (Sophia/Christ) does not create but "arranges/joins" these chaotic bits into complex, meaningful Gestalts (Noos/persuading necessity).
- The Secret Partnership: Contrary to Gnostic/Manichaean dualism, these two are "secret partners" regulated by the Tao (Love). The "war" is a dialectical interaction.
- The 3 Temporal Ages: (1) O.T. Power (Yang), (2) N.T. Wisdom (Yin/Intercessor), (3) Future Spirit (Love/Holy Spirit).
- The Holy Spirit's Role: The Holy Spirit is the "Advocate" who ensures the Savior's victory. It is not a contestant but the "hallmark of the absolute outcome."
- Saving from Fate: James-James leaves creations to their "fate" (determinism/chaos); the Intercessor (Zebra) saves them through "interventive grace."
- 3-74 as Victory: For Dick, 3-74 was the entrance of the Holy Spirit, ending his battle and closing the book on his individual fate.
- Denying the Creator: Dick reconciles his denial of the "Creator God" by asserting that "Wisdom" (The 2nd person) is the only one who cares for us; the Creator is already onto a "new, improved creation" (Evolution/Survival of the Fit).
