# The Phagocytes of God: Dissolving the Imperial Complex
## concise_summary
Dick identifies himself and other "secret Christians" as "phagocytes" or "metabolic toxins" sent into the world-organism to dissolve the "malignant complex" that is the Black Iron Prison (BIP). He describes a "Theophany" in April 1978 where reality itself became alive and spoke to him in "tiny church bells" (Koine Greek), taking over his actions to save him. He refines his "Model C" and "D" cosmogonies, viewing the BIP as the "Yin" center of a teaching machine and the PTG (Palm Tree Garden) as the "Yang" periphery.
## key_claims
- Phagocytes of God: Secret Christians are biological agents ("metabolic toxins") whose purpose is to dissolve the BIP malignancy.
- Theophany of 1978: On April 25, 1978, reality became alive and spoke to PKD in "tiny church bells," giving him instructions in Koine Greek.
- The BIP as a Jungian Complex: The BIP is a "complex in the Jungian sense" within the mind of God (Noös) that warps thoughts via "gravity" and must be dissolved.
- Model C/D (Androgynous Godhead): Our world mirrors the diploid (Androgynous) nature of the Godhead:
    - **Yang/PTG**: Light, airy, moving (Set).
    - **Yin/BIP**: Dark, hard, metal, unmoving (Ground).
- The World as Teaching Machine: God permits the BIP to exist as a "testing" mechanism for moral (choosing good) and intellectual (detecting the hidden BIP) growth.
- Isomorphism of Highest and Lowest: The "lowest" stratum (trash/humble) is isomorphic with the "highest" (divine), and they are separated by the "slice of ham" that is the BIP.
