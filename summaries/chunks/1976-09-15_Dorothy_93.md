# The Deus Absconditus: Gnosis vs. Natural Theology
## concise_summary
Dick rejects "Natural Theology" (finding God in nature), asserting that God is a "Deus Absconditus" (Hidden God) who is NOT in the world. He argues that Zebra mimics the world to infiltrate it, and that a "fake fake" (God's mimicry) results in the "real."
## key_claims
- God is Not in Nature: Gnosticism is correct: the world is "fucked up" and not real. Nature does not reveal God; revelation from "beyond" is required.
- Zebra as Invader: Zebra is a "world-mimicking invader" who counterfeits the demiurge's counterfeit world.
- A Fake Fake = Real: The demiurge's world is a fake; God's mimicry of that fake is an infusion of "good ontology" (True Being) that repairs the cosmos.
- Transubstantiation of the Totality: God is assimilating the whole cosmos through the "Corpus Christi," repairing the primordial breach in the Godhead.
- Christ as Trap-Breaker: Christ "took the blow" of the Archon's justice for us. In 3-74, Christ entered the "trap" for Dick and burst it.
- St. Sophia's Birth: The Paraclete (St. Sophia) is being "born again" to reveal mysteries because she was "not acceptable before" (in her previous forms).
- Recognition of Origin: The goal is to discover our identity as "living cuttings" from the "True Vine" (Christ).
