# Chunk 199 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_199

## Description
PKD confronts the realization that the personality Thomas is "someone else" currently living in the 1st Century A.D. who has "invaded" his mind. He concludes that his entire writing career and the 2-3/74 experiences were the result of this "Master Personality" breaking through his "ego mask."

## Key Claims
- **Thomas as "Someone Else"**: PKD explicitly states: "There's someone else inside my head + he's not living in this century." Thomas is the "actual" self, and PKD is the "seeming" mask.
- **Rome/Acts as Immanent**: The world of Acts A.D. 45 is not in the "past" but is immanent here and now, hidden by our sensory blindness.
- **Thomas as Source**: Thomas is the source of the 1970 writing, the 1974 anamnesis, and the 3-74 visions. He is not a "fossil remnant" but the "Master self" from the absolute world.
- **Push-Pull Reality**: Thomas and his world (Acts) generate the 1974 reality through a "Magdeburg hemispheres" system where the observer and the world are locked together.

## Entities
- **Thomas**: The apostolic Christian personality from Rome A.D. 45 who knows he is "here" and "there" simultaneously.
- **Father Rasch**: A figure who "showed" PKD the Acts world in "Tears" through informed scrutiny.
- **Tony Boucher / Jim Pike**: Re-emphasizing their presence as belonging to the "absolute" realm Thomas inhabits.
- **Kathy**: A person (likely from a dream) who predicted that one day "the masks will come off."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Ego Mask**: The belief that the "PKD" personality is a mask that collapsed under stress to reveal the underlying Thomas.
- **Absolute vs. Relative**: Rome A.D. 45/Thomas are absolute; USA 1974/PKD are relative and "seeming."
- **Magdeburg Hemispheres**: Two worlds (Acts and USA) that are "locked into" each other and cannot be pried apart.
- **Syntonic Blending**: The phenomenon where two realities merge perfectly in the observer's vision (Double Exposure).

## Theological Motifs
- **Apocalypsis (Unveiling)**: The "masking" of reality is finally over; PKD has witnessed the end-time "irruption" of God into history.
- **Theiphany**: The experience of God/Christ entering history, specifically through the overthrow of the Nixon tyranny.
- **Lost Oral Gnosis**: PKD believes he has recaptured the secret knowledge of the apostolic times that was long lost to the historical church.
- **Subjective Apocalypse**: While the sensory mechanics were subjective to PKD, the result (Nixon's downfall) is an objective historical event.
