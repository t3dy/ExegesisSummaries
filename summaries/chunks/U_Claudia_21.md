# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his experience as an "evolutionary advance" (Cosmic Consciousness) and the "next step up" for the human species. He reinterprets the "Fall" as a lack of perception and argues that the new 3-D perceptual acuity (Logos) provides an "inability to be shucked" by the lies of the world.
## key_claims
- Cosmic Consciousness (Dr. Bucke) is a permanent evolutionary step-up, not a temporary mystical state.
- Dick is a "next step up" human (Slan/New Man) whose "metasystem" fired after a lifetime of programming/trial.
- The "Fall" is the loss of the ability to perceive the Logos (meaningful structure of reality).
- New perception is 3-D and color-enhanced, created by the superimposition of data from both brain hemispheres.
- This new faculty provides an absolute defense against deception ("Inability to be shucked").
- Evolution is God's method of continuous creation, using pain/stress as the trigger for metamorphic jumps.
