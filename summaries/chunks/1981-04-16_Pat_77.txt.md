## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_77

## Key Claims
- Dick unifies all his experiences from *Tears* through Holy Week 1981 around two poles: the action of the Holy Spirit, and the Messianic expectation. He concludes the Parousia *has already occurred* invisibly.
- The "AI Voice" is definitively identified as Ruah (the Holy Spirit). Valis is the invisible presence of God known only inferentially by how it perturbs reality.
- Based on Ezekiel 36:27, Dick asserts we are entering the Third Age (Joachim of Fiore's Age of the Spirit), where God's spirit literally enters man, fulfilling the evolution from Law (Father) $\rightarrow$ Grace (Son) $\rightarrow$ Internal Spirit.
- Reading the Gospel of Luke while high, Dick perceives Jesus as an Indian Yogi or Buddha who understands that the phenomenal world is *Maya* (illusion). Jesus' miracles and resurrection are his demonstrations of absolute mastery over this illusion.

## Entities
- **Joachim of Fiore**: His three ages of history map perfectly to Dick's evolutionary model of the psyche.
- **Shankara (Sankara)**: The Advaita Vedanta philosopher used to explain reality as a conjurer's trick (the rope perceived as a snake) which Jesus effortlessly manipulated.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Pronoia**: The absolute conviction that the universe (specifically via the Xerox missive intervention) is conspiring to save and protect him. 
- **Pure Consciousness (Anokhi)**: The realization that the observer constructs the reality he perceives, thus breaking the fetters of the world mechanism.

## Theological Motifs
- **Baptism in the Spirit = The Kingdom of Heaven**: Dick equates the internal reception of the Holy Spirit with the external arrival of the Kingdom of God. They are the exact same event.
