# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick explores "Spontaneous Psychic Integration" (Jungian) and the "Bardo Thodol" journey he underwent between 1970 and 1974. He identifies the "Vast Active Living Intelligence System" (VALIS) as the supporting life of the universe and reinterprets his 1974 experience as the "re-entry" of the racial unconscious (St. Luke/The Physician) into his psyche.
## key_claims
- VALIS: The universe is alive, wise, and active (Vast Active Living Intelligence System); Dick discovered "God" by the process of elimination.
- The 4-Year Journey (1970-1974): Dick was in an "underwater world" of archetypal projections (schizophrenic/primitive) until 3-74, which was a "healed re-entry."
- Integration of Opposites: Spontaneous integration of the psyche (attaining the "Pearl of Great Price") occurred without external help, withdrawing all projections.
- St. Luke as Racial Wisdom: The new personality was created from the "collective experience and wisdom of the race" (St. Luke/Physician) to kontest the ego.
- Jigsaw Blueprint: The "Red and Gold" activity is a developing blueprint/jigsaw of the future that plunders the past to assemble its plan.
- The Malqosh (Latter Rain): Dick craved "cold water" and "mist" in 1974, identifying it as the *Malqosh* (Spirit power) that begins just before the harvest.
