# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-24

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-24
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 100 to 107
- **Themes:** Dual Psychoi, Alien Host, Absorption by Aliens, Travel off Planet.

## Key Claims
- **The Rider Mechanism:** Thomas entered PKD as a "rider" (plasmate); upon PKD's death, the crossbonded Firebright will enter a new human host.
- **No Reincarnation:** PKD rejects reincarnation in favor of "dual psychoi" (two people from different centuries in one head).
- **Seeding Program:** An alien, higher race is "riding" humans (selecting only the healthiest) to eventually absorb the human race into their own.
- **Conversion as Blessing:** Being "ridden" is a blessing because it frees man from DNA programming and grants immortality.
- **Migration off Planet:** The goal of this symbiosis is travel off the planet (as suggested by Wilson/Leary), but for plasmates, not rocket ships.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The 1st-century "rider" who underwrote PKD's writing.
- **Zebra:** The macro-brain in communication with all pluriform plasmates.
- **Firebright:** The augmented, immortal plasmate produced by the crossbonding.
- **The "Rider":** The alien psyche that governs the human host covertly.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Homoplasmate:** The temporary dual-psychoi state (Homoplasmate Alpha).
- **HP-Omega:** The pure, augmented plasmate that emerges after crossbonding.
- **Sirius Connection:** The potential stellar source of the invader race.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Holy Spirit:** Redefined as the chain of human-hosts who have become plasmates back to Christ.
- **Sacred Marriage:** The crossbonding of the human and the alien.
- **Exaltation:** The human part is eventually converted into the superior, immortal alien form.
