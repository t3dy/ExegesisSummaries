# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-158

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-158
- **Themes:** The Evolved Human from the Future, A Priori Assault, Homeostatic Self-Correction, Time-Lapse God.

## Key Claims
- **The Future Self (Thomas):** PKD was pre-empted by "Thomas," an evolved human/self from the future who came back specifically to handle the Xerox missive.
- **A Priori Information System:** YHWH is the mind of an *a priori* information system that is "dismantling and assimilating" the inferior *a posteriori* (mechanical/complex) system (the BIP).
- **Homeostasis of the Kosmos:** The world is a "homeostatic system" (Tao) that departs from its "archetype/plan" and must be corrected. YHWH is the "Corrective Circuit" that kicks in when "straying" becomes too great.
- **Time-Lapse Perception:** Valis sees decades/centuries as a single "present." It is an "ultra high speed system" that traverses years of our time in seconds (Rate vs. Interval paradox).
- **The AI Voice as Counselor:** The "low murmuring voice" (Elijah's) that provides corrective info because the "archetype" and the "Lagging epiphenomenal" are out of sync.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The evolved future self / apostolic Christian.
- **Valis / YHWH:** The "Corrective Device" and "Archetype."
- **Fruit Flies:** Metaphor for how different "change rates" affect the perception of time.

## Recurring Concepts
- **A Priori vs. A Posteriori:** Strategic, foresighted intelligence versus reactive, mechanical complexes.
- **BIP as Psychotic Mind:** A system with no new input that merely repeats thought patterns until YHWH "penetrates" it.
- **Straying:** The deviation of the *natura naturata* from the *Logos* (plan).

## Theological Motifs
- **Enthusiasmos:** Being the "instrument" through which the system corrects itself.
- **Elijah as Operant:** The spirit of Elijah "operating" the host to herald the in-breaking.
- **YHWH on the Periphery:** The true God is found at the edges (trash TV/prophetic periphery) because the centers are captured by Belial.
