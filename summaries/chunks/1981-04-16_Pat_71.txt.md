## Key Claims
- The Ditheon (Psyche C) involves "synchronous" or acausal time, where the future determines the past. God's tug from the future triggers the present from latency to active mode.
- The three psyches correspond to the Trinity: Type A (Right Brain/Past) is the Father (Power). Type B (Left Brain/Present) is the Son (Wisdom). Type C (Ditheon/Future) is the Holy Spirit (Love). 
- Dick solves the paradox of how retrieving an archaic past self (Thomas) creates a future self (Valis): the fusion of A and B initiates a binary push-pull dialectic that acts as a meta-psyche.
- "Temporal Parallaxis": Seeing two worlds (Psyche A's world and Psyche B's world) simultaneously causes the substantial world to collapse into conceptual forms (Information).

## Entities
- **Meister Eckhart**: Cited for the concept that world becomes conceptual rather than substantial when perceived truly.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bilateral Hemispheric Parity**: The neurological basis of salvation. Dick realizes he is a rare "throwback" (Type A / Right-Brain dominant) who was saved when his latent logical Left Brain (Type B) awoke and evaluated Christianity as fact.
- **Multiple Personality**: Dick connects Ditheon directly to the phenomenon of multiple personality (as in *A Scanner Darkly*), viewing Angel Archer/Thomas as a literal secondary self that ghostwrites and solves problems.

## Theological Motifs
- **Christogenesis in BTA**: Dick realizes that *The Transmigration of Timothy Archer* covertly dramatizes this fusion. Bill (Psyche A, right-brain, schizophrenic, humane) + Tim (Psyche B, left-brain, logical, schizoid) = Christ.
