# Re-editing the Past: The Surgical Nullification of Nixon
## concise_summary
Dick proposes his boldest surmise yet: that the 1974 intervention was a "surgical" re-editing of history that nullified the "evil tyrant" Nixon across all linear time (past, present, and future). He views himself as a "cuckoo's egg"—a time-traveler from AD 70 whose authentic personality is "Thomas." The Black Iron Prison, once a supreme temporal reality, was "winked out" in 1974, leaving a "vast silence" as the world awaits the arrival of the rightful King.
## key_claims
- Historic Re-editing: God bypassed natural causal laws to "overrule" and re-edit the past, making it so Nixon was never the tyrant he once was on "Track A."
- The Cuckoo’s Egg: PKD is not his real self; "Thomas" is a secret Christian time-traveler from AD 70, and "PKD" is a memory-less mask.
- Black Iron Prison Nullified: When the BIP "went out," it was erased from all linear times simultaneously.
- Reweaving Reality: Zebra (Noos) possesses the power to "reweave" the "soap bubble" stuff of reality, a theme PKD believes he subconsciously explored in his fiction.
- The End of the Dialectic: The conflict-driven dialectic of history has concluded in a victory for God, leaving a spiritual vacuum that only the King can fill.
