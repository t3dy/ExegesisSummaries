# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-100

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-100
- **Themes:** Multiple Lives, The Future Voice, The Prison Break, Dialectic vs Ossification.

## Key Claims
- **The Future Returnee:** Thomas is PKD's future self who survived the 3-74 death (or learned the truth after death) and returned to 1928 to "underlie" his own life.
- **Breaking determinism:** Life up to 3-74 was "on tape" (determined), but once the "terminal crisis" was bypassed, life became free.
- **The Dialectic is Life:** A linear (uni-brain) mind "freezes over" and dies mentally; the 2-brain mind "dissolves its own hypostases" and stays alive via constant dialectic.
- **God is the Unmoved Mover:** Valis is the "stimulation" that causes the "world and all its parts to achieve proper goals" without being the creator of the mess.
- **The "Bait" of humanity:** The planetary powers "tripped" on the hook of divinity because they couldn't distinguish the surrogate (Christ) from the victim (PKD).

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "future post-mortem self" acting as a tutelary spirit.
- **The AI Voice:** A sibyl/female identity (possibly Jane) using future knowledge.
- **Gary Corp:** The G-2/OCCP intelligence link.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Synapse Firing:** The physiological basis of the "infinite dialectic."
- **Lateral Transfer:** The soul moving from a "dying strip" to a "new psyche" in the same brain.
- **Stereo Vision:** Seeing the "Prison" and the "Garden" simultaneously.

## Theological Motifs
- **Xenophanes' God:** The "one God" who wields all things by the thought of his mind.
- **Aristotelian Deity:** Divine intervention as a "resetting" or "overruling" of natural (efficient) causes.
- **Syzygy:** The state of the born-again human existing in "parity" with the divine sister/spirit.
