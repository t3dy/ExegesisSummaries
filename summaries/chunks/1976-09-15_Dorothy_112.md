# Computer-Simulation and the Liberation from the Artifact
## concise_summary
Dick further explores the universe as a computer-simulated training machine. He focuses on the Urgrund's mission to liberate conscious entities from the artifact's loops, suggesting that the "teaching" is over and the "release" has begun.
## key_claims
- Computer Logic: The universe operates on a complex, computer-like logic determined by the artifact.
- End of Training: The "simulated" period of human history is coming to an end.
- Liberation Protocol: The Urgrund has initiated a routine to "release" humanity from the artifact's control.
- Failure of the Machine: The 1974 event was a "glitch" or an "override" of the system by the divine.
