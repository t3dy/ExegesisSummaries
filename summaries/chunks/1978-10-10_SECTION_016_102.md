# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-102

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-102
- **Themes:** Macro-Brain Assembly, Plasmate Traffic, The "Joint" Paradigm, Bardo Thodol Simulation.

## Key Claims
- **The World is a Brain:** God is structuring the world into a giant "macro-brain." The "plasmate (info)" is the traffic of this brain.
- **Assimilation into Function:** In 3-74, PKD was "assimilated" into the macro-brain's *thinking* process; it thought through him.
- **Reassembly of Humans:** The macro-brain may have "disassembled" itself into individual humans and is now "relinking" them to function again.
- **Joint/Soft Drink Stand:** Confirmation that reality is a simulation based on verbal information (Info-as-World).
- **Projections of memory:** We are in the "Ubik" cold-pac state, worldifying our own forgotten memories. Recognition (anamnesis) of this breaks the programming.

## Entities
- **Valis / Ubik:** The macro-mind/brain.
- **Christ / St. Sophia:** The "internal mind" that gives birth to God in the soul.
- **Stephane / Kathy:** The specific individuals (daughter/friend) for whom the whole system is justified.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Homeostasis:** The self-regulating nature of Valis as it evolves toward completion.
- **Eidos + Matter:** The imprint of the mind's "form" onto the stuff of reality.
- **The Choice:** Choosing the "creation" (Stephane) as fairer than the "creator" (God) is the "correct choice" that saves the individual.

## Theological Motifs
- **Giving Birth to God:** The soul as the creator of God (Eckhart's "Grund").
- **Apostolic Christianity:** The recognition of the Acts era as the "true" time.
- **Emancipation from Maya:** The moment the soul "bottoms out" and returns home.
