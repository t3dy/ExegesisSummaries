# Chunk 166 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_166

## Description
This chunk details the "Corporate Becoming" of the cosmos, where St. Sophia is "born again" as the universe itself. PKD discusses the "stately" and "stealthy" way God re-penetrates a counterfeit world from its "trash" side to transform purposelessness into life.

## Key Claims
- **Macrocorpus of Zebra**: Zebra is transubstantiating reality into its own body; the entire cosmos will essentially become the "body" of St. Sophia.
- **God as Stranger/Fool**: God enters the counterfeit world as the "lowly," "fool," or "ignorant" to hide from the "cruel blind adversary."
- **Precision Surgery of Fate**: Divine "melting" (deflection) of causal chains is "precision surgery" that alters the total outcome of history without being detected.
- **The Sheep and the Goats**: The "stockpile" contains "parts" that only resemble usable material; the true cosmos is built by Noös selecting from this stockpile.

## Entities
- **St. Sophia**: The incoming "Macrocorpus" or soul of the universe.
- **Zebra**: The transforming agent mimicking and transubstantiating reality.
- **The Adversary**: The "cruel blind" entity that substitutes the irrational for the rational.
- **Spinoza / Hume**: Philosophers who failed to view "miracles" as normal, necessary factors in an evolving creation.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Transubstantiation of Reality**: Turning inanimate/purposeless matter into sentient matter.
- **Invisible Re-weaving**: God's method of fixing the universe "where he is not expected to be."
- **Camouflage / Playfulness**: The idea that the Parousia is present but "almost in a playful 'Nya, nya... you can't find me'" manner.
- **Evolutionary Structure**: The universe is not yet a "cosmos" but a work in progress.

## Theological Motifs
- **Deus Absconditus**: God as the hidden, disguised intruder.
- **Bait Motif**: God uses disguises to appraisal humans ("opportunity") and detect the evil one ("bait").
- **The Irrational World Soul**: Reiteration of the idea that the universe (or God's soma) has a "streak of insanity" that is being cured.
