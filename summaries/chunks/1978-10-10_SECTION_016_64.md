# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-64

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-64
- **Themes:** Internalized Script, The Two Gods (OT vs NT), Disinhibition of Thomas, Information as Salvation.

## Key Claims
- **Script-Reality Gap:** The "script" (fiction) was implanted by a powerful entity. The shock of 3-74 was the brain perceiving its absolute powerlessness as it acted out scenes written years prior.
- **The Two Gods:**
    - **O.T. God (Samael):** Blind, inventive, creates intoxicating delusions (ignorance), enslaves via "Dokos."
    - **N.T. God (Logos/Ubik):** Pure intelligence, restores life through info (knowledge), wins via absolute foresight (foresight).
- **Analog Problem Solving:** The macro-mind uses "topological transfer" (comparing patterns to a model template) rather than digital reasoning. It needed the "Script" (PKD's books) as the model.
- **Zones of Approximation:** We don't switch "worlds"; we see "zones" of reality. "Tears" is the actual, dystopic substrate, while our world is an approximate camouflage.
- **The "Bicolor" Secret:** Only total wisdom could foresee the 1974 "Golden Fish" girl from the 1960s writing of *MITHC*.

## Entities
- **The Monstrous CP-Su Deity:** The O.T. God/Empire authorities.
- **Thomas:** The apostolic personality who is the "right-brain processor."
- **King Felix:** The space-time index for the Second Advent.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Hologram Realities:** Time as "Takes" provided to a stationary brain.
- **Mimicry (Again):** The deity mimicked *Ubik* because it was the most "syntonic" form.
- **Apostolical Secret:** The awareness that the world of *Acts* has never ended.

## Theological Motifs
- **Amnesia and Return:** Salvation as remembering one's true identity (Anamnesis).
- **"Your ancestors ate manna... and are dead":** Contrast between the "old man" (dying in the desert/Bardo) and the "new birth" (Spirit/Logos).
- **Logos as Information:** The living information that "rides" on text/symbols.
