# The Metamorphosis to Full Life
## concise_summary
Dick posits that humans are currently in a "half-alive" or "half-born" state, trapped in a "prison" of the material artifact. The process of salvation is a literal metamorphosis into a higher state of being, resembling a second birth or the maturation of an embryo.
## key_claims
- Half-Alive Existence: Normal human life is a state of "suspended animation" or incomplete birth.
- The Prison-Cocoon: The material world acts as both a prison for the soul and a protective cocoon for its maturation.
- Metamorphosis: The transition to divine life is a biological and spiritual transformation.
- The Second Birth: 3-74 was the beginning of PKD's "birth" into the real world.
