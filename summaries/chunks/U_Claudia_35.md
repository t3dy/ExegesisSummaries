# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his role as a "Wolf in Sheep’s Clothing" (or vice versa), covertly delivering the Gospel to Marxists through his fiction. He identifies his "Spirit Guide" as St. Luke (the "Beloved Physician") and concludes that his 23 years of writing were a "Functional Success" in building the Underground.
## key_claims
- Mission Accomplished: Dick has already "Freed the people" from within the State by infecting Marxists with theological concepts they can't see but absorb (Subliminal Gospel).
- Luke the Physician: The man in the "Toga and Greaves" with the "Huge Book" is St. Luke, the Greek evangelist and friend of Paul (not Paul himself).
- *To Scare the Dead* is the final revelation: It uses the "Possession" of a government agent to restore lost Essene truths.
- The "Heart with Arrow" drawing in *Tears* is a wordless graphic for *Agape* (The embrace of life).
- The "Kingdom of God is Within": Dick’s experience shows that the Parousia is an individual event of breaching time.
- Assault on Materialism: Dick's probe into the "illusory nature of reality" is his primary weapon against the "Enemy" (Marxism).
