# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_20
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_20
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: The Twin Infinities of Thrall, Zeno's Paradox, Preprogrammed Salvation, Occasionalism as the Solution
- **Key Entities**: Zeno, Paul, Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz

## Key Claims
- Dick identifies two "Chinese finger traps" of the Maze: 
  1) Scrutinizing the world makes it more detailed, convincing, and deeply trapping. 
  2) Fleeing the world dilates spurious time to infinity.
- Because spurious time expands infinitely (Zeno's paradox), a Savior traveling from 'outside' could never reach us in time.
- Therefore, the Savior (Christ) had to be introduced *at the very beginning*, camouflaged inside the Maze, remaining dormant until the exact right moment to fire.
- This perfectly matches Leibniz's monads and Malebranche's occasionalism: the salvation mechanism was pre-synchronized locally, not injected casually from outside later on.
- Dick concludes the 1974 event was this pre-synchronized alarm firing, proving that the soteriological plan outwitted Satan's twin infinities of space and time.

## Entities
- **Zeno**: His paradox is applied to the endless dilation of spurious time that blocks external rescue.
- **Paul**: Cited regarding the theological concept that salvation was God's plan "from the beginning."
- **Leibniz**: His concept of "monads"—windowless entities that unfold according to pre-established harmony—is used to explain how the Savior operates inside the maze entirely via synchronization.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Plasmate / Dormant Seed**: Christ encoded as living information, buried within the maze for 2000 years, waiting for the synchronized moment to awaken.
- **Pretextual Cause**: Re-evaluated as the experience of witnessing pre-synchronized commands firing, rather than true external physical causality.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Ultimate Trick**: God outwitted the Demiurge/Satan by hiding the rescue mechanism *inside* the prison before the prison doors even locked.
