# The Spurious Time: God's Purpose in the 2000-Year Delay
## concise_summary
Dick explores the theological purpose of the "spurious long time" (the 2000-year gap since the 1st Advent). He argues that it serves as a "snare" for evil, allowing the "fallen ones" to reveal themselves while the "saved" are prematurely brought home through anamnesis.
## key_claims
- Spurious Time as Deception: The appearance of 2000 years is a divine trick ( Maya) to make the "deceivers" believe they have won, prompting them to reveal their true evil nature for judgment.
- 70 AD is Now: Ontologically, it is still 70 AD. 1974 is a "dream reality" or "sleep" state from which Dick woke into the "real continuum."
- Hegel and the Incarnation: Using Hegel, Dick argues that God works within history to promote human freedom. 3-74 was a specific "fragment" of this historical plan, not just an individual rescue.
- The Eschatological Composite: Comparing *Tears*, *Deus Irae*, and *Scanner*, Dick sees a single message: God is breaking through, Christ is returning, and a doorway to a Hellenistic "next world" has opened.
- Engagement with Post-Modernity: He notes that Zebra uses stimuli (like Paul McCartney's "Teddy Boy") to control human engramming, but then extricated Dick from that very system in 3-74.
- Nitrous Oxide and the Vine: Connects his earlier "great grape vine" revelation to his current anamnesis. This world is a "prison" only for those cut off from the Vine (God).
