# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-46

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-46
- **Themes:** Bardo Thodol World, Ubik as Pre-programmed Rescue, Psychomorphism, Determinism vs Noos.

## Key Claims
- **World as Decomposition:** Our world/state is identical to the Bardo Thodol (decomposing/degenerating). We are "half-life" entities who don't know we are dead.
- **Ubik as Rescue Entity:** PKD pre-programmed his own rescue in 3-74 by writing *Ubik* in the 60s. The thought-formation "Ubik" became real to rescue him from the "Bardo" of 1974.
- **Psychomorphism:** VALIS/Ubik responds to the percipient's will and beliefs. It is "titanic information" that heals and guides based on the user's conceptualization.
- **Noos vs Ananke:** The goal of the cosmic process is for Micro-noos (individual mind) to subdue Ananke (blind fate/determinism), converting chaos/entropy into order/negentropy.
- **Assumption of Responsibility:** To control reality, one must assume moral responsibility for all past events, tracing causal strands back to oneself as author, not victim.

## Entities
- **VALIS/Ubik:** The objectified thought-formation/rescue entity.
- **The Paraclete:** The second comforter promised by Christ, acting as the rescuer.
- **Joe Chip (on the stairs):** The archetype of the dying/enervated self calling for Ubik.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bardo Thodol:** The state of post-mortem delusion where one takes thought-projections for reality.
- **Acosmism:** Radical rejection of the objective reality of the world.
- **Time Lag:** The delay between a thought-formation and its objectification in "reality."

## Theological Motifs
- **Enlightenment:** Knowing that nothing experienced is anything other than objectified prior thought-formations.
- **Grace as Negentropy:** The introduction of new energy/life into a dying, entropic system.
- **The Primordial Fall:** Identified with the "congealing" of the process-mind into objective, external "fate."
