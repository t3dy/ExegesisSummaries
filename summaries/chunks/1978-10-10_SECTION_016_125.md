# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-125

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-125
- **Themes:** The Transubstantiated Universe, Absolute Faith as Key, Tagomi's Crossing, The Eucharist doorway.

## Key Claims
- **Apotheosis vs. Projection:** 3-74 was the "Apotheosis" where PKD sat on the judgment seat. Whether it was "projected unconscious" or "outer God" is irrelevant; the content was the "Divine Mind/Sophia" objectified as World.
- **The Miracle of Faith:** If you absolutely believe in the Xtian universe, that richer universe *actually replaces* the smaller mundane one (ontological replacement).
- **The Eucharist Doorway:** The eucharist is a scientific-fictional "portal" to a parallel/alternate world (the Kingdom). It is the "narrow gate."
- **Thomas' Alternate Reality:** Thomas is the "alternate self" in a "Tears-like" police state who crossed over into PKD's world (summoned by the fish sign). 
- **The Xerox Missive as Inter-Universal:** The Xerox missive is a "constant" that spans both universes, explaining why it was both "Pigspurt" (Thomas' world) and "G2" (PKD's world).

## Entities
- **Hagia Sophia:** The projected/real Divine Wisdom.
- **Dionysos:** The "King of Joy" who overthrows the "King of Tears."
- **Thomas:** The "Secret Xtian Revolutionary" from the alternate universe.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Idios Kosmos vs. Koinos Kosmos:** Personal vs. Shared worlds; here, the "Idios" is revealed as the "Alt-Universal" truth.
- **Matrix Projection:** The theory from "Tears" where multiple worlds are projected onto a single matrix.
- **Transubstantiation:** The accidents remain (bread/wine/California) but the essence is now Christ/The Kingdom.

## Theological Motifs
- **Ich bin der Retter:** The recurring "Automatic Writing" identity.
- **Real Presence:** Christ not as a symbol but as the "Sentient Environment."
- **The Narrow Gate:** Christ as the topological exit from the "closed simulation."
