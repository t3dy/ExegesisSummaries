## Key Claims
- Dick realizes that his novel *The Transmigration of Timothy Archer* perfectly depicts the Teilhardian evolutionary process: Tim's resurrected psyche merging with Bill creates the "Ditheon" (Christ-entity). 
- To perceive the true nature of reality, one cannot use a single psyche. Two psyches functioning in unison but separated by time create **Temporal Parallaxis** (dys-phasing). 
- Just as two eyes spaced apart in space create spatial depth perception, two psyches spaced apart in time (e.g., AD 70 and 1974) create temporal depth perception, allowing the entity to see eternal concepts hiding within linear time.
- The term "Ditheon" specifically denotes the class of entity Jesus belonged to: Two Natures (one human, one divine) residing perfectly in One Person.

## Entities
- **Teilhard de Chardin**: His theory of scattered "parts" converging to form the Cosmic Christ perfectly describes the fusion occurring in the Ditheon.
- **Jesus Christ**: Identified as the paradigmatic first Ditheon.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Temporal Parallaxis**: The evolutionary cognitive leap achieved by possessing two distinct temporal vantage points simultaneously.
- **Dys-phasing**: The necessary out-of-sync relationship between the two psyches that generates the stereoscopic vision of time.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Adoptionist Christology**: Re-emphasized; the "theon" (divine half) of the Ditheon is supplied by the Holy Spirit merging with the human host.
