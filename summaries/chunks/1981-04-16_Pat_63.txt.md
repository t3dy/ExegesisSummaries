# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_63
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_63
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Deus Ludens, Recombinant Information, Right Hemisphere Awakening, Justification by Surrogation

## Key Claims
- The Third Age (Age of the Spirit/Ditheon) is driven by "recombinant information"—there is no fixed canon or precedent. Information is generated *ex nihilo* uniquely for each situation and person.
- Evolution moves from rigidity to flexibility, culminating in "Deus Ludens" (the playing God), associated with beauty, joy, and the divine girl tossing the world playfully. 
- The "meta-abstraction" of 1974 is pinpointed neurologically: it was the right hemisphere (the archaic, appositional mind) coming to consciousness and saying "I Am" (Anokhi). The subsequent fusion of the right and left hemispheres is the Ditheon.
- Dick receives a hyper-clarified understanding of Protestant theology via a vision ("The Messenger"): his neurotic, self-accusing guilt is definitively cured by God presenting a blank sheet of paper in place of his sins (Justification by Surrogation).

## Entities
- **Paul and the Reformers**: Their doctrine of unmerited grace through surrogate atonement is completely validated by Dick's "Messenger" vision.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Deus Ludens**: The theological transition from God as Lawgiver (Fear), to God as Savior (Mercy), to God as Playful Companion (Joy).
- **The Accusing Conscience**: Identified with Satan in the heavenly court; Dick recognizes that attempting to save himself via moral perfection ("legalism") is doomed.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Flawless Sheet of Paper**: A powerful visual metaphor for vicarious atonement, where Christ's perfection literally covers the believer's karmic debt.
