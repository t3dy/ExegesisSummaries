# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies "Possession by God" (Dionysian mystery) as the secret core of Christianity, rediscovered by Meister Eckhart. He reinterprets his 1974 experience as a "recollection" (anamnesis) of his own divinity and a "Discernment of the Present" as the Seventh Sphere where the "Angry God" is ready to do battle against the State.
## key_claims
- Christianity = Dionysian Mystery: Crossing into the "Kingdom of God" is actually being possessed by the God, as in the worship of Dionysos/Zagreus.
- Eckhart's Secret: Eckhart rediscovered the lost "Dionysian" core—that God is born in the soul—which the Church had suppressed for 1,500 years.
- Dionysos as Savior: The "God of Fruitfulness" (*Felix*) is the outsider god who breaks the back of astrology (Ananke) and the State.
- Anamnesis of Divinity: 3-74 revealed that we are "divine in nature" but fell into "nonbeing" (forgetfulness).
- "He hides himself for them": God (Amon) is the "workman concealed in the workshop" (immanent Ptah/transendent Re).
- Eros vs. Thanatos: The State serves Thanatos (crushing life/spontaneity), while the God serves Eros (life/wild power).
