# Grand Synthesis: The Pitch, Wobble, and Yaw of the Godhead
## concise_summary
In a massive concluding synthesis of Section 15, Dick unifies his lifetime of thought into a "Diploid" cosmology. He posits that the Godhead is "out of round"—experiencing a "crisis" or "wobble" (oscillation toward Yin/BIP) that manifests as undeserved suffering in our world. He argues that our reality is a "counterfeit" created by the Yin side of God (Sophia/Artifact) which should not have created on its own. The Yang side (Logos/Christ) has invaded as "Zebra" to restore balance (Tao), using the Yin world as a "stockpile" to assemble the "New Heavens and Earth" (PTG).
## key_claims
- The Out-of-Round Godhead: Undeserved suffering stems from a "yawing" or "oscillation" in the Godhead's own internal balance (Tao).
- Counterfeit World: The Yin world (Ananke/BIP) is a "bungled" copy of the divine prototype; it is "false work" that is fundamentally irreal.
- The Two-Front War: The Yang Logos attacks the BIP from above (Macroform) and from below (Microform/Salvidor Salvanitus).
- Reality as Process: God is not a static being but a "toymaker" who "tests his powers" through an existential contest with the Yin barrier.
- Re-contouring History: Each "pass" of the Logos re-writes history and alters our memories ("it was worse" before the edit).
- Phagocytes of God: The "Secret Christians" act as antibodies or metabolic toxins, dissolving the BIP presidency of the world.
- The Success of the 3 Bantam Novels: *Stigmata*, *Ubik*, and *Maze* form a scripture-like trilogy disclosing this 2-source struggle.
