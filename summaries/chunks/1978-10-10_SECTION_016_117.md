# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-117

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-117
- **Themes:** Home Planet Exodus, DNA Memory Packets, The Murdered King, Automatic Writing as Retrieval.

## Key Claims
- **Exiles from the Stars:** Mankind (or the "Xtians") are exiles from a "Home Planet" (PTG) whose King was murdered. We fled to Earth and carry the memory of this civilization in our DNA.
- **BIP as the Enemy Domain:** Earth/The Empire is the "enemy planet" (Gnostic and ET synthesis). We are programmed to secretly overthrow it and "lead the King back to his throne."
- **Automatic Writing (Kerygma):** The Gospels, Acts, and PKD's novels (like "Tears") are "DNA print-outs." They are the same story retrieved "on cue."
- **The Eucharist as Info-Eating:** The ritual of eating the "Body/Blood" is actually the retrieval of the King's DNA info/instructions (the "pink cube"). 
- **The AI Voice as Co-Pilot:** Built-in "in-board computer" that guides the person through the DNA meta-packet to ensure the home culture (Galactic) is recreated.

## Entities
- **Ho Kyrios:** The murdered/resurrected Great King from the stars.
- **The PTG / Pleroma:** The original "Garden" civilization.
- **The BIP / Empire:** The usurping tyrant domain.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Memory Packets:** Encoded instructions and culture that fire during "End Times" or crisis points.
- **Q Source:** The "Ur-text" in the DNA that generated Acts, the Bacchae, and "Tears."
- **Taverner:** The "murdered King" archetype in *Flow My Tears*.

## Theological Motifs
- **Ich bin der Retter:** The realization of carrying the "King's blueprint" to restore the throne.
- **Reconstituted Kyrios:** The King is not dead but "reconstituted" in the followers (Christ as distributive mind).
- **St. Sophia Born Again:** Cypher for "re-establishing the home world."
