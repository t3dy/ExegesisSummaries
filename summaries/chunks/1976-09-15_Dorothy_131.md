# Unscrambling the Diploid Hologram
## concise_summary
Dick refines his "2-Source" theory, identifying our world as a "projected mixture" or hologram of two hyper-realities: Form I (Palm Tree Garden/Heaven/Light) and Form II (Black Iron Prison/Hell/Night). He claims his 3-74 experience was a "set-ground discrimination" where these two sources pulled apart. He argues that the world is a fusion of order and chaos, and that the Logos (Zebra) works through "mimicry" to modulate causal trains from within the hologram.
## key_claims
- 2-Source Theory: Reality is an admixture of two distinct sources, Heaven and Hell, as per the "McKenna 2-Source theory."
- Set-Ground Discrimination: The 3-74 experience allowed PKD to distinguish Form I (the alive "Set") from Form II (the dead "Ground").
- The Mock World: Our world is an irreal, projected hologram; only the two hyper-real sources are truly real.
- Zebra's Mimicry: The entity Zebra mimics objects and causal processes to hide its presence while reweaving reality.
- Victory of Form I: In the final dialectic, Form I (Light) will defeat and eliminate Form II (Darkness).
