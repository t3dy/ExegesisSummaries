# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick provides a 24-point list documenting his transformation after the 1974 "Spirit Birth." He argues that he has been "remade in the most fundamental way," comparing his experience to a "spiritual transcendental car wash" and rejecting the idea that human standards of "sanctity" are the only measure of divine change.
## key_claims
- Spiritual Car Wash: 3-74 was a "human being refurbishing system" that stripped him to his "skeletal plating" to rebuild him.
- "Born from Above": The transformation required a "change from fleshy mortal to immortal being" (Megiddo Mission terminology).
- Personal Remaking (24 Points): Dick lists changes including believing he was someone else (Essene), speaking Attic Greek, changing habits (smoking/alcohol), increased intelligence, and withdrawal of projections.
- "The Blind Man to See": Dick credits God's love for his healing, particularly the "Physician" (St. Luke/Asklepios) who spoke to him.
- Non-Pious Transformation: God retains the individual; "goodness" by human standards (pious/mild) is not the goal.
- Green World / New World: Precog dreams of "Vinland" and "Green Grass" indicate that *this* world will be changed into a new world (Rebirth).
