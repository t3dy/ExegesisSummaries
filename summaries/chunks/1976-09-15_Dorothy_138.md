# The Seven Stages of Liberation from the Black Iron Prison
## concise_summary
Dick outlines a seven-stage process for escaping the Black Iron Prison (BIP) and entering the Palm Tree Garden (PTG). He identifies the BIP not as a hologram but as the "real" underlying template of our world, which is concealed by a "nice-type" veiling hologram. He views his own novels as a steganographic message intended to trigger "anamnesis" (memory) in "secret Christians" who are waging a multi-lifetime, supratemporal war against the BIP.
## key_claims
- Seven Stages of Liberation:
    1. Disobedience to the Artifact.
    2. Vision of the BIP (via anamnesis).
    3. Realization of the Hologram as a fake concealment.
    4. Perception of Zebra "cooking" the hologram to send messages.
    5. Recognition of "secret Christians" at work.
    6. Fulfilling one's own "deed" in the plan.
    7. Reward (Entry into the PTG/Pleroma).
- The Multi-Lifetime War: The "secret Christians" (phagocytes of God) have been fighting Rome for 2000 years, moving across the DNA gene pool line with immortal memories.
- Steganography in Fiction: PKD's novels (*Ubik*, *Maze*, *Tears*, *Joint*) were written to "advertise" his knowledge to these secret agents and trigger their awakening.
- Fascism as Self-Replication: The BIP thrives on attackers becoming like it; Christ's "miracle" is defeating the BIP (Nixon) without occupying the citadel of power.
- Centennial Revolutions: The American Revolution and Protestant Revolt are seen as periodic, temporary victories of Zebra over the persistent BIP.
