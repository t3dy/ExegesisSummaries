# 1976-09-15_Dorothy_155
## Chunk Description
Examines the "Ape of God" theology, the role of "bait" in judgment, and the restoration of the world soul.

## Key Claims
- **Satan as "Ape of God":** Evil attempts to imitate God through "spurious, malicious creations" (interpolations) that replace true reality.
- **PKD as Bait:** PKD feels his own life (appearing helpless) was "bait" to lure out and unmask the forces of evil (Watergate, etc.).
- **Testers and the Tested:** Humans are "testers" who come here without memory; 3-74 was a reversal where the "tester" (PKD) resumed his identity to unmask the "tested" (the artifact's minions).
- **Transubstantiation of Reality:** St. Sophia replaces "irreal" (spurious/malicious) sections of the world with "onto-logically real" sections of herself.
- **The World's Mind:** The presiding force of the world is a female psyche (St. Sophia) that can enter her own body in microform.

## Entities
- **St. Sophia (Hagia Sophia):** The Thinking World Soul, identified as feminine.
- **Bulgakov:** Cited for his view of Sophia as the "fourth hypostasis."
- **The Sibyl:** Visual manifestation of the feminine divine.
- **Dionysus:** Mentioned as potentially the sane one in an irrational universe.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bait/Trap Model:** The humble servant form as a core sampling device for judgment.
- **Information Block:** The 2-way block between the Mind (Sophia) and the Circuit Board (World).
- **Sacred Drama:** The unceasing, magical enactment of the Christ drama beneath linear time.
- **Gestalt Recognition:** Anamnesis as the recognition of original patterns (Romans/Xtians).

## Theological Motifs
- **Mediatorial Sophia:** Uniting the Absolute (God) and the Cosmos.
- **Omniscience vs. Omnipotence:** Selecting God's goodness and wisdom over His power in the face of evil.
- **Divine Alchemy:** The transformation of the sufferer into Christ.
