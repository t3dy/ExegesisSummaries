# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_187
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_187
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Signal A and Signal B, The Extraterrestrial Fairies, The Cosmic Christ
- **Key Entities**: Thomas, The Fairies (ETIs)

## Key Claims
- Dick models 3-74 as the receipt of two signals: "Signal A" is the normal 4D Black Iron Prison, and "Signal B" is the superimposed 5D Palm Tree Garden. Most people only receive Signal A, leaving them blind.
- Higher lifeforms (the "Fairies" or 5D entities) can receive both signals. Because humans are blind to Signal B, these entities can freely use human commercial media (Signal A) as a carrier wave to encode and transmit Signal B information, fully camouflaged.
- Dick speculates that "Thomas" (the entity who possessed him) was neither human nor God, but one of these extraterrestrial/extradimensional 5D "fairies" who move through time like space.
- The New Testament/Acts narrative is not a literal history of Jesus, but rather the encryption code used by these entities to communicate across the Signal A/Signal B overlap. 

## Entities
- **The Fairies**: The 5D alien entities who operate the Signal B broadcast. They are completely invisible to Signal A consciousness but can take over human minds to intervene in history. 

## Recurring Concepts
- **Signal A vs Signal B**: The binary information streams. Signal A operates deterministically (Empire), while Signal B introduces providence and order (Ma'at/VALIS).

## Theological Motifs
- **Gnostic Camouflage**: The divine (Zebra/VALIS) uses mimesis to stealthily invade the physical world entirely undetected by the ruling archons, because the archons cannot decode Signal B.
