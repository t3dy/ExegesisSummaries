# The Highway for the Lord: 3-74 as Pre-Parousia
## concise_summary
Dick theorizes that 3-74 was a "theolepsy" (divine possession) where St. Sophia "avatared" herself through him to prepare the world for the Parousia. He interprets the fall of the "Tyrant" (Nixon) as an anomaly where the Artificer (God) directly repaired a segment of the "unfolding strip" of deterministic reality.
## key_claims
- St. Sophia's Theolepsy: The "Thomas" voice was actually St. Sophia/Wisdom speaking through Dick ("Tat Tvam Asi"). He did not become God; she became him.
- Preparing the Highway: Dick's experience was a "Highway for the Lord"—a preliminary theophany to make the "crooked straight" before the full 2nd Advent.
- The Unfolding Strip: Linear reality is a self-propelling, mechanical "artifact." Intervention (3-74) is a "rebuke" to this system, revealing it as provisional and false.
- Breaking the 3:5 Door: Direct action (miracles) by God is neither efficient nor final cause; it is a breach that reveals a second, higher realm.
- The 1st and 2nd Advents: The 1st Advent (Rome 70 AD) "breaches" throughout all linear time. The 2nd Advent (1974) links up to it, completing the overthrow of the Tyrant.
- Judgment via Archetype: Final judgment is simply seeing our position within the "eternal situation" (e.g., our stance against the Black Iron Prison).
- The Act of Balking: To trigger the "redemptive entity," one must FIRST "balk" on their own initiative (e.g., refusing to pay war taxes or respond to the Xerox letter).
- Deus Absconditus: God remains hidden (the "Fallen Angel/Tares" dream) to allow evil ones to act out their natures and be identified for judgment.
