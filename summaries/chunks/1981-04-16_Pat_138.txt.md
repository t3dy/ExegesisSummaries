## Metadata

## Key Claims
- History is a battle between two great antagonists: Ecstasy (Dionysus/Christ) and Sobriety/Truth (Apollo/YHWH). Dick experienced the ecstatic secret of Christianity in 1974, and the sober truth of Judaism in 1981.
- Christianity is fundamentally an ecstatic religion. The Passion and Crucifixion (Tears/Grief) are merely the mechanism; when pushed to their absolute limit, grief undergoes enantiodromia (reversal) into its mirror opposite: resurrection and ecstatic joy (Felix).
- *Flow My Tears* is functionally a Christian Gospel operating on these two levels: the exoteric story of grief (King Pentheus) and the esoteric hidden promise of Joy (King Felix).
- The ultimate goal of the Messianic mission is the "Third Status" (prophesied by Joachim of Fiore): direct, unmediated spiritual knowledge and union with God the Father, moving beyond the intermediary of Christ.

## Entities
- **King Felix vs King of Tears**: The cyphers representing the hidden ecstatic core of Christianity (Joy) versus the ostensible suffering of the material world.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Enantiodromia**: The psychological principle where an extreme force turns into its exact opposite. Absolute sorrow and humiliation (the Cross) necessarily convert into absolute joy (the Resurrection).

## Theological Motifs
- **The Three Stages**: (1) Pre-Fall Unity with God, (2) The Fall and Separation, (3) Christ arriving to provide the path back to the Father via rebirth in the Spirit.
