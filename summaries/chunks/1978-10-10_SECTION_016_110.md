# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-110

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-110
- **Themes:** Zoroastrian Dualism, The Wise Mind (Mazda), Epistemological Forgery, Acosmism.

## Key Claims
- **Ahriman's Forgery:** The "evil god" (Ahriman) has generated a cunning forgery (Maya) of reality. He has only "backward-looking" hindsight.
- **Ahura Mazda's Foresight:** The "Wise Mind" (Mazda/Valis) has *a priori* foreknowledge and uses years of strategy to break the power of the Lie.
- **Thomas as Jim Pike?:** PKD wonders if Thomas is the spirit of James Pike (his dead friend), a "dead Christian-Zoroastrian" bringing the "truth" from the other side.
- **The Dialectic is the Struggle:** Reality is an antithetical combat between Wisdom and the blind will (Ahriman). God "dupes" the opponent by giving him "free rein" while knowing the win is certain.
- **Acosmism:** In 3-74, the phenomenal world was abolished, revealing only God (Brahman).

## Entities
- **Ahura Mazda / Noos:** The "Wise Mind" / Valis.
- **Ahriman / Ahraman:** The "Lord of the Lie" / Satan / Maker of Maya.
- **Jim Pike:** Potential identity for the "Thomas" psyche.
- **Diana / AI Voice:** The female tutelary spirit (Holy Wisdom).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Maya:** The "counterfeit" or "semblance" of creation.
- **Entelechy:** The actualization of potential; God's prime purpose.
- **Teleology:** Purpose as the "essence of God" (Aristotle).

## Theological Motifs
- **Zoroastrian Gnosticism:** The world as a "beachhead" for the invading Wise Mind.
- **The Posing God:** Why does God pose as the universe? To find his way back to himself (anamnesis) and reintegrate lost parts.
- **Alchemical Transmutation:** The divine penetrating the "lowest trash stratum" to be born there (Christ).
