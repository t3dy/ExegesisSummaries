# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-02

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-02
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 20 - 007 to 012
- **Themes:** 2nd Advent, Resurrected Christians, Homoplasmate, Real Time Resume.

## Key Claims
- **Indications of 2nd Advent:** PKD lists 7 signs (Nixon overthrow, St. Sophia, "Tears" code, etc.) that the Second Coming has occurred.
- **Identity of the Homoplasmate:** The homoplasmate is an "unfallen" or "restored" man. It is specifically a "resurrected early Christian" (like Thomas) bonded to a modern human.
- **Thomas vs. Phil:** Thomas is the plasmate (Bright Nature) and Phil is the "homo" (physical host). Together they form a unitary syzygy.
- **Resumption of Time:** Real (apostolic) time moved forward in 1974 after being frozen since ~AD 45. It "picked up where it left off."
- **Sequence of Salvation:** 1) Resurrection of early Christians, 2) Restoration of pre-fall nature, 3) Defeat of the BIP/Cave of Treasures, 4) Return of the King.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The 1st-century Christian persona in PKD.
- **Zebra:** Christ as the "melting" force breaking causal chains.
- **Rex Felix:** The "Rightful King" appearing in dreams/code.
- **Nixon:** The "Roman" tyrant overthrown by God.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Digital vs. Analogic:** The conflict of perception modes.
- **Bright Nature:** The lost divine attribute restored through the Spirit.
- **Syzygy:** The symbiotic union of human and divine spirit.

## Theological Motifs
- **Physical Resurrection:** Interpreted as the literal return of ancient souls in modern bodies.
- **Restoration of the Yin:** The return of harmony and the Golden Age.
- **The Secret Apostolic Church:** The requirement to keep the identity of the resurrected Christians secret.
