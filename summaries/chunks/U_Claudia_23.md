# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his experience as a literal fulfillment of Biblical promise (Psalm 116) and a commissioning into the class of "prophets." He concludes that "Santa Sophia" (Holy Wisdom) is returning to save the world from destruction and that the "Attic Greek" served to prove the information was not an internal fantasy.
## key_claims
- 3-74 was a visitation by the "Holy Spirit" / "Christ Consciousness" / "Grace of God" (Identical states).
- Dick has "discovered" God a priori and corroborated the Sacred Scriptures through independent experience.
- "Santa Sophia" (Wisdom) will be reborn because she was "not acceptable before" (rejected by men).
- Attic Greek was essential to establish the "veracity" and "outside origin" of the message.
- The "Second Advent" involves the "Light Body" of the fellowship of Christ re-entering human history.
- The "Good Shepherd" vision (Animals seeing the tall man) is a "Nativity-like" sign of the Savior's return.
