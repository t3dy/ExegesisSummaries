# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the "Passover/Exodus" as the primary paradigm for his 1974 experience, concluding that Yahweh (the Nature-God) intervened from "outside the state" to free him. He reinterprets his cat Pinky's death as a "Surrogate Sacrifice" (the Paschal Lamb) that shielded his family from the "Destroying Angel."
## key_claims
- Yahweh is a "Nature-God" who pits Himself against the State to free slaves (Exodus model).
- Passover/Springtime = Deliverance from slavery/Redemption.
- The 1974 "Xerox Crisis" was a modern Egypt; the "Destroying Angel" came to the door in August 1974.
- Pinky the cat was the "Paschal Lamb": he took the blow of the Destroying Angel as a surrogate for the firstborn (Christopher).
- God is an "Agape" being who voluntarily replaces the victim (animal or human) to abolish the sin and suffering.
- New Exodus: Humans must leave Earth (Egypt) for other star systems to avoid the master-victim trap.
