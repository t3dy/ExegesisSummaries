# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_35

## Key Claims
- Dick recalls a dream featuring the Greek word "Ditheon" (two-God nature), representing the fusion of two human psyches into a single divine entity (a Hierogamy).
- He parallels this concept with Pierre Teilhard de Chardin's evolutionary theory of convergence toward the "Omega Point"—the idea that humanity is evolving into the Cosmic Christ.
- He concludes that the Parousia (Second Coming) is happening physically right now via this psychological merging/convergence: scattered fragments of humanity are combining to form the body of the Cosmic Christ.
- The Plasmate (living information) is identified as Teilhard's "noosphere," the sphere of human thought driving this spiritual evolution.
- Dick celebrates his grand synthesis of Judaism (Torah as World), Christianity (Logos-Christ as source), and Greek philosophy (reality as Structure/Forms). 

## Entities
- **Pierre Teilhard de Chardin**: His theories of the noosphere, evolutionary convergence, and the Cosmic Christ perfectly match Dick's vision of the "Ditheon" and the Plasmate.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The World as Book**: Returning to the theme that the universe is actually an informational narrative (Logos/Torah) generated by the Mind of God.
- **The Plasmate**: Redefined as the noosphere, the evolving layer of conscious intelligence covering the Earth, destined to become the Cosmic Christ.

## Theological Motifs
- **Evolutionary Parousia**: The Messianic age does not arrive via external apocalypse, but through the internal, evolutionary fusion of human minds into the collective Christos.
