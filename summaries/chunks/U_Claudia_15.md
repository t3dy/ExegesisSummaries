# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the "Second Advent" as a "Secret Kingdom" existing in eternity while the "usurper" (Satan/Empire) remains in control of linear time. He uses a "Regicide" story (the rightful heir returning in riddles) to model the current state of the world and links his experience of "Anamnesis" to the Christian Eucharist.
## key_claims
- The "Kingdom of God" is a "secret kingdom" that can be entered individually through anamnesis.
- The "Secret Partnership" (Horus/Seth) means that while conflict exists in time, there is unity in eternity.
- Christ is the "Path" or "Guide" (Pinky/Guru) who rescues individuals and leads them to the Kingship of God.
- Reality is a "Regicide" drama: the rightful heir (Jesus/Dionysos/Hamlet) has returned to a usurped throne (Empire) and speaks in riddles.
- "Anamnesis" is the biological/ontological transformation of the subject into the "Host" (Bread/Wine).
- Dick's vision of the "Corn King" dancing in the furrows represents the awakening of "seeds" (us) in the "winter" of the grave.
