# Chunk 175 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_175

## Description
In this final synthesis of the batch, PKD decodes the "King Felix" message as the definitive proof that Christ has already won and linear time is a "Satanic negative hallucination." He concludes that his writing is an "excavation" of the geological strata of reality, revealing the "Fossil Remnant" of Rome underneath.

## Key Claims
- **Three-Layer Occlusion**:
    1. **Layer 1 (Fusion)**: Christ hasn't returned (Untrue).
    2. **Layer 2 (BIP/Rome)**: Christ will never return (Untrue " truth").
    3. **Layer 3 (PTG/Felix)**: Christ has ALREADY returned (Absolute Truth).
- **The Satanic Hallucination**: Linear time since A.D. 70 is a "deliberate negative hallucination" to blind us to the victory of the Christian underground.
- **Geological Strata**: (1) is the present, (2) is the buried past (BIP), (3) is the future (PTG) descending onto the present like "UBIK."
- **Irradiation Theory**: 3-74 was caused by "Soviet satellite microwave irradiation" which speeded up neural firing and nullified the "inner tape."

## Entities
- **King Felix**: The "Bottom Line" message from the network/VALIS.
- **Dionysos / Jason Taverner / Christ**: The "drinker" who opposes the "Pentheus" tyranny of the police.

- **Stanislaw Lem**: Referenced again as someone who "knew before I did."
