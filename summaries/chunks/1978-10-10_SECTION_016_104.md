# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-104

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-104
- **Themes:** Return of Christ, Cosmic Eucharist, Synchronicity design, The Master Strategist.

## Key Claims
- **Christ's Presence (King Felix):** The 1970 "King Felix" cypher and the 1974 rescue prove that Christ has returned (Parousia).
- **Valis + Plasmate = Host:** The "two species" of the host are Valis (Body/Structure) and the Plasmate (Blood/Information).
- **Teleological Tug:** Synchronicity (Jung-Pauli) is not acausal; it is a "horizontal" pattern prearranged by a teleological mind (God) to disclose his existence via "horizontal synchronization."
- **Pretextual Cross-Firing:** Natural law is the "pretext" for God's will. Everything seems deterministic because his will pervades the total system.
- **Master Strategist:** God creates an adversary (Satan/Dialectic) to "unwittingly aid" in fulfilling the divine plan.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The "instinctive acts" cluster that carried out the 3-74 mission.
- **Gary Corp:** The G-2 intelligence officer behind the Xerox missive.
- **St. Sophia:** The female aspect of Christ being born again.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Ajna Eye:** The "3rd eye" of discernment needed to see Valis.
- **Internal Physical Causes:** Aristotle's idea of purpose built into nature.
- **Tao / Brahman:** The unity of will between the organism and the absolute.

## Theological Motifs
- **Transubstantiation:** Turning a "thing" into "God," which is what Valis does to reality.
- **Felix Culpa:** The "Hapyy Fall" that necessitates the salvific replication of Christ.
- **Angel of Death:** The "passover" where Christ (the lamb/Pinky) dies so the individual survives.
