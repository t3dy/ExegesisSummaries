# September 15, 1976
# Fallen Angels and the Parousia "Out of Phase"
## concise_summary
Dick proposes a radical theory: humans are not "fallen men" but "fallen angels" with degenerated memories and powers. He views 3-74 as a "pre-experience of the Parousia" occurring out of sync with linear time. He identifies the messenger as the angel Gabriel and defines the Second Coming as a "restoration of original image" on a global scale.
## key_claims
- Fallen Angels Theory: Humans are debased angels in exile with deformed faculties. "Sin" is just a debased concept of this fall.
- Occham’s Razor Conclusion: Dick concludes "we are gods with deformed memories."
- Time "Out of Phase": 3-74 was the Parousia experienced "temporarily out of phase" with its locus in linear time (due to Dick's prophetic nature).
- Gabriel: Identifies the 1974 messenger as the Angel Gabriel.
- The Narrow Gate: The "Golden Rectangle gate" was the opening between the sublunar and supralunar realms.
- Restoration: The Second Coming will be the restoration of the "original image" (God’s image) to all mankind.
- 70 A.D. as Locus: Confirms he experienced Rome 70 A.D. and felt placed back in the past via "anamnesis."
