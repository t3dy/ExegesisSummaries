# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-49

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-49
- **Themes:** Feedback Loop of Belief, Thought-Form Arcing, Ubik vs Anselm, Plasmate Transubstantiation.

## Key Claims
- **Self-Perpetuating Feedback:** Thought-formations generate belief systems, and belief systems generate new objectified thought-formations in a 0-1 flip-flop grid.
- **Arcing from the Past:** A thought-formation (like *Ubik*) "arcs" from its origin in the 60s to 1974, becoming "supercharged" because the percipient (PKD) has diminished/eroded over time.
- **Anselmian Ubik:** Assigning ubiquity, wisdom, and the power to save leads to a concept that cannot be denied existence without logical contradiction.
- **Plasmate Transubstantiation:** The plasmate "rides" the information in *Ubik* and *Tears*, transubstantiating the fictional into the living real/Logos.
- **Intermingling in Cold-Pak:** The personality "Thomas" invaded PKD because we are all together in a Bardo/Cold-Pak state where "bleeding across" is possible.

## Entities
- **Ubik (The Concept):** The target for the Plasmate's inhabitation.
- **Thomas:** The 1st-century personality who "bleeds across" in the Bardo.
- **Jory / Ella Runciter:** Archetypes of the "invasion of vitality" in a weakened state.

## Recurring Concepts
- **0-1 Flip-Flop Grid:** The binary pattern of truth/untruth, life/death in the world-soul.
- **Sustains:** Elements that allow memory to be laid down across frames.
- **Mimesis/Mimicry:** The deity's ability to take the most "syntonic" (acceptable/familiar) form for the percipient.

## Theological Motifs
- **Second Advent:** The era where the roles of Christ (Victim) and Rome (Pilate/Felix) are reversed (Christus Victor).
- **Holy Spirit as Inhalation:** The force drawing "sparks" back to the source.
- **Real Memory (Anamnesis):** Memory not derived from the current situational cues of the frame.
