## Key Claims
- Plato's Doctrine of Forms is a "tragic misconstrual." The Forms aren't eternal ideals in heaven; they are simply the repetitive blueprints of our single, re-tracked life. We mistake the re-tracking of the exact same life for eternal Forms instantiating in new particulars.
- Anamnesis (remembering) is not recalling a past life or a heavenly pre-existence; it is remembering the *current* life that we have lived countless times before in the counterfeit time-loop.
- Philo's concept of *Pronoia* perfectly matches Dick's "pulley" vision: YHWH directly intervening in the world to suspend natural causal law (Heimarmene) to save a deserving, pious person.
- The 4D world is a maze/initiation rite. The solution to the maze is both cognitive (the meta-abstraction) and moral (Philanthropia/Agape), triggering God's miraculous intervention.

## Entities
- **Philo**: Jewish philosopher whose integration of Torah, Pronoia, and Platonic Forms provides Dick with the exact theological framework for his 1974 rescue.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Ditheon**: The "Di" (dialectic) + "theon" (God). It represents reality as a dialectic between an irrational destructive will and the "bright/Logos" side (which always wins, as it did in 3-74).

## Theological Motifs
- **Philanthropia**: The moral basis of Dick's life—giving to Covenant House—which he believes triggered the divine grace (Pronoia) that broke him out of the causal prison.
