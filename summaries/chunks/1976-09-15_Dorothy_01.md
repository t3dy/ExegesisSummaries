## concise_summary
Dick reflects on an interview with a reporter that clarifies his 1974 experience as the "answer" to his lifelong philosophical question "What is real?". He lists the physical and symbolic "causal factors" of his theophany and introduces the concept of right-brain firing (metasystems) and the "Bardo Thodol" journey.
