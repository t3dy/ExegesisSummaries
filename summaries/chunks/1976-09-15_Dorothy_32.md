# Acceleration of Consciousness and the 2000-Year Memory
## concise_summary
Dick theorizes that 3-74 was an "expansion of the present slot" via "perceptual memory," allowing him to see 2000 years of change in months. He identifies the "constants" that emerged from this acceleration: God, the Black Iron Prison, and the building of the New Earth.
## key_claims
- Expanded Present Slot: Dick's internal "neural firing" speeded up, expanding his perception of the "now" to include 2000 years of racial memory.
- Perceptual Memory: Just as a movie film creates motion from stills, his accelerated perception revealed the "purpose and direction" of reality (Zebra).
- Constant Forms: Out of the "ephemera" of history, certain "Platonic Forms" became visible: the BIP (as the 2000-year constant) and the New Earth (as the emerging structure).
- The True "I": He realizes the real "I" spans millennia and is immortal; 3-74 was the "disinhibition" of this trans-temporal personality.
- The Golden Fish Excitor: The necklace was the stimulus that triggered the firing of the entire collective unconscious.
- Spiritual Rome: Our world is "Spiritual Rome," a near-permanent condition lifted only by divine intervention (Apocalypse).
- Monad Projection: He considers a Leibnizian view: we are "pre-set monads" projecting our own reality, which only seems objective because we are synchronized.
