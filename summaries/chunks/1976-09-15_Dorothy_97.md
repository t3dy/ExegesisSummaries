# The Invasion of History: Deposing the False King
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his writing as a sequence of "Gnostic signals" to other amnesiac "invaders." He interprets the fall of Nixon (1974) as the mythic/archetypal "deposing of the false king" (Klingsor's Castle) by the true king, Zebra (Dionysos/Christ).
## key_claims
- Writers as Messengers: Dick's stories (especially *Maze*, *Impostor*, *Ubik*) contain "disinhibiting signals" for others who are amnesiac invaders for the "good deity."
- Deposing the False King: The 1974 Nixon resignation was a "Holy Ritual" of deposing the "Demented False King" (BIP I).
- Zebra as Dionysos/Shiva: Dick equates Zebra with Shiva and Dionysos. The "3rd Eye" is both a restorer (for the good) and a destroyer (for the illegitimate authorities).
- Klingsor's Castle Shattered: The "Iron Prison" world is like Klingsor's castle in *Parsifal*—it appeared substantial but was "irreal" and vanished when judged.
- The "Goose Girl" Myth: The King and the Jester have exchanged places. Our world is "somber madness," but the true king (The Jester/Christ) is returning to save the "least."
- 3rd Kind Contact: Dick affirms the Metz speech—he encountered a "superior life form" (Zebra) that manipulated history and perceptions.
- Underground Church: There is a "true, underground, secret church" in league with this entity, existing since antiquity.
