# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick describes a "blissful mystical experience" where he perceives God's activity as a "Tao-istic mechanism" (Ma'at) that re-establishes equilibrium below the surface of evil. He concludes that 3-74 was a "parabolic reverse" where the "Empire" (Rome/Nixon) was hollowed out and transformed from within into its opposite.
## key_claims
- Tao-istic Ma'at: God's activity in history is the "perpetual re-establishment of equilibrium" (Law of Karma/Ma'at).
- Parabolic Return: 3-74 was the "turning point" where Retreat transformed into Advance; a new roll of film was inserted into history.
- Hollowing the Evil: Evil (Rome/Tyranny) appears in control, but God has "made it hollow" and turned it into a shell from within.
- Parousia as Waiting/Battle: The current interval is a "harsh preparing for combat" (muffled drums), not a time of love.
- History Engineered: *Tears* and *Frolix 8* were engineered by the Logos to carry stenographic codes to pull down the tyranny.
- The "Je BORG" Figure: A mysterious female figure (Santa Sophia/Ma'at) who has been "close by" since the start.
