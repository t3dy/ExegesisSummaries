# Causality as a Mask for Noos
## concise_summary
Dick analyzes how our sense of causality is manipulated by a higher intelligence (Noos). He suggests that what we perceive as "cause and effect" is often a concealment used by Zebra to move the world in a specific direction without being noticed.
## key_claims
- Noos Manipulation: The higher mind (Noos/Zebra) uses the laws of physics and causality as a camouflage.
- Hidden Mechanism: The "true" causes of significant events (like the fall of Nixon) are divine, but they appear "normal" to observers.
- Illusion of Free Will: Human choices are "bent" or "deflected" by Zebra to serve the greater plan.
- Zebra's Stealth: The entity "hides in the open" by behaving like natural law.
