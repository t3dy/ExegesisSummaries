# Laminated History: The Three-Thousand-Year Event
## concise_summary
Dick posits that our linear history is actually a "lamination" of segments from a single, supratemporal event: the war between Christ and the Black Iron Prison. He identifies himself as "Thomas," an immortal agent who has been fighting this war since A.D. 70. He argues that his 3-74 vision allowed him to see 2000 years of "freeze frames" speeded up into a single motion, revealing that the "intervening years" are a mere thought or hologram that God can repeal at will to fulfill the Second Coming.
## key_claims
- Laminated Time: 1000s of years of events are actually "layers" of one single supratemporay event (Christ vs. Rome).
- Immortal Continuity: PKD/Thomas possesses a 2000-year sequence of memories; he is a "phagocyte" or "metabolic toxin" sent to dissolve the BIP.
- The Repeating Pass: History is in its "second (or more) pass," where God re-directs events (Tracks A and B) to correct "worse" outcomes.
- Repeal of Time and Death: If God can repeal time, He can repeal death; Thomas was "dead" for 2000 years but "woke up" in 1974.
- Reality as Thought: The universe is a "thought in the mind of God" without intrinsic substance, explaining how the past can be altered or erased.
- Miracle for the Sane: Divine "melting" of causal trains is rare because most cannot endure the discovery that the universe is a hologram.
