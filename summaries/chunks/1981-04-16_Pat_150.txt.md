## Key Claims
- The 2-74 meta-abstraction escalated "part-whole compatibility" into true "part-whole isomorphism," where the microcosm (individual) fully and structurally contains the macrocosm (universe).
- When this occurs, the universe acts as a "Universal Language" (information) generated acausally within the individual (synchronicity). 
- The Plasmate is not an independent lifeform; rather, it is the "information metabolism" of the true, unified Kosmos. It is the linguistic/informational connective tissue that maintains the structure (negentropy) of reality.
- Salvation is the restoration of the "extinct true Kosmos," reversing the disintegration of reality into discontinuous matter (the atomist error). The Holy Spirit acts as the interface, facilitating this part-whole isomorphism.

## Entities
