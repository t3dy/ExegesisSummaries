# Time Out of Joint: The Reverse Revelation
## concise_summary
Dick realizes that his 1959 novel *Time Out of Joint* was a prophetic "reversal" of his 3-74 experience. While the novel depicts a fake 1950s that is actually the future, 3-74 revealed a fake 1974 that was actually 70 AD. He explores his "transtemporal" psyche and the archetype of the Black Iron Prison.
## key_claims
- Reverse Prophecy: *Time Out of Joint* (fake present/real future) perfectly anticipated 3-74 (fake present/real past). The "temporal locus" is the core of the world-delusion.
- Transtemporal Psyche: Dick experiences himself as spanning far-past, near-past, and near-future, suggesting his "soul" is an eternal, immortal spirit.
- The Black Iron Prison Archetype: A timeless Platonic form that "prints out" into history repeatedly: Babylon, Rome, the Police Building in *Tears*, and the USA.
- Abreaction to Medievalism: In 3-74, Dick "abreacted" to a medieval worldview, breaking through the "eikoi" (images) of space-time into the "edoli" (real forms).
- Christ as Intercessor: In August 1974, Christ "died for me" by interposing himself against a programmed death Dick was scripted to undergo.
- Form Regression: Evolution isn't just linear; it's a "peeling back" (like geological strata) to expose earlier, more real layers along an "orthogonal" axis.
