# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-11

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-11
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 010 to 013
- **Themes:** Fake Time, Frozen Empire, Competing Technologies, One-by-One Drawing.

## Key Claims
- **Frozen History:** Time stopped in AD 45. Rome perpetuates itself in a "plethora of disguises" through a counterfeit ontological matrix.
- **The Iron Age Freeze:** The Iron Age refuses to yield to the Golden Age due to a "world psychosis" or entropy (hysteresis).
- **Competing Technologies:** PKD initially theorizes two competing technologies (enslaving vs. rescuing) but then moves toward one hierarchical technology (the Macrobrain).
- **"One by One":** Christ is "drawing us out of this world" – a process of individual extrication from the stagnant, toxic pool.
- **Anamnesis as Breach:** Restored memory (prong 4) is the weak link that collapses the other three prongs of the deformation.

## Entities
- **The Empire:** The entropic, frozen state of Rome.
- **Zebra:** The macro-brain counterpart of the homoplasmate.
- **The Good Shepherd:** The one drawing individuals out of the simulation.
- **Firebright:** The plasmate form of the restored spirit.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Mors Ontologica:** Spiritual death as the prerequisite for inauthentic existence.
- **Hysteresis:** The persistence of an image (Rome) that should have vanished.
- **Stagnant Pond:** A vision of our world where only a few "tadpoles" escape.

## Theological Motifs
- **Individual Salvation:** Not all are saved at once; it's a process of ripening.
- **Gnostic Rescue:** We are "rescued off" a dying, toxic planet.
- **The "Drawing" Mechanism:** Like a fisherman's line pulling the soul up from the lower levels.
