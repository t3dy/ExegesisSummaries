# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies "Conscience" (Socrates' Daimon) as the entry point for the "Holy Spirit" into social action, leading to a "Godly Anarchy." He explores the brain's "Right Hemisphere" as the location of this "Inward Light" and proposes that humans are evolving into a "Superior Type" (typified by characters like "Kathy") who manufacture their own worlds.
## key_claims
- "Conscience" is God teaching us; 3-74 was conscience evolving into the Holy Spirit as a force for social action.
- The 1st Advent = The Lamb (Sacrifice); 2nd Advent = The Powerful Judge (Overthrowing evil).
- Godly Anarchy: Future society will have no need for laws or authority because the "Internal Radio Set" (Logos) will coordinate individuals.
- "Kathy/Stephanie" Type: A person whose values are *sui generis* (not from society), appearing "sociopathic" to the establishment but actually following "distant drummers."
- Right Hemisphere: The "Inward Light" resides here; it takes over when the "Linear/Left" brain paints itself into a corner.
- *Ma'at* (Law/Truth) is the voice of this "Superior Part" of the mind, which has no infancy and is perfected in ontology.
