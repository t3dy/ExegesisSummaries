## Key Claims
- The "meta-abstraction" was fundamentally an act of Dick's latent Jungian *Thinking/Judging* function waking up. After years of using his *Intuitive* function to entertain possibilities (fiction), his Rational function engaged and declared Christianity "The Fact of Facts" (it is literally true).
- Angel Archer personifies this buried rational, thinking function. When she merged with his conscious intuitive function, it created Ditheon (bilateral parity).
- Faith (Pistis), for Dick, is not an irrational leap, but a compelled assent by the rational faculty discovering an absolute, necessary truth.
- The core of the revelation is that *Acts is contemporaneous*. The Holy Spirit lifts the Passion and Resurrection out of history and applies it seamlessly to the present moment, meaning AD 70 and 1974 are syntonically the exact same time and place.

## Entities
- **Carl Jung**: His typological compass (Thinking, Feeling, Sensation, Intuition) is used to explain the mechanics of Dick's revelation and the birth of his internal female counterpart.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Fact of Facts**: The shift from aesthetic appreciation of myth (possibility) to absolute epistemological certainty (truth).
- **Contemporaneous Passion**: The eradication of historical distance separating the believer from the cross.

## Theological Motifs
