# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 222

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 222
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 03 - 065 to 069
- **Themes:** Form I vs. Form II, The Smuggled Savior, "Tears" as Truth, Zebra/Christ, The Macro Mirror.

## Key Claims
- **The Smuggled Savior:** The mission of the secret Christians is to "smuggle" the immortal man (perhaps as a baby) into the "obvious world" to eventually rule it. This is a "born again" Pansophia.
- **Form I (Latent) vs. Form II (Obvious):** Form I (Upper/Inner/Latent) is real and has the power to "make plastic" Form II (Lower/Outer/Obvious), which is simulated and a snare (*Dokos*).
- **"Tears" as True Narrative:** PKD asserts that *Flow My Tears, the Policeman Said* is the true narrative of the latent world (Level #3), where Christ/Zebra is the victor (the "Wise Old King") and Caesar/BIP is being dissolved.
- **Zebra as Christ/Logos:** PKD equates Zebra with Christ, Holy Wisdom (St. Sophia), and the Logos (Ubik). He describes Zebra as camouflaged, modulating people and objects.
- **The Master Circuit Flip:** The potential of the latent world builds up until it "discharges," converting the obvious world into its opposite (like a "flipping" of light and dark in the game of Othello).
- **Man as Micro-Mirror:** Man contains the "map" or "Logos" of the macrocosm in miniature; thus, self-awareness is awareness of the latent realm.

## Entities
- **The Wise Old King:** The victorious Christ figure in "Tears."
- **Zebra/Ubik/St. Sophia:** The various names for the divine invading force.
- **BIP (Black Iron Prison):** The "heavy metal particle" of Caesar's empire.
- **Thomas:** The waking self/early Christian in PKD's head.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Phagocyte Model:** Christ/Zebra as a white blood cell engulfing the "poisonous" BIP.
- **Anamnesis:** Triggered by the Brotherhood to open the *Dibba Cakkhu* (Third Eye).
- **Syntonic Worlds:** The latent world is syntonic (aligned) with the obvious world but exists in a different time/state (Acts).

## Theological Motifs
- **Absolute Rule of Christ:** Christ reigns in the Platonist "real world" (*Dinge-an-sich*), which "perturbates" our phenomenal world.
- **The Narrow Door:** Redemption re-entering the fallen realm to re-annex it.
- **Freedom from Karma:** Grace freeing the individual from "astral determinism."
