# Chunk 170 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_170

## Description
This chunk explores the dissolution of the "Alibi World" (USA 1974) and the emergence of the real world of *Acts* (C.E. 45). PKD theorizes that his inner "tape" (programming) sped up, causing him to mentally "die" and be replaced by his future/next personality (Thomas).

## Key Claims
- **Separation from World**: Salvation requires a "saying no" to the coercive power of the world (BIP), thus aligning with God and outranking the "angels" (the yin system).
- **The Tape Malfunction**: 3-74 was a "malfunction" where PKD's inner time-tape ran faster than the outer world, causing a "synch error."
- **Astral Determinism vs. Holy Spirit**: The Holy Spirit's primary role is to break the "inner-outer feedback programming" that locks a person into a specific space-time world.
- **USA 1974 as Alibi**: The "surface PKD personality" and the "1974 world" are BOTH expendable Alibis designed to conceal the reality of *Acts*.

## Entities
- **Ho on**: The world-generating mind (God).
- **Cephalos**: The Cosmic Christ/Head of the "supratemporal entity" (Corpus Christi).
- **Zebra**: Identified as the "Cosmic Christ" extensive in time and space.
- **Thomas**: PKD's "next self" or "future life" identity that entered in 3-74.
- **Felix**: The "judgement" resulting in joy (as opposed to Culpa/Guilt).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Inner-Outer Feedback Loop**: The mechanism that creates the illusion of a substantial world through constant reinforcement.
- **Simulation and Programming**: The "radical epistemology" where world is a brain-simulation which we give "assent" to.
- **Bardo Thodol Journey**: The 3-74 experience as a mental death and "post-mortem" segment of the tape.
- **Ajna Chakra / Double Helix**: Symbols of the "master key" to the DNA/gene-pool programming.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Dancing King**: Beneath the "solemn" and "majestic" mask is the hidden joy of Dionysos.
- **Freeing the Slave (Android)**: Only the Holy Spirit can liberate the "android" (slave to DNA) by allowing them to perceive their programming.
- **End Time Disposition**: 3-74 was a "final disposition" and judgment of PKD as part of Christ's flock.
