## Key Claims
- The suffering and death of the savior (Christ) is no longer a historical event happening to one man; it is a "macro-crucifixion" happening simultaneously worldwide to the entire ecosphere.
- Tagore's incarnation is a cautionary warning. If humanity continues its toxic destruction, it will drive the divine presence (Valis) away. Tagore is dying now in order to save the ecosphere from this fate.
- Dick understands his own timeline through God's "backward-working strategy": the 1974 epiphany of Valis in nature was a preemptive vision given so that he would understand the 1981 Tagore revelation.
- The novel *VALIS* was thus completely prophetic; it served to foretell the coming of Tagore and to document the underlying premise of the Third Dispensation (that the Logos transubstantiates nature).
