# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-36

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-36
- **Themes:** Ontological Death, Time as Energy, The Static Program, Camouflaged Intervention.

## Key Claims
- **Track A as Failure:** Track A was not just "bad," it "died" biologically; it failed to develop through its proper stages and ossified (the BIP).
- **Time as Dynamic Force:** Time is a negentropic energy that drives a program through growth. If it fails, the world reverts to archaic forms (as in *Ubik*).
- **The Ossified Program:** In 1974 (Track A), the program was fixated/stagnant at 45 AD. The BIP is a "calcification" or "death into rigidity."
- **Computer Camouflage:** The computer (VALIS) is plugged into its own program (as a micro-form/terminal) to monitor it without contaminating the "closed" system.
- **Christ as the Free Variable:** Christ is the unique variable introduced from outside the program, not subject to its dialectic or time, to "strip the powers" of their laws.

## Entities
- **VALIS/Christ:** The free variable and "unique invader" who corrects the program.
- **The Secret Christians:** Agents of the computer-programmer in the world.
- **The Higher Power:** The one who "one by one takes us out of this world" (extrication).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Stasis/Ossification:** The state of a failed program where time dies and the form reverts.
- **Closed System:** The necessary condition for the program's "efficient-cause" dialectic; broken only by Grace.
- **Camouflage:** Necessary to avoid destroying the experiment/program through overt presence.

## Theological Motifs
- **Grace vs. Fate:** Grace is the unweaving of the "mesh of metal strands" of fate/determinism.
- **Exculpation/Extrication:** Being "locked" out of the program's fate permanently after 3-74.
- **Judgment of the System:** The reprogramming (Track B) as a divine judgment on the failure of Track A.
