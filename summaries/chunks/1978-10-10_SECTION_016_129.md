# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-129

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-129
- **Themes:** Anamnesis Mythos, The Hainowele Mystery, The Rational Hero vs. Chaos, The Soviet America Scenario.

## Key Claims
- **Hainowele Concept:** Christ/Valis is camouflaged into reality like the girl "Hainowele" (whose dismembered body became food for mankind). This is "Rural Cosmic Xtianity"—Christ as the "landscape as food/info."
- **The Hero vs. Chaos:** Valis is the "Hero-Mind" (Rational) invading and conquering the "Primordial Chaos" (Irrational/BIP).
- **The Revolutionary Underground Timeline:** Collation of *Frolix 8* and *Tears* shows the "Cordonite" revolution became successful in *Tears*, resulting in a "Sovietized America" (where the revolutionaries are now the State).
- **Thomas vs. Lem:** PKD wonders if Thomas is a "defector" from the secret group who decided not to go "home" and instead helped PKD thwart a "Pigspurt" probe.
- **A priori Logic Failure:** The Xerox missive was "rationally/digitally" a forgery, yet "empirically/right-hemispherically" a Pigspurt truth. Deductive logic fails in the face of Valis.

## Entities
- **Hainowele:** The primordial sacrificial being (plant/food giver).
- **Thomas:** The "escaped" or "returned" master self/defector.
- **The Paraclete:** The agent of "Second Birth" who restores unfallen status.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Phylogenic Recollection:** Continuity of identity across lifetimes achieved via anamnesis.
- **Sovietized USA:** The "Tears" scenario of 5 Marshals and labor camps.
- **Macro-Self Incorporation:** The "Born Again" experience as joining a master macro-organism.

## Theological Motifs
- **Birth in the Spirit:** The retrieval of the "Unfallen State" via the Holy Spirit.
- **Rational Invasion:** The divine "sorting" of reality into Kosmos.
- **Sacramental Info-Eating:** Consuming the "landscape" as a way of retrieving Star-home memory.
