# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-123

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-123
- **Themes:** Feedback Loop of Doubts, Recognition of the Tape, The Disintegrating Dokos, Buddha's Enlightenment.

## Key Claims
- **The Script in the Mirror:** Reality is a "tape" of forgotten memories fed back as world. If one starts to doubt it (as in *Joint* or *Eye*), that "doubt" is eventually fed back, weakening the "reality-lock."
- **Noise in the Circuit:** Doubts act as "noise" that builds on itself in a positive feedback loop. Each pass (e.g., recurrence of the 1950s in the 1970s) becomes more "vitiated" and transparent.
- **Breaking Maya:** This disintegration of the "Dokos" (semblance) is the "Dibba Cacchu" enlightenment of the Buddha—the "burning up" of Maya and Karma.
- **The 1963-1974 Overlap:** Anamnesis of "Rome" may actually be a return to the 1950s/60s (the previous pass), which only *seems* ancient because of the "time dysfunction."
- **Thomas as "Waking Me":** Thomas is the "waking self" who left notes (the novels) for the "sleeping self" to find in the next cycle.

## Entities
- **Maya:** The counterfeit authoritative reality.
- **The Frolixan / Valis:** The "macro-entity" that assists in the breakdown of the simulation.
- **The Pharmacy Girl:** The 2-74 encounter that started the reality-disintegration.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Positive Feedback Loop:** Doubts magnifying each other across time-passes.
- **Dokos:** Semblance/illusion posing as binding reality.
- **Notes to Self:** The idea that *Ubik*, *Faith of Our Fathers*, etc., are memoes from a future-past self.

## Theological Motifs
- **Karma Burning:** The dissolution of deterministic cycles via recognition/awareness.
- **Dibba Cacchu:** The "Divine Eye" that sees through the illusion of time.
- **Transmutation of Reality:** The shift from being controlled by reality to having power over it.
