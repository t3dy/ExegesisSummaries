# Soviet Weaponry and the Rescue by God
## concise_summary
Dick explores the "genealogical table" of the Second Incarnation (St. Sophia). He reacts to a news report about Soviet microwave weapons causing "words in the head," initially fearing 3-74 was a telepathic attack but concluding God used the duress to trigger a rescue. He identifies himself as an "alienated" apocalyptic writer chosen for these revelations.
## key_claims
- Geneological Table of Christ: Two essences (Divine/Human) forming one person; masculine outcome from feminine inner nature (Ch'ien).
- The "Rolling Stone" theory (EB mentions): Prosperous people don't see the evil system; only the alienated (like Dick) recognize the need for divine intervention.
- Aphrodite/Cyrenaic Vision: Recalls the vision of Aphrodite as the "Steersman" of gestalts (*krasoi*).
- Soviet Microwave Payoff: NBC reports Soviet microwave beams causing "words in the head." Dick identifies this as the *initial* cause of 3-74 (phosphenes, "Boris Romanov" dreams), which then triggered his prayer for help and the subseqent Holy Spirit rescue.
- Noosphere Overload: Theorizes Soviet transmissions overloaded the "noosphere" (Teilhard de Chardin), causing it to impinge on him.
