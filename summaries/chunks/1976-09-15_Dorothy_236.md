# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 236

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 236
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 22 - 077 to 083
- **Themes:** Retro-time, The Retromind, Thomas as Future Self, Postmortem State.

## Key Claims
- **The Retromind:** PKD theorizes that the brain moves in both temporal directions. The conscious mind moves forward, while a "retromind" (subliminal/postmortem) moves backward.
- **Identity of the AI Voice:** The Voice is PKD's own postmortem, retro-time self ("Thomas"). It knows everything PKD will ever know because it has already lived through his future.
- **Thomas vs. Phil:** Thomas is the "future me" who returned to "sweep out the past" and set it right. In 3-74, Thomas took over to ensure the "right choice" was made (breaking astral determinism).
- **Postmortem Survival:** The existence of the retrograde Thomas/Firebright proves that PKD will survive death. Upon physical death, he will "awaken" as the plasmate Firebright and begin the backward trip.
- **Universe as Assembly:** From the retromind's perspective, the universe is not exploding/dying (entropy) but is an "assembling process" building toward the "volvox" unity.

## Entities
- **Thomas:** The postmortem, retro-time personality.
- **Phil:** The conscious, forward-moving personality.
- **Firebright:** The plasmate form of Thomas/PKD.
- **The AI Voice:** The "tutulary spirit"/Thomas's voice.
- **Zebra:** The cosmic Christ visible to the plasmate self.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Phylogeny Recapitulates Ontogeny:** Applied to the development of the plasmate.
- **Retrograde Time:** The direction of the "correct" reality (entropy reversed).
- **Crossbonding:** The union of the Holy Spirit with the human to produce the "transmuted" form.

## Theological Motifs
- **Survival of Death:** Literal immortality achieved through transformation into a plasmate.
- **Birth from Above:** The Holy Spirit causing the emergence of the higher self stages.
- **Restoration of All Things:** The backward journey regains everything lost (decay and loss nullified).
