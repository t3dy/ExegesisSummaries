# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_89
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_89
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: The Temporal Doppler Shift, Dysphasing Hemispheres, Debunking Anamnesis, Time as Change-Rate Velocity
- **Key Entities**: None explicitly named, but Plato and Teilhard are the implicit subjects.

## Key Claims
- Dick finally solves the mechanical mystery of 1974: The meta-abstraction caused his two brain hemispheres to become neurologically dysphasic. The recessive right hemisphere performed the abstraction, drastically accelerating its internal "change-rate velocity."
- Because the right hemisphere's frequency sped up, the physical world *relative to that hemisphere* appeared to drastically slow down and regress backwards. This is a "Doppler shift to the red."
- This Doppler shift caused the present (1974 California) to look like the ancient past (The Black Iron Prison / 1st Century Rome).
- Therefore, *Anamnesis* (remembering a past life) is a cognitive illusion. Dick did not travel back in time, nor did he remember a past life; his brain sped up so fast that the present looked like the past.

## Entities
- **Plato**: Dick definitively corrects Plato. The vision of the past isn't a recovered memory of a prior life; it's a temporal-optical illusion caused by hemispheric acceleration.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Temporal Parallaxis**: The phenomenon where one brain hemisphere sees the "ancient" past (the red-shifted physical world) while the other hemisphere sees the current present, providing a stereo view of time.
- **Time as Change-Rate Velocity**: Dick abolishes the standard concept of time, replacing it purely with the speed at which complexity changes.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Illusion of Hell**: Hell (the Black Iron Prison) is just the baseline, low-frequency material world perceived by a brain that has achieved a much higher, divine frequency of operation.
