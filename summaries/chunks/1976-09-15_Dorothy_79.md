# The Apotheosis through Overstepping: The Law of Ma'at
## concise_summary
Dick formulaizes a law of "Cosmic Justice" (Ma'at/Tao): when an oppressor (B) "oversteps" its limit against an innocent victim (C), it triggers a "justification" by Valis that confers divinity (Apotheosis) on the victim and destroys the oppressor. He argues the Crucifixion is the paradigm for this "alchemical" mechanism.
## key_claims
- The Law of Overstepping: Extremes of "Atelos" (Irregularity/Injustice) attract "Telos" (Correction/Order). An unmerited blow (The Xerox letter/Crucifixion) forces Valis to "justify" the victim by making them part of itself.
- Apotheosis as Escape: This "justification" (Apotheosis) is a permanent transformation that releases the soul from reincarnation (Karma) and the "BIP."
- The "Heat Exchange": In the collision between B and C, energy is transferred: the oppressor is rendered entropic (humbled/destroyed) and the victim negentropic (elevated/divinized).
- "Maneuvering" Evil: A "C" (Jedi/Christian) can use this law to obtain apotheosis by "luring" the Empire into a massive overstepping.
- The Christ Story as Manual: The N.T. is a code explaining how to use "unmerited suffering" as a catalyst for the Second Birth. The secret was lost when Christians began to believe in "vicarious atonement" (a passive lie).
- The Two-Stage Organism: Humans are two-stage beings. Stage 1 is "Rebirth-Loop/Sleep." Stage 2 is "Apotheosis/Valis." The overstepping-intervention is the "kick" needed to transition to Stage 2.
- Short-Circuiting the Tape: In 3-74, Valis "justified" Dick, causing his deterministic "fate-tape" to play out at high speed in a vacuum, rendering his future irrelevant.
- Acts is the "Acts" of the Rebels: *Acts of the Apostles* describes the *current* war in the *future* slave state (USA-1974) where "transactions" with evil occur.
