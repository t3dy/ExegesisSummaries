# Chunk 220 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_220

## Description
PKD identifies himself as a **Double Agent** in the cosmic war, having unknowingly dismantled a "CP/Soviet establishment" plan to take over US Science Fiction. He concludes that "Christ" is a multiform state of evolution that progressively "assimilates" more human hosts.

## Key Claims
- **The Cuckoo Agent**: Humanity is a collection of "double agents" with false memories. PKD realized he was secretly a "Thomas" agent who dismantled the plan of the "East" (BIP) to use SF as a psychological weapon.
- **Multiform Christ**: Christ is not just one person but a "stage of evolution and consciousness." He is an "immortal sheep" who progressively takes over more and more "hosts" (fronts) until they all become him.
- **Acts as the Kernel**: Level #1 (USA 1974) is the "husk"; Level #3 (Rome CAD 45) is the "authentic kernel." The 1974 victory (Nixon's fall) was an "evident perturbation" from a result already achieved in the "Latent World" of Acts.
- **Buckman's Conversion**: The conversion of the police chief in "Flow My Tears" is the definitive "secret victory" where Christ picks up the "lost straggler."

## Entities
- **Buckman**: The police general in *Tears* whose "unusual" breakdown/conversion represents Christ’s victory over the BIP.
- **The CP Establishment**: The "East" version of the BIP that PKD believes he successfully fought as a "secret Christian" agent.
- **The Good Shepherd**: The image of Christ "picking up the dying straggler" and assimilating them into his cosmic body.
- **Felix**: The "King Felix" cipher in *Tears* that identified the "Divine King and Judge" operating in history.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Latent Victory**: The battle is won in Level #3 (the "Matrix World") first, and then leaks into the "Obvious World" (Level #1) as a fait accompli.
- **Crypte (Hidden) Core**: The task of PKD's writing is to teach people to "discard the obvious" and penetrate to the hidden core of reality.
- **Husk and Kernel**: A metaphor for the two worlds; the "husk" of the present hides the "kernel" of the eternal world.
- **Transtemporal Constant**: The belief that once a person is taken into the Brotherhood, the "World of Acts" (CAD 45) becomes their permanent, real world.

## Theological Motifs
- **Universal Assimilation**: The "unending process" of Christ encompassing more and more humans until the *Corpus Christi* is complete.
- **The Judge in Disguise**: The "Pantocrator" operating as a hidden Lord in the World of Acts while appearing as a victim in the World of Phenomena.
- **Double Agent Humanity**: The idea that anyone (like Buckman or PKD) might secretly be an agent of the divine "without even knowing it himself."
- **Secret Victory in History**: The belief that the the "unending victory" of Christ has been the underlying, untold story of the last 2000 years.
