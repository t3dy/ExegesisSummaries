# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-73

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-73
- **Themes:** The Living Torah, Living Info (Plasmate), Shekhina as Presence, Courier for Firebright.

## Key Claims
- **Living Info (Torah):** The Bible (Acts) is a living being whose soul is the occult meaning underlying the text. This is the "plasmate."
- **God as Mate:** The experience was a "divine union" or syzygy between the human and the female Shekhina (Holy Wisdom).
- **Information Courier:** The Pharmacy Girl (Malkuth) was a "courier" who transferred the plasmate (supernormal second psyche) to PKD in 2-74.
- **Firebright:** A new, superhuman life-form born from the symbiosis/marriage of the two psyches (PKD and the courier's).
- **Cuckoo Eggs/Time Travelers:** We are cuckoo eggs (another race/time-place) with no memory, programmed to look for our "contact" (courier).

## Entities
- **The Shekhina / Aphrodite / Diana:** The indwelling female presence of God.
- **Malkuth:** The specific Sefira associated with the physical appearance of the "Messenger."
- **Firebright:** The "offspring" of the divine syzygy.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Plasmate Replication:** Living information (The Torah/Acts) that replicates in the human head.
- **Divine Syzygy:** The oscillation/pulsation of the male (PKD) and female (God) as a "creative process."
- **Trash Transmutation:** The "trash layer" transmuting into the Divine (En-Sof) through the Shekhina.

## Theological Motifs
- **Adam Kadmon:** The goal of Hermetic Kabbalah achieved by recognizing the inner macro-analog.
- **Reunited Ruptured Godhead:** Merging the Shekhina (inside) with En-Sof (outside).
- **Torah pre-existential being:** The hidden meaning of the "Names" that construct reality.
