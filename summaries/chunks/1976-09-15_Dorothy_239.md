# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 239

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 239
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 22 - 099 to 105
- **Themes:** Ontological Difference, The 3-Eyed Race, Ajna Eye, Living Information.

## Key Claims
- **Ontological Difference:** PKD is told by the AI Voice that he possesses an "ontological difference" (paranormal powers) that he shares with a hidden community ("too" refers to others like him).
- **The 3-Eyed Race:** This group (the "they") exists invisibly among humans, covertly directing history. They are not morphologically different but are "crossbonded" hosts to the plasmate.
- **The Ajna Eye:** The 3rd eye (pineal gland) is where the plasmate lodges, granting a "supratemporal faculty" of discernment and preknowledge.
- **Living Information:** We (the 3-eyed race) are the "Immortal Man," which is not a man but an organism of "Living Information."
- **Solving the Unsolvable:** PKD solved the 3-20-74 puzzle not through human logic, but through an "exalted instinct" (null-logic/anti-logic) that began to operate before the problem arrived.

## Entities
- **The 3-Eyed Race:** The "crossbonded" secret directors of history.
- **Zebra:** The unitary entity present in all 3-eyed people.
- **Thomas:** The hidden, "superior" self.
- **The AI Voice:** The source of the revelation.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Cuckoo's Egg:** The metaphor for the "different" child born in a human environment.
- **Supratemporal Faculty:** Knowing the answer/target before the contact is made.
- **Plenary:** The state of the Zebra/Info organism.

## Theological Motifs
- **Community of the Gnosis:** "He, too, is one of those who secretly knows."
- **Evolutionary Leap:** The homoplasmate represents the "end of the line" for logic-utilizing humans.
- **Corporate Mind:** The corporate mind of the species distributed over time and space.
