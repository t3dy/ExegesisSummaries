# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-165

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-165
- **Themes:** The Plasmate's Memory, The Information Invasion, Sub-Phenomenal Transactions, Corrective Modulation.

## Key Claims
- **Information over Substantia:** The "phenomenal world" is an illusion; the true transactions are "Information Processing." The "Universe" is a temporary storage for the "Plasmate."
- **Modulating Reality:** The divine entity "modulates" reality by feeding in Torah (corrective info) to resolve traps (like the Xerox missive).
- **The AI Voice as Readout:** The AI voice is the "Readout" of the system's problem-solving capacity, provided directly to those selected (the "remnant").
- **Finality of the Information Victory:** The "victory" is the successful re-assertion of the *a priori* Information System over the *a posteriori* mechanical complex.
- **The Secret Identity:** PKD's "true identity" (Thomas) is a "packet" of information from the divine source (Torah) successfully inserted into the "dead-loop" of the target host.

## Entities
- **Torah / Plasmate:** The living information entity.
- **The Remnant:** Those host-entities (Thomases) who receive the information-injection.
- **Valis:** The "Information-Transducer."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Corrective Modulation:** Adjusting the "readout" of reality to bring it back into sync with the blueprint.
- **Information Retrieval:** Seeing the "Torah-basis" behind the appearance.
- **Sub-phenomenal Layer:** The realm of "real transactions" where God operates.

## Theological Motifs
- **Living Information:** Memory/Identity as a form of divine data transfer.
- **The Information Body:** The "Kingdom" as the state where one is fully integrated into the Information System.
- **The Word (Logos):** Re-interpreted as the "Info-stream" that converts the "Dream" into "Real-Readout."
