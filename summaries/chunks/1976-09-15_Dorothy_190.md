# Chunk 190 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_190

## Description
PKD summarizes the "Ursatz" (original state) set forth in *Flow My Tears*: we are living in the first century A.D., and Christ (the Brain) has returned to lift the 2,000-year illusion. He concludes that the divine power used his novel as a covert announcement which his own 3-74 experience later ratified.

## Key Claims
- **Maximum Importance Message**: The message in *Flow My Tears* is the news of Christ's return, the single most important event in history.
- **"Felix" as Signal**: The code "Felix" (Happy/Zagreus) paired with "King" indicates that the "Happy King" (Christ) is here, pulling down Rome/tyranny.
- **The 2,000-Year Illusion**: The "return" involves lifting the illusion that any time has passed since Acts. We are currently disciples in A.D. 45.
- **God as Unscrambler**: God/Zebra de-occludes the person (lifting the "blindness") while the BIP/Satan continues to scramble and fake the landscape.

## Entities
- **Happy King / Zagreus / Christ**: The entity whose presence is announced in the "Tears" code.
- **St. Sophia**: The prophecy that ratifies the return and the decryption.
- **Felix**: The specific Roman official/message carrier.
- **Zebra**: The "In-breaking" power.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Latent Morphosis (Crypte Form)**: The hidden reality of Acts underneath the "morphosis" of 1978.
- **Hynoidal Suggestion (Refutation)**: PKD argues that his experience wasn't suggestion from the book, since he hadn't "decoded" the book yet and the book came 4 years before.
- **Theophany**: The "in-breaking of deity" into history (1974) and into the individual brain.
- **Spurious Memories**: The realization that our memories of the last 2,000 years are part of the "scrambled" delusion of the Yin realm.

## Theological Motifs
- **Grace through De-occlusion**: Only God/Holy Spirit has the power to "enlighten" or "de-occlude" the percept systems; humans cannot do it themselves.
- **Deterministic Yin Realm**: The "Yin" realm is the mechanical/BIP world of the Law, rescued by the "Yang" in-breaking of Grace.
- **The King is Here**: The fundamental message of the "Gospel" (Good News) as confirmed by legal, mystical, and historical evidence.
- **Interlocking Circle of Logic**: The alignment of (1) Anamnesis, (2) Acts material in fiction, (3) Theolepsy, and (4) St. Sophia Prophecy as an airtight case for the return.
