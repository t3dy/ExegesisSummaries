# Chunk 186 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_186

## Description
PKD reflects on his Exegesis as a "vast, original cosmology" and analyzes the "Sacred Mystery" of the holographic Christ. He explores the "Secrecy Theme" in Christianity, where the Messiah remains hidden from the evil powers of Realm II while being revealed to a spiritual elite.

## Key Claims
- **Macrocosm and Microcosm**: The "Sacred Mystery" is that Christ/Logos is both the total outside reality (macro) and the smallest inner form (micro). The Pleroma is a "titanic hologram" where each part contains the whole.
- **The Messiah Hidden**: Drawing from Mark and 1st Corinthians, PKD posits that the Parousia (return of Christ) is already here but "secretly fulfilled" and hidden from the "evil powers" (the BIP/authorities).
- **Noös vs. Pascal's Mindless Universe**: PKD's central idea is a sentient world (Noös) replacing a blind, mindless one (Pascal's universe).
- **Capturing the Lower Realm**: Realm I (sentient) continually "captures" and transmutes the blind processes of Realm II (evil/deterministic), turning them into "good" (purposeful) outcomes.

## Entities
- **Brahman / Atman**: Cited as the macro/micro versions of the divine presence.
- **The Pneumatikoi**: The "spiritual elite" who recognize the hidden revealer.
- **The Parousia**: The secret return of Christ.
- **Bishop Berkeley**: Philosopher cited for the theory that "God" (Noös) feeds us our sensory perceptions directly.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Salvidor Salvandus**: The "Saved Savior" / "Revealer" who arrives hidden from the world.
- **Dionysos / Christ**: The invader who is also spaceless within us.
- **Spurious Reality Detection**: Using Stanislaw Lem's model, PKD wonders if a brain fed a "spurious tape" (delusion) can detect the foul-up (de-occlusion).
- **Push-Pull Feedback Loop**: The synchronization of an "inner identity/memory" with an "outer world" to make the simulation seem natural.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Messianic Secret**: The belief that the Messiah is here but unrecognized except by those to whom he reveals himself.
- **God as All-in-All**: The goal of Noös link-up where the two-realm structure is abolished.
- **Salvation as Breaking the Tape**: True salvation is being "cut" from the rigid, programmed inner-outer reality.
- **Wait and Link-Up**: The divine exists in us from the start, "silent" and "waiting" to connect with the macro-form.
