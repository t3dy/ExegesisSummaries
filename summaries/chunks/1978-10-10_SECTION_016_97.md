# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-97

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-97
- **Themes:** Cold-Pac Reality, 3-Eyed People, Technological kontos, The Memory Loop.

## Key Claims
- **We are in Cold-Pac:** 3-74 verifies that we are in the "Ubik" situation: mentally living in the past (1974) while actually in the far future (cold-pac).
- **3-Eyed People (Remotes):** The 3-eyed people are our remote descendants (or "3rd eye" souls) sustainng our mental lives from the "outside."
- **Valis as Computer:** Valis is the "world-generator" or "computer" running the memory-program. It keeps itself hidden to ensure the "simulation" works.
- **Anamnesis as Lever:** Recognizing the rerun (Deja Vu/Memory leak) is the lever that pries open the whole technological simulation.
- **Information manual:** The Bible (Acts) is the "manual" that comprises the eternal substructure of the world (the "Book" the world is a movie of).

## Entities
- **Valis / Ubik:** The technological construct/monitor.
- **The 3-Eyed People:** The "unfallen" superior beings in the real present.
- **Gary Powers / Gary Corp:** A symbol for G-2 intelligence hidden behind "CP" signals.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Pretextual Causality:** The forgery of an outer world used to keep the brain "harmonized."
- **Evaluation Program:** Life as a series of repeated situations to "refine" responses (Karma).
- **Stationary Brains:** The true status of humans who believe they are moving through time.

## Theological Motifs
- **God as "Deus Absconditus":** Hidden so we don't know we are in a workshop/simulation.
- **The "Great Assize":** The moment of evaluation (3-74) where the "slave-tape" reprise was prevented.
- **Leibniz's Harmony:** Being synchronized to the totality via the mediator (Valis).
