# 1975-09-17_Doris_01
# SECTION_011
# September 17, 1975
# Doris
## concise_summary
Dick identifies the "active spirit" of 3-74 with the feminine personification of Wisdom (Haggia Sophia) from the apocryphal "Wisdom of Solomon." He proposes that the world is undergoing a secret "transubstantiation" into the mystic body of Christ/Sophia, with Nixon's fall serving as proof of divine judgment.
## key_claims
- Haggia Sophia as Logos: The "Logos" of the Greeks and the "Wisdom" of the Hebrews are the same entity, originally perceived in feminine form.
- The Sophianic Savior: The spirit that intervened in March 1974 to "untangle the troubles" and reveal the "great tyranny" (Nixon) was Saint Sophia.
- Cosmic Transubstantiation: Our world is being secretly transmuted into a new essence (salvation) while the outward appearance (accidents) remains unchanged.
- Immortal Justice: Death is not part of God's creation; justice is immortal, and "Lady Wisdom" ensures the fall of the unjust.
- Teilhardian Convergence: Christ/Sophia is the "plasmatic principle" into which all reality is converging.
- Passover-to-Passover: The period from March 1974 to March 1975 was a "Full year of Spring" within Christ/Sophia.
