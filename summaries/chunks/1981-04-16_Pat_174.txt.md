## Key Claims
- Finding evidence that their color-music-math transmissions are external requires the "biochip" model: a 5D organism (symbiote) has physically grown like a *mycelium* or root system into the human brain, allowing an interface.
- This symbiote lies dormant (inhibited) in the human brain until triggered by specific visual configurations, such as the Christian fish sign (*ikhthys*).
- When activated, the symbiote generates the phosphene graphics via radiation instead of light, functioning as the actual true self or "Thomas" identity from the 5D world (Rome 70 AD).
- Sacraments operate smoothly because in 5D, a unitary object can exist at multiple times and places on the 4th axis (our time scale). For them, our timeline is just a spatial array allowing access across millennia simultaneously.

## Recurring Concepts

## Theological Motifs
