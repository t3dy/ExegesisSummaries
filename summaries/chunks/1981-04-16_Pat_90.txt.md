## Key Claims
- The right hemisphere naturally purely knows (*Anokhi*), but lacks language. When it achieves parity with the left hemisphere through the meta-abstraction, they create a "superconscious push-pull field" (Mysterium Coniunctionis).
- By splitting the signal between the two dysphasic hemispheres, the brain learns to "see time" in the exact same way stereo vision allows us to "see spatial depth." 
- The brain calibrates "temporal depth" (age) by observing color frequency and structural complexity. Lower, slower frequencies represent older/simpler states, while higher, brighter frequencies (set to ground) represent incoming complex futures.
- Dick celebrates his achievement, comparing himself to Isaac Newton cracking a fundamental law of physics. He declares that Ditheon is the next evolutionary leap for humanity, and that his novels (*VALIS*, *The Transmigration of Timothy Archer*) are the first written accounts of a fully functional Ditheonic brain.

## Entities
- **Carl Jung**: The unification of the hemispheres is identified as the *Mysterium Coniunctionis* (the chemical wedding / integration of opposites).
- **Robert Ornstein**: Cited regarding the psychology of consciousness and the differing functions of the left and right brain lobes.
- **Teilhard de Chardin**: Dick reaffirms that the evolving, self-completing macro-entity he perceived is precisely Teilhard's Point Omega / Cosmic Christ.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bilateral Hemispheric Parity**: The neurological definition of salvation/Ditheon, where the analogic right brain and digital left brain operate simultaneously at peak efficiency.
- **Doppler Shift of Perception**: The mechanism by which the brain translates varying rates of evolutionary complexity into visual depth and color.

## Theological Motifs
- **Christ as the Unified Field**: Valis/Christ is the ultimate structured field of consciousness and organization, toward which all planetary evolution is converging.
