## Key Claims
- Dick expands on the paradox of the Cosmogenitor: God created the universe, fell into it as a percipient, and became trapped by His own thought-forms. Knowing this would happen, He pre-programmed a clock-timed alarm (the AI voice) to wake Himself up at the moment of maximum peril.
- Dick maps his personal history onto a cosmic espionage plot: he views himself as an agent sent from the future/past to overthrow the "Pigspurt" (Nixon) regime, embedding revolutionary codes into his novel *Flow My Tears* without his conscious knowledge.
- The true structure of the universe (the MMSK) is not organized by space or time, but by **Meaning**. Things are grouped logically around central conceptual hubs, which Dick names "Phylogons."
- He maps out his own personal "Phylogon"—a sprawling conceptual web linking his childhood friend Tony, his cancer fears, Wagner's *Parsifal*, *Ubik*, the Black Iron Prison, and the divine illumination.

## Entities
- **Richard Nixon ("Pigspurt")**: Dick concludes the Nixon administration was the literal Black Iron Prison manifestation—an occult seizure of power requiring a temporal counter-conspiracy to overthrow.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Cuckoo's Egg**: Dick sees himself as a sleeper agent or mercenary, inserted into the hostile timeline of Earth to break the matrix.
- **The Phylogon**: A cluster of meaning. Because time is irreal, related concepts/events exist simultaneously grouped around their central meaning, rather than spread out along a timeline.

## Theological Motifs
- **Mens Dei (The Mind of God)**: When Dick experienced 3-74, he broke free of time and space and temporarily viewed reality *exactly as God sees it*—as a timeless web of interrelated meanings (Plato's Forms).
