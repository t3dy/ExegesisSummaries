# The Nine Stages of the Divine Descent
## concise_summary
Dick outlines a radical "Nine-Stage" trajectory of the human soul's descent into and ascent out of the Black Iron Prison (BIP). He defines our world as a "fake fake"—a layered illusion where the initial rejection of the world leads to the perception of the BIP, which must then itself be rejected as a final "Dokos" (dream) to reach the "Palm Tree Garden" (PTG). He identifies himself as a "Salvidor Salvanitus" (Savior Saved), a microform of God who intentionally "forgot" his divinity to rescue those trapped in the BIP.
## key_claims
- Nine Stages of Enlightenment:
    1. Mixed World perception.
    2. BIP Detection.
    3. Disobedience/Balking.
    4. Punishment/Refusal.
    5. Guidance by St. Sophia (The Psychopomp).
    6. Realizing the BIP is Illusory.
    7. Return to the PTG/Kingdom of God.
    8. Realization that the self *is* God (Microform).
    9. Final Goal: "Emptying Hell" (the BIP).
- The Fake Fake (Zen Paradox): The BIP is a "deeper veil" than the visible world; enlightenment involves realizing the BIP is also an illusion, even while it has the power to torment.
- Salvidor Salvanitus: The "Savior who is Saved." God descends, forgets his nature to enter the BIP at the lowest level, and is "awakened" by an external stimulus (a macroform of God).
- Disobedience to the Architect: One must "balk" against the Artifact's script to trigger the indwelling Spirit's actualization.
- Non-Reality of Form II: Following Parmenides, Dick argues that the "dense and night-like" world (Yin/BIP) has no true ontological reality; only the "Light-like" Set (Yang/PTG) is real.
- Cuckoo's Eggs: The Divine inserts "polyforms" (agents) into the human "nest/womb" to trigger collective awakening.
