## Key Claims
- VALIS (the entity experienced in 1974) is explicitly identified as Christ. Christ had seized upon Dick, invaded him, and dwelt within him (enthusiasmos), abolishing the inner-outer dualism and resulting in part-whole compatibility.
- *VALIS* and *The Divine Invasion* (referred to as BTA) are structural inversions of each other. In *VALIS*, the narrator (Horselover Fat/Dick) represents the madman evaluating the death of Gloria; in *BTA*, Angel Archer (representing Gloria) is the sane narrator evaluating the death of the madman.
- The fundamental role of God is sutainer/tutelary spirit. Radical Protestant existentialism (via Paul Tillich) demands that humans place themselves directly and solely under the tutelage of God, bypassing all earthly authorities.

## Entities
- **Angel Archer**: Identified as the conceptual analog to Gloria Knudson. Angel represents the spirit of analytical reason evaluating the events of the "fool."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Cognitive Estrangement**: The dualistic separation of self and world, which is reversed when Christ is perceived ubiquitously in all things.

## Theological Motifs
- **Tutelage**: The state of being adopted and sustained directly by God, guaranteeing true freedom and autonomy from worldly powers.
