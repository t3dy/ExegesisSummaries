# Chunk 176 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_176

## Description
PKD reflects on the logical necessity of rejecting sensory evidence in favor of the "a priori" truth of Christ's presence. He compares the occlusion of the divine to the way physical objects appear to shrink with distance—a perceptual error that doesn't change the underlying reality.

## Key Claims
- **Invisibility vs. Absence**: Just because Christ (Zebra) is currently invisible due to "occlusion" does not mean he has departed. His presence in 1974 was momentarily seen and is now inferred.
- **Negative Hallucination**: The human condition is defined by a "negative hallucination" where we see a fake world (Form II) instead of the true one (Form I).
- **Zebra as the Breaker of Fate**: Christ's primary task is breaking "Astral Determinism" (blind causality) and replacing it with teleological sentience.
- **Triangulation of Truth**: The coincidence of the novel *Tears* (1970), the anamnesis (1974), and the decoding of the "Felix" message (1978) points to a "divine substance" underlying his life.

## Entities
- **Zebra**: The divine intruder/Christ/Logos.
- **Parmenides / Kant**: Philosophers cited for their distinction between appearances and the "thing-in-itself."
- **Nixon Government**: Identified as the "Babylonian" tyranny that Zebra pulled down.
- **Felix**: The key codeword meaning "happy" or "prosperous."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Form I vs. Form II**: The authentic reality vs. the phenomenal fake.
- **Spurious Interpolation**: The 1974-1978 time period as a false timeline.
- **Anamnesis**: The recovery of suppressed memories of early Christianity.
- **Steganography**: The idea that PKD's work contains hidden, coded messages.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Good News (Godspell)**: The message of exculpation and the return of the Lord.
- **Illegal Christianity**: The memory of being a first-century Christian in a time when the faith was a secret underground movement.
- **The Fall of Babylon**: Interpreted as the collapse of corrupt political systems through divine intervention.
