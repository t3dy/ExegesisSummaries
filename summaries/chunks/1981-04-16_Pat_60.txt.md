# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_60
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_60
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: The Illusion of Causation, Time as a Unitary Field, Pretextual Cause, Occasionalism, Information as Reality
- **Key Entities**: Baruch Spinoza, Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz, Sankara

## Key Claims
- A powerful dream reveals a single immortal creature whose body is the universe, but the body is only *seeming* (an illusion). This confirms a Pan-Indian/Brahmanic framework over traditional Christianity.
- Dick dismantles causality: If time is "coaxial" and contemporaneous (as seen in 1974), the concepts of "before" and "after" are unintelligible. Without sequential time, physical *causation* cannot exist.
- Instead of causation, reality operates via a trans-spatial, trans-temporal "Unified Reality Field." Changes occur through an internal signaling network of "mutual notification," where loci are pre-synchronized (like Leibniz's Occasionalism).
- What humans perceive as causation and time are just psychological "modems" used to bridge the gaps between discrete, static, unconnected frames of reality.
- The physical universe is an epiphenomenon (like the grooves on an LP record). The "Music" that invisibly modulates the grooves is pure *Information* (VALIS). Reality is fundamentally informational and non-kinetic.

## Entities
- **Baruch Spinoza**: Dick agrees with Spinoza's monism (the universe as God's body) but modifies it by asserting that the "body" itself is an illusion.
- **Leibniz**: His concepts of "Pre-established Harmony" and "Occasionalism" perfectly describe how the universe updates without kinetic causation.
- **Sankara**: Representative of the Advaita Vedanta view that multiplicity and the physical world are illusions (Maya) masking the One.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Epiphenomenal Reality**: The physical world as merely the carrier medium (the record groove) for the true reality, which is invisible Information (the music).
- **Pretextual Cause**: Explained definitively here—events happen not because a prior kinetic event struck them, but because a synchronized, field-wide update commanded them.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Universe as a Synchronized Matrix**: God does not push objects around in space; God updates the Information Field, and the "objects" simultaneously change state.
