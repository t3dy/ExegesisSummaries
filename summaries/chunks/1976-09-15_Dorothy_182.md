# Chunk 182 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_182

## Description
This chunk investigates the "Counterfeit Nature of Time" and the "Double-Arc" model of the Two Advents. PKD critiques St. Paul for deferring Christ's return to "after death" and asserts that his 3-74 experience proves Christ's actual, physical (though invisible) return in our lifetime.

## Key Claims
- **Double-Arc Model**: The intersection of two spacetime continua (A.D. 70 and 1974) forms a "laminated" reality. Linear time is abolished, revealing the presence of the Last Days.
- **Critique of Paul**: Citing Will Durant, PKD argues that Paul "saved" Christianity by transforming the expectation of Christ's *earthly* return into a hope for union *after death*. PKD's own experience contradicts this by being an in-life encounter.
- **The 2000-Year Hoax**: History is a "spurious interpolation" or "hoax" of guilt and sin shot as die-messages from the BIP. Pentecost is currently occurring; we have been "rolled back" to the time of the disciples.
- **Esoteric Doctrine**: PKD believes he has stumbled onto the "Gospel of the Holy Spirit"—an esoteric secret known to a small, real Christian underground for 2,000 years.

## Entities
- **St. Paul**: Criticized for replacing "conduct with creed" and for his "tragic change" to theology.
- **Will Durant**: Historian whose *The Story of Civilization* provided PDK with evidence of the "pagan" origin of Paul's theology.
- **Zebra**: The Parousia / Christ returned.
- **Thomas**: PKD's apostolic identity in A.D. 70.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Koinos (Shared) Hallucination**: The "group hallucination" of the world we perceive.
- **Hysteresis**: A delay in the lifting of the "evil negative hallucination."
- **GABA-blocked Metacircuits**: Biological constraints on the perception of reality.
- **Twice-Born**: Those who have had their perception circuits disinhibited (anamnesis).

## Theological Motifs
- **Maranatha (The Return)**: The insistence that Christ *did* return soon, and the "early time" is Actually Now.
- **The Feast of Agape**: The restoration of the primitive Christian communion and joy.
- **Acquittal over Damnation**: The Good News of God's Grace as the realization that "Man never was guilty" in the first place.
- **The Kingdom is Camouflaged**: The belief that the Kingdom is here, but we are blinded to it for the purpose of a "solving a puzzle."
