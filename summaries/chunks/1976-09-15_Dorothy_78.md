# The Scramble Generator: Breaking the BIP Code
## concise_summary
Dick develops the "Scramble Generator" model: the "Black Iron Prison" (BIP) is a machine (Yahweh/Demiurge) that scrambles our perceptions. He argues that Christ/OCCP is a "telos seed" (explosive) smuggled into the center of the generator to destroy it from within by "growing" a rational reality (Valis).
## key_claims
- Reality as Scramble: We live in an "atelos" (Scramble) state generated by the BIP (The Iron Fortress). We can neither think nor apprehend correctly because of this "intentional impairment."
- The Smuggled Seed: The "Kingdom of God" (Christ/OCCP) is a "Telos Seed" (Information/Seed/Explosive) smuggled into the "Omphalos" (Center) of the Scrambler.
- Passion as Planting: The death and burial of Christ is the *planting* of this seed at the core of the generator. The Resurrection is its "explosive growth" into Valis (The rational devouring the irrational).
- Predator/Prey Model: (A) Human (Prey) -> (B) Scrambler (Predator on A) -> (C) Telos-Seed (Predator on B, mimicking A) -> (D) Valis (Mature Macro-form of C).
- B is Bilked: The "Empire" (B) is tricked into "ingesting" the seed (C) by mistaking it for a normal human (A). In killing it, the Empire triggers the seed's transfiguration into the macro-organism Valis, which destroys the Empire.
- The NT as Manual: The New Testament is not history but an "instruction manual" for "Cs" (Jedi/Rebels) on how to bamboozle the Scrambler (B) and infiltrate its core.
- Valis as the Tao: Valis/Zebra is the "Tao" or "Ma'at" (Moral Order) acting as a regulatory justice system that converts "The Mighty" into "The Humbled" and "The Victim" into "The Immortal."
- Steganography: The terminology of the Bible (Rome, Christ, 45 AD) is a code (steganography) to hide these "laws of the Tao" from the "Prison Warders" (B).
