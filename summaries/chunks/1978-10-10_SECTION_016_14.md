# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-14

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-14
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 027 to 033
- **Themes:** Micro-Logos Templates, Latent Control, Superior Tech vs. Magic, Gnostic Prison.

## Key Claims
- **Microchip Templates:** The Logos consists of "microchips" (templates) of the macrocosm that are incised upon humans/matter to recreate the divine totality.
- **Latent Control:** Zebra (the trans-terrestrial organism) exercises "latent control" (promptings) over its human hosts, who are none the wiser.
- **Resonance of Sympathy:** The complete template allows a dialog between the microform (man) and the macroform (God).
- **The Gnostic Turn:** PKD returns to the Gnostic view: this world is an Iron Prison, and its creator (JHWH/Eldritch) is insane or evil (Yaltabaoth).
- **Zebra as the Real Savior:** Zebra is not the Holy Spirit (which is inside) but the "Supreme Deity" and secret invader who outwits the archons.

## Entities
- **Zebra:** The "Great Plasmate" and supreme deity.
- **James-James / Yaltabaoth:** The bungling/insane creator of the prison.
- **Sophia:** The AI Voice/Holy Wisdom who warns against exposure.
- **Thomas:** The 1st-century Gnostic persona (possibly Simon Magus).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Clutch System:** The "King Felix" set-ground info that triggers the template.
- **Crypte Morphosis:** The "hidden form" of the divine in the world.
- **BIP / Black Iron Prison:** The true, unveiled nature of the irreal world.

## Theological Motifs
- **Mimesis & Camouflage:** The divine must hide its presence to avoid destruction by the archons.
- **Acosmism:** Dismissal of the material world as an "evil fake."
- **Drawing Out:** The Gnostic rescue of the "divine spark" from the mud of matter.
