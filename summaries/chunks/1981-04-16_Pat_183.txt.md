# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_183
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_183
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Double Predestination, The Eternal Horizontal Track, 5D as Atavistic Primordial State
- **Key Entities**: Calvin, Parmenides (Eleatics)

## Key Claims
- Applying Eleatic logic, Dick concludes that "horizontal tracking" (living endless reincarnations of the same narrative) is an absolute, eternal state. But vertical extrication (salvation along the 5th orthogonal axis) is also an absolute, eternal state. 
- Therefore, if you are ever pulled up into the 5D realm via the pulley, you were always already there. There is no chronological sequence of cause and effect across the 4D timeline; salvation operates via "backward causality," proving Calvin's doctrine of Double Predestination.
- Dick makes a startling realization regarding his "Ape" vision: the 5D hypertime world is not some futuristic sci-fi evolution, but a *drastically primitive, atavistic Dasein*. 
- Early "dawn-age" man (the Nearman) lived in the 5D world and possessed total right-hemisphere cognitive powers (using color and math without verbal language). 

## Entities
- **Calvin**: His grim theology of Absolute Predestination is proven structurally true by Dick's 5D geometry and backward causality; the saved were never lost, and the damned cannot be saved.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Primordial Garden**: The 5D reality of the ape-men is literally the Garden of Eden. The Fall was the human species cognitively degrading from 5D color/math telepathy into the 4D verbal, left-hemisphere atomized state.

## Theological Motifs
- **Christ as Atavism**: Jesus Christ was not an evolutionary leap forward, but a drastic atavism—a return to the unfallen, 5D cognitive functioning of the dawn-age Nearman.
