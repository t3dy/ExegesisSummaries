## Metadata

## Key Claims
- Valis is the "mystical cosmic Corpus Christi"—God's body systematically eating and replacing the mechanical universe (*transubstantiation* on a cosmic scale).
- This invasion was kept completely secret to surprise the evil, irrational planetary powers holding the universe hostage. 
- Dick reinterprets the 3-74 event and the Xerox missive trap through the "Bait and Hook" theory: Dick’s innocent, helpless human self was the bait, used by God to lure the evil powers into attacking. When the evil powers snapped the trap shut, Christ ("Thomas") emerged like a barbed hook to vanquish them.
- This represents a micro-enactment of the eternal sacerdotal macro-drama (The Book of Acts), where the King of Light constantly invades the Kingdom of Darkness, lures the evil rulers into committing a cosmic crime (attacking the innocent), and strips them of their authority.

## Entities
- **The King of Light**: The rational mind of the universe invading the irrational domain of darkness to free humans. 
- **The Evil Spirits (Thrones and Dominions)**: The blind, mechanical, and malevolent rulers of the Signal A world, lured into overstepping their authority so they can be legally deposed by Ma'at.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Trapped Innocent (Bait)**: The mechanism of salvation. Because Dick did not initiate the hostility and was a helpless victim of Nixon's political apparatus, his persecution provided the legal avenue for the divine to intervene.

## Theological Motifs
- **The Atonement Model of Irenaeus**: Re-contextualized as a cosmic sting operation. The devil assumes he is destroying a weak human, only to trigger the absolute, overwhelming retaliation of the hidden divine nature.
