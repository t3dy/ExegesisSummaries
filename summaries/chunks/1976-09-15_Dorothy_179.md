# Chunk 179 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_179

## Description
PKD links *Flow My Tears* to *The Bacchae*, identifying "Jason Taverner" with Dionysos/Christ and the "King of Tears" (Buckman) with King Pentheus. He interprets his own 3-74 experience as a form of "Theolepsy" (divine possession) and a technological "rescue" from the BIP's RF transmissions.

## Key Claims
- **Dionysos/Christ Parallel**: Jason Taverner (the "drunkard") is the stranger (Dionysos) who enters the prison realm, is arrested, and thereby destroys the "King of Tears" (Pentheus/Nixon).
- **Theolepsy**: PKD characterizes his experience as a "shamanic" possession by the "Joy-God" Dionysos.
- **BIP as Malfunctioning Circuitboard**: The prison is a literal "metal fortress" that warps "Noös" (thought) and "signals" into endless circles, preventing growth and newness.
- **RF Die-Messages**: PKD theorizes that the BIP transmits literal radio frequencies ("die messages") to lower human self-esteem and enforce "labor and sacrifice."

## Entities
- **Dionysos**: The "Joy-God" who sabotages the "King of Tears."
- **Nixon / Pentheus**: The "King of Tears" archetype.
- **Goldie Hahn**: Reference to a movie about a "Russian girl" punished for "not being serious" (a parasite).
- **Murray Burns**: The protagonist of *A Thousand Clowns*, cited as an "arch-criminal" in the eyes of the BIP for his refusal to toil.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Homo Ludens (Playing vs. Toiling)**: The conflict between the "serious" work-drive of the BIP and the "joyful play" of the spirit.
- **Parasite**: The BIP's term for those who "play" instead of working.
- **Gravity Well of the BIP**: The way the prison deflects "signals" (thoughts) like a dark star.
- **Structured Criticism**: The BIP's tactic of "taming" subversive art by making it "serious" (citing Ursula Le Guin and Stanislaw Lem).

## Theological Motifs
- **God in Disguise**: The "Stranger" who enters the realm to overthrow the tyrant.
- **Exculpation Over Punishment**: The "Felix" message indicates that the "serious king's" judgment (death) fails to stay the "Joy-God."
- **The Christian Story as Fact**: The Gospel as a record of what the BIP does to "Spotless God" and anyone who identifies with him.
- **Theolepsy**: Divine intoxication that "possesses" the individual to break the "tin mind" of the establishment.
