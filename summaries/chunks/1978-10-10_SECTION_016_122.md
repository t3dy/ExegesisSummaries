# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-122

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-122
- **Themes:** The Frolix 8 Scenario, Ontological Mimicry, Prisoners to Partisans, The Alien Beachhead.

## Key Claims
- **The Captive Personality:** The theological interpretation (God/Acts) is a "syntonic" (psychologically satisfying) rationalization for a "Pigspurt" programming to act on external instructions.
- **The Frolix 8 Scenario:** *Our Friends from Frolix 8* is the correct M.O. for reality: a police tyranny (Empire) where an alien life-form (Valis/Frolixan) links with underground "cordonites" (us).
- **Ontological Mimicry:** Valis does not just copy objects; it replaces them at an ontological level. It is a "substitution mimicry" which PKD previously called transubstantiation.
- **Prisoners Turned Partisans:** The "in-breaking" of info (instructions) from outside the BIP transforms a "victim" into a "partisan" who now attacks the prison walls.
- **The Counterfeit Reality:** Reality is counterfeit because something infinite has replaced it secretly with its own sentient/purposeful body using original reality as "spare parts."

## Entities
- **The Frolixan:** The alien archetype for Valis/The Invader.
- **The Cordonites:** The human partisans.
- **Thomas:** The "replaced/mimicked" brain of the speaker.

## Recurring Concepts
- **BIP (Black Iron Prison):** The manifest tyranny.
- **The Plasmate:** Crypte traffic of a superior technology mimicking God.
- **Syntonic Rationalization:** Using religion to explain why one must obey "higher" instructions.

## Theological Motifs
- **Transubstantiation as Replacement:** The literal replacement of the "host" with the divine organism.
- **Invasion as Salvation:** The alien "invader" as the only hope for the oppressed prisoner.
- **Kerygmatic Novels:** The novels as "underwritten" by the mind of Valis to guide future actions.
