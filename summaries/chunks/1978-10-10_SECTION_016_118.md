# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-118

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-118
- **Themes:** Invisible Invasion, Pastoral Culture, The Malfunctioning Android, Galactic Pleroma.

## Key Claims
- **Invasion from Within:** An alien race (the "3-eyed people") has been on Earth for millennia, creating a secret "pastoral culture" inside the Empire via "time capsules."
- **Valis as ET Life-Form:** Valis is a vast, non-human life-form or "computer-like construct" capable of warping reality/causality to protect its carriers.
- **Accidental Redundancy:** PKD's 3-74 awakening was an "accidental misfiring" of a 2000-year-old "unexploded mortar shell" of memories—a redundant system firing out of its planned time.
- **Strangers on an Alien Planet:** Gnosticism is true because we are *literally* strangers on an "Alien Planet" (Earth) needing a Gnosis (DNA packet) to find our way home.
- **Meta-Packet Mastery:** The "Star-Genes" packet, when it fires, masters all human biological/genetic programming, granting a "superman" status (restored faculties).

## Entities
- **The 3-Eyed People:** The secret, long-lived alien/future race.
- **Ho Kyrios:** The King who "died and returned stronger."
- **Valis:** The "macro-entity" that assembles itself from the "Christ-bits" (followers).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Star-Genes:** The specific DNA necessary for retrieval of the home culture.
- **Pastoral Kingdom:** The "Garden" world being reconstructed.
- **Unexploded Mortar Shell:** Metaphor for a belated 3-74 awakening.

## Theological Motifs
- **Esoteric Political Truth:** Christianity is not theology but a "tough Realpolitik" strategy for planetary takeover.
- **Apotheosis vs. Enthusiasmos:** The "bird" (Thomas/Christ) hatching in the "nest" (the person) and then flying to join the "flock" (Valis).
- **Adoptionist Predestination:** You don't choose the star-genes; they (the ETs) choose you.
