# Claudia
## concise_summary
Dick identifies his 1974 merger with the "Daimon" as the "Second Birth" and the "Pearl of Great Price." He concludes that the Daimon (Eros/Will) is the source of his precognitive writing and that he was rescued from the "Xerox/COINTELPRO" threat due to his acute need, not his worth.
## key_claims
- The "Daimon" (Will) is the Ground of Being (Nietzsche/Buber) and is superpersonal/transcendent.
- Dick's writing is "daemon-driven": the Daimon constructs a *Weltbild* from the future that Phil merely transcribes.
- The 1974 experience was a "Becoming" (merger) rather than a "Situational advice": he attained the "most treasured state possible."
- The "Holy Spirit" (Elijah) was the "fierce refiner" that preparing him for the merger.
- The 2,000-year "dry period" (150 AD - 1970 AD) was a withdrawal of the Holy Spirit/Contact.
- Aid to Phil (rescue from COINTELPRO) was a "private matter" based on his desperate need, not social importance.
