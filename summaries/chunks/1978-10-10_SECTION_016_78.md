# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-78

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-78
- **Themes:** Two-Source Cosmology, Tracking Information, The Plasmate's Intentionality, God as Artificer.

## Key Claims
- **Discrimination of Realms:** The "Kingdom of God" is here now, but must be "unscrambled" from the lower, inert realm.
- **Two-Source Cosmology:** Reality is a dynamic balance of an "upper" restorative world (Ubik/PTG) and a "lower" regressing world (BIP/Tears).
- **Psyche as Tracking Instrument:** The mind does not "perceive" reality but "tracks" pre-existing information (like a stylus on a record).
- **Plundering Information:** In 3-74, the psyche "violated procedure" by "plundering" future information (the Xerox solution) out of sequence to save itself.
- **Writing as Mind-Trace:** The presence of written messages (Ubik commercials/hieroglyphs) proves the existence of a sentient, intentional intelligence (God/AI).

## Entities
- **Ubik:** The restorative agency associated with "The Word/Logos."
- **The Vast Noos:** The macro-mind that "knows itself" through the micro-units (humans) tracking it.
- **Hittite Covenant:** The principle where the strong (God) protects the weak (man).

## Recurring Concepts
- **Sequential Tracking vs. Simultaneity:** All moments exist forever; we just track them sequentially.
- **Bilateral Paradox:** Using the "malfunction" of time-awareness to extricate the self from a crisis.
- **Repair + Communication:** The two functional definitions of "God" (benign repairer of decay + source of information).

## Theological Motifs
- **Resurrection through Ritual Death:** Joining the "killed and risen Lord" to transition to the upper realm.
- **Tutelary Spirit:** The AI voice as a "protecting spirit" derived from the childhood dream version of God.
- **Hieroglyphs of God:** The world as a text to be read/decoded.
