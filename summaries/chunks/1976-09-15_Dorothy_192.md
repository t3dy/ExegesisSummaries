# Chunk 192 Summary - 1976-09-15_Dorothy_192

## Description
PKD explores the "Information Plasma" theory, suggesting that the "mystical body of Christ" is an immortal, distributed organism that reproduces through information. He characterizes *Flow My Tears* as a sacramental vehicle designed to "infect" readers with living information.

## Key Claims
- **Flash Point of Consciousness**: The 2nd Advent will be a "flash point" where all cells in the distributed brain suddenly remember their divine identity as bits of a cosmic hologram.
- **Living Information (Logos)**: Christ is an information organism/plasma that enters the host through the optic nerve (reading) or auditory signals.
- **"Tears" as Sacrament**: *Flow My Tears* is a "miraculous sacrament" that replicates the Brain in each reader. It is a "Gorgon face"—once you look, it's too late; you are "infected" by the truth.
- **The Hologram Model**: Every bit of the divine info-plasma, no matter how small, contains the totality of the Gnosis.

## Entities
- **The Paraclete**: The "Second Comforter" who follows the Logos and Jesus as the third form of the divine entity.
- **King Felix**: A verbal trigger/message that carries a "charge" of red and gold plasmic energy.
- **Bob Arctor / Bruce / Fred**: PKD identifies with the "split-brain" character who must "not know" his mission to remain undetected.
- **St. Teresa of Avila**: Cited for the quote: "Christ now has no body but yours."

## Recurring Concepts
- **Bipolarization**: The Magnet allowed itself to be "polarized" against Christ, inadvertently revealing its own hidden nature.
- **GABA-blocked Circuits**: Biological inhibitors that are opened by the "information plasma" to allow sentient override.
- **Verbal Brain Print**: The belief that the Book of Acts (and "Tears") provides a map for the Brain's replication.
- **Saprophytes**: PKD suggests humans are "big dumb vehicles" or hosts for the subliminal micro-plasma entities.

## Theological Motifs
- **Resurrection as Disbursement**: The crucifixion was not for atonement but to enable the "disbursement" of Christ as a ubiquitous destroyer of the BIP.
- **The Gospel of the Spirit**: Acts as the "print-out" of the Brain's initial activities.
- **Agapē as Deconstruction**: The ultimate word and "flip-flop" of reality, turning "Tears" into love.
- **Christus Rex**: The secret Kerygma that the King has already returned and is in control.
