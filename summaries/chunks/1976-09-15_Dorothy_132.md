# The Death of the Artifact in A.D. 70
## concise_summary
Dick proposes that the "psyche" of the Artifact (Form II) was slain by Christ during the First Advent (c. A.D. 70 at Masada/Temple Fall), and it has since been a "carcass" or "sarx" projecting a frozen, decaying image of Rome. He argues that our linear time is "spurious time" spun out by this dead entity, and that *Ubik* (Form I) is an "invader" that must dismantle this dead machinery to build a "New Heavens and Earth."
## key_claims
- The Slaying of Form II: Christ dealt a "death blow" to the psyche of Form II in A.D. 70; it now only possesses "hysteresis" or mechanical habit.
- Frozen Time: Linear time stopped in A.D. 70; everything since has been a "balloon of ersatz time" projecting the Black Iron Prison.
- Zebra as Usurptive Psyche: Zebra (the living psyche of Form I) invades the "soma" of Form II to "steer" it and save us.
- The 4th Option: Instead of healing or killing Form II, the Divine has chosen to *dismantle* it and reuse its pieces for a new construction.
- Ubik-as-Yang: *Ubik* is the vitalistic force fighting the entropy and "form-regression" of the dead world-carcass.
