# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-20

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-20
- **Folder Reference:** Folder 21 - 071 to 078
- **Themes:** Counterfeit Time, Astral Determinism, Dogon Dualism, The Jim Pike Hit.

## Key Claims
- **Time as Tautology:** Time flow rate is just a tautology for the rate at which things look different. There is no independent "clock" for the universe.
- **Astral Determinism as Ignorance:** Astral determinism is a "spurious mode of being" characterized by ignorance regarding the true self and world (Maya).
- **The Reversion of Self:** In 3-74, PKD's world AND self reverted to Rome AD 45 (Acts), proving both are "seeming" under a volitional "He's" control.
- **The Sirius Connection:** PKD identifies the Dogon (Nommo/Yurugu) dualism as the dialectic he saw in 3-74, linking it to a superior technology/intelligence.
- **Conspiracy Theory:** PKD suspects the Nov 17 1971 hit on his house was part of a conspiracy to discredit him, potentially linked to Jim Pike's "murder."

## Entities
- **James-James:** The mad creator/demiurge (YHWH/Eldritch).
- **Nommo/Yurugu:** Dogon principles of dialectic order vs. chaos.
- **Jim Pike:** Murdered civil rights leader whose death is linked to PKD's troubles.
- **The Bip (Black Iron Prison):** The true, unveiled nature of the irreal world.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Maya:** The Hindu concept of delusion/illusion.
- **Geworfenheit:** Being "thrown" into the world-prison.
- **Vortex/Perturbation:** The visible break in the reality field caused by Zebra.

## Theological Motifs
- **Salvador Salvandus:** The impaired Godhead extricating its own fallen parts.
- **Wait for the 5th Savior:** He will take the "burden" (Atlas/World) off PKD.
- **Inertness vs. Sentience:** The transition from the Age of Iron (deterministic) to Gold (volitional).
