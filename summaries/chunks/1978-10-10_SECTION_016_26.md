# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 16-16

## Metadata
- **Chunk ID:** 16-16 (Re-labeled as 16-26)
- **Note:** The previous summary task reached 16-25. This summary covers chunk 26 of Section 16.

## Key Claims
- **Inversion of Reality:** The creator "James-James" has inverted the proposition (AB = C = T) so that we perceive time (T) even when no change (C) exists.
- **Ontological Contamination:** This inversion contaminates all perception; we cannot know if time is passing at all.
- **Filter Theory:** God/Creator imposes the ordering matrices of time and space, filtering reality for us. We do not experience the "Dinge-an-sich".
- **Psychotic God:** If the Gnostics are right, the grid-imposer is sick/psychotic, making our reality a "maze" or "trap".
- **Zebra as Anti-Occlusion:** The macrobrain (Zebra) removes the blinders, removing us from the power of the space-time grid (Geworfenheit).

## Entities
- **James-James:** The mad genius god, creator of the "maze."
- **Zebra/Valis:** The salvific invader/macrobrain currently in the world.
- **Thomas:** The anti-occluded plasmate part of PKD.

## Recurring Concepts
- **Geworfenheit:** Facticity/thrownness, expressed here as space-time "infliction."
- **Maze/Prison:** The double-prison situation of the Gnostics.
- **Ubik/Scanner paradigm:** The combination of "simulated reality" and "occluded vision."

## Theological Motifs
- **The Joy God:** Forbidden to us, locked away by the creator.
- **Samael/Blind God:** The demiurge as "blind" and insane.
- **Ascent to Pleroma:** The goal of the "despised" Gnostics who have the truth.
