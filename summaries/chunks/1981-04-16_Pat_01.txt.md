# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_01
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_01

- **Key Entities**: A.N. Whitehead, Jakob Boehme, Paul

## Key Claims
- Dick writes to "Pat" to outline his finalized reality model, based securely on Alfred North Whitehead's definition of process deity: God as a "principle of selection in the universe that chooses the good."
- God operates as a binary system making choices at maximum branchings. The system tries "no" tentatively (in the void/off phase) and cancels it in favor of "yes" (the actualized on/frame).
- Time is truly discrete and disjunctive. Prior frames have no causal effect on subsequent frames. Human minds supply causality as a modem to convert these static frames into a flow.
- The 1974 "Xerox missive" event was an example of the Creator choosing the "yes" over the "no," rewriting reality. Dick experienced anamnesis of the discarded "zero" choice, which allowed him to see the decision-making process.
- He links the "secret stolen through the angels" to the idea that the Cosmic Christ has penetrated the physical universe and short-circuited its retributive, karmic machinery.

## Entities
- **A.N. Whitehead**: Providing the foundational framework of process theology that validates Dick's binary selection revelation.
- **Jakob Boehme**: Correlated with the Yes/No dialectic of creation.
- **Paul / Captivity Letters**: Cited for the concept of Ancient angelic powers (Thrones, Dominions) being overthrown by the invading Christ.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Binary Pulse-Flicker**: The off-on frequency of reality where choices are made in the off (void) state.
- **Pretextual Cause**: A decision made by the system ex nihilo, overriding linear causal momentum.

## Theological Motifs
- **Apocalyptic In-breaking**: God no longer functioning as Spinozan pantheism, but rather breaking into the deterministic universe to perform an exculpatory miracle for true Christians.
