# Exegesis Chunk Summary: 1981-04-16_Pat_84
## Metadata
- **Chunk ID**: 1981-04-16_Pat_84
- **Section ID**: SECTION_017
- **Themes**: Retrograde Evolution, The Info Life Form, Dianthropos, The Failed Ditheon of Schizophrenia
- **Key Entities**: Pierre Teilhard de Chardin, Meister Eckhart

## Key Claims
- Evolution works backward: the higher entity (the Info Life Form) does not "evolve" from the lower, but instead *utilizes and subsumes* the lower, assembling the preconditions for its own birth by acting retrograde in time.
- The Info Life Form crosses the threshold from physical bodies to pure information by yoking two human psyches together (Ditheon/Dianthropos). Just as protein molecules combined to form the first cell, human minds combine to form the first meta-entity.
- The bicameral mind in *A Scanner Darkly* (Fred/Bob) was a failed evolutionary attempt at formulating a Ditheon. The true Ditheon requires a supernatural introjection of an adventitious psyche (Christ/Holy Spirit).
- This adventitious psyche provides the necessary "temporal parallaxis" (two superimposed spatiotemporal fields) that gives the human brain the "Data" needed to trigger Eckhart's fourth level of the soul and see the world abstractly.

## Entities
- **Teilhard de Chardin**: Dick identifies his Info Life Form with Teilhard's Noosphere and the Omega Point, where consciousness converges and concentrates.

## Recurring Concepts
- **The Information Life Form**: A post-biological organism that treats reality as digital bits and uses human brains as processing nodes.
- **Ditheon as Necessary Condition**: Anamnesis and the Ditheon aren't the ultimate goal; they are the mechanical *means* to supply the data required for the meta-abstraction.

## Theological Motifs
- **Salvation as Cognitive Thresholding**: Supernatural revelation is redefined as a higher rational faculty processing novel multi-temporal data, resulting in deification.
