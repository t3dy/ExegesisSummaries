# SECTION_014: VALIS and the Cosmic Game
## Date: September 12, 1976 (Letter to Dorothy)

### Section Overview
Section 14 consists of a pivotal letter to Dorothy (recipient) where Dick provides a high-level summary of his 3-74 experience. This section is historically significant as it contains one of the first explicit definitions of **VALIS** (*Vast Active Living Intelligence System*) and reveals the deep political stakes of 3-74, including his anti-war tax protests and the subsequent "intervention" that cleared his record with the FBI.

---

### Core Themes & Breakthroughs

#### 1. The Nomenclature of VALIS
Dick moves from identifying the entity as "St. Sophia" or "The Spirit" (Section 13) to the more technical and science-fictional **VALIS**. 
- **Definition**: Vast Active Living Intelligence System.
- **Function**: A "superior thinking entity of vast power" that temporarily displaced his ego to solve massive personal and legal crises.

#### 2. The Political Undercurrents of 3-74
Dick reveals that his "religious conversion" had a specific legal motivation:
- **Tax Protest**: He had signed the *Ramparts* War Tax Protest Manifesto during the Vietnam War, risking 15 years in prison.
- **The FBI Letter**: He claims the entity (VALIS) spoke through him to the authorities, resulting in a letter of commendation from FBI Assistant Director William Sullivan, effectively "extracting" him from the legal trap.
- **Anti-Tyranny Agent**: He views his letters to Congressman Wiggens and the *Wall Street Journal* (dictated by VALIS) as a direct participation in the "divine attack" on Nixon.

#### 3. The "Two Worlds" and the "Dokos"
Dick reiterates the binary vision of reality:
- **The Iron Empire (Rome)**: The "obscure oppression" mentioned in Daniel and Revelation.
- **The Palm Garden (Holy Land)**: The pastoral kingdom of "unchange" that represents the future/eternal state.
- **Dokos**: The Greek term for the "veil" of illusion that obscures these two hidden realities.

#### 4. The Cosmic Game
The universe is a contest between:
- **The Wise Mind (God)**: Sees everything and "wins every trick" (saves every soul).
- **The Dark Counterplayer (Blind Evil)**: Possesses immense physical power but ultimately aids the evolution of the divine plan.
- **Trinity Colors**: Logos (Red/Gold), Savior (Blue).
