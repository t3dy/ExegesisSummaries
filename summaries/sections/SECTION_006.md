# SUMMARY: SECTION_006
## March 4, 1975 — Letter to Dr. Robert Ornstein

Dick writes to Robert Ornstein (author of research on bilateral brain hemisphere function) explaining his experience through a neuroscientific lens. He describes his novel-in-progress, *TO SCARE THE DEAD* (later *VALIS*), outlining its premise: an ordinary American named Nicholas Brady has an Essene personality from ~45 AD awaken in his right brain hemisphere. This entity, "Thomas," informs Brady that the New Testament is in cypher, that "Jesus" is really Zagreus-Zeus, and that Christianity is a later form of Dionysiac mystery religion. The Essene personality gradually prepares Brady for the Parousia through *anamnesis* (remembering his own divinity) and introduces him to "Erasmus," a station in the noosphere.

Dick proposes that Dionysos, the god of vegetation who sleeps during Winter, has been slumbering in the right hemispheres of human brains from 45 AD to 1974 — and is now reborn. Winter = the historical period of political chaos and moral ruin. Spring = the awakening of Zagreus-Dionysos-Orpheus-Jesus within each person.

## key_claims
- The "2-3-74" experience may be explained as right-hemisphere activation.
- Christianity = a late form of Dionysiac mystery religion.
- "Thomas," the Essene personality, is now alive in Dick's right hemisphere.
- The noosphere is fully charged around Earth and can be consciously drawn from.

## scholarly_value
This section is invaluable as a direct mapping between Dick's mystical experience and the novel *VALIS*. The character of Nicholas Brady is evidently a proto-Horselover Fat, and the "Thomas" entity explains the Gnostic Twin motif central to the later novel. It also shows Dick strategically deploying neuroscience (Ornstein) as legitimization for his visions.
