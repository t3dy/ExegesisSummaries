# SECTION_013: The Parousia and the Absolute Landscape
## Date: November 5, 1975 (The Long Letter and Afterword)

### Section Overview
Section 13 is one of the most intellectually cohesive and radical sections of the *Exegesis*. Primarily composed of a massive letter to Gene Savoy (of the International Community of Christ) and subsequent theoretical notes, it represents Dick’s "final" (at this stage) synthesis of 3-74. He moves beyond mere theology into a **radical epistemology**: the theory that linear time is an illusion (*dokos*) and that the "real" world is the Biblical landscape of 70 A.D., which persists "above" or "behind" our 3-D movie reality.

---

### Core Themes & Breakthroughs

#### 1. The Epistemology of the "Dokos" (Illusion)
Dick concludes that the modern world (1974-76) is a **hypnotic 3-D movie** or "vistavision" projection designed to test human souls. 
- **The Truth Drug Catalyst**: He identifies his use of "Sodium Pentathol" during oral surgery as the "truth drug" that de-hypnotized him, allowing him to see the "authentic landscape" (Rome 70 A.D.) and speak its language (Koine Greek).
- **Vertical Axis**: The Parousia is not an event at the "end" of linear time, but an entry of the **Eternal/Absolute Reality** into our relative universe on a vertical axis.

#### 2. The 1974 Parousia as Historical Intervention
The "Second Coming" has occurred invisibly (*Parousia*), though it is not yet fully visible (*Apokalupsis*).
- **The Removal of the Tyrant**: The primary evidence for the Parousia is the unique, supernatural toppling of the "Prince of this World" (Richard Nixon) in 1974. Dick identifies Nixon as the **666 Beast/Ahab** figure.
- **The Stage of Elijah**: He identifies the fierce, zealous spirit that possessed him as **Elijah (John the Baptist)**, whose task was to "prepare the way" by unseating the usurper before the "Rightful King" (Christ/Sophia) claims the throne.

#### 3. The Identification of St. Sophia
Dick clarifies the "St. Sophia" message ("St. Sophia will be born again; she was not acceptable before").
- **Second Incarnation**: This refers to a new "fertilization" of the human race by the Holy Spirit.
- **The Failed Graft**: He theorizes that the original "graft" of the Spirit into humanity via the disciples failed due to Roman erasure; 3-74 was a "re-adoption" of the process.
- **Syzygy**: Sophia is the feminine aspect of the Logos, the "Pantocratrix" (Judge) of the end times.

#### 4. Key Allegories & Dreams
- **The Golden Groundsquirrel**: A poignant fable where Dick (the squirrel) is rescued from a "Roman trap" by a "mysterious gigantic figure" (Christ) who still carries the trauma of the cross.
- **The Zenith TV Set**: A dream where a dormant circuit (the **3rd eye**) registers the Parousia once a "dark green cellophane strip" (occluding veil) is removed.
- **The Helium Balloons**: A 3rd-eye vision of the elect ("the 144,000") zig-zagging up from the stagnant ocean of mass humanity.
